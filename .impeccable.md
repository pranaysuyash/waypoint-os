## Design Context

### Users
Travel agency operators — agents processing inbound inquiries, owners reviewing escalated decisions, and managers monitoring pipeline health. Used during business hours in offices or remote setups. Primary job: convert messy travel requests into precise, safe, actionable decisions using the two-loop pipeline (Intake → Decision → Strategy → Output).

### Brand Personality
**Precise. Data-driven. Authoritative.**

- **Precise**: Pixel-perfect alignment, tabular numerals for data, consistent spacing
- **Data-driven**: Charts, metrics, and pipeline visualization are first-class UI citizens
- **Authoritative**: Decisions backed by confidence scores, audit trails, and governance controls

Not "modern", "elegant", or "professional" — those are dead categories. This is a tool for people who make decisions with money and logistics on the line.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Dark-mode operations dashboard** with cartographic inspiration. Think: marine navigation instruments, flight operations centers, high-end CAD tools — not consumer travel sites.

- **References**: Marine chronometer dials, aviation glass cockpit displays, financial trading terminals
- **Anti-references**: Generic SaaS dashboards (Inter/blue/white), Expedia-style booking sites, AI "futuristic" slop (cyan-on-dark, purple gradients), overdesigned luxury (gold accents, script fonts)
- **Theme**: Dark mode (color-scheme: dark) — reduces eye strain for all-day use, fits office environments

### Design Principles
1. **Data is the hero** — typography must serve numbers, tables, and metrics first
2. **Trust through precision** — consistent spacing, tabular numerals, aligned decimals
3. **Dark surfaces, intentional accents** — no AI-gradient slop, brand cyan (#58a6ff) and aqua (#39d0d8) only
4. **No border-left stripes** — absolute ban on `border-left: 4px` accent stripes (current industry cards violate this)
5. **Fixed rem scale for app UI** — this is a dashboard, not a marketing page; use `rem` not fluid `clamp()` for workspace components
6. **IBM Plex Mono must go** — replace `--font-data` with a non-banned monospace that pairs with Sora/Rubik
