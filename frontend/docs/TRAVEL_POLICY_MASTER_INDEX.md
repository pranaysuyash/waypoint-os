## Series Overview

This series explores how Waypoint OS supports corporate travel management — from configurable policy rule engines and real-time compliance detection to employer duty of care obligations and travel program analytics. Corporate travel is a high-value segment for boutique agencies, requiring sophisticated policy management that large TMCs (SAP Concur, Navan) provide but small agencies currently cannot.

**Target Audience:** Product managers, backend engineers, corporate account managers, compliance officers

**Compliance Target:** Indian OSH Code, EU Directive 89/391/EEC, OSHA General Duty Clause

## Key Themes

### 1. Policy as Code
Travel policies are expressed as executable rules, not PDF documents. Rules evaluate in real-time as travelers search and book, providing just-in-time guidance instead of after-the-fact policing.

### 2. Duty of Care as Legal Obligation
Employers have legal obligations for employee safety during business travel. The platform provides risk assessment, traveler tracking, emergency protocols, and audit trails that demonstrate compliance.

### 3. Compliance Through Design
Rather than policing violations after the fact, the platform steers travelers toward compliant choices through smart defaults, filtered search results, and contextual policy explanations.

### 4. Data-Driven Policy Optimization
Analytics measure policy effectiveness, identify unrealistic rules, quantify savings, and recommend policy changes — turning travel management from intuition-based to data-driven.
