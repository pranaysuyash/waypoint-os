## Key Themes

- **Templates as force multipliers** — A well-designed template turns 30 minutes of work into 5 minutes. The best templates capture institutional knowledge.
- **Components are composable** — Hotels, activities, transfers, and meals are building blocks. Agents mix and match to create unique trips from standard components.
- **Quick-start doesn't mean low quality** — Express workflows have mandatory quality gates (visa checks, dietary needs, accessibility). Speed comes from smart defaults, not skipping steps.
- **Marketplace is community-driven** — The best templates come from agents who've done the trips. Community creation + platform curation = quality content.
- **Clone over create** — Reusing a successful past trip is faster and more reliable than starting from scratch. Clone + customize is the dominant workflow for experienced agents.

## Integration Points

- **Trip Builder** — Templates and components feed directly into the trip assembly workflow
- **Spine Pipeline** — AI extraction can match customer inquiries to existing templates
- **Pricing Engine** — Template pricing integrates with the dynamic pricing system
- **Booking Engine** — Quick-start flows create trips ready for the booking pipeline
- **Agent Training** — Templates serve as training material for new agents
- **Analytics & BI** — Template usage and conversion data feed into business intelligence
