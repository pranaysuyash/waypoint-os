## Key Themes

- **Knowledge is surfaced, not searched** — The best knowledge system delivers relevant information without the agent asking. Context-aware surfacing during trip building is the primary access pattern.
- **Agent tips are gold** — "Ask for the sea-facing room" is worth more than a 10-page destination guide. Quick tips from experienced agents are the most valuable content.
- **Content has a shelf life** — Visa rules change, restaurants close, prices increase. Every piece of knowledge has a review schedule and staleness indicator.
- **Community over documentation** — A Q&A where an expert answers "How do I handle this visa edge case?" in 5 minutes beats a 20-page visa manual.
- **Contribution must be frictionless** — If sharing knowledge takes more than 60 seconds, agents won't do it. Quick tips, one-click flags, and auto-generated templates are essential.

## Integration Points

- **Trip Builder** — Destination and supplier knowledge surfaced in-context during trip assembly
- **Agent Training** — Knowledge base is training material; contributions count toward certification
- **Spine Pipeline** — AI extraction can reference knowledge base for validation
- **Notification System** — Community questions and answers trigger notifications
- **Gamification** — Knowledge contributions earn points and badges
- **Customer Portal** — Select destination knowledge can be shared with customers (sanitized)
