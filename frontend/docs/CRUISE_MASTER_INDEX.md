## Key Questions Across Series

### Provider Landscape
- Which cruise lines serve Indian travelers?
- What are the popular departure ports?
- What are the regional cruise options?
- What API integrations exist?

### Product Types
- **Ocean Cruises** — Large ships, multiple destinations
- **River Cruises** — Smaller ships, inland waterways
- **Expedition Cruises** — Adventure, remote destinations
- **Luxury Cruises** — Premium service, smaller ships

### Integration Considerations
- Cabin categories and pricing
- Dynamic pricing like airlines
- Dining and activity selections
- Shore excursions booking
- Special requirements (mobility, dietary)
