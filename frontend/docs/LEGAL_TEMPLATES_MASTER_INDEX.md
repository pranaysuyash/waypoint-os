## Series Overview

This series covers the legal foundation of running a travel agency in India — from required business registrations and customer-facing documents (booking T&C, invoices, vouchers) to cancellation policies, liability frameworks, and consumer protection compliance. Most agencies operate with outdated or missing legal documents, exposing them to consumer disputes, GST penalties, and liability claims.

**Target Audience:** Agency owners, operations managers, compliance teams, legal advisors

**Key Insight:** The average Indian travel agency has 3-5 legal documents that haven't been updated since creation. With the Consumer Protection Act 2019, DPDP Act 2023, and evolving GST/TCS rules, outdated terms are a ticking time bomb. A single consumer dispute (₹50K-2L typical award) costs more than a decade of legal template maintenance. System-generated, always-current legal documents are both a compliance tool and a competitive advantage.

## Key Themes

### 1. Templates, Not Blank Pages
Every agency needs the same core legal documents. Rather than each agency drafting from scratch, Waypoint OS provides standardized, legally-reviewed templates customized per agency (name, GSTIN, terms). Templates update automatically when regulations change.

### 2. Cancellation Policies Must Be Specific
Vague cancellation policies lead to disputes. Specific tiers (45+ days, 30-45, 15-29, 7-14, <7) with component-level exceptions (airline fares have their own rules) are defensible in consumer court and prevent misunderstandings.

### 3. Liability Limitation Is Essential
Agencies can't be responsible for airline delays or hotel quality, but they ARE responsible for booking accuracy and communication. Clear liability limitation (capped at booking value, excluding consequential losses) protects the agency while being fair to customers.

### 4. GST and TCS Are Non-Negotiable
GST on travel services and TCS on international packages are legal requirements with penalties for non-compliance. Automated invoice generation and TCS collection tracking prevents costly errors.
