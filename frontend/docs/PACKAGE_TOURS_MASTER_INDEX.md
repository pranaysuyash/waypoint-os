## Key Questions Across Series

### Provider Landscape
- Who are the major tour operators for Indian travelers?
- What are the outbound vs. inbound operators?
- What API integrations exist?
- What are the commission structures?

### Product Types
- **Fixed Departure Packages** — Set dates, guaranteed departures
- **Flexible Packages** — Date ranges, customizable
- **Group Tours** — Large groups, tour guides
- **Independent Packages** — Flights + hotels booked together
- **Special Interest** — Adventure, pilgrimage, honeymoon, wildlife

### Integration Considerations
- Static inventory vs. live availability
- Pricing components (land + air)
- Single supplier vs. multi-component
- Lead passenger requirements
- Group size minimums
