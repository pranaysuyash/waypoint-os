# Customer Lifetime Value & Retention — Master Index

> Research on CLV modeling, churn prediction, retention strategies, and personalized engagement for travel agencies.

---

## Series Overview

This series covers the science of customer retention in travel — from calculating lifetime value and predicting churn to designing retention strategies, win-back campaigns, and personalized engagement that maximizes long-term revenue.

**Target Audience:** Product managers, marketing teams, CRM engineers, agency owners

**Key Constraint:** Travel purchases are infrequent (1-3x/year), making churn detection harder than in e-commerce. Retention must be relationship-driven, not just discount-driven.
