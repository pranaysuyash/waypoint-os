## Key Themes

- **Measure first, reduce second** — Every trip gets a carbon footprint calculation. You can't reduce what you don't measure. DEFRA/ICAO methodologies provide credible baselines.
- **Optional, not obligatory** — Sustainability is offered as a choice, not a mandate. Carbon offsets, eco-certified hotels, and green alternatives are suggestions, not requirements.
- **Transparency over perfection** — Emission estimates are disclosed with confidence levels and methodology. Acknowledging uncertainty is better than claiming false precision.
- **Corporate demand drives adoption** — BRSR, ESG, and Scope 3 reporting requirements create pull from corporate clients. Sustainability features become a B2B differentiator.
- **India-first approach** — Leverage IGBC certifications, Indian emission factors, and local offset projects. International standards adapted for the Indian travel market.

## Integration Points

- **Trip Builder** — Carbon footprint calculated per component during trip assembly
- **Booking Engine** — Carbon offset offered as optional add-on during booking
- **Supplier Integration** — Eco-certification data fetched and displayed in search results
- **Document Generation** — Sustainability section in quotes, itineraries, and invoices
- **Analytics & BI** — ESG dashboards, emission tracking, corporate sustainability reports
- **Customer Portal** — Post-trip sustainability impact report and offset certificates
