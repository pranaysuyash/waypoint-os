## Series Overview

This series covers pet-friendly travel — the emerging segment where pet owners (30M+ Indian households) bring their companion animals on vacation. From pet-friendly accommodation verification and vaccination tracking to international pet transport logistics and destination pet services, pet travel is a high-premium niche (20-40% spend premium) with fierce customer loyalty.

**Target Audience:** Product managers, agents, operations managers

**Key Insight:** Pet owners choose their travel based on whether their pet can come. A pet-friendly Manali cottage at ₹8,000/night beats a better ₹5,000/night cottage that doesn't allow pets. Pet owners are the most brand-loyal travel segment — get their dog's first trip right and they return for every vacation, spending 20-40% more each time.

## Key Themes

### 1. Pet Comes First in Decision-Making
Pet owners prioritize pet-friendliness over price, location, and amenities. The accommodation that welcomes Bruno gets the booking, even if it costs 40% more. Design for the pet, sell to the owner.

### 2. Documentation Is Complex but Mandatory
International pet travel requires 4-6 months of preparation: microchip, rabies vaccination, titer tests, import permits, health certificates. One missing document means denied boarding or quarantine. The platform must track and manage this complexity.

### 3. Safety and Welfare Non-Negotiable
Not every pet should travel. Elderly dogs, anxious cats, and unvaccinated puppies shouldn't be on flights. The agent's role includes honest assessment of whether travel is appropriate for the specific animal.

### 4. Destination Pet Services Are the Differentiator
Knowing the nearest vet, listing pet-friendly restaurants, arranging a dog walker during a museum visit — these micro-services transform a pet-tolerant trip into a pet-welcoming experience.
