## Key Themes

- **Short surveys, high response rates** — Every survey is 3-5 questions, 2-3 minutes. WhatsApp-native one-click ratings maximize response rates.
- **Close the feedback loop** — Every piece of customer feedback gets acknowledged, analyzed, acted upon, and communicated back. Customers see that their voice matters.
- **Agent satisfaction drives customer satisfaction** — There's a direct correlation between agent platform satisfaction and customer NPS. Improving agent tools improves customer outcomes.
- **VoC is multi-source** — Surveys alone aren't enough. Chat transcripts, support tickets, social media, and behavioral data all contribute to customer understanding.
- **Feedback is transparent** — Agents can see the product roadmap and track their suggestions. Transparency builds trust and encourages more feedback.

## Integration Points

- **Notification System** — Survey distribution uses WhatsApp and email channels
- **Analytics & BI** — NPS and CSAT data feed into the data warehouse
- **Agent Training** — Low-scoring areas in customer feedback identify training needs
- **Gamification** — High CSAT scores contribute to agent achievement badges
- **CRM** — Customer satisfaction history is part of the customer profile
- **Workflow Automation** — Negative feedback triggers escalation workflows
