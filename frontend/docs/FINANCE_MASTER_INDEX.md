## Key Themes

- **Every trip is a profit center** — Travel agencies live on thin margins (8-15% net). Per-trip profitability tracking isn't optional — it's survival. Know which trips make money and which don't.
- **Cash flow is king** — A profitable agency can go bankrupt from poor cash flow. Managing the gap between customer collection and supplier payment is the core financial challenge.
- **Accounting must be travel-native** — Generic accounting software doesn't understand trip advances, BSP settlement, or seasonal cash cycles. The platform must speak travel finance.
- **Tally integration is mandatory** — 70%+ of Indian SMEs use Tally. The platform generates journal entries; Tally handles statutory filing and audit. Don't fight the ecosystem.
- **Tax compliance is automated** — GST, TDS, and TCS are complex but rule-based. Auto-generate returns, auto-calculate liabilities, auto-remind deadlines. The platform is the agency's accountant.

## Integration Points

- **Booking Engine** — Every booking creates journal entries (revenue, cost, GST, advances)
- **Payment Processing** — Payment collections and supplier payments feed cash flow
- **Supplier Integration** — Supplier invoices and BSP data feed cost of sales
- **Commission Management** — Agent commissions as expense entries
- **Regulatory & Licensing** — Tax filing deadlines from compliance calendar
- **Analytics & BI** — Financial dashboards and profitability reports
- **Document Generation** — Tax invoices, financial statements, audit reports
