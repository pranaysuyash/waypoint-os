## Key Questions Across Series

### Provider Landscape
- How do we integrate with Indian Railways?
- What about international rail (Eurail, JR Pass)?
- What are the booking APIs?
- Who offers rail passes?

### Product Types
- **Point-to-Point Tickets** — Single journey tickets
- **Rail Passes** — Multi-day/multi-journey passes
- **Reserved Seating** — Specific seat assignments
- **Sleeper Berths** — Overnight trains

### Integration Considerations
- Quota systems (especially in India)
- Tatkal (emergency) booking
- Waitlist management
- Dynamic pricing
- Cross-border complexity
