## Part 3: Future Feature Visions

### 3.1 Autonomous Trip Management

**The Vision:** AI handles 80% of trips end-to-end

**How It Works:**

```
1. Customer sends inquiry
   ↓
2. AI extracts requirements (97% accuracy)
   ↓
3. AI searches supplier APIs for options
   ↓
4. AI evaluates scenarios (200+ factors)
   ↓
5. AI generates quote and sends to customer
   ↓
6. AI answers questions via chat
   ↓
7. Customer confirms → AI books everything
   ↓
8. AI handles all changes and coordination
   ↓
9. Human agent only involved for:
   - Complex cases (<20%)
   - Customer requests
   - Exception handling
```

**Timeline's Role:**
- Complete audit trail of AI decisions
- Human can inspect at any point
- Learning data for AI improvement
- Customer transparency
