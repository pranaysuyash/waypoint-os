## Series Overview

This series covers student and youth travel — the budget-conscious, experience-hungry segment that represents future high-value customers. From school educational tours with full minor-compliance protocols to college graduation trips with split-payment group bookings, student travel is where lifetime customer relationships begin. Today's Goa graduation trip customer is tomorrow's honeymoon and corporate travel buyer.

**Target Audience:** Product managers, operations managers, agents

**Key Insight:** A college student's first agency-booked trip to Goa (₹18K) creates a relationship that, over 15 years, can be worth ₹25+ lakh: honeymoon (₹4L), anniversary trips (₹3L each), family holidays (₹2L each), corporate retreats (₹5L+). Student travel is not a budget segment — it's an investment in future CLV. Plus, one great graduation trip generates 5-8 referrals.

## Key Themes

### 1. First Trip Creates Lifetime Customers
The first agency experience shapes a young traveler's expectations forever. A smooth, well-organized college trip to Thailand makes them trust the agency for their honeymoon, family trips, and corporate travel for decades.

### 2. Group Dynamics Drive Everything
Student travel is group travel. The group leader makes decisions, peer influence drives choices, and group payment collection is the operational bottleneck. Building for group dynamics (not individual booking) is essential.

### 3. Safety Is Non-Negotiable for Minors
School tours with students under 18 require rigorous compliance: parental consent, proper documentation, medical information, adult-to-student ratios, and 24/7 monitoring. One safety incident can end the agency's school tour business.

### 4. Instagram Drives Discovery
Gen-Z discovers travel destinations on Instagram and YouTube, not through traditional channels. The agency must meet young travelers where they are — Reels, Stories, and influencer partnerships.
