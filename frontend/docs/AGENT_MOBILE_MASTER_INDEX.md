## Series Overview

This series covers the agent mobile experience — the phone-first workflow that matches how Indian travel agents actually work. Most agents live in WhatsApp, not a desktop workspace. They confirm bookings between phone calls, send proposals from the back of an auto-rickshaw, and handle emergencies while walking through an airport with a tour group. The mobile experience isn't a smaller version of the desktop — it's a fundamentally different interaction model: notification-driven, WhatsApp-integrated, voice-first, and optimized for ₹10K Android phones on patchy 4G networks.

**Target Audience:** Product managers, UX designers, engineering leads

**Key Insight:** An Indian travel agent handles 80% of their daily work from their phone — but the current experience is fragmented across WhatsApp, phone calls, spreadsheets, and mental notes. A mobile-first agent tool that brings trip management, customer communication, booking actions, and payment tracking into one notification-driven home screen saves 2-3 hours per day and eliminates the "I forgot to follow up" problem that loses customers.

## Key Themes

### 1. Notification-Driven, Not App-Driven
The agent doesn't open the app to check status — the app pushes actionable notifications: "Confirm Sharma booking [Confirm] [Decline]" with one-tap action. The notification IS the interface for 80% of daily tasks.

### 2. WhatsApp Is the Operating System
Agents won't leave WhatsApp. The best mobile experience integrates into WhatsApp — actions triggered from chat context, proposals sent as PDFs in conversation, confirmations via WhatsApp buttons.

### 3. Voice and Camera as Input
Typing on a phone is slow. Voice dictation for replies, voice commands for navigation, and camera for document scanning make the phone faster than desktop for common tasks.

### 4. Optimized for ₹10K Android Phones
The agent's phone is not a flagship device. The mobile experience must be lightweight, battery-efficient, data-efficient, and fast on 2-3GB RAM Android phones with patchy 4G connectivity.
