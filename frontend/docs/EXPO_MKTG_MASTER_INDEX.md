## Key Themes

### 1. Conversations Over Collateral
The biggest ROI driver at events is agent conversation quality, not booth size. A 2-agent team having genuine conversations converts 3x better than a flashy booth with a lucky draw. Train agents to qualify and capture in real-time.

### 2. Speed of Follow-Up
Event leads have a 48-hour half-life — if you don't reach out within 24 hours, they've forgotten you. Hot leads get a call the same evening; warm leads get WhatsApp within 12 hours. Automated sequences handle the rest.

### 3. Local Events Beat National Expos
Housing society events (₹5K cost, 20-30% conversion) outperform national expos (₹2L+ cost, 8-12% conversion) on ROI. For smaller agencies, local community presence is the smarter investment.

### 4. 90-Day Attribution Window
Event impact extends well beyond the event dates. Track leads for 90 days and attribute bookings to the event even if the customer came through WhatsApp or website later. Multi-touch tracking captures the full impact.
