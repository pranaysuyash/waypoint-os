# Customer Onboarding Journey — Master Index

> Research on first-time customer experience, from discovery and trust-building to profile capture, proposal delivery, booking commitment, and pre-trip handholding for the Waypoint OS platform.

---

## Series Overview

This series covers the customer onboarding journey — the critical first experience that determines whether an inquiry becomes a booking. From the first WhatsApp message to the pre-trip call 2 days before departure, every touchpoint either builds or erodes trust. Agencies that respond in under 15 minutes, personalize proposals, and follow up persistently convert at 2-3x the rate of slow, generic competitors.

**Target Audience:** Product managers, sales agents, marketing leads

**Key Insight:** 70% of conversions happen after the 3rd-5th follow-up, but most agents give up after 1-2 attempts. The single biggest conversion lever isn't better proposals or lower prices — it's systematic, value-adding follow-up that keeps the agency top-of-mind without being pushy.

## Key Themes

### 1. Speed Wins the Inquiry
The first agent to respond meaningfully wins the customer. <15 minutes response time converts at 3x vs. >2 hours. Auto-acknowledge immediately, then follow up with substance.

### 2. Trust Before Transaction
First-time customers are choosing between your agency and 10 others (or Booking.com). Social proof, credentials, transparency, and personal expertise must be demonstrated before asking for money.

### 3. Progressive Profiling Over Interrogation
Ask 3 questions now, 3 more later, rest after booking. Every question is friction. Collect only what's needed for the current stage — the rest comes naturally as the relationship develops.

### 4. Referral Is the Highest-Converting Channel
Referred customers convert at 50-60% because trust is pre-built. Every onboarding experience should be designed to generate a referral — the next customer's journey starts with this customer's satisfaction.
