# Research Roadmap: Self-Evolving Operational Systems

**Status**: Research/Draft
**Area**: Autonomous Optimization, Synthetic Data & Logic Distillation

---

## 1. Context: The 'Recursive-Improvement' Layer
The final frontier is a system that doesn't just execute logic, but **improves** its own logic. This track focuses on the "Self-Correction Engine" that analyzes its own performance and autonomously updates its playbooks.

## 2. Exploration Tracks (Avenues)

### [A] Synthetic Scenario Generation (Batch 06+)
- **The 'Edge-Case' Generator**: AI agents analyzing the existing 374 scenarios to find "Missing Intersections" (e.g., "Bankruptcy + Minor Traveling + Coup").
- **The 'Adversarial-Simulator'**: Running agents against each other in a virtual GDS environment to find logical "Escape Paths" where the system's rules are too loose.

### [B] Agent-Led Policy Refinement
- **The 'Policy-Drift' Detector**: Monitoring instances where humans consistently override the AI's suggestion, and autonomously proposing a policy update to match human intent.
- **The 'Constraint-Evolution' Loop**: Automatically tightening or loosening budget/risk constraints based on the `Financial_Audit` success rate.

### [C] Inference Distillation (Knowledge Compression)
- **Ultra-to-Flash Distillation**: Using high-cost Tier 3 reasoning (o1) to generate a massive dataset of "Perfect Recoveries," then fine-tuning a Tier 1 Flash model to replicate that reasoning at 1/100th the cost.
- **The 'Lesson-Learned' Payload**: Every successful recovery generates a "Lesson" that is indexed in the Vector-DB for future few-shot injection.

## 4. Long-Term Vision: The 'Zero-Day' Integrity
A system that can predict and simulate a crisis (like a pandemic or mass-strike) before it happens, and has the "Synthetic Playbook" ready before the first traveler is affected.
