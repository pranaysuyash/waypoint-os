# Con Spec: Agentic 'Traveler-Psychographic' Profiler (CON-REAL-029)

**Status**: Research/Draft
**Area**: Traveler Intelligence & Hyper-Personalization

---

## 1. The Problem: "The Surface-Level-Preference-Trap"
Most travel agents know their traveler's surface preferences: "Likes luxury hotels, prefers window seats, vegetarian." This is "Transactional-Knowledge." It fails to capture the "Psychographic-Layer" — the traveler's core values, identity aspirations, emotional triggers, and the hidden narrative they are trying to live through travel. Without this layer, itineraries feel "Generic-Luxury" rather than "Deeply-Resonant."

## 2. The Solution: 'Deep-Persona-Protocol' (DPP)

The DPP acts as the "Traveler-Psychologist."

### Profiling Actions:

1.  **Multi-Signal-Psychographic-Capture**:
    *   **Action**: Synthesizing signals from booking history, feedback language analysis, conversation tone, preferred activities, and optional traveler-completed "Persona-Surveys" to build a multi-dimensional profile.
2.  **Identity-Archetype-Mapping**:
    *   **Action**: Classifying the traveler across dimensions: "Explorer vs. Curator," "Status-Signal vs. Authentic-Seeker," "Adventure-Appetite Level," "Social-Charge vs. Solitude-Recharge," and "Legacy-Builder vs. Present-Hedonist."
3.  **Desire-Prediction-Engine**:
    *   **Action**: Using the psychographic profile to predict trip concepts the traveler would love but has never explicitly requested (e.g., "This traveler's 'Solitude-Recharger' archetype and 'Nature-Spirituality' signal suggests they would deeply value a private silent retreat in Kyoto before the Tokyo leg").
4.  **Communication-Style-Adaptation**:
    *   **Action**: Adapting all AI-generated communication to match the traveler's "Psychographic-Resonance-Language" (e.g., not using "5-star property" for an "Authentic-Seeker" who responds to "Locally-rooted boutique hideaway").

## 4. Key Logic Rules

- **Rule 1: The 'Consent-First' Profile**: Psychographic profiles MUST be built only from data the traveler has explicitly shared or consented to have analyzed. Optional surveys must clearly explain their purpose.
- **Rule 2: The 'No-Manipulation' Boundary**: The profile MUST be used only to serve the traveler's genuine interests. It MUST NOT be used to upsell products that exploit psychological vulnerabilities.
- **Rule 3: Profile-Transparency-Right**: Travelers MUST have the right to view, correct, and delete their psychographic profile at any time.

## 5. Success Metrics (Psychographic)

- **Itinerary-Resonance-Score**: Traveler satisfaction ratings for trips designed using psychographic vs. transactional profiles.
- **Surprise-Delight-Rate**: % of "Desire-Predicted" trips that travelers rated as "exceeding expectations."
- **Traveler-LTV-Growth**: % increase in lifetime value for travelers with active, high-confidence psychographic profiles.
