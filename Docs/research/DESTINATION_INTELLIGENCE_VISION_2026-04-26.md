## Data Categories — The Full Inventory

Every destination should eventually have structured data for:

### Environmental
- Weather (hourly forecast, historical patterns, micro-climate)
- Air quality / AQI (current, forecast, seasonal patterns)
- Water quality (tap safety, beach quality, swimming advisories)
- UV index (sun exposure risk, peak hours)
- Pollen count (allergy data)
- Noise levels (by neighborhood, time of day)
- Natural disaster risk (earthquake, tsunami, volcano, wildfire, flood, hurricane)

### Safety & Security
- Crime data (tourist-specific: theft, scam, assault — by neighborhood, time of day)
- Political stability (protests, elections, civil unrest)
- Government travel advisories (US, UK, AU, India — real-time)
- Terrorism risk level
- LGBTQ+ safety (legal status, social acceptance)
- Solo female safety (harassment data, reliable transport)
- Emergency services (police, ambulance, fire — numbers, quality, response time)
- Scam database (known tourist scams per city)

### Health & Medical
- Disease outbreaks (dengue, malaria, COVID, cholera — real-time)
- Vaccination requirements (by nationality × destination)
- Hospital quality and location (English-speaking staff, tourist-oriented)
- Pharmacy availability (24-hour, medication restrictions by country)
- Altitude and acclimatization requirements
- Dangerous wildlife (jellyfish, snakes, mosquitoes — seasonal)

### Transport & Connectivity
- Flight reliability (on-time performance by route, airline, season)
- Airport experience (terminals, lounges, transport, Wi-Fi, security wait)
- Public transit quality (metro, bus — coverage, frequency, safety, tourist-friendliness)
- Walkability score (per city, per neighborhood)
- Ride-hail availability (Uber, Grab, Bolt, Careem — per city)
- Internet speed (hotel, cafe, coworking — per city)
- SIM/eSIM availability (providers, plans, activation)
- Driving conditions (road quality, driving side, license requirements)

### Cost & Economy
- Daily budget by travel style (backpacker, mid-range, luxury)
- Meal costs (breakfast, lunch, dinner, coffee, beer — by venue type)
- Transport costs (taxi/km, metro ride, airport transfer)
- Tipping customs (who, how much, when not to)
- Currency exchange (best methods, ATM fees, card acceptance)
- Tax refund eligibility (VAT refund thresholds, process)
- Seasonal price variation (when is it cheapest?)

### Culture & Customs
- Local laws that surprise tourists (per country)
- Dress code requirements (religious sites, restaurants, beaches)
- Alcohol and drug laws
- Photography restrictions
- Drone regulations
- Religious observance impact (Ramadan, Sabbath, holidays)
- Bargaining norms
- Greeting and social etiquette
- Taboo topics
- Business hours and weekend structure

### Practical Services
- Embassy/consulate directory (by nationality × destination)
- Lost passport procedures (per country)
- Police report filing process (for insurance claims)
- Laundry, dry cleaning availability
- Power outlet types and voltage
- Local apps to download

### Experiences & Activities
- Crowd density (peak times at attractions)
- Event and festival calendar (with travel impact)
- Nightlife guide (bars, clubs, safety, hours)
- Wildlife calendar (whale watching, migration, nesting)
- Photography spots (with best times, permits, crowd tips)
- Shopping guide (local products, markets, duty-free)
- Sunrise/sunset and golden hour times
- National park access and permits

### Accommodation Intelligence
- Neighborhood guides (by vibe, budget, safety)
- Hotel renovation tracking
- Noise levels by area
- Airbnb regulations by city

## Data Acquisition Strategy

### Free/Open Data
- Weather: OpenWeather, Open-Meteo, Copernicus
- AQI: OpenAQ, government stations
- Earthquakes: USGS, EMSC
- Transit: GTFS feeds (available for 1,000+ cities)
- Disease: WHO, CDC, HealthMap
- Travel advisories: Government APIs (State Dept, FCDO, DFAT, MEA)

### Paid/Premium Data
- Weather: Tomorrow.io, Weatherbit (higher resolution, forecasting)
- Flights: Cirium, OAG (reliability data, schedules)
- Safety: GeoSure, OSAC (structured safety scores)
- Hotels: OTA APIs (pricing, availability)
- Crowd: Placer.ai, Google Popular Times

### Proprietary Data (Our Moat)
- Agent-verified supplier ratings
- Traveler safety ratings per destination
- Booking patterns and seasonal trends
- Client satisfaction correlated with destination conditions
- Trip quality scores mapped to weather/crowd/AQI at time of travel

### Partnerships
- Tourism boards: Official data in exchange for promotion
- Insurance companies: Risk data in exchange for embedded insurance
- Airlines/hotels: Pricing data in exchange for distribution
