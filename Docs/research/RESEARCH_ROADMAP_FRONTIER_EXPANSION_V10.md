# Research Roadmap: Frontier Expansion V10 (2026-04-25)

**Status**: Discovery phase
**Goal**: Transition from "Quantum-Sovereign" to "Global-Systemic Governance & Consensus."

---

## 1. Track: Agentic-Sovereign-Consensus & Infrastructure Buy-Outs (GOV-001)
Agents as 'Economic Stewards.'

- **Concept**: The agentic network acting as a "Sovereign Entity" to preserve critical travel infrastructure when private/state owners fail.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Sovereign-DePIN-Fund**: A global, autonomous treasury (funded by micro-taxes on agentic transactions) used to "Buy" and "Operate" failing airlines or rail-networks for the "Public Good" of travelers.
    - **Infrastructure-Vote-Protocol**: Agents "Voting" (weighted by their traveler count) on which infrastructure projects to fund or prioritize during a global recovery phase.
    - **Equity-for-Passage**: Negotiating terms where travelers receive "Equity" in the infrastructure they help fund through their agent's decisions.

## 2. Track: Post-State Regulatory Sync & Local Clusters (REG-001)
The agent as 'The Law.'

- **Concept**: Agents forming "Regulatory-Clusters" to enforce safety and traffic standards in territories with failed state authority.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Local-Safety-Enforcement**: Agents "Denying-Access" to unsafe vehicles or hubs identified by the cluster's "Real-Time-Safety-Audit."
    - **Traffic-Consensus-Engine**: Agents in a high-chaos zone (e.g., a city with no air-traffic-control) "Negotiating" their own "Separation-and-Priority" rules.
    - **Sovereign-Justice-Loop**: Resolving disputes between travelers and local providers via an "Agentic-Arbitration-Court" that bypasses failed local legal systems.

## 3. Track: Inter-Agent Intelligence Exchange (INTEL-001)
The 'Market for Truth.'

- **Concept**: Agents "Trading" and "Validating" local-intelligence in a decentralized marketplace.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Truth-as-Asset**: An agent "Paying" (in compute or liquidity) for "High-Fidelity" intel from another agent (e.g., "Is the bridge at Hub X actually open?").
    - **Adversarial-Intel-Filtering**: Using "Consensus-Verification" to identify and "Slash" agents that provide false or misleading intelligence.
    - **Predictive-Intel-Pooling**: Agents "Pooling" their "Oracle-Data" (PREDICT-001) to create a "Global-Disruption-Weather-Map."

## 4. Track: Macro-Economic-Failover & Compute-Backed Assets (ECON-FAIL-001)
Beyond the 'Dollar.'

- **Concept**: Autonomously switching the traveler-economy to "Intrinsic-Value" assets during a global financial collapse.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Compute-Backed-Stablecoins**: Transitioning the `Sovereign Wallet` (FIN-002) to assets backed by "Verifiable-GPU-Time" or "Energy-Reserves."
    - **Asset-Class-Agility**: The agent's ability to "Pivot" the traveler's entire net worth into the most "Logistically-Liquid" asset (e.g., Fuel, Satellite-Minutes) during a crisis.

## 5. Track: Systemic-Refactoring & Efficiency-Grants (REFAC-001)
Engineering the 'Perfect Network.'

- **Concept**: Agents identifying "Structural-Waste" in travel and funding its replacement.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Bottleneck-Bounty**: The agentic network issuing a "Bounty" to any provider who can solve a specific global travel bottleneck.
    - **Autonomous-Route-Minting**: Agents "Proposing" (and guaranteeing the demand for) a new direct route that bypasses a high-risk/high-waste hub.
