# Research Roadmap: Frontier Expansion V5 (2026-04-25)

**Status**: Discovery phase
**Goal**: Transition from "Sovereign Agency" to "Macro-Systemic & Deep-Space Intelligence."

---

## 1. Track: Geopolitical Statecraft & Micro-Diplomacy (STATE-001)
The agent as a 'Diplomatic Entity' in high-chaos zones.

- **Concept**: Navigating travel in territories where central state authority has collapsed or borders are in flux.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Micro-Authority Negotiation**: Protocols for agents to "Bargain" with local gatekeepers (e.g., private security, municipal councils) for safe passage or fuel.
    - **Extraction-Path-Autonomy**: Autonomously identifying and securing "Non-Traditional" exit routes (e.g., private air-strips, maritime corridors) when commercial hubs close.
    - **Digital Passport Sovereignty**: Using decentralized identity (DID) to prove traveler status to local authorities when central servers are offline.

## 2. Track: Black-Box Mesh Agency (BLACK-001)
Post-Internet systemic survival.

- **Concept**: Maintaining global human logistics during a total internet/backbone collapse (e.g., Solar Storm, Global Cyber-war).
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Satellite-to-Mesh Bridge**: Using low-bandwidth satellite links (e.g., Starlink/Iridium) to "Sync" critical recovery packets between regional mesh networks.
    - **Physical Evidence Handover**: Agents generating "Printable-Verification-Tokens" that can be physically handed to other agents/humans to prove PNR/Wallet status offline.
    - **Autonomous Convergence Points**: Pre-defining "Emergency Gathering Hubs" where agents guide travelers to meet when all digital communication fails.

## 3. Track: Quantum-Optimal Chaos Resolution (QUANTUM-001)
Solving 'The Great Hub Collapse' via quantum logic.

- **Concept**: Moving from "Local Heuristics" (fixing one traveler) to "Global Optimums" (fixing the entire network) simultaneously.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Q-Bit State Representation**: Modeling 100,000 broken itineraries as a single quantum state to find the "Global Minimum" of traveler dissatisfaction.
    - **Entangled-Negotiation**: Multiple agents "Entangling" their decisions so that re-booking Traveler A instantly optimizes the path for Traveler B-Z.
    - **Simulated Annealing for Logistics**: Using quantum-inspired algorithms to solve the "Sudden-Capacity-Drop" problem in real-time.

## 4. Track: Digital Twin & Machine-Traveler Logistics (TWIN-001)
Agency for the post-human economy.

- **Concept**: Managing the travel of "Non-Human Assets" (e.g., robotic fleets, mobile server clusters, autonomous cargo) using the same integrity logic.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Maintenance-Gated Routing**: Autonomously diverting a robotic fleet if the "Repair-Window" at the destination is missed.
    - **Resource-Consumption-Sync**: Syncing travel speed with the asset's "Battery/Fuel" state and "Cooling" requirements.
    - **Asset-to-Asset Negotiation**: Autonomous cargo "Negotiating" with autonomous ships for slot allocation.

## 5. Track: Relativistic & Multi-Year Logistics (LONG-001)
Deep-space and multi-generational travel logic.

- **Concept**: Preparing for the ultimate frontier—disruptions where "Time" is a variable.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Relativistic-Time-Sync**: Managing PNR and financial interest accumulation when "Traveler-Time" diverges from "Earth-Time" during high-velocity transit.
    - **Cryo-Interruption-Protocol**: Logic for "Emergency-Waking" a traveler due to a systemic mission failure or destination collapse.
    - **Generational-Handoff**: Passing 'Agency Sovereignty' from parent to child during a multi-decade transit (Sovereign Legacy Transfer).
