# Research Roadmap: Frontier Expansion V9 (2026-04-25)

**Status**: Discovery phase
**Goal**: Transition from "Existential" to "Quantum-Sovereign & Multi-Temporal Intelligence."

---

## 1. Track: Quantum-Sovereign Identity & Intent-Locking (Q-ID-001)
Agency in the post-decryption era.

- **Concept**: Protecting "Traveler Intent" and "Sovereign Identity" against quantum-grade decryption and spoofing.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **QKD Agentic Handshakes**: Using "Quantum-Key-Distribution" (QKD) for secure, un-hackable communication between a traveler's agent and a sanctuary hub.
    - **Entangled-Identity-Verification**: Verifying a traveler's identity using "Quantum-Entanglement" markers that cannot be replicated or spoofed by classical AI.
    - **Immutable-Intent-Anchors**: Locking "Core Travel Goals" into a quantum-secure vault that even the agent cannot modify without a dual-key neural signal.

## 2. Track: Post-Quantum Adversarial Integrity (Q-ADV-001)
Defending against the 'Quantum Scammer.'

- **Concept**: Detecting supply-chain fraud and "Dark Patterns" that leverage quantum-computing to bypass classical security.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Quantum-Fraud-Throttling**: Identifying "Probability-Anomalies" in flight-pricing or availability that suggest a "Quantum-Arbitrage-Bot" is manipulating the market.
    - **Adversarial-Q-Sim**: Running "Quantum-Simulations" of the supply chain to predict where a quantum-adversary might strike next.

## 3. Track: Multi-Temporal Itinerary Management (TIME-001)
Logistics across 'Subjective-Time' zones.

- **Concept**: Managing travel where "Time" itself is non-uniform (e.g., Earth-time vs. High-Velocity-time vs. Lunar-Gravity-time).
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Relativistic-Buffer-Sync**: Autonomously calculating "Clock-Drift" for travelers on hypersonic or sub-orbital routes to ensure ground-logistics (cars, hotels) are synced to their "Subjective-Arrival."
    - **Biological-Time-Dilation-Triage**: Adjusting recovery protocols (BIO-001) for travelers who have experienced significant "Time-Drift" compared to their destination.

## 4. Track: Agentic-Diplomatic-Immunity (DIP-001)
The agent as a 'Neutral Carrier.'

- **Concept**: Establishing "Diplomatic Immunity" for agent-managed travelers during international conflicts.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Neutral-Logistics-Declaration**: A protocol for agents to declare a specific vehicle or path as a "Humanitarian-Logistics-Corridor" recognized by global agentic networks.
    - **Immunity-Token-Verification**: Local authorities "Scanning" an agent's "Immunity-Token" to grant safe passage without searching or detaining the traveler.

## 5. Track: Bio-Digital Memory-Lease (MEM-001)
Cognitive-load sharing.

- **Concept**: Travelers "Leasing" their idle cognitive capacity to the agent for "Heavy-Logic-Processing" during a crisis.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Neural-Compute-Pooling**: Multiple travelers in a crisis "Pooling" their neural-links to create a "Local-Supercomputer" for re-routing optimization.
    - **Cognitive-Sanctuary-Recovery**: The agent managing the "Recovery-of-Identity" after a heavy compute-lease period.
