# Con Spec: Agentic 'Life-Stage-Transition' Intelligence (CON-REAL-031)

**Status**: Research/Draft
**Area**: Traveler Life-Arc Awareness & Longitudinal Relationship Design

---

## 1. The Problem: "The Static-Traveler-Profile"
Most travel agents treat travelers as static archetypes. The traveler who booked a backpacker trip at 24 is treated the same way at 34 when they've just had their first child. The solo adventurer who lost a spouse is offered the same honeymoon suites she used to book with her partner. Without "Life-Arc-Awareness," the agency's recommendations feel tone-deaf and the relationship fractures at the exact moments when the traveler needs the most sensitive and intelligent support.

## 2. The Solution: 'Life-Arc-Protocol' (LAP)

The LAP acts as the "Life-Companion-Designer."

### Life-Stage Actions:

1.  **Life-Event-Signal-Detection**:
    *   **Action**: Monitoring for major life transition signals from authorized conversation sources, booking patterns, and optional profile updates: marriage, divorce, birth of a child, bereavement, retirement, career change, health diagnosis, "empty nest."
2.  **Archetype-Transition-Mapping**:
    *   **Action**: Defining known life-stage transitions and their corresponding travel philosophy shifts:
        - "New Parent → Safety-First, Flexibility-Max, Resort-Anchored"
        - "Recent Retiree → Time-Rich, Deeper-Slower, Meaning-Seeking"
        - "Bereaved Traveler → Solitude-Healing, No-Reminders-of-Past-Trips"
        - "Post-Divorce → Rediscovery, Solo-Empowerment, Agency-Building"
        - "Empty-Nester → Couple-Reconnect, Adventure-Reawakening"
3.  **Sensitive-Transition-Communication**:
    *   **Action**: When a life-stage transition is detected, the agent autonomously adjusts its communication register to be more human, slower-paced, and empathetic rather than pushing bookings.
4.  **Transition-Appropriate-Curation**:
    *   **Action**: Generating trip concepts specifically calibrated to the traveler's new life stage — not recycling old favorites that may now feel painful or irrelevant.

## 4. Key Logic Rules

- **Rule 1: The 'Grief-Brake'**: On detection of bereavement or divorce signals, the agent MUST NOT surface any trip marketing or promotions for a minimum 30-day "Silence-Period" unless the traveler explicitly requests travel options.
- **Rule 2: The 'Never-Assume' Standard**: The agent MUST treat all life-stage signals as hypotheses requiring human agent confirmation before dramatically shifting trip philosophy. A human must acknowledge the transition.
- **Rule 3: Empathy-Over-Revenue**: The agent MUST prioritize the traveler's emotional wellbeing over booking conversion at all life-stage transition moments.

## 5. Success Metrics (Life-Arc)

- **Post-Transition-Retention-Rate**: % of travelers who continue booking after a major life transition (vs. churning to a different agency).
- **Transition-Sensitivity-Score**: Traveler feedback rating on how well the agency handled their life stage change.
- **Life-Stage-LTV-Multiplier**: Average lifetime value increase for travelers whose profiles were actively managed across multiple life stages.
