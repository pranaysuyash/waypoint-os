**Status**: Research/Draft
**Area**: Augmented Human Logistics & Hardware Safety

## 1. The Problem: "The Environment-Component Clash"
A traveler with a "Critical Cybernetic Organ" (e.g., a synthetic heart with specific electromagnetic shielding) moves through a "High-Interference" transit zone or a country with a "Unstable Power Grid." If the local environment compromises the component's integrity, the traveler's life is at risk. Current travel systems ignore "Human-Hardware" requirements.

## 2. The Solution: 'Component-Compatibility-Audit' (CCA)

The CCA allows the agent to treat "Human-Cybernetics" as "Critical Infrastructure" that must be "Environmentally-Validated" before any booking.

### Hardware Actions:

1.  **EMI-Zone-Mapping**:
    *   **Action**: Comparing local "Radio-Frequency/Electromagnetic" telemetry at a destination hub against the traveler's "Hardware-Shielding-Spec."
2.  **Grid-Reliability-Sync**:
    *   **Action**: If the traveler requires a "Component-Charging-Node," the agent autonomously verifies the "Power-Grid-Uptime" and "Voltage-Compatibility" of the accommodation.
3.  **Emergency-Firmware-Bridge**:
    *   **Action**: If a component failure is detected, the agent autonomously identifies and routes the traveler to the nearest "Certified-Hardware-Bio-Clinic" (BIO-001).

## 4. Key Logic Rules

- **Rule 1: Component-Sovereignty**: Raw hardware serial numbers and specific firmware versions are kept on the "Traveler's Sovereign Device." The agent only receives "Minimum-Safety-Constraints."
- **Rule 2: Interference-Pre-emption**: If a route contains a "High-Risk-Zone" (e.g., a tunnel with specific magnetic resonance), the agent MUST propose a "Hardware-Safe" alternative, even if it adds 12 hours to the trip.
- **Rule 3: Maintenance-Sync**: The agent cross-references "Hardware-Service-Intervals" with the itinerary to ensure the traveler isn't in a "Resource-Desert" when a component update or service is due.

## 5. Success Metrics (Cyber-Integrity)

- **EMI-Incident-Rate**: 0 incidents of hardware interference during agent-led travel.
- **Hardware-Uptime**: % of trip duration where the traveler's critical components maintained "Optimal-Function."
- **Mitigation-Accuracy**: % of "Environmental-Risks" correctly identified and mitigated by the agent.
