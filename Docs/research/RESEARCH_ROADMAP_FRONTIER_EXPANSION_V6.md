# Research Roadmap: Frontier Expansion V6 (2026-04-25)

**Status**: Discovery phase
**Goal**: Transition from "Macro-Systemic" to "Bio-Digital & Planetary-Engineering Intelligence."

---

## 1. Track: BCI-Speed & Sub-Perceptual Agency (NEURAL-001)
The agent connected to the 'Mind-Share.'

- **Concept**: Leveraging Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI) for "Thought-Speed" decision loops.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Implicit-Approval-Sync**: The agent proposing re-booking options directly to the traveler's visual cortex and receiving "Implicit-Acceptance" via neural feedback loops.
    - **Emotional-Pre-emption**: Detecting "Pre-Incident-Anxiety" via BCI and autonomously initiating "Stress-Reduction-Logistics" (e.g., booking a lounge, ordering a familiar meal) before the traveler consciously realizes they are stressed.
    - **Dream-State-Briefing**: Uploading "Trip-Recovery-Briefings" during REM sleep so the traveler wakes up with a pre-conscious understanding of the day's plan.

## 2. Track: Cybernetic-Hardware Integrity (CYBER-001)
Logistics for the 'Augmented Traveler.'

- **Concept**: Managing travel for humans with "Critical Cybernetic Components" (e.g., synthetic organs, neural-implants, advanced prosthetics).
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Electromagnetic-Interference (EMI) Routing**: Autonomously avoiding "High-EMI Zones" (e.g., specific transit hubs, construction zones) that could interfere with the traveler's hardware.
    - **Firmware-Compatibility-Audit**: Verifying that the local "Technical Environment" (e.g., power-grid, medical-support-API) at the destination is compatible with the traveler's "Component-Firmware."
    - **Emergency-Hardware-Swap-Logistics**: Pre-identifying "Certified-Hardware-Repair-Centers" at every hub in the itinerary.

## 3. Track: Inter-Species & Augmented Companion Agency (SPECIES-001)
Agency for the 'Biological-Companion.'

- **Concept**: High-fidelity logistics for "Service Animals" or "Augmented Companions" (e.g., AI-synced K9s).
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Companion-Sensory-Sync**: Monitoring the "Biometrics/Sensory-Load" of a service animal to ensure the travel environment isn't causing "Canine-Exhaustion."
    - **Species-Specific-Recovery**: Autonomously booking "Species-Optimized" rest zones (e.g., therapeutic-relief-gardens, hydration-nodes).
    - **Bi-Directional-Handoff**: Allowing the "Companion-Agent" (the animal's AI link) to "Negotiate" with the "Traveler-Agent" for resource allocation.

## 4. Track: Macro-Engineering Disruption Management (MACRO-001)
Planetary-scale infrastructure logistics.

- **Concept**: Travel protocols for "Tier-1 Megaprojects" (e.g., Space Elevators, Global Tunnels, Planetary Weather-Control-Arrays).
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Mechanical-Mega-Failure-Logic**: Recovery protocols for "Elevator-Cable-Oscillations" or "Tunnel-Decompression" where the scale of failure is planetary.
    - **Weather-Control-Sync**: Negotiating with "Planetary Weather-Control-Agents" to ensure a "Clear-Passage-Window" for a specific high-priority flight.
    - **Static-City-Divergence**: Logistics for "Mobile Cities" or "Floating-Islands" where the "Destination" is constantly moving.
