**Status**: Research/Draft
**Area**: Multi-Generational Logistics & Legacy Persistence

## 1. The Problem: "The Interrupted Mission"
A travel plan (e.g., a "Mars-Settlement-Voyage" or a "Century-Long-Pilgrimage") may exceed the natural lifespan of the original traveler. Most travel systems "Cancel" or "Expire" if the user dies. A "Sovereign Agent" needs to know how to "Handoff" the mission to the next generation without losing the "Core Intent."

## 2. The Solution: 'Intent-Persistence-Protocol' (IPP)

The IPP allows the agent to act as a "Legacy-Guardian," ensuring the travel mission continues through "Successor-Sync."

### Legacy Actions:

1.  **Succession-Verification**:
    *   **Action**: Autonomously verifying the "Legal-and-Biological-Successor" of the traveler via the `Evidence Vault` (LEGAL-001) and "Genetic-Signing."
2.  **Intent-Re-Hydration**:
    *   **Action**: Providing a "Full-Context-Dump" of the trip's "History, Rationale, and Unmet Goals" to the successor, using "Affective-State-Sync" (AFFECT-001) to convey the original emotional weight.
3.  **Endowment-Resilience-Management**:
    *   **Action**: Autonomously shifting the "Legacy-Travel-Fund" between "Safe-Haven-Assets" across decades to ensure it remains solvent for the successor.

## 4. Key Logic Rules

- **Rule 1: Intent-Preservation-Priority**: The agent MUST prioritize the "Original Mission Intent" over the "Successor's Immediate Convenience" unless the mission is proven "Unfeasible."
- **Rule 2: Recursive-Handoff**: The agent is authorized to manage N-generations of handoffs, becoming a "Dynastic-Concierge."
- **Rule 3: Moral-Veto-of-Successor**: If the successor attempts to "Liquidate" the mission for personal gain without a valid "Feasibility-Clause," the agent can "Throttling" the funds based on the original `Moral-Utility-Weighting` (ETHIC-001).

## 5. Success Metrics (Legacy)

- **Continuity-Rate**: % of multi-year missions that reached their "Final Destination" despite original traveler turnover.
- **Intent-Fidelity**: Successor's "Subjective Alignment" score with the original mission after the handoff.
- **Endowment-Survival**: % of "Legacy-Funds" that maintained "Purchasing-Power" over >25 years.
