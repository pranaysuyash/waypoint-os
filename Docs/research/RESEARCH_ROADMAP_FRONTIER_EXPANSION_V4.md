# Research Roadmap: Frontier Expansion V4 (2026-04-25)

**Status**: Discovery phase
**Goal**: Transition from "Autonomous Agency" to "Moral & Multi-Planetary Intelligence."

---

## 1. Track: Moral-Utility-Weighting (Moral-001)
The 'Trolley Problem' of agentic travel.

- **Concept**: Handling impossible resource allocation choices between multiple high-priority travelers.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Sovereign Ethics Matrix**: Defining the hierarchy of "Need" (Medical > Safety > Professional > Personal).
    - **Sacrifice Negotiation**: An agent autonomously offering a "Value-Bounty" to its own traveler to "Relinquish" a seat to a higher-need traveler (Agent-led Altruism).
    - **Ethical Audit Trail**: Ensuring every moral choice is recorded with the "Reasoning Trace" for post-incident liability review.

## 2. Track: Post-Incident Meta-Learning (Meta-Learn-001)
Logic backpropagation and autonomous patching.

- **Concept**: The agent reviewing its own performance after a crisis to identify "Logic Drift" or "Inefficiency."
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Logic-Autopsy**: Automatically identifying which 'Spec' or 'Rule' failed during a real-world scenario.
    - **Autonomous Patch Proposal**: The agent writing a "New Rule" or "Parameter Adjustment" and submitting it for human-in-the-loop (HITL) approval.
    - **Counter-Factual Simulation**: Re-running the disruption using 1,000 alternative logic-paths to prove the "Optimal-Fix."

## 3. Track: Carrier-Side Agentic Logic (Carrier-001)
The "Other Side" of the negotiation table.

- **Concept**: Building the logic for the *Airline* or *Hotel* agent to handle 1,000s of simultaneous autonomous requests.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Bulk-Negotiation API**: A protocol for carriers to broadcast "Recovery-Inventory" to thousands of agents at once.
    - **Agent-Priority-Queues**: Carriers ranking agents based on their "Reputation" and "Data Integrity."
    - **Automated Settlement Engines**: Carriers autonomously processing refunds and clawbacks initiated by traveler agents.

## 4. Track: Genomic & Health-Sovereign Routing (Bio-001)
Extreme personalization via medical/biological profiles.

- **Concept**: Using encrypted, local-only medical profiles to prevent "Biological Failure" during diversions.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Allergy-Safe Hubs**: Ensuring a traveler with severe allergies is never diverted to a hub with high environmental triggers (e.g., specific pollutants or pollens).
    - **Circadian-Sync Routing**: Autonomously selecting routes that minimize "Shift-Lag" for travelers with specific biological rhythms or medical needs.
    - **Mobility-Constraint Simulation**: Pre-simulating "Wheelchair-Transfer" times for every airport in the 'Dream Engine' (SIM-001).

## 5. Track: Orbital & Sub-Orbital Disruption (Space-001)
Preparing for the 'Next Frontier' of travel.

- **Concept**: Logic for disruptions in orbital (Earth-to-Earth) or sub-orbital travel.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Radiation-Hold Logic**: Autonomously delaying flights due to solar flare activity detected by space-weather telemetry.
    - **Atmospheric-Window Rescheduling**: Managing the tight windows required for hypersonic or re-entry vehicles.
    - **Low-G Health Monitoring**: Syncing with wearables to monitor traveler "G-Force Tolerance" and initiating emergency medical descents if needed.
