# X-Spec: Planetary Evacuation & Sanctuary Triage (X-EVAC-001)

**Status**: Research/Draft
**Area**: Existential Risk & Species-Level Logistics

---

## 1. The Problem: "The Final Bottleneck"
In a planetary-scale extinction event (e.g., Asteroid impact, Nuclear Winter), "Seat-Capacity" to hardened sanctuaries or off-world colonies becomes the most valuable resource in history. A "Market-Based" allocation (whoever pays most) leads to civilizational collapse. An "Agentic-Triage" based on "Species-Survival-Utility" is required.

## 2. The Solution: 'Species-Survival-Matrix' (SSM)

The SSM provides a "Hard-Logic-Framework" for prioritizing passage when the goal is "Civilizational-Continuity."

### Triage Tiers:

1.  **Tier 1: Foundational Infrastructure (The Builders)**:
    *   **Examples**: Energy-Physicists, Geneticists, Agricultural-Engineers, Infrastructure-Maintainers.
    *   **Action**: 100% Pre-emption Rights.
2.  **Tier 2: Genetic-Diversity-Nodes (The Seed)**:
    *   **Action**: Autonomously selecting individuals to maximize the "Shannon-Entropy" of the survivor-population (GENE-DRAFT-001).
3.  **Tier 3: Cultural-and-Historical-Keepers (The Memory)**:
    *   **Examples**: Librarians, Ethicists, Historians, Artists.
    *   **Action**: Allocated 15% of remaining capacity to ensure "Humanity" survives, not just "Biology."
4.  **Tier 4: General-Population**:
    *   **Action**: Random-Lottery-Allocation for remaining capacity.

## 4. Key Logic Rules

- **Rule 1: Zero-Financial-Weight**: During an `X-EVENT` state, the traveler's bank balance is IGNORED. Only "Utility" and "Diversity" markers are read.
- **Rule 2: Family-Unity-Weighting**: To prevent psychological collapse of survivors, the SSM attempts to "Group-Evacuate" families, even if it slightly lowers the "Utility-Density" of a specific node.
- **Rule 3: Autonomous-Sanctuary-Handshake**: Agents at the sanctuary gate must "Handshake" with the traveler's agent using "One-Time-Quantum-Keys" to prevent "Sanctuary-Spoofing."

## 5. Success Metrics (X-Resilience)

- **Species-Utility-Rate**: % of "Tier-1" professionals successfully secured in sanctuaries.
- **Genetic-Diversity-Coefficient**: Maintenance of >0.95 "Population-Entropy" in the survivor pool.
- **Sanctuary-Utilization**: 100% fill-rate of available survival-capacity before "Window-Closure."
