## AG. CONTENT PRISM STRATEGY: ONE TOPIC → THREE AUDIENCES

> **Core Insight**: Every single travel topic can be written for **three audiences simultaneously**: the **Traveler** (B2C — drives free tool usage, brand awareness, agent referrals), the **Agent** (B2B — our primary product user), and the **Vendor/Supplier** (B2B-supply — hotels, airlines, DMCs, activity providers who want to serve these travelers better). This triples our content surface area and creates a self-reinforcing ecosystem: travelers find us → agents serve them through us → vendors optimize for them through our insights.

### AG1. The Content Prism Framework

Every topic below follows this structure:
- 🟢 **Traveler angle** (B2C): Helps the end traveler — drives free tool traffic, waitlist signups, brand awareness. Travelers who find value may ask their agent to use our product, or we refer them to agencies on our platform.
- 🔷 **Agent angle** (B2B): Helps the travel agent — drives product adoption. "Here's how to handle this better/faster with the right tools."
- 🟠 **Vendor/Supplier angle** (B2B-supply): Helps hotels, airlines, DMCs, activity providers — drives supplier-side engagement, potential marketplace or data product revenue. "Here's how to capture/serve this segment better."

### AG3. The Vendor Content Flywheel 🔵

> Beyond individual content pieces, there's a **strategic flywheel** in vendor-facing content:

- 🔵 **"How to Get More Bookings from Travel Agencies" — Master Guide for Hotels**: Comprehensive guide covering agency relationships, commission structures, marketing, technology integration. Positions us as the expert on agency-supplier relationships.
- 🔵 **"Travel Agency Partner Program Playbook for Hotels"**: How hotels should structure agent programs — this content gets shared BY hotels TO their agency partners, who then discover us.
- 🔵 **"Supplier Directory for Travel Agents"**: We build a free supplier directory — vendors list themselves (free leads for us), agents search it (engagement), vendors pay for premium placement (revenue).
- 🔵 **"Supplier Insights Report"**: Anonymized data on what agencies book, prefer, and avoid — suppliers pay for this intelligence.
- 🔵 **"Agency-Ready Certification" for Vendors**: We create a standard/badge that vendors can earn ("Agency-Ready" verified supplier) — vendors market the badge, agents trust the badge, both come to our platform.
- 🔵 **"Vendor Content Hub"**: Section of our site specifically for suppliers — gets them into our ecosystem, cross-pollination with agent audience.
- 🔵 **"How Suppliers Can Use AI to Work Better with Agencies"**: Targets vendors searching for AI solutions — introduces our product from the supply side.
