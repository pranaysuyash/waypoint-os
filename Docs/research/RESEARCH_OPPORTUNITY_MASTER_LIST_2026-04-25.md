## AG. CONTENT PRISM STRATEGY: ONE TOPIC → THREE AUDIENCES

> **Core Insight**: Every single travel topic can be written for **three audiences simultaneously**: the **Traveler** (B2C — drives free tool usage, brand awareness, agent referrals), the **Agent** (B2B — our primary product user), and the **Vendor/Supplier** (B2B-supply — hotels, airlines, DMCs, activity providers who want to serve these travelers better). This triples our content surface area and creates a self-reinforcing ecosystem: travelers find us → agents serve them through us → vendors optimize for them through our insights.

### AG1. The Content Prism Framework

Every topic below follows this structure:
- 🟢 **Traveler angle** (B2C): Helps the end traveler — drives free tool traffic, waitlist signups, brand awareness. Travelers who find value may ask their agent to use our product, or we refer them to agencies on our platform.
- 🔷 **Agent angle** (B2B): Helps the travel agent — drives product adoption. "Here's how to handle this better/faster with the right tools."
- 🟠 **Vendor/Supplier angle** (B2B-supply): Helps hotels, airlines, DMCs, activity providers — drives supplier-side engagement, potential marketplace or data product revenue. "Here's how to capture/serve this segment better."
