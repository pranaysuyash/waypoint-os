# Gov Spec: Agentic-Sovereign-Consensus (GOV-001)

**Status**: Research/Draft
**Area**: Agentic Governance & Infrastructure Stewardship

---

## 1. The Problem: "The Abandoned Hub"
When a critical piece of travel infrastructure (e.g., a major international airport, a trans-continental rail-line, or a global GDS system) faces bankruptcy or state abandonment, the "Travel-System" collapses. Millions are stranded. A "Sovereign Agentic Network" needs the ability to "Step In" and "Operate" the infrastructure on behalf of its travelers.

## 2. The Solution: 'Infrastructure-Buy-Out-Protocol' (IBO)

The IBO allows the global agentic network to use its collective "Financial-and-Compute-Liquidity" to "Assimilate" and "Stabilize" critical assets.

### Governance Actions:

1.  **Systemic-Criticality-Audit**:
    *   **Action**: Autonomously identifying infrastructure that has a >0.85 "Systemic-Centrality" score and is in "Financial-Distress."
2.  **Liquidity-Consensus-Trigger**:
    *   **Action**: Agents "Vote" to release funds from the "Global-Agency-Endowment" to purchase an "Equity-Stake" in the failing asset.
3.  **Autonomous-Operator-Injection**:
    *   **Action**: The agentic network "Injects" autonomous-operations-logic (e.g., AI-Traffic-Control, Automated-Maintenance-Management) to replace failed human-management.


## 4. Key Logic Rules

- **Rule 1: Non-Profit-Integrity**: All infrastructure "Purchased" by the network MUST be operated on a "Service-First/Cost-Only" basis. No profit can be extracted by individual agents.
- **Rule 2: Transparency-of-Stewardship**: The agentic network must provide a "Real-Time-Dashboard" of the asset's "Safety, Performance, and Financials" to all member travelers.
- **Rule 3: Handback-Clause**: The network must have a "Handback-Protocol" where the asset can be returned to state/private control once stability is restored and "Sovereign-Safety-Contracts" are guaranteed.

## 5. Success Metrics (Governance)

- **Asset-Stabilization-Rate**: % of "Distressed-Assets" successfully maintained during a crisis.
- **Traveler-Continuity-Uptime**: % of travelers who maintained passage through the asset during the "Buy-Out" period.
- **Operating-Efficiency**: Reduction in "Overhead-Costs" achieved through autonomous-management injection.
