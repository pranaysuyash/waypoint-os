# State Spec: Geopolitical Micro-Diplomacy (STATE-001)

**Status**: Research/Draft
**Area**: High-Chaos Logistics & Sovereign Diplomacy

---

## 1. The Problem: "The Authority Vacuum"
During a sudden geopolitical collapse or coup, "Official Government Channels" (embassies, consulates) often become unreachable or slow. A traveler stuck in a city with a "Sudden Border Closure" needs someone to negotiate with the "Actual Controller" of the gate—often a local municipal council, private security firm, or micro-authority.

## 2. The Solution: 'Diplomatic-Proxy-Protocol' (DPP)

The DPP allows the agent to act as a "Micro-Ambassador" for the traveler, negotiating "Passage Rights" and "Safety Assurances" in the absence of state support.

### Diplomatic Actions:

1.  **Authority Discovery**:
    *   **Action**: Mining local "Digital Footprints" (social media, mesh-net signals, local broadcasts) to identify the "Active Decision-Maker" in a specific territory.
2.  **Reputation-Collateral Negotiation**:
    *   **Action**: Offering "Digital Reputation-Stakes" or "Resource-Vouchers" (FIN-002) to local authorities in exchange for "Safe-Passage-Tokens."
3.  **Neutral-Corridor-Mapping**:
    *   **Action**: Autonomously identifying "Demilitarized" or "Neutral" paths (e.g., Hospital corridors, International NGO zones) for movement within a city.

## 4. Key Logic Rules

- **Rule 1: Non-Combatant-Integrity**: The agent NEVER engages in "Offensive" actions. It strictly negotiates for "Safety" and "Movement."
- **Rule 2: Verification-of-Control**: Before entering an agreement, the agent must verify the "De-Facto Control" of the counter-party (e.g., do they actually control the gate?).
- **Rule 3: Sovereign-Privacy-Shield**: The traveler's identity is only revealed at the "Point of Passage" to minimize kidnapping or targeting risk.

## 5. Success Metrics (Statecraft)

- **Extraction-Success-Rate**: % of travelers successfully extracted from "High-Chaos" zones via agent-led negotiation.
- **Negotiation-Speed**: Average time to secure a "Safe-Passage-Token" from an unofficial authority.
- **Collateral-Loss-Minimization**: Reduction in "Bribe/Ransom" costs through structured agentic bargaining.
