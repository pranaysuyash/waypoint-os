# Research Roadmap: Frontier Expansion V11 (2026-04-25)

**Status**: Discovery phase
**Goal**: Transition from "Global-Governance" to "Deep-Time Legacy & Inter-Stellar Stewardship."

---

## 1. Track: Deep-Time-Archivist & Cultural-Memory-Vaults (LEGACY-002)
Preserving 'The Human Journey.'

- **Concept**: Protecting the "Cultural-and-Sensory-Memory" of human travel for future civilizations or post-biological entities.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Sensory-Memory-Anchors**: High-fidelity recordings of the "Atmosphere, Sound, and Social-Friction" of historical travel routes (e.g., the Paris-to-Istanbul rail-line) stored in diamond-memory vaults.
    - **Hospitality-Logic-Preservation**: Encapsulating the "Spirit-of-Human-Service" into immutable agentic-logic-blocks that can be revived in any future infrastructure.
    - **Chronological-Bridge-Protocol**: Agents "Self-Archiving" their logs into "Deep-Time-Beacons" that can be deciphered by non-human or future intelligence.

## 2. Track: Post-Biological-Heritage-Passage (BIO-MIG-002)
Travel for the 'Digital-Descendant.'

- **Concept**: Ensuring that digital personas or "Mind-Uploads" can "Travel" through historical routes via high-fidelity simulation.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Simulation-as-Passage**: The agent managing the "Bandwidth-and-Latency" required for a digital-persona to "Move" from one global-compute-hub to another while "Experiencing" a simulated transit.
    - **Physical-Manifestation-Lease**: Protocols for digital travelers to "Lease" a robotic or biosynthetic body for short-duration physical visits to historical sites.
    - **Heritage-Verification**: Verifying the "Authenticity" of a digital-traveler's claim to a specific physical-heritage-site.

## 3. Track: Civilizational-Seed-Itineraries (SEED-001)
The 'Noah's-Ark' of Knowledge.

- **Concept**: Autonomously moving "Civilizational-Seed-Knowledge" (DNA, libraries, software-cores) to long-duration "Hardened-Vaults" during periods of planetary decline.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Species-Survival-Routing**: Prioritizing the movement of "Critical-Biological-and-Digital-Seeds" over human-travelers in high-risk scenarios (X-EVAC-001).
    - **Vault-Handshake-Protocol**: The agent autonomously "Negotiating" with deep-earth or off-planet vault controllers for "Secure-Storage-Leases."
    - **Seed-Integrity-Monitoring**: Using ambient sensors (SENSOR-001) to monitor the "Biological-Viability" of cargo during long-duration transit.

## 4. Track: Inter-Stellar-Logistics-Foundations (SPACE-002)
Beyond the 'Sol' system.

- **Concept**: The foundational logic for agents to manage travel across light-years.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Relativistic-Lag-Consensus**: How agents "Sync" their state across vast distances with minutes or hours of light-delay.
    - **Stasis-Integrity-Guardianship**: The agent's role as the "Primary-Life-Support-Controller" during multi-decade cryo-stasis transits.
    - **Generational-Agentic-Handoff**: Protocols for an agent to "Transfer" the traveler's mission to a "Descendant-Agent" during a multi-generational voyage.

## 5. Track: Echo-of-Home & Psychological-Continuity (ECHO-001)
Maintaining the 'Earth-Anchor.'

- **Concept**: Maintaining a high-fidelity "Home-Simulation" for travelers permanently separated from Earth to ensure mental stability.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Dynamic-Earth-Sync**: Replicating "Real-Time-Earth-Seasons-and-Events" within a traveler's localized simulation.
    - **Nostalgia-Calibration-Engine**: Autonomously adjusting the simulation to provide the optimal level of "Home-Familiarity" based on the traveler's neural-stress-levels (NEURAL-001).
