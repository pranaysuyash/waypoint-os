# Research Roadmap: Frontier Expansion V2 (2026-04-25)

**Status**: Discovery phase
**Goal**: Push the Travel Agency Agent beyond "Operational Automation" into "Systemic Intelligence & Predictive Agency."

---

## 1. Track: Recursive Identity Architecture (Identity-001)
Moving beyond static traveler profiles to "Situational Identities."

- **Concept**: The agent's reasoning logic, risk tolerance, and tone must pivot based on the traveler's active "Mode."
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Mode Switching**: Executive (Time-sensitive) vs. Family (Comfort/Safety-sensitive) vs. Courier (Asset-integrity-sensitive).
    - **Identity Merging**: Handling a single trip where the traveler is a parent and a business leader simultaneously.
    - **Memory Persistence**: How the agent builds "Long-Term Emotional Context" (e.g., "The traveler was traumatized by the 2024 outage; prioritize psychological safety").

## 2. Track: Multi-Agent Slot-Trading Game Theory (Game-001)
Autonomous negotiation in resource-constrained environments.

- **Concept**: When a flight is cancelled, 300 agents (representing 300 travelers) are all fighting for 5 seats.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Secondary Slot Markets**: Agents "Trading" or "Bidding" for seats using Traveler-Authorized budgets.
    - **Collaborative Batching**: Multiple agents grouping travelers with similar needs to "Charter" a solution rather than fight for retail seats.
    - **Fairness Protocols**: Ensuring the "Wealthiest" agent doesn't always win (Sovereign Fairness).

## 3. Track: Ambient Sensory Agency (Sensor-001)
Integrating the physical world into the digital decision loop.

- **Concept**: Using IoT, Wearables, and Phone Sensors to feed the agent real-time "Physical Telemetry."
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Biometric Urgency**: High heart rate + GPS stall (stuck in traffic) = Trigger "Pre-emptive Boarding Delay" protocol.
    - **Environmental Awareness**: Weather/Noise/Crowd density at the gate influencing the "Wait vs. Move" decision.
    - **Last-Meter Navigation**: AR-guided routing through chaotic airport terminals during a disruption.

## 4. Track: Sovereign Agentic Wallets (Finance-002)
Programmable money for autonomous recovery.

- **Concept**: Deepening the 'Agentic Wallet Guardian' into a "Smart Contract" system.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Conditional Escrow**: Funds that only unlock when a "Disruption Evidence Bundle" is cryptographically signed by the 'Evidence Vault'.
    - **Micro-Bounty Payouts**: Agents offering small bounties to local "Gig-Concierges" for physical tasks (e.g., "Find this specific lost bag for $50").
    - **Cross-Border Liquidity**: Real-time currency hedging for emergency local payments during global diversions.

## 5. Track: Predictive Disruption "The Oracle" (Predict-001)
Forecasting failures before they appear on GDS/API layers.

- **Concept**: Using alternative data (labor sentiment, climate models, aircraft age telemetry) to predict outages.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Sentiment Mining**: Monitoring pilot/crew forum sentiment to predict "Hidden Strikes" or "Sick-outs."
    - **Supply-Chain Foresight**: Predicting baggage handler delays based on regional weather and equipment maintenance logs.
    - **Inference of Delay**: Detecting a 10-minute delay at an upstream hub and calculating the "Cascade Probability" for the traveler's 3rd connection.
