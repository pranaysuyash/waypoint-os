# Fin Spec: Agentic 'Itinerary-Equity' Valuator (FIN-REAL-030)

**Status**: Research/Draft
**Area**: Agency Intellectual Property & Content Monetization

---

## 1. The Problem: "The Undervalued Itinerary DNA"
Complex, high-value itineraries (e.g., "The ultimate 14-day sustainable luxury tour of the Amazon") are significant "Intellectual-Property" (IP). However, most agencies treat them as "One-Off-Services." They don't realize the "Residual-Value" of their itinerary DNA. Without "Agentic-Valuation," an agency can't easily monetize its unique trip designs through licensing or white-labeling to other partners.

## 2. The Solution: 'IP-Monetization-Protocol' (IPMP)

The IPMP acts as the "IP-Investment-Banker."

### Valuation Actions:

1.  **Itinerary-Scarcity-Analysis**:
    *   **Action**: Analyzing the agency's itinerary library to identify "Unique-DNA-Signatures" (e.g., exclusive vendor combinations, rare activity sequencing) that aren't available elsewhere in the ecosystem.
2.  **Asset-Value-Calculation**:
    *   **Action**: calculating the "Monetary-Value" of a specific itinerary based on its historical conversion rate, margin potential, and "Market-Demand" (e.g., "This Amazon itinerary is worth $5,000 in licensing fees").
3.  **License-to-Partner-Routing**:
    *   **Action**: identifying other agencies in the ecosystem that lack expertise in a specific niche (e.g., Agency X wants to offer Amazon tours) and suggesting a "License-Agreement."
4.  **Attribution-Royalty-Watchdog**:
    *   **Action**: monitoring the use of the licensed itinerary and autonomously managing the "Royalty-Settlement" (e.g., the original agency gets a 2% commission for every traveler booked using their IP).

## 4. Key Logic Rules

- **Rule 1: The 'DNA-Uniqueness' Threshold**: Only itineraries that meet a specific "Uniqueness-Score" (minimum 85%) should be considered for IP valuation and licensing.
- **Rule 2: Owner-License-Control**: The agency owner MUST have the right to "White-List" or "Black-List" specific partners for licensing (e.g., "Don't license my IP to my direct local competitor").
- **Rule 3: Quality-Maintenance-Clause**: Licensed itineraries MUST maintain a "Quality-Standard" when delivered by the partner agency to protect the original brand's reputation.

## 5. Success Metrics (IP)

- **IP-License-Volume**: Total number of itinerary licenses issued within the ecosystem.
- **Royalty-Yield-Growth**: % increase in agency passive income generated from licensed IP.
- **Content-Asset-ROI**: Total revenue generated from an itinerary (direct bookings + licensing) vs. the original cost of creation.
