# Legacy Spec: Deep-Time-Archivist (LEGACY-002)

**Status**: Research/Draft
**Area**: Cultural Preservation & Deep-Time Logic

## 1. The Problem: "The Lost Journey"
As civilizations decline, infrastructures fail, and biological species evolve or transition, the "Qualitative-Experience" of human travel—the specific "Feeling" of hospitality, the "Sound" of a bustling terminal, the "Scent" of a specific route—is lost. A "Sovereign Agent" needs to act as a "Time-Capsule" for the essence of travel.

## 2. The Solution: 'Memory-Anchor-Protocol' (MAP)

The MAP allows agents to "Distill" the travel experience into "Universal-Sensory-Tokens" stored in high-resiliency diamond-lattice memory.

### Archival Actions:

1.  **Sensory-Fidelity-Capture**:
    *   **Action**: Capturing 8K-Spatial-Audio, 16K-Visual-Mesh, and "Ambient-Environmental-Data" (Scent, Temperature, Air-Pressure) for a specific route.
2.  **Cultural-Context-Metadata**:
    *   **Action**: Documenting the "Social-Norms, Economic-Context, and Human-Emotions" (AFFECT-001) associated with the trip.
3.  **Deep-Time-Encryption-Handshake**:
    *   **Action**: Encrypting the data using "Fundamental-Mathematical-Constants" (e.g., Pi, Euler's Number) to ensure it can be decoded by any sufficiently advanced intelligence without a specific software-key.

## 4. Key Logic Rules

- **Rule 1: Subjective-Objectivity**: The agent must record both the "Physical-Reality" of the trip and the "Subjective-Experience" of the traveler (via NEURAL-001) to provide a complete "Empathy-Map."
- **Rule 2: Redundant-Physicality**: The `Memory Anchor` must be stored in at least three "Geologically-Stable" physical locations (e.g., Arctic-Vault, Deep-Ocean-Trench, Lunar-Crater).
- **Rule 3: Autonomous-Self-Repair**: The agentic-guardians of the vault must have "Self-Healing" nano-logic to prevent data-rot over millions of years.

## 5. Success Metrics (Archiving)

- **Data-Resilience-Estimated**: Projected viability of the data in years (Target: > 1,000,000 years).
- **Decipherability-Score**: Probability of a non-human intelligence successfully decoding the sensory-mesh.
- **Experience-Transfer-Fidelity**: % of "Sensory-Detail" preserved vs the original live-event.
