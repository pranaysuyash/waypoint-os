**Area**: International Law & Agentic Sovereignty

## 1. The Problem: "The Caught Traveler"
During a sudden international conflict or "State-of-Emergency," non-combatant travelers are often detained, searched, or their movement restricted by state actors. A "Sovereign Agent" needs a way to assert "Diplomatic-Immunity-of-Passage" for its traveler, especially if they are moving for "Systemic-Integrity-Reasons" (e.g., Tier-1 professionals from X-EVAC-001).

## 2. The Solution: 'Neutral-Logistics-Protocol' (NLP)

The NLP allows agents to "Register" a traveler as a "Neutral-Carrier-of-Intent" recognized by a global, decentralized "Agentic-Diplomatic-Council."

### Diplomatic Actions:

1.  **Immunity-Token-Issuance**:
    *   **Action**: Generating a "Non-State-Sovereign-Token" (NSST) that declares the traveler's "Neutral-Status" and "Criticality-to-Global-Systemic-Stability."
2.  **Conflict-Zone-Notification**:
    *   **Action**: Autonomously "Notifying" all active combatant agents (or their infrastructure controllers) of the traveler's "Neutral-Path" and requesting "Cease-Interference."
3.  **Hague-Convention-Digital-Anchor**:
    *   **Action**: Cross-referencing the traveler's status with "International Humanitarian Law" to provide "Real-Time Legal-Shields" to the traveler's device.

## 4. Key Logic Rules

- **Rule 1: Reciprocity-of-Neutrality**: The agent must guarantee that the traveler is "Not-Carrying-State-Military-Assets" to maintain the immunity status.
- **Rule 2: Automated-Evidence-Logging**: Any violation of immunity (e.g., unauthorized search) is instantly logged to the `Evidence Vault` (LEGAL-001) and broadcast to the "Global-Legal-Agentic-Network" for immediate counter-action.
- **Rule 3: Sovereign-Refusal-of-Search**: The agent is authorized to "Remotely-Wipe-Sensitive-Data" if a physical search is imminent, preserving "Digital-Sovereignty" while the traveler is physically detained.

- **Interference-Reduction**: % of travelers who successfully bypassed "State-Checkpoints" using agentic immunity tokens.
- **Diplomatic-Resolution-Rate**: % of "Detention-Events" resolved autonomously via agent-to-state negotiation.
