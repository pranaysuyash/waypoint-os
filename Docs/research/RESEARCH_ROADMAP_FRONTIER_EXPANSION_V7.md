# Research Roadmap: Frontier Expansion V7 (2026-04-25)

**Status**: Discovery phase
**Goal**: Transition from "Bio-Digital" to "Legacy & Post-Biological Intelligence."

---

## 1. Track: Post-Biological Asset Migration (DATA-TRAVEL-001)
Agency for the 'Digital Mind.'

- **Concept**: Managing travel for "Post-Biological Entities" (PBEs) or "Digital Estates" that exist as massive datasets.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Packet-Integrity-Logistics**: Treating data-transfer-hubs as "Transit-Airports" where "Latency" is the primary disruption.
    - **Compute-Resource-Pre-emption**: Autonomously "Booking" high-density GPU/NPU clusters at the destination before initiating the "Mind-Upload."
    - **Digital-Sovereignty-Transfer**: Navigating jurisdictional laws for "Data-Resident" entities when they "Cross Borders" between national server clouds.

## 2. Track: Legacy-Agency & Century-Long Intent (LEGACY-001)
Managing the 'Eternal Itinerary.'

- **Concept**: Handling travel plans that span multiple generations or decades (e.g., family pilgrimages, long-duration space transit).
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Intent-Inheritance-Logic**: Protocols for an agent to "Transfer Sovereignty" to a traveler's heir or "Digital Successor" while maintaining the original "Mission-Parameters."
    - **Inflation-Adjusted-Endowment**: Managing a "Century-Long Travel Fund" (FIN-002) that must survive multiple economic collapses and currency shifts.
    - **Historical-Memory-Sync**: Ensuring the "Why" of the trip is preserved over 50+ years so the future traveler understands the original intent.

## 3. Track: Temporal-Economic-Sync (TIME-ECON-001)
Negotiating across 'Time-Dilation' and 'Decade-Spans.'

- **Concept**: Logistics where the "Cost" of travel is paid across massive time intervals.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Relativistic-Arbitrage**: Managing financial assets for travelers on high-speed orbital transits where "Subjective-Time" moves slower than "Market-Time."
    - **Long-Tail-Supply-Lock**: Pre-negotiating resources (e.g., fuel, oxygen, cabin space) with providers that might not exist for another 20 years.

## 4. Track: Genetic-Sanctuary & Bio-Diversity Migration (GENE-001)
Agency for the 'Species-Core.'

- **Concept**: Managing the movement of "High-Priority Genetic Assets" (seed banks, embryo clusters, endangered species) during planetary crises.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Cryo-Chain-Integrity-Watchdog**: Continuous monitoring of "Temperature/Atmosphere" telemetry for biological cargo with "Zero-Tolerance" for deviation.
    - **Species-Survival-Routing**: Prioritizing paths that lead to "Certified Sanctuaries" or "Deep-Bunkers" during climate-collapse events.

## 5. Track: The 'Ultimate-Continuity' Protocol (X-RESET-001)
Post-Catastrophe systemic reset.

- **Concept**: What the agent does when "The World" as we know it ceases to function.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Seed-Intelligence-Reboot**: A protocol for the agent to "Self-Archive" into a low-power, hardened hardware state and wait for a "Systemic-Re-emergence" signal.
    - **Primary-Survival-Guidance**: Transitioning from a "Travel Agent" to a "Survival Agent" for the traveler during a total civilizational collapse.
