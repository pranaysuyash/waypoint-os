# Seed Spec: Civilizational-Seed Routing (SEED-001)

**Status**: Research/Draft
**Area**: Species-Survival & Logistics

---

## 1. The Problem: "The Fragile Library"
During a rapid planetary collapse (X-EVAC-001), human-travelers are often the focus. However, the "Knowledge-and-Biological-Foundation" of humanity (libraries, genetic-databases, seed-banks, software-repositories) is frequently left behind or destroyed. A "Sovereign Agent" needs to prioritize the "Passage-of-Civilization" over individual comfort.

## 2. The Solution: 'Seed-Passage-Protocol' (SPP)

The SPP allows the agent to treat "Knowledge-Assets" as "Premium-Passengers" with absolute priority in the logistics-chain.

### Routing Actions:

1.  **Seed-Criticality-Categorization**:
    *   **Action**: Identifying "Tier-0" assets (e.g., Global-Genetic-Database, Seed-Vault-Keys, Core-OS-Source-Code).
2.  **Autonomous-Container-Lease**:
    *   **Action**: The agent "Hijacks" (or leases via prior-contract) the most "Hardened-and-Stealthy" transit-containers for these assets.
3.  **Ghost-Routing**:
    *   **Action**: Moving "Seed-Assets" via "Non-Standard/Non-Public" routes to avoid seizure by state actors or collapse-scavengers.

## 4. Key Logic Rules

- **Rule 1: The 'Ark-First' Mandate**: In a survival-choice between "10 Human Travelers" and "The Global-Human-Genome-Core," the agent MUST prioritize the Genome-Core. (This is a hard-coded "Civilizational-Utility" rule).
- **Rule 2: Decoy-Logistics**: The agent is authorized to create "Decoy-Manifests" to draw attention away from the actual location of the `Seed-Asset`.
- **Rule 3: Custodial-Suicide-Protocol**: If the `Seed-Asset` is about to be seized or compromised, the agent is authorized to "Remotely-Destroy" the asset to prevent "Misuse-of-Humanity's-Foundation" by a hostile entity.

## 5. Success Metrics (Seed-Survival)

- **Seed-Delivery-Probability**: % chance of successful vault-delivery in simulated "Total-Collapse" scenarios.
- **Stealth-Duration**: Time the asset remained in "Ghost-Transit" without detection by third-party sensors.
- **Knowledge-Recovery-Fidelity**: % of data-integrity maintained after a 50-year "Deep-Stowage" period.
