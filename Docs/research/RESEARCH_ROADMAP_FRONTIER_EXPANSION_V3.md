# Research Roadmap: Frontier Expansion V3 (2026-04-25)

**Status**: Discovery phase
**Goal**: Transition from "Intelligent Agency" to "Sovereign Autonomous Ecosystems."

---

## 1. Track: Sovereign Regulatory Autonomy (Reg-001)
Navigating multi-jurisdictional legal conflicts in real-time.

- **Concept**: When a flight is diverted across borders, the agent must autonomously switch its "Legal-Reasoning-Core" to match the local jurisdiction (e.g., EC261 vs. US DOT vs. Canada CTA).
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Dynamic Law Loading**: Injecting local consumer protection statutes into the few-shot context based on GPS coordinates.
    - **Autonomous Claim Generation**: Instantly filing compensation claims with regulators the moment a qualifying delay is confirmed by the 'Evidence Vault'.
    - **Jurisdictional Arbitrage**: Choosing the most favorable legal path for a traveler in multi-leg international disruptions.

## 2. Track: Synthetic Experience Simulation (The 'Dream' Engine) (Sim-001)
Pre-emptive simulation of potential futures.

- **Concept**: Before a trip begins, the agent runs 10,000 "Disruption Simulations" (Dreams) to identify "Fragility Points" in the itinerary.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Soft-Reservation Stacking**: Pre-emptively holding "No-Cost-Cancellation" backups for the top 3 highest-probability failure modes (e.g., 20% chance of missing connection at CDG).
    - **Fragility Scoring**: Assigning a "Chaos-Probability" score to every leg of a trip based on real-time and historical telemetry.
    - **Scenario Rehearsal**: The agent "rehearsing" its communication tone and strategy for specific high-stress failures before they happen.

## 3. Track: Emotional State-Sync (Affective-001)
Deep physiological and psychological alignment.

- **Concept**: Moving beyond "Stress Detection" to "Affective Control." The agent adjusts its internal logic-gates based on the traveler's physical and mental state.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Fatigue-Aware Reasoning**: If the traveler has been awake for 20 hours, the agent prioritizes "Hotel/Sleep" over "Fastest Re-booking," even if it violates the standard 'Efficiency' mode.
    - **Anxiety Dampening**: Using specific linguistic patterns and "Micro-Assurances" to stabilize the traveler during a disruption (Emotional-State-Stabilization).
    - **Cognitive Load Reduction**: Consolidating information into "Single-Action" prompts when the traveler is in a high-stress "Sprinting" state.

## 4. Track: Inter-Agent Barter Economy (Economy-001)
Non-monetary value exchange between autonomous agents.

- **Concept**: Agents trading "Information Assets" or "Operational Favors" to solve local problems.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Information Arbitrage**: Agent A trades a "Hidden Shuttle Tip" to Agent B in exchange for "PNR-Status-Telemetry" that Agent B has accessed.
    - **Group-Leverage Negotiation**: Multiple agents representing different travelers at a hotel "Forming a Union" to negotiate a group discount or better room allocation.
    - **Trust-Tokens**: A cross-agency reputation system where agents gain "Reputation" for reliable behavior during high-stress disruptions.

## 5. Track: DePIN Infrastructure Resilience (DePIN-001)
Decentralized physical and digital infrastructure.

- **Concept**: Ensuring the 'Evidence Vault' and 'Spine' state are immutable and accessible even if the central cloud provider is compromised.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Decentralized Evidence**: Mirroring 'Defense Snapshots' on IPFS or a private blockchain to ensure they are legally tamper-proof.
    - **Offline-First Agency**: Allowing the mobile agent to perform "Local-Decision-Making" using a distilled model on the device when cell service is lost in a remote diversion.
    - **Mesh-Network Comm**: Agents communicating with each other via Bluetooth/Mesh at the gate when the airport Wi-Fi/LTE is overloaded during a crisis.
