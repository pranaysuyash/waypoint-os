# Research Roadmap: Frontier Expansion V8 (2026-04-25)

**Status**: Discovery phase
**Goal**: Transition from "Legacy" to "Existential & Species-Level Intelligence."

---

## 1. Track: Planetary Evacuation & Sanctuary Logistics (X-EVAC-001)
Agency for 'The Great Bottleneck.'

- **Concept**: Managing the movement of humans to "Certified Sanctuaries" during a planetary-scale extinction event (e.g., Asteroid impact, Nuclear Winter, Super-Volcano).
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Triage-by-Utility-and-Diversity**: Autonomously applying "Species-Survival-Algorithms" to prioritize the movement of individuals based on "Skill-Sets, Genetic-Diversity, and Role-Criticality."
    - **Bunker-Node-Synchronization**: Coordinating the "Closing-of-the-Gates" at multiple global sanctuaries to ensure 100% capacity utilization without overcrowding.
    - **Logistics-of-the-Last-Mile**: Solving the "Final-Passage" to hardened shelters when all surface infrastructure is compromised.

## 2. Track: Post-Currency Social-Stakes Barter (BARTER-001)
Negotiation in a 'Zero-Bank' world.

- **Concept**: Managing passage and resources when global financial systems (SWIFT, Credit-Cards, Sovereign Currencies) have collapsed.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Labor-as-Currency**: Agents "Escrowing" the traveler's future labor or skill-commitments in exchange for immediate passage (e.g., "I will repair your grid at the destination for 100km of passage").
    - **Reputation-Collateral**: Using "Historical-Integrity-Scores" from the `AuditStore` to prove the traveler's "Trustworthiness" to local gatekeepers.
    - **Resource-Fractionalization**: Dividing "Physical Assets" (fuel, food, medicine) into "Agent-Verified-Credits" for barter.

## 3. Track: The 'Memory-Keeper' & Stasis Agency (STASIS-001)
Agency for the 'Sleeping Traveler.'

- **Concept**: Managing the "Digital Identity" and "Social Continuity" of a traveler during long-duration "Stasis" or "Hibernation."
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Social-Presence-Maintenance**: The agent "Simulating" the traveler's presence in digital networks to maintain their "Social-Capital" and "Inheritance-Status" while they sleep.
    - **Stasis-Interruption-Triage**: Autonomously deciding whether to "Wake" a traveler based on "Destination-Viability" or "Systemic-Risk" changes.
    - **Wake-Up-Cognitive-Re-Integration**: Managing the "Information-Dump" to the traveler upon waking to minimize "Stasis-Sickness/Disorientation."

## 4. Track: Autonomous-Logistics-Governance (GOV-001)
The agent as the 'New State.'

- **Concept**: Agents forming a "Shadow-Infrastructure-Government" when central state authorities fail.
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Inter-Agent-Legislative-Loop**: Agents "Voting" on new "Safety-Laws" for a specific transit corridor in real-time.
    - **Enforcement-by-Resource-Access**: Denying passage or resources to "Malicious-Actors" identified by the global agentic network.
    - **Global-Inventory-Pooling**: Treating all "Fragmented-Resources" (e.g., remaining private jets, trains, trucks) as a "Single-Pool" managed by agentic consensus.

## 5. Track: Genetic-Draft & Diversity-Hold (GENE-DRAFT-001)
Ensuring the 'Species-Future.'

- **Concept**: Autonomously "Drafting" individuals for migration based on the need for "Genetic-Bottleneck-Prevention."
- **Exploration Areas**:
    - **Mandatory-Diversity-Routing**: Forcing a traveler to divert to a specific hub because their "Genetic-Markers" are required to maintain diversity at that location.
    - **Founder-Population-Sync**: Coordinating the arrival of "Optimized-Founder-Groups" at a new settlement or sanctuary.
