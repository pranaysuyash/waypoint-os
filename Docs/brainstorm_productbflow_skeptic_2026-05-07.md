# Product B Flow Brainstorm - Skeptic - 2026-05-07

## 10,000ft
Main risk: Product B becomes a novelty analyzer users try once and never use again. If outputs feel generic, trust dies instantly.

## 1,000ft
Weak assumptions:
- Travelers will do long intake without immediate payoff
- Agencies will respond positively to externally generated critiques
- Users will distinguish confidence-weighted findings from hard facts

Failure patterns:
- Too many findings -> cognitive overload
- No evidence snippets -> "AI opinion" dismissal
- Weak phrasing -> no negotiation leverage
- Over-strong phrasing -> legal/compliance risk

## Ground level
What not to build now:
- Full itinerary builder
- Community/social layer
- Agency marketplace routing
- Heavy personalization engine

What to validate first:
- Does a traveler actually send findings to their agent?
- Does the agent revise meaningfully?
- Does this exposure create inbound demand for Product A?

## The thing most people miss about this
"Useful" is not enough. Findings must be conversation-usable in the exact language travelers can forward without edits.
