# Domain Knowledge: IATA Resolutions & Legal Contracts

**Category**: Advanced Technical  
**Focus**: The global legal framework that governs the relationship between airlines and agencies.

---

## 1. IATA Resolution 818g (The "Foundation")
- **Logic**: This is the "Agency-Airline" contract. It defines how money flows through the **BSP (Billing and Settlement Plan)**.
- **SOP**: The agency must maintain a **"Financial Guarantee"** (Bank Guarantee) to IATA to prove they won't run away with the airline's money.

---

## 2. IATA Resolution 780 (Interline Traffic)
- **Logic**: Defines how one airline accepts another's ticket.
- **Impact**: Without Res 780, an agent cannot "Stitch" two airlines together in one PNR.

---

## 3. The "Preferred Supplier Agreement" (PSA)
- **Logic**: A private contract between an agency and a supplier (e.g., Marriott or BA).
- **Target**: "If you sell $1M of our flights, we will pay you an extra 3% override at the end of the year."
- **SOP**: The agent must prioritize these suppliers in the "Display" (Search results) to meet the target.

---

## 4. Duty of Care & Liability (Legal)
- **Liability**: If an agent fails to warn a client about a visa, the agency is legally liable for the "Loss of Trip."
- **T&Cs**: Every booking must be accompanied by the **"Agency Terms & Conditions"** which limit liability for "Force Majeure" (Acts of God).

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "BSP Default"**: An agency's bank account has insufficient funds on "BSP Day." IATA immediately "Locks Out" the agency from all ticketing. The agency must pay a "Penalty" and provide a higher guarantee.
- **PSA Target Miss**: It is December 28th. The agency is $50,000 short of its $1M Marriott target. The agent must "Incentivize" all current bookings to move to Marriott properties.
- **Liability Dispute**: A traveler is denied entry to Thailand because their passport had 5 months validity (Required: 6 months). The traveler sues the agency. The agent must prove they sent the "Passport Requirement Email."
- **The "Unsigned" T&C**: A client claims they never saw the "Non-refundable" clause. The agent must produce the "Digital Audit Trail" of the client clicking "Accept" on the invoice.
