# Product B Flow Brainstorm - Trickster - 2026-05-07

## 10,000ft
Reframe Product B as a "Trip Red Team." Travelers submit a plan, the system stress-tests it like a pre-flight safety review.

## 1,000ft
Three playful but useful modes:
- Negotiation Coach: "Questions that change the quote"
- Leak Detector: "Where money/time/risk is leaking"
- Scenario Mirror: "If X changes, what breaks first"

Use badges for clarity, not gamification noise:
- Fragile assumption
- Hidden fee risk
- Traveler-profile mismatch

## Ground level
Output style:
- "Say this to your agent" scripts
- "Ask for this evidence" prompts
- "If they push back, reply with this" fallback phrasing

## The thing most people miss about this
People do not need perfect plans. They need confidence in hard conversations. Product B wins when it gives language, not just analysis.
