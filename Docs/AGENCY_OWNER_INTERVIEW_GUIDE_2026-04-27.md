# Agency Owner Interview Guide
**Date:** April 27, 2026  
**Purpose:** Validate onboarding simulation assumptions with real agency owners  
**Interview Length:** 45-60 minutes  
**Target:** 5-8 travel agency owners (mix of experience levels)

## Pre-Interview Setup

### Screening Criteria
- Currently operating travel agency (1+ years)
- Handle trip planning and booking directly (not just referral)
- 10-500 customers per year (small to medium size)
- Mix of technical comfort levels
- Various specializations (adventure, luxury, cultural, etc.)

### Interview Logistics
- Video call with screen sharing capability
- Recording permission (for analysis, not sharing)
- Prepared demo of current system capabilities
- NDA if discussing competitive sensitive topics

## Interview Structure

### Phase 1: Background & Context (10 minutes)

**Opening:**
"We're developing technology to help travel agencies create personalized trips more efficiently. I'd love to understand your current process and challenges. Everything you share will help us build something genuinely useful."

**Agency Background:**
1. "Tell me about your agency - how long have you been operating?"
2. "What's your specialization? What types of trips do you focus on?"
3. "How many customers do you typically work with per month?"
4. "What's your team size? Who handles what parts of the process?"

**Technology Comfort:**
5. "How would you describe your comfort level with technology? (1-10 scale)"
6. "What software tools do you currently use in your business?"
7. "When evaluating new software, what's most important to you?"

### Phase 2: Current Customer Journey (15 minutes)

**Discovery to First Contact:**
8. "Walk me through how a typical customer finds and contacts you."
9. "What information do you collect from them initially?"
10. "How do you qualify whether you're a good fit for their needs?"

**Trip Planning Process:**
11. "Once you decide to work together, what's your step-by-step process for creating their trip?"
12. "How do you gather their preferences, fitness level, budget constraints?"
13. "What's the most time-consuming part of trip planning for you?"
14. "How do you handle customers who might not be suitable for certain activities?"

**Booking and Confirmation:**
15. "How does the customer actually book and pay for the trip you've planned?"
16. "What systems do you use for payments and booking confirmations?"
17. "How do you coordinate between trip planning and actual reservations?"

**Pain Point Deep Dive:**
18. "What's the most frustrating part of your current process?"
19. "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing, what would it be?"
20. "How many customers do you turn away or lose during the process? Why?"

### Phase 3: Business Operations (10 minutes)

**Daily Workflow:**
21. "Describe a typical day running your agency."
22. "How many hours per week do you spend on trip planning vs. other business tasks?"
23. "What business metrics do you track? Revenue, customer satisfaction, etc.?"

**Scaling Challenges:**
24. "What prevents you from taking on more customers?"
25. "Have you tried to hire help? What challenges did you face?"
26. "How do you handle peak seasons or busy periods?"

**Success Factors:**
27. "What makes a customer experience 'successful' from your perspective?"
28. "How do you measure whether a trip recommendation was good?"
29. "What keeps customers coming back or referring others?"

### Phase 4: Technology Evaluation (10 minutes)

**Current Tool Assessment:**
30. "What do you like most about your current software setup?"
31. "What gaps exist that you wish technology could solve?"
32. "Have you evaluated other trip planning or CRM tools? What did you find?"

**AI and Automation Attitudes:**
33. "How do you feel about AI helping with trip recommendations?"
34. "What would make you trust an AI system's suggestions?"
35. "What parts of your process would you never want automated? Why?"

**System Requirements:**
36. "If we built a new platform, what would be the make-or-break features?"
37. "How important is it that your customers can self-serve vs. working directly with you?"
38. "What would a successful onboarding experience look like for you?"

### Phase 5: Simulation Validation (10 minutes)

**Present Key Findings:**
"Based on our research, we identified these common challenges. I'd love your reaction..."

**Friction Point Validation:**
39. "Does the 3-day delay from interest to access match your experience with new tools?"
40. "How accurate is this estimate: 3+ hours per customer for complete trip planning?"
41. "Do you experience the manual coordination burden we described?"
42. "Is the 60+ hours/week time investment realistic for solo agency owners?"

**Solution Priority Validation:**
43. "If you could have three of these capabilities tomorrow, which would you choose?"
    - Customer self-service booking interface
    - AI-powered trip recommendations
    - Business analytics dashboard
    - Automated email workflows
    - Integrated payment processing
    - Staff training and delegation tools

**Gap Assessment:**
44. "What did we miss in our analysis?"
45. "Are there challenges you face that we didn't discuss?"

### Phase 6: Wrap-Up (5 minutes)

**Future Collaboration:**
46. "Would you be interested in testing an early version of the platform?"
47. "What would make you feel confident trying a new system?"
48. "Who else should we talk to? Any agency owner contacts you'd recommend?"

**Follow-up:**
49. "Can we follow up with you in a few weeks as we develop this further?"
50. "What's the best way to stay in touch?"

## Interview Analysis Framework

### Response Categorization

**For each key topic, capture:**
- Exact quotes (for pain point evidence)
- Frequency of mention across interviews
- Intensity of frustration (1-10 scale)
- Workaround strategies currently used
- Willingness to pay for solution

### Validation Scoring

**For each simulated friction point:**
- ✅ CONFIRMED: 4+ interviewees mention similar issue
- 🟡 PARTIAL: 2-3 interviewees mention, varies by context  
- ❌ INVALID: <2 mentions or actively contradicted
- ⚡ NEW: Issue not in simulation but frequently mentioned

### Priority Matrix

**Create 2x2 grid for each identified need:**
- X-axis: Technical difficulty to implement (Low/High)
- Y-axis: Business impact for agency owners (Low/High)
- Focus on High Impact + Low Difficulty first

## Post-Interview Actions

### Immediate Analysis (Within 24 hours)
1. Transcribe key quotes and insights
2. Update simulation document with corrections
3. Identify patterns across interviews
4. Flag any urgent misconceptions in our assumptions

### Synthesis Report (Within 1 week)
1. Consolidated findings across all interviews
2. Updated priority ranking for development features
3. Revised customer personas based on real data
4. Modified success metrics based on actual business needs

### Stakeholder Communication
1. Executive summary for leadership team
2. Technical requirements update for development
3. Go/no-go recommendation for agency partner launch
4. Timeline adjustments based on validated priorities

## Success Metrics for Interview Process

**Quality Indicators:**
- Average interview length >40 minutes (indicates engagement)
- >3 specific pain points identified per interview
- >2 quotable insights per interview
- >1 new friction point discovered per interview (not in simulation)

**Validation Targets:**
- 80%+ of simulation friction points confirmed or invalidated
- Clear priority ranking of solution features
- Confidence level >8/10 on product-market fit assessment
- Go/no-go decision on agency partner launch timeline
