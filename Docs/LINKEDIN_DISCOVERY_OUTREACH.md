# LinkedIn Discovery Outreach — Customer Conversations

**Goal**: Find 10 travel agency owners. Connect. Schedule a 20-min call. Listen. Don't pitch.
**Source**: LinkedIn search "travel industry agency owner" returned 10+ pages of results.

---

## 1. The Approach

Different from before. Not selling. Not building pipeline. Just learning.

```
BEFORE: Connect → DM → qualify → demo → trial → paid
NOW:    Connect → DM → "Can I pick your brain for 15 min?" → Call → Listen → Learn
```

## 2. Connection Request (Same Day)

```
Hi [Name] — I'm researching how travel agencies handle
incoming trip inquiries. Would love to hear your perspective.
Happy to connect.
```

Short. No pitch. No "I built something." Just genuine curiosity.

## 3. DM After They Accept (Same Day)

```
Thanks for connecting, [Name].

I'm doing some research on how travel agencies actually
handle the day-to-day of new trip inquiries — from first
WhatsApp message to booked trip.

Would you be open to a 15-min chat this week? No pitch,
just genuinely trying to understand the workflow.

Happy to work around your schedule.
```

## 4. The Call (15-20 min)

**Don't mention your product. Don't mention you're building anything.**

Questions to ask:

```
Warm-up:
  "How long have you been running your agency?"
  "What kind of trips do you focus on?"

The workflow:
  "Walk me through what happens when a new inquiry comes in on WhatsApp."
  "Who does what? How long does it take?"
  "What's the most annoying part of that process?"

Pains:
  "What happens when things go wrong?"
  "What's the worst mistake that's happened with an inquiry?"
  "What have you tried to fix this?"

Current tools:
  "What tools do you use day-to-day?"
  "What do you wish existed but doesn't?"

Wrap-up:
  "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about
   how inquiries come in, what would it be?"
```

## 5. Cadence

```
Day 1: Search → Send 10 connection requests (15 min)
Day 2-3: As accepts come in, send the DM (5 min each)
Day 4: Schedule calls for the following week
Day 5-14: Do the calls (20 min each)
```

5 calls completed = enough data to know if the problem is real.

## 6. What Success Looks Like

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Connection requests sent | 10-20 |
| Accept rate | 30-50% (3-10 connections) |
| DM → call booked | 50% (2-5 calls) |
| Calls completed | 5 |
| Clear pattern emerges | "Everyone says the same pain" or "Everyone has different problems" |

After 5 calls: synthesize. If 4 out of 5 say inquiry processing is a real pain → build. If they all say something else → pivot.

## 7. Do Not

```
❌ Mention Waypoint OS
❌ Say you're building something
❌ Try to sell them
❌ Demo the product
❌ Ask "Would you use a tool that...?"
❌ Correct their answers
❌ Defend your ideas
```
