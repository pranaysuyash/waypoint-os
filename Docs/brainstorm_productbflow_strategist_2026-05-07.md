# Product B Flow Brainstorm - Strategist - 2026-05-07

## 10,000ft
Product B is not a consumer travel product. It is a trust-conversion engine that turns traveler uncertainty into agency-side urgency. The core strategic wedge is negotiation leverage: generate specific, credible findings the traveler can use with their existing agent.

Differentiation is not "AI trip advice." Differentiation is: (1) structured audit against hidden failure modes, (2) evidence-backed explainability, (3) share-ready outputs optimized for agency revision loops.

## 1,000ft
Strategic flow thesis:
1. Intake with freeform text or uploads
2. Clarification pass (only high-impact gaps)
3. Risk + value audit (pricing, logistics, suitability, policy traps)
4. Action packet traveler can use in a conversation with their current agent
5. Optional "agent-ready revision brief" artifact

## Ground level
Must-win KPI chain:
- Time-to-first-insight under 90 seconds
- % users who copy/share at least one finding
- % users who return after agency revision
- % agencies exposed who request Product A demo

Do not build bookings, payments, or referrals in Product B.

## The thing most people miss about this
The moat is not detection quality alone. The moat is behavior design: converting findings into social pressure that reliably changes agency behavior.

## Addendum - Decision Boundary Tightening (2026-05-07)
Assessment: Real and strategically correct.

What to tighten:
1. Primary success event should be "traveler took findings back to existing agent and got a revision" not just "shared/copied finding." Sharing is a proxy, not the outcome.
2. KPI chain should separate leading vs proving metrics:
   - Leading: time-to-first-insight, share/copy, clarification completion
   - Proving: agency revision within 7 days, measurable quote/itinerary improvement, Product A demo pull-through
3. Keep Product B scope hard-limited to audit + negotiation artifact generation. No referral routing, no booking flow, no payment primitives.
4. Instrument causality, not correlation. Require traceable event chain from audit generated -> artifact shared -> revision observed -> Product A interest signal.

Strategic call: Keep Product B as GTM wedge only if it consistently changes agency behavior, not if it only drives consumer engagement.
