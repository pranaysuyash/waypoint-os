# Feature: Hyper-Personalized Dynamic Content Engine

## POV: User POV (Engagement)

### 1. Objective
To turn a boring itinerary into a high-engagement "Media Experience" by generating a custom digital magazine for every trip, tailored to the traveler's specific interests and the local destination.

### 2. Functional Requirements

#### A. The "Daily Pulse" Magazine
- **AI-Curated Articles**: Every morning of the trip, the user receives 3 short, personalized articles:
    - *The History*: "Why this specific neighborhood was built in the 1920s."
    - *The Local Secret*: "The hidden jazz club 2 blocks from your hotel."
    - *The Practical*: "How to pay for the subway in this city today."
- **Visual Storytelling**: Using AI-generated or licensed photography to make the itinerary look like a high-end luxury travel magazine.

#### B. The "Audio-Guide" Podcast
- **Trip-Specific Narratives**: Generating a custom 10-minute "Intro Podcast" for the trip, narrated by an AI voice that matches the traveler's "Vibe" (e.g., "Professional & Formal" vs. "Casual & Adventurous").
- **Real-time "Walking Tours"**: Audio content that triggers via GPS when the user is near a point of interest.

#### C. The "Shopping & Style" Curated List
- **"What to Pack/Wear"**: A visual mood-board of clothing and gear suitable for the destination's weather and the traveler's "Taste Graph" (e.g., "Chic but comfortable for your museum tour today").
- **Local Boutique Links**: Links to local shops that match the user's "Genome" (e.g., "You like sustainable fashion; check out this local designer").

### 3. Core Engine Logic
- **Content Synthesis Engine**: An LLM-based system that scrapes local news, history, and cultural data to synthesize the "Daily Pulse" articles.
- **Visual Layout Engine**: Automatically formatting text and images into a "Mobile-First" magazine layout.

### 4. Safety & Governance
- **Accuracy Audit**: AI checks every "Local Secret" for recent closures or safety warnings before publishing.
- **Copyright Compliance**: Ensuring all images used in the magazine are properly licensed or AI-generated to avoid legal liability.
