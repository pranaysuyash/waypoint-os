# FEATURE: Inventory Yield Arbitrage Engine (Real-Time Decisioning)

## 1. Overview
The **Inventory Yield Arbitrage Engine** is the operational counterpart to the "Autonomous Resource Futures" module. While "Futures" handles long-term betting, the **Arbitrage Engine** focuses on real-time decisioning: deciding whether to book a net-fare flight now, hold a group hotel block, or wait for a predicted price drop based on live market volatility and agency-exclusive net-rate access.

## 2. Business POV (Agency/Business)
- **Problem**: Agencies leave money on the table by booking too early (missing price drops) or too late (losing net-rate availability).
- **Solution**: An AI that monitors thousands of net-rate contracts and public fares, executing "Just-in-Time" booking triggers.
- **Value**:
    - **Markup Optimization**: Increase effective margin by 2-5% through better timing.
    - **Risk-Adjusted Holding**: Automatically hold room blocks when volatility is high and release them when demand peaks to avoid cancellation fees.
    - **Net-Fare Protection**: Ensure that exclusive agency rates are utilized before they expire or are pulled by the supplier.

## 3. User POV (Traveler/Admin)
- **Problem**: Travelers worry they are overpaying or that the "Last Room" will disappear while they decide.
- **Solution**: A system that "Guarantees" the best price by managing the risk in the background.
- **Value**:
    - **Price Protection**: "We've locked in your rate at ₹50K. If the price drops in the next 48 hours, our system will automatically re-book and pass the savings to you."
    - **Invisible Holding**: "We've secured a 72-hour hold on your preferred villa using our agency credit line, giving you more time to decide without risk."

## 4. Technical Specifications
- **Real-Time Volatility Monitor**: Scrapes GDS, NDC, and OTA prices every 15-60 minutes for active quotes.
- **Yield Logic Controller**: Decision tree that balances `SAVING_POTENTIAL` vs. `AVAILABILITY_RISK`.
- **Automated Re-Ticketing**: Integration with GDS "Auto-Queue" to cancel and re-issue tickets within the void window if a lower fare appears.
- **Arbitrage Ledger**: Tracking the "Alpha" (extra profit or savings) generated by the engine for agency reporting.

## 5. High-Stakes Scenarios
- **Scenario 315 (LCC Collapse)**: The engine detects early volatility in a carrier's pricing (anomalous drops or spikes) and suggests shifting active holds to a more stable carrier.
- **Scenario 44 (Pricing Negotiation Loop)**: The engine provides the agent with "Real-Time Floor Price" data to win the negotiation with a price-sensitive customer.
