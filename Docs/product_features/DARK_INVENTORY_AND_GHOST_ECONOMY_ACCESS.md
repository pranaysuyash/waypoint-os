# Feature: Dark Inventory & Ghost Economy Access

## POV: Business POV (Exclusivity/Margin)

### 1. Objective
To provide automated access to "Off-Market" travel assets that are not listed on GDS or OTAs, including private villa collections, unlisted hotel suites, and empty-leg private jet segments.

### 2. Functional Requirements

#### A. The "Ghost" Inventory Crawler
- **Direct API "Backdoors"**: Integrating with the private portals of luxury hotel groups (e.g., Aman, Four Seasons) to see "Owner's Suites" or "VVIP Held" rooms that are marked "Sold Out" publicly.
- **Private Home Networks**: Accessing "Ultra-Luxe" home rentals (e.g., $50k/night villas) that require a manual "Vetting" process for the traveler.

#### B. The "Empty-Leg" Arbitrage
- **Private Jet Matching**: Real-time monitoring of private jet "Empty Legs" (planes flying back empty) and matching them to travelers whose "Vibe" or "Schedule" aligns.
- **Slot Snatching**: Monitoring for last-minute cancellations at "Impossible to Book" restaurants (e.g., Michelin 3-star) and instantly "Snatching" them for the traveler.

#### C. The "Founder's Access" Network
- **Local Fixer Integration**: Automating the request to "Local Fixers" (e.g., "The guy who can get you into X club") via WhatsApp/Telegram and tracking the "Fixer Fee."

### 3. Business Logic
- **"The Scarcity Premium"**: Automatically calculating the markup for "Hard-to-Get" assets (e.g., 25% markup for a last-minute Super Bowl ticket).
- **Membership Level Gating**: Restricting "Dark Inventory" access based on the traveler's "Agency Tier" (e.g., Platinum members only).

### 4. Safety & Ethics
- **Supplier Vetting (High-Density)**: Extreme vetting of "Private" suppliers who don't have public reviews, using the "Blacklist Network" for intel.
- **Anti-Money Laundering (AML)**: Ensuring "Off-Market" transactions (which are often high-value) are fully compliant with global financial regulations.
