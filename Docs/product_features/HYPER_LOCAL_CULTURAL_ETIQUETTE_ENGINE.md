# Feature: Hyper-Local Cultural Etiquette Engine

## POV: User POV (Social Intelligence)

### 1. Objective
To provide real-time cultural "situational awareness," guiding travelers through complex local customs, unspoken social rules, and high-stakes diplomatic etiquette to ensure respect and safety.

### 2. Functional Requirements

#### A. The "Contextual Whisperer"
- **Geofenced Etiquette Alerts**: "You are entering a sacred temple; please ensure your shoulders and knees are covered."
- **Real-time Tipping & Haggling Logic**: "In this market, the first price is usually 30% higher; here is the 'Fair Market Price' for a silk scarf today."
- **Gender & Social Hierarchy Norms**: Providing nuanced advice for specific social contexts (e.g., "In a business dinner here, wait for the eldest person to start eating").

#### B. The "Soft-Skills" AI Tutor
- **Micro-Language "Icebreakers"**: 3 essential local phrases for the specific moment (e.g., "Hello," "Thank you," "The bill please") with audio pronunciation guides.
- **Body Language & Gesture Audit**: "Avoid using your left hand for eating in this region," or "The 'Thumbs Up' gesture is offensive here."

#### C. High-Stakes Diplomatic Guidance (VVIP/Corporate)
- **Gift-Giving Protocol**: "If meeting a government official in this country, a gift of X value is appropriate, but avoid Y color."
- **Meeting Seating & Precedence**: Visual maps of where to sit and who to address first in a formal business setting.

### 3. Core Engine Logic
- **The "Culture Graph"**: A database of thousands of local customs, religious calendars (e.g., Ramadan, Diwali), and social "Faux Pas" logic.
- **Tone-of-Voice Transformation**: An AI feature that "Polishes" the traveler's outgoing messages to local suppliers to match the local "Formality Level."

### 4. Safety & Ethics
- **Hate Speech & Taboo Monitoring**: Warning the traveler against using specific words or topics that are illegal or dangerous in the host country (e.g., "Discussion of X topic is a crime here").
- **Cultural Sensitivity Score**: Rating itineraries on their "Respect for Local Culture" (e.g., avoiding over-touristed zones or culturally sensitive sites during holy days).
