# Feature: Inter-Agency Specialist Agent Marketplace

## POV: Business POV (Scalability)

### 1. Objective
To allow agencies to "Hire" or "Trade" specialized AI agents (Shadow Agents) from other agencies on a per-trip basis, allowing a small agency to handle niche travel (e.g., "Bhutan Trekking") with expert-level proficiency.

### 2. Functional Requirements

#### A. The "Agent Registry" (Specialists)
- **The "Maldives Wedding" Agent**: Specialized in local marriage laws, high-end resort sourcing, and photography logistics.
- **The "Antarctic Expedition" Agent**: Specialized in ice-class vessel compliance, extreme-weather clothing, and polar medical safety.
- **The "Japanese Ryokan" Agent**: Specialized in "Omotenashi" etiquette, traditional dietary rules, and "Hidden" onsen sourcing.

#### B. The "Rental" Handshake
- **Fee-Per-Trip Model**: Agency A hires Agency B's "Tuscany Specialist Agent" for $20 to handle a specific booking.
- **Context Sharing (Clean Room)**: Sharing the "Traveler Genome" with the rented agent in a "Privacy-Safe" way, ensuring no sensitive PII is leaked to the other agency.

#### C. Commission Sharing Logic
- **Automated Settlement**: The system splits the booking commission between the "Primary Agency" and the "Specialist Agent Owner" automatically.
- **Service Level Agreements (SLAs)**: Ensuring the "Rented" agent responds with the same speed and quality as the primary agency's own agents.

### 3. Business Logic
- **"The Knowledge Marketplace"**: Agencies are rewarded for "Training" and "Licensing" their niche AI agents to the rest of the platform.
- **Reputation Scoring**: Specialist agents are ranked by the "Conversion Rate" and "CSAT" they deliver for hiring agencies.

### 4. Safety & Governance
- **Competitive "Non-Compete"**: Ensuring a rented agent cannot "Poach" the customer for its owner agency.
- **Liability Routing**: Clearly defining who is liable if a "Rented" specialist agent makes a compliance error (The Owner vs. The Hirer).
