# Feature: Agency Workforce Gamification & Learning

## POV: Business POV (Internal Training)

### 1. Objective
To turn the "Agency Workspace" into a continuous learning environment, using real-world data and "Crisis Simulations" to upskill junior agents and reward high-performing seniors.

### 2. Functional Requirements

#### A. Crisis Simulation "War Games"
- **Synthetic Disruptions**: The system generates a "Ghost PNR" with a complex crisis (e.g., "The client is at the airport, flight cancelled, all hotels full"). Junior agents must "Solve" it in a sandbox mode.
- **Speed & Accuracy Scoring**: Agents are ranked on how quickly they find a compliance-safe, high-margin alternative.

#### B. The "Knowledge Bounty" System
- **Capturing Tribal Knowledge**: If a Senior Agent discovers a "Hidden Hack" (e.g., "A specific way to get a free upgrade at the Savoy"), they can "Codify" it into a system rule and earn "Platform Credits."
- **Task Meritocracy**: High-complexity/High-margin "Golden Leads" are automatically routed to agents with the highest "Skill Score" in that specific category (e.g., "Luxury Maldives Specialist").

#### C. Real-time "Correct-as-you-Type" Learning
- **Compliance Nudges**: As a Junior Agent drafts an itinerary, the AI suggests improvements: "Did you check the Visa requirements for this 8-hour layover? Click here to learn why."
- **Markup Coaching**: "You're pricing this 10% below market average. You could increase your margin by $20 without losing the sale."

### 3. Business Logic
- **The "Expertise Ledger"**: A blockchain or immutable log of an agent's certifications and successful high-stakes "Saves."
- **Adaptive Training**: The system identifies an agent's weak points (e.g., "Frequent errors in GDS Split PNRs") and serves them 5-minute "Micro-Learning" modules during downtime.

### 4. Safety & Governance
- **"The Learner's Permit"**: New agents have a "Shadow Period" where every PNR they issue must be "Soft-Approved" by the AI Compliance Guardian before being sent to the GDS.
- **Skill-Based Access Control**: Preventing junior agents from accessing high-stakes "Crisis" dashboards until they pass the "Disruption Level 3" simulation.
