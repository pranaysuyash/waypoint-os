# Feature: Autonomous Diplomacy & Legal Shields

## POV: User & Business POV (Legal/Safety)

### 1. Objective
To provide real-time diplomatic and legal support for travelers navigating high-stakes international transitions, including "Safe Passage" negotiations and jurisdictional dispute resolution.

### 2. Functional Requirements

#### A. The "Safe Passage" Engine
- **Corridor Negotiation**: AI monitors "Green Zones" and "Safe Corridors" in active conflict zones or during civil unrest, providing real-time routing that avoids the highest-risk checkpoints.
- **Digital Diplomatic Pouch**: Secure, encrypted storage for "Official Letters of Passage," "Government Sponsorship," or "Journalist Credentials."

#### B. Real-time Legal Arbitration
- **The "Border Defender"**: If a traveler is detained at a border, the app provides instant access to local "Visa & Immigration" law for that specific country, including a "Rights & Responsibilities" summary.
- **Local Lawyer Handoff**: A pre-vetted network of 24/7 on-call international lawyers who can be "Injected" into the chat thread immediately during a legal impasse.

#### C. Sanction & Treaty Compliance
- **Real-time Sanction Checking**: Automatically checking if a local hotel or DMC has been added to a "Sanctions List" (OFAC, EU) *during* the trip and initiating an immediate "Emergency Relocation" if so.

### 3. Business Logic
- **The "Jurisdiction Router"**: When a dispute happens (e.g., a hotel refuses a refund), the system identifies the most "Favorable Jurisdiction" for the agency to file a claim (e.g., Home Country vs. Destination Country).
- **Automated "Note Verbale" Generation**: Generating formal diplomatic requests for assistance to embassies in the event of a lost passport or arrest.

### 4. Safety & Governance
- **"The Guardian" Oversight**: Every diplomatic or legal escalation is automatically cc'd to the Agency's "General Counsel" and "Global Head of Safety."
- **Privacy Shield**: Ensuring the "Identity of the Traveler" is protected during negotiation with local authorities to prevent kidnapping or ransom risk.
