# Feature: Hyper-Local Last Mile Orchestration

## POV: User & Agent POV

### 1. Objective
To solve the "Messy Middle" of travel—the transitions between airports, hotels, and activities that often cause the most stress and friction.

### 2. Functional Requirements

#### A. Seamless Connectivity (SIMs & WiFi)
- **The "Digital Landing" Kit**: Automatic delivery of eSIM profiles or local SIM card instructions 24 hours before arrival.
- **Lounge & WiFi Access**: Proactive delivery of lounge passes or "Secret" airport WiFi passwords for VVIPs.

#### B. The "Inter-Modal" Handshake
- **Rail-to-Road Continuity**: If a traveler takes a train (e.g., Eurostar), the system monitors the *train* arrival time and auto-adjusts the *driver* pickup time at the station.
- **Local Porterage**: Coordinating "Luggage Transfers" between hotels so the traveler can spend the day sightseeing unburdened.

#### C. Local Context "Gems"
- **The "Neighborhood Vibe" Guide**: Micro-guides that trigger via geofence when a traveler checks into their hotel (e.g., "The best coffee is 200m away at 'The Local Bean'; mention your agency for a free pastry").
- **Tipping & Culture Wallet**: A quick-guide on "How to tip in this city" and "Emergency local phrases" for the specific destination.

### 3. Business Logic
- **Supplier "Last-Mile" Graph**: A database of vetted local drivers, guides, and translators that aren't on the GDS.
- **Real-time Driver Tracking**: Integrating with local ride-hailing APIs (Uber, Grab, Bolt) or private fleet telematics to show the traveler exactly where their car is.

### 4. Safety & Security
- **Safe-Transition Verification**: "Did you meet your driver 'Marco' (License Plate: ABC-123)?" check-in.
- **Local Emergency Numbers**: Auto-pinning the nearest hospital, pharmacy, and police station to the "Itinerary Pulse."
