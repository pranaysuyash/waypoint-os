# FEATURE: Community Host Direct Orchestration (Hyper-Local CBT)

## 1. Overview
Traditional platforms (Airbnb, Booking.com) take 15-30% in fees and often distance the traveler from the actual host. **Community Host Direct Orchestration** uses the OS to connect agencies directly to hyper-local hosts (Community Based Tourism - CBT) while providing the "Institutional Safety" and "Payment Security" that travelers expect from a professional agency.

## 2. Business POV (Agency/Business)
- **Problem**: Booking a "Local Homestay" in a remote village is high-risk (unreliable communication, no standard safety checks).
- **Solution**: A "Safety & Trust Layer" that verifies local hosts via community-vouching and provides direct, secure payment rails.
- **Value**:
    - **Unique Inventory**: Access to "Real" experiences that aren't on Expedia or Airbnb.
    - **Higher Margin / Lower Cost**: By bypassing global aggregators, more money goes to the host and more margin stays with the agency.
    - **Social Capital**: Position the agency as a "True Partner" to local communities.

## 3. User POV (Traveler/Admin)
- **Problem**: Travelers want "Authentic" experiences but are afraid of getting stranded or being scammed in remote areas.
- **Solution**: "Agency-Verified" local hosts with the ease of a luxury booking.
- **Value**:
    - **The "Local Guardian"**: "Your host, Mr. Tsering, has been verified by our local safety partner. We have a 24/7 sat-link to his village in case of emergency."
    - **Direct Impact**: "95% of your payment goes directly into the village's education fund. Here is your receipt from the village council."
    - **No-Friction Language Bridge**: Real-time voice translation between you and your host integrated into the OS.

## 4. Technical Specifications
- **Community Vouching Protocol**: A reputation system where local NGOs or "Master Hosts" verify new community participants.
- **Direct-to-Host Payment Rails**: Integration with local wallets (M-Pesa, UPI, GCash) to ensure instant, low-fee payments to remote hosts.
- **Offline-First Safety Monitoring**: Lightweight GPS tracking and "Check-in" triggers that work in low-connectivity areas.
- **CBT Asset Digitization**: AI-driven tools to help local hosts photograph and describe their offerings in a professional "Agency-Ready" format.

## 5. High-Stakes Scenarios
- **Scenario 325 (Safe Passage Request)**: A traveler needs to move through a remote area; the system uses its network of "Community Hosts" to provide safe-houses and local guides.
- **Scenario 64 (Cultural Etiquette Guidance)**: The system provides a "Host-Specific" etiquette guide before the traveler arrives (e.g., "In this village, please do not offer money directly to children; give to the elder instead").
