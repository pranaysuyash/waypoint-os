# FEATURE: Legacy & Milestone Visioning Engine (Life-Mapping)

## 1. Overview
The **Legacy & Milestone Visioning Engine** shifts the agency's relationship with the customer from a "Transactional Booking" model to a "Life-Stage Partnership". It allows travelers (specifically HNWIs and growing families) to map out their travel aspirations over decades, ensuring that "Bucket List" items are executed at the optimal age and fiscal moment.

## 2. Business POV (Agency/Business)
- **Problem**: Agencies lose customers to "The Next Shiny Thing". There is no lock-in beyond the current trip.
- **Solution**: Own the customer's *Future*. If you've mapped their next 10 years, they are 90% more likely to stay with you.
- **Value**:
    - **AUM (Aspirations Under Management)**: Track the total future value of a customer's life-map.
    - **Predictive Inventory Acquisition**: If you know 50 families want a Private Jet to Antarctica in Dec 2028, you can secure the inventory *now* at better rates.
    - **Generational Retention**: Capture the travel goals of children/grandchildren, becoming the "Family Office for Travel".

## 3. User POV (Traveler/Admin)
- **Problem**: People have a "Bucket List" but forget to do it, or do it at the wrong time (e.g., trying to hike the Inca Trail when they are too old).
- **Solution**: A "Travel Financial Advisor" that tracks your "Fitness Window" and "Fiscal Window" for every dream.
- **Value**:
    - **The "Fitness Window" Alert**: "You mentioned wanting to dive the Galapagos. Based on your current health trends, the optimal window for this high-activity trip is the next 3 years. Shall we slot it into 2027?"
    - **Milestone Automations**: "In 2030, your daughter turns 21. We've already scouted the top 3 vineyards in Tuscany for her celebration, as per your 2024 visioning session."
    - **Asset-Backed Travel**: Integration with wealth management to fund "Big Years" (Gap years, Sabbaticals) through long-term savings.

## 4. Technical Specifications
- **Decadal Timeline Visualizer**: A Gantt-style view of the next 20 years, mapping traveler ages against destination "Seasonality" and "Complexity".
- **Aspiration Graph**: Connecting "Soft Goals" (e.g., "Build resilience in my kids") to "Hard Destinations" (e.g., "Wilderness survival course in Alaska").
- **Financial Predictive Integration**: Hooks for wealth management APIs to show "Feasibility Confidence" for future high-cost trips.
- **Continuity Lock**: Encrypted storage of legacy goals that can be passed to heirs or successor agents.

## 5. High-Stakes Scenarios
- **Scenario 326 (Anniversary Loop)**: The system remembers a "Legacy Goal" from 10 years ago—to return to the honeymoon spot on the 25th anniversary—and initiates the "Perfect Mirror" rebooking process 2 years in advance.
- **Scenario 330 (Ethical Impact Report)**: The "Legacy" view includes the cumulative carbon and social impact of 20 years of travel, suggesting "Offset Milestones".
