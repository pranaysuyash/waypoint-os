# Feature: Post-Trip Afterglow & Retention Loops

## POV: User & Business POV

### 1. Objective
To extend the "Trip Experience" beyond the flight home, turning a one-off booking into a lifetime customer relationship through automated sentiment analysis and proactive retention.

### 2. Functional Requirements

#### A. Automated Sentiment Audit
- **"The Afterglow" Check-in**: A personalized "Welcome Home" message that uses NLP to detect "Hidden Frustrations" or "Moments of Delight" in the user's response.
- **Refund/Recovery Trigger**: If the AI detects a bad hotel experience, it automatically initiates a "Customer Service Credit" or "Supplier Refund Request" without the user asking.

#### B. Memory Curation (User POV)
- **The "Digital Keepsake"**: Automatically compiling the traveler's "Live Itinerary" notes, photos, and map data into a shareable "Trip Summary."
- **Expense Summarization**: Generating a "Final Trip Statement" that breaks down spending by category (Flight, Food, Fun) for personal or corporate review.

#### C. Proactive Retention (Business POV)
- **The "Anniversary" Trigger**: "One year ago today, you were in Paris! Want to see what's new there this year?"
- **Loyalty Sync**: Automatically updating the traveler's "Loyalty Vault" with the points earned on the trip and suggesting how to spend them on the *next* trip.

### 3. Business Logic
- **"Next-Trip" Prediction**: Based on the "Taste Graph" and the success of the current trip, the system pre-packages a "Proposed Next Trip" (e.g., "Since you loved the skiing in Zermatt, how about Whistler next Feb?").
- **Referral Momentum**: Prompting the user to "Gift a Friend" a travel consultation credit if their CSAT (Customer Satisfaction) is > 9/10.

### 4. Safety & Governance
- **GDPR Data Archival**: Allowing the user to "Vault" their trip data (encrypted) or "Forget" it completely.
- **Supplier Review Loop**: Automatically feeding user feedback back into the "Supplier Blacklist Network" to protect future travelers.
