# Feature: Predictive Health & Medical Logistics

## POV: Traveler & Business POV (Safety)

### 1. Objective
To protect travelers from local health risks through predictive intelligence and provide seamless medical escalation in the event of illness or injury.

### 2. Functional Requirements

#### A. Environmental Health Prediction
- **Outbreak Monitoring**: Real-time monitoring of local disease spikes (e.g., Dengue, Malaria, Norovirus) at the traveler's destination.
- **Environmental Triggers**: Identifying high "Pollen Counts," "Air Quality Indexes (AQI)," or "Heat Wave" alerts for travelers with respiratory or cardiovascular sensitivities stored in their Genome.

#### B. The "Digital First-Aid" Kit
- **Personalized Medical Packing**: Generating a custom "Med-Kit" list based on the destination and the traveler's medical history (e.g., "Bring your inhaler; Delhi's AQI is currently 300+").
- **Local Medical "Gems"**: Identifying the nearest "Expat-Standard" hospital and English-speaking doctors for the specific neighborhood the traveler is in.

#### C. Tele-Medical Escalation
- **One-Tap Doctor Handoff**: Connecting the traveler to a remote medical consultant (e.g., AirDoctor, Teladoc) who has access to the "Traveler Medical Summary" (Allergies, Meds).
- **Insurance Liaison**: Automatically submitting medical reports to the traveler's insurance provider to authorize "Direct Billing" and avoid out-of-pocket costs.

### 3. Business Logic
- **"The Health Guardian"**: If a traveler reports a symptom in the chat (e.g., "I have a fever"), the system automatically pulls up the "Local Health Risk" map to check for matching outbreaks.
- **Medical Evacuation Orchestration**: Coordinating with specialized medevac suppliers if the local medical standard falls below the "Safety Invariant" for that customer tier.

### 4. Safety & Governance
- **"Need-to-Know" PII Access**: Only the medical responder receives full health details; the travel agent only sees "Medical Event in Progress."
- **Audit of Care**: Logging all medical touchpoints for future insurance and liability review.
