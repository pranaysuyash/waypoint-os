# FEATURE: Regenerative Tourism Impact Tracker (Net-Positive Metrics)

## 1. Overview
Sustainable tourism aims to "do no harm". **Regenerative Tourism** aims to "leave it better than you found it". This engine tracks the net-positive impact of a trip, moving beyond simple carbon offsets to measuring local ecosystem restoration, wealth redistribution, and cultural revitalization.

## 2. Business POV (Agency/Business)
- **Problem**: Sustainability is seen as a "Cost Center" or "Tax". It lacks commercial drive.
- **Solution**: Turn "Impact" into a competitive advantage and a "Loyalty Asset".
- **Value**:
    - **Impact-Driven Premium**: Charge a premium for "Verified Regenerative" trips where the traveler sees exactly where their money goes.
    - **ESG Reporting for Corporate Clients**: Provide "Net-Positive" certificates that companies can use in their annual reports.
    - **Supplier Tiering**: Rank suppliers not just on price, but on their "Regeneration Score" (e.g., how much of their revenue stays in the local village).

## 3. User POV (Traveler/Admin)
- **Problem**: Travelers feel "Climate Guilt" and don't trust generic carbon offsets.
- **Solution**: Tangible proof of positive impact.
- **Value**:
    - **The "Impact Wallet"**: See your trip's contribution in real numbers: "Your stay funded 4 days of reef restoration and supported 12 local farming families."
    - **Regenerative Rewards**: Earn "Impact Credits" for choosing low-carbon transport or participating in local conservation efforts.
    - **The "Ethical Footprint"**: A visual dashboard showing how your presence helped revitalize a dying craft or protected a local ecosystem.

## 4. Technical Specifications
- **Local Income Multiplier (LIM) Calculator**: Tracking the % of every dollar that stays within a 50km radius of the destination.
- **Ecosystem Restoration Hooks**: Integration with biodiversity tracking platforms (e.g., satellite monitoring of reforestation projects funded by the trip).
- **Cultural Preservation Index**: A qualitative-to-quantitative mapper that tracks support for local languages, arts, and traditions.
- **Blockchain Verification**: Immutable ledger of "Impact Transactions" to prevent greenwashing.

## 5. High-Stakes Scenarios
- **Scenario 330 (Ethical Impact Report)**: The traveler receives a 5-year "Cumulative Impact Report" showing how their loyalty to the agency has resulted in the protection of X hectares of forest.
- **Scenario 115 (Sustainable Hotel Swap)**: The system automatically suggests a "Regenerative" hotel over a "Sustainable" one, explaining the difference in net-positive impact.
