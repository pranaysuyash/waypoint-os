# FEATURE: Ethical Sensitivity Advisory Engine (CQ & Etiquette)

## 1. Overview
In a globalized world, "Cultural Blunders" can range from mildly embarrassing to legally dangerous. The **Ethical Sensitivity Advisory Engine** provides real-time **Cultural Intelligence (CQ)** to travelers and agents, ensuring that every interaction respects local norms, laws, and ethical standards.

## 2. Business POV (Agency/Business)
- **Problem**: Agents cannot be experts in every culture. A mistake in a VVIP quote (e.g., suggesting alcohol in a dry region) can lose a client instantly.
- **Solution**: An "Ethical & Cultural Filter" that audits every itinerary and message for sensitivity issues.
- **Value**:
    - **Risk Mitigation**: Avoid legal trouble (e.g., public displays of affection in strict jurisdictions).
    - **Brand Protection**: Ensure the agency is never associated with unethical practices (e.g., orphan tourism, animal cruelty).
    - **High-Stakes Diplomacy**: Assist agents in crafting "Perfectly Respectful" communications for elite international clients.

## 3. User POV (Traveler/Admin)
- **Problem**: Travelers are often afraid of "doing the wrong thing" in a new culture, or accidentally supporting unethical businesses.
- **Solution**: A "Cultural Guardian" in your pocket.
- **Value**:
    - **Real-Time Etiquette Alerts**: "You are entering a sacred site. Please cover your shoulders and turn off your camera. Here is why it matters..."
    - **Ethical Vetting**: "The activity you're looking at involves 'Elephant Riding'. Our ethical engine flags this as a high-risk animal welfare issue. We suggest the [Elephant Sanctuary] instead."
    - **Conversation Cues**: "You are meeting the Village Chief. It is customary to offer a small gift with both hands. Here are 3 respectful topics of conversation."

## 4. Technical Specifications
- **Global Etiquette Database**: A structured repository of cultural norms, religious taboos, and local laws, updated via local 'Cultural Fixers'.
- **Adversarial Ethical Auditor**: An AI layer that scans itineraries for "Red Flag" keywords (e.g., "Voluntourism", "Shark Fin", "Photos with Cubs").
- **CQ Score (Cultural Intelligence)**: A metric for both the agent and the traveler, rewarding respectful and ethically-conscious behavior.
- **Context-Aware Geofencing**: Triggers alerts based on the traveler's precise location (e.g., near a mosque, a border zone, or a protected natural site).

## 5. High-Stakes Scenarios
- **Scenario 64 (Cultural Sensitivity Guidance)**: A traveler is invited to a local wedding; the engine provides a "What to Wear / What to Say" guide in 2 seconds.
- **Scenario 181 (Political & Diplomatic Travel)**: The engine flags a potentially sensitive political meeting and suggests "Neutral Ground" and "Safe Conversation" topics.
