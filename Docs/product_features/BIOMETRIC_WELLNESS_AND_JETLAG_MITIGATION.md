# Feature: Biometric Wellness & Jetlag Mitigation

## POV: Traveler POV (Wellness)

### 1. Objective
To use the traveler's physiological data (via wearables) to proactively mitigate travel-related stress, jetlag, and physical fatigue, ensuring they arrive "Ready to Go."

### 2. Functional Requirements

#### A. Wearable Data Sync (The "Bio-Link")
- **Multi-Device Integration**: Syncing with Apple Watch, Oura, Garmin, and Whoop to monitor Sleep, HR (Heart Rate), and HRV (Heart Rate Variability).
- **Stress-Load Monitoring**: If the system detects high physiological stress during a trip, it prompts the "Concierge" to suggest a later wakeup call or a relaxing spa treatment.

#### B. The "Circadian Shield" (Jetlag Logic)
- **Pre-Trip Priming**: 72 hours before a cross-timezone flight, the app provides a "Light Exposure" and "Melatonin/Sleep" schedule to start shifting the traveler's internal clock.
- **Flight-Phase Nutrition**: Recommending specific meal times and hydration levels during the flight based on the destination timezone.
- **"The Anti-Jetlag Pulse"**: Real-time notifications on arrival: "Go outside for 20 minutes of direct sunlight now to lock in your new timezone."

#### C. Post-Trip Recovery
- **The "Re-Entry" Protocol**: Monitoring sleep recovery post-trip and suggesting "Light Duty" or "Rest Days" to corporate admins for traveling employees.

### 3. Core Engine Logic
- **The "Bio-Genome"**: Extending the "Customer Genome" to include physiological resilience patterns (e.g., "User recovers faster from Westward travel").
- **Predictive Fatigue Engine**: If a traveler has 3 back-to-back red-eye flights, the system auto-alerts the agency: "High burnout risk; suggest a 24-hour layover."

### 4. Privacy & Safety
- **Medical-Grade Encryption**: Biometric data is stored with end-to-end encryption; the agency only sees "Aggregated Wellness Status" (Green/Yellow/Red), not raw heart rate data.
- **HIPAA/GDPR Compliance**: Ensuring all health-related data handling meets global privacy standards for medical information.
