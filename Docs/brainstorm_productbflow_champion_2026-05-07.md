# Product B Flow Brainstorm - Champion - 2026-05-07

## 10,000ft
The structural market problem is trust asymmetry: travelers cannot evaluate plan quality, and agencies cannot easily prove rigor. Product B is a mechanism that makes hidden quality visible.

## 1,000ft
Why this approach wins on first principles:
- Low-friction input (freeform + upload) broadens entry
- Action-oriented outputs convert analysis into behavior
- Share-back dynamic creates organic exposure to agencies
- Product A then solves the agency execution problem

Why this founder/project can win:
- Clear thesis on negotiation mechanism
- Direct access to agency user research
- Ability to align both sides without becoming a marketplace

Hardest assumption:
- Findings must be credible enough to change real conversations.

## Ground level
Immediate execution priorities:
- Improve evidence-linked findings
- Tune output language for real traveler-agent dialogs
- Measure pull-through from Product B exposure to Product A demos

## The thing most people miss about this
Product B is not a free tool. It is the top of a behavior funnel whose success is measured only by downstream agency adoption pressure.
