# Pricing Packaging Discussion (Draft) — 2026-04-17

## Status
- **Draft / Not Final**
- Captures active discussion direction for packaging and monetization.
- Intended for review and iteration before any public pricing launch.

## Discussion Context
This discussion builds on prior pricing exploration and converges on a simpler packaging entry point:
- one default plan around ₹6,000/month
- includes 1 owner/admin + 4 team members
- additional team packs available
- premium capabilities handled as add-ons (e.g., live ops)

## Current Working Direction

### 1) Core Default Plan
- **Plan name (working):** `Core_6000`
- **Price:** ₹6,000/month
- **Included seats:** 1 owner/admin + 4 team members
- **Positioning:** single clear default to reduce early pricing friction

### 2) Team Expansion Packs
- Add incremental seat packs (recommended to keep one simple pack size in v1).
- Example model (working, not locked):
  - `+3 Team Pack` as repeatable expansion unit

### 3) Optional Add-on Components
- **Live Ops / Flight disruption module**
- **Price Intelligence module**
- **Traveler map/share premium module**

Add-ons remain separate from the core package to preserve:
- simple base messaging
- clearer upsell path
- value-aligned monetization

## Why This Direction Is Strong
- Reduces decision fatigue in sales conversations.
- Matches common agency team structure at entry stage.
- Supports ARPU growth through team expansion and optional modules.
