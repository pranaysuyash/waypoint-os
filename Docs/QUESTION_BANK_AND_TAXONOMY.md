# Reusable Question Bank & Taxonomy

## 1. Destination Taxonomy
- **Status**: `fixed` | `open` | `semi-open`
- **Vibe**: `relaxing` | `shopping-focused` | `food-first` | `sightseeing-heavy` | `activity-heavy`
- **Friction Tolerance**: `low` | `medium` | `high`

## 2. Budget Brackets
- **Lean**: Budget-focused, value maximization.
- **Mid**: Balance of comfort and cost.
- **Premium**: High comfort, select luxury.
- **Luxury**: Top-tier, exclusive experiences.

## 3. Traveler Classifications
- `family_with_toddler`
- `family_with_elderly`
- `honeymooners`
- `shopping_enthusiasts`
- `foodies`
- `adventure_seekers`

## 4. Priority Question Bank

### Destination (High Priority)
- "Do you already want a specific destination, or are you open to similar places if they fit your group better?"
- "What kind of trip do you want this to feel like: relaxing, shopping-focused, food-first, or activity-heavy?"

### Budget & Spending (High Priority)
- "What total budget range are you considering for this trip?"
- "Does that budget include flights, food, shopping, and local travel, or are some of those separate?"
- "Do you want to keep a separate amount aside for shopping, or should that be part of the main trip budget?"

### Group Suitability (Medium-High Priority)
- "Who all are traveling, and are there kids, toddlers, or elderly parents in the group?"
- "Is there anything we should keep in mind around walking, stairs, long days, naps, or comfort?"
- "Are there any activities only some members care about strongly?"

### Audit Mode Specifics
- "What are you most unsure about in this plan: price, hotel choice, pacing, activities, or whether it fits your group?"
- "Do you want a fit audit only, or should we also suggest a stronger alternative?"
