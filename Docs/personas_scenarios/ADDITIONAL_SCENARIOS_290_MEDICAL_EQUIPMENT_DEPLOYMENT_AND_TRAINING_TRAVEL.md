## Scenario: Medical Equipment Deployment and Training Travel

### Situation
Medical device companies, clinical trainers, and hospital implementation teams need travel arrangements for equipment deployment, training sessions, and clinical support visits.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate site access, arrange transport for medical devices, support training schedules, and manage service team logistics.

### Why this matters
Medical equipment travel requires regulatory awareness, trained staff coordination, and careful handling of sensitive clinical tools.

### Success criteria
- Equipment deployment and clinical training coordination
- Medical device transport and handling logistics
- Training schedule and support team planning
- Compliance with clinical and regulatory requirements
