# Additional Scenario 31: Health Restrictions

**Scenario**: A traveler has medical or dietary restrictions and wants the agency to ensure the itinerary is safe and comfortable.

---

## Situation

A customer discloses a medical condition or special dietary need while booking.
They expect the agency to handle accommodation, transport, and on-trip logistics with care.

## What the system should do

- Capture the restriction details clearly
- Check whether the destination or hotels can support the need
- Offer compliant options or raise a red flag if the request is unsafe
- Communicate the plan with empathy and confidence

## Why this matters

Health-related requests are non-negotiable.
Poor handling here risks customer safety and agency liability.

## Success criteria

- The system records restrictions accurately
- It validates the itinerary against the restriction
- It provides a safe, trustworthy response
