## Scenario: Wellness and Spiritual Retreat Travel

### Situation
Wellness seekers and spiritual practitioners need travel arrangements for meditation retreats, yoga centers, and holistic health experiences with peaceful environments and wellness activities.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange wellness retreat accommodations and programs, coordinate spiritual practice facilities, provide holistic health experience access, and ensure peaceful environment logistics.

### Why this matters
Wellness travel involves specialized accommodations, spiritual guidance, and health-focused experiences that require knowledge of wellness industry standards and retreat facilities.

### Success criteria
- Wellness retreat accommodation and program arrangements
- Spiritual practice facility and meditation center coordination
- Holistic health experience and therapy access
- Peaceful environment and wellness activity support</content>
