# Additional Scenario 44: Pricing Negotiation Loop

**Scenario**: A customer and internal margin team are negotiating a quote, and the system must maintain version history and show the impact of each concession.

---

## Situation

A high-value booking is under negotiation.
The customer pushes for lower pricing while the agency must preserve margin and maintain transparency.

## What the system should do

- Track quote versions and the concessions made at each step
- Show the margin impact of each request clearly
- Recommend which items to discount, remove, or keep unchanged
- Help the agent close the loop without losing history or context

## Why this matters

Negotiation is a business-critical workflow.
Poor version control leads to confusion, lost margin, and broken trust.

## Success criteria

- The system preserves quote history cleanly
- It supports margin-aware decisions during negotiation
- The customer gets a consistent, professional experience
