## Scenario: Backpacking Budget Travel Planning

### Situation
Budget-conscious backpackers need affordable transportation, accommodation, and activity options while maintaining safety and flexibility in their travel plans.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should identify budget-friendly transportation options, recommend hostels and budget accommodations, suggest free or low-cost activities, and provide safety tips for independent travel.

### Why this matters
Backpacking travel allows for authentic, immersive experiences but requires careful budget management and safety planning to ensure enjoyable and sustainable journeys.

### Success criteria
- Cost-effective transportation and accommodation options
- Free or low-cost activity recommendations
- Safety planning for independent travel
- Flexible itinerary allowing for spontaneous changes</content>
