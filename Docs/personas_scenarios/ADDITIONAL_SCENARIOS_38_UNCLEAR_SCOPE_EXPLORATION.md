# Additional Scenario 38: Unclear Scope Exploration

**Scenario**: A customer provides an ambiguous brief, and the system must ask the right follow-up questions instead of guessing.

---

## Situation

A customer says they want "a beach holiday somewhere nice" without giving dates, budget, or party details.
The agency needs to clarify the real objective before proposing options.

## What the system should do

- Surface missing trip parameters clearly
- Ask targeted questions to establish budget, travel style, and priorities
- Avoid making assumptions about destination, star level, or trip length
- Keep the flow conversational and easy for the customer to answer

## Why this matters

Ambiguous briefs lead to wasted quotes and poor recommendations.
Good exploration saves time and improves customer satisfaction.

## Success criteria

- The system identifies the gaps in the brief immediately
- It asks precise follow-up questions rather than offering a generic quote
- The customer feels understood and supported
