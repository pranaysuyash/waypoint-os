# Additional Scenario 130: Last-Minute Sustainable Alternative Swap

**Scenario**: A customer asks for a sustainable alternative at the last minute, and the system must swap the plan without compromising the trip.

---

## Situation

A traveler is nearing confirmation and requests a greener hotel, transport, or activity option.
They want an alternative that is more sustainable but still feasible on short notice.

## What the system should do

- Find viable sustainable alternatives that fit the current itinerary
- Evaluate timing, cost, and availability impacts quickly
- Explain the trade-offs of switching last minute
- Confirm the swap only if it preserves the itinerary’s core value

## Why this matters

Sustainability decisions often surface late in planning.
A responsive swap avoids losing the customer or forcing them into a less eco-friendly option.

## Success criteria

- The traveler receives a realistic last-minute sustainable alternative
- The system balances impact, availability, and trip continuity
- The agency delivers the swap with transparent cost and timing implications
