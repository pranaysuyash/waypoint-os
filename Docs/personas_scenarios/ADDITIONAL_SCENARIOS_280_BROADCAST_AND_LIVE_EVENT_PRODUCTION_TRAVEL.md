## Scenario: Broadcast and Live Event Production Travel

### Situation
Broadcast producers, live event crews, and media technicians need travel arrangements for on-site production, live broadcasts, and event staging.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange crew transportation, coordinate venue access, manage equipment logistics, and support broadcast schedule reliability.

### Why this matters
Live production travel demands dependable logistics, last-minute flexibility, and coordination with technical crews to avoid costly delays.

### Success criteria
- Crew and venue coordination for live production
- Equipment logistics and transport planning
- Reliable scheduling for broadcast timelines
- Support for contingency and last-minute changes
