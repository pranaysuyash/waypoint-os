# Additional Scenario 93: Delivery of Physical Travel Documents

**Scenario**: A customer needs physical travel documents delivered before departure, and the system must ensure timing, courier reliability, and document security.

---

## Situation

A traveler requires visas, tickets, or other physical documents to arrive before travel.
They expect the agency to coordinate secure delivery and confirm arrival.

## What the system should do

- Arrange reliable courier or express delivery for travel documents
- Track delivery timing against the departure schedule
- Confirm receipt with the customer and provide backup instructions
- Protect the documents during transit and explain any insurance 

## Why this matters

Physical document delivery is a time-sensitive mission-critical task.
Failure here can cancel an otherwise ready trip.

## Success criteria

- The system secures a reliable physical document delivery method
- The customer receives confirmation that documents are in transit
- The agency has a backup plan if delivery is delayed
