# Additional Scenario 84: Experience Booking Conflicts

**Scenario**: Two or more booked experiences overlap in time, and the system must resolve the conflict before the customer arrives.

---

## Situation

A customer books multiple tours or events that unintentionally overlap.
The agency must identify the conflict and offer a revised ordering or alternative.

## What the system should do

- Detect time overlaps across all booked experiences
- Suggest a revised schedule or comparable alternative activity
- Notify the customer of the conflict and the resolution plan
- Avoid last-minute surprises during the trip

## Why this matters

Booking conflicts are easy to create and frustrating to discover on arrival.
A proactive fix preserves the customer’s experience and trust.

## Success criteria

- The system catches the overlap before service delivery
- It provides a clear, conflict-free itinerary
- The customer perceives the agency as attentive and reliable
