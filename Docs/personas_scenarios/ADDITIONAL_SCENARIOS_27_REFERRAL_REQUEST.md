# Additional Scenario 27: Referral Request

**Scenario**: A loyal customer asks how they can refer friends and earn credits, and the system needs to present the referral program clearly.

---

## Situation

A repeat traveler is happy with their booking and asks the agent if there is a referral incentive.
They want to know how to share, what rewards they get, and whether their friend’s trip qualifies.

## What the system should do

- Confirm whether a referral program applies to this customer
- Provide the exact reward structure and redemption process
- Explain any eligibility conditions clearly
- Offer a shareable link or next step

## Why this matters

Referral programs drive organic growth and keep satisfied customers engaged.
If the agency can make this easy, it turns happy travelers into advocates.

## Success criteria

- The system answers the referral question without ambiguity
- The customer receives a ready-to-share option
- The customer’s trust in the agency grows through clarity
