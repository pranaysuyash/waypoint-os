# Additional Scenario 69: Currency and Cash Shortage

**Scenario**: A traveler runs low on local cash or hits currency controls, and the system must provide cash access alternatives and payment guidance.

---

## Situation

A customer is in a destination with limited ATM availability, foreign exchange restrictions, or unexpectedly high cash needs.
They need the agency to help them access funds safely.

## What the system should do

- Identify local currency access constraints and common cash needs
- Recommend safe cash sources, local bank services, or mobile payment options
- Advise on currency exchange timing and fees
- Warn the customer about risky informal currency exchange

## Why this matters

Cash issues are a common source of traveler stress.
Prepared guidance protects customers from being stranded without funds.

## Success criteria

- The system explains cash access clearly and safely
- The customer is directed to reliable funding options
- The itinerary adjusts to avoid cash-dependent risks when possible
