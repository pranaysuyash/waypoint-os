## Scenario: Fintech RegTech Conference and Partner Travel

### Situation
Fintech founders, regulatory technology teams, and compliance partners need travel arrangements for conferences, partner meetings, and regulatory workshops.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate conference attendance, arrange meetings with regulators and partners, manage corporate hospitality, and support compliance-focused networking travel.

### Why this matters
Fintech travel requires balancing innovation with regulatory engagement to build trust and accelerate market entry.

### Success criteria
- Conference and partner meeting coordination
- Regulatory workshop and hospitality logistics support
- Compliance networking travel planning
- Alignment with fintech go-to-market and regulatory strategies
