# Additional Scenario 107: Zero Cash / Cardless Destination Guidance

**Scenario**: A traveler is going to a destination where cash is required and electronic payments are limited, and the system must prepare them accordingly.

---

## Situation

A customer is visiting a destination where ATMs are scarce and credit cards are not widely accepted.
They need the agency to prepare them for cash-based transactions and local payment realities.

## What the system should do

- Identify destinations with limited card acceptance or frequent outages
- Advise on how much local cash to carry and where to obtain it safely
- Recommend backup payment methods and security precautions
- Warn about common cash-only expenses and local vendor practices

## Why this matters

Cardless travel can leave customers stranded or overpaying.
A proper plan keeps the trip functional and secure.

## Success criteria

- The system prepares the traveler for a cash-first destination
- The customer knows where and how to access local funds safely
- The agency reduces the risk of payment frustration abroad
