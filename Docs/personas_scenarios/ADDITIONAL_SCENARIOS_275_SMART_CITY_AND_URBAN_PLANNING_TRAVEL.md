## Scenario: Smart City and Urban Planning Travel

### Situation
Urban planners, smart city teams, and municipal leaders need travel arrangements for city showcases, planning workshops, and infrastructure pilot programs.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate city visits, arrange access to pilot installations, support stakeholder workshops, and manage interagency logistics.

### Why this matters
Smart city travel requires alignment with municipal schedules, infrastructure site access, and collaboration across public and private partners.

### Success criteria
- City showcase and planning workshop coordination
- Pilot installation access and stakeholder meeting logistics
- Interagency travel planning support
- Alignment with municipal timetables and urban development goals
