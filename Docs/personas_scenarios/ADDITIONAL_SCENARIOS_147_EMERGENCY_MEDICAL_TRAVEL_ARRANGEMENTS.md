## Scenario: Emergency Medical Travel Arrangements

### Situation
A traveler experiences a medical emergency while abroad and requires immediate repatriation, medical evacuation, or emergency medical care coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should activate emergency protocols, coordinate with medical providers and insurance companies, arrange appropriate transportation (air ambulance if needed), and provide real-time support to the traveler and their family.

### Why this matters
Medical emergencies abroad can be life-threatening and require rapid, coordinated response to ensure the traveler receives appropriate care and can return home safely.

### Success criteria
- Immediate activation of emergency response protocols
- Coordination with medical professionals and insurance providers
- Safe and timely medical transportation arrangements
- Ongoing communication with family and medical teams</content>
