## Scenario: Higher Education and Recruitment Travel

### Situation
University admissions teams, recruiters, and academic program representatives need travel arrangements for campus tours, recruitment fairs, and international outreach.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange campus visit logistics, coordinate recruitment fair attendance, support faculty travel, and manage student-suitable itineraries.

### Why this matters
Higher education travel must balance academic schedules, admissions cycles, and student experience considerations for effective enrollment outreach.

### Success criteria
- Campus tour and recruitment fair coordination
- Faculty and admissions team travel planning
- Student-friendly itinerary and support services
- Alignment with academic calendar and outreach goals
