## Scenario: Green Finance and Impact Investment Travel

### Situation
Sustainable finance teams, impact investors, and ESG leaders need travel arrangements for green bond roadshows, impact site visits, and environmental due diligence.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate investor site visits, arrange sustainability-focused meeting logistics, support environmental due diligence travel, and manage secure transportation.

### Why this matters
Green finance travel requires ESG-aware planning, evidence-based site engagement, and seamless coordination to support impact-focused investment decisions.

### Success criteria
- Investor site visit coordination for impact assets
- ESG and sustainability meeting logistics support
- Secure travel for high-value finance teams
- Alignment with environmental due diligence timelines
