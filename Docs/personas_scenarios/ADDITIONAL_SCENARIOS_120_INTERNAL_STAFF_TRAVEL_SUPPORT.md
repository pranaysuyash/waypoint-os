# Additional Scenario 120: Internal Staff Travel Support

**Scenario**: A company’s internal team travel needs operational support, and the system must manage travel for employees with corporate policies and centralized invoicing.

---

## Situation

A corporate client books travel for employees and needs the agency to handle centralized approval, invoicing, and policy compliance.
They expect a smooth internal process.

## What the system should do

- Support central billing, employee travel profiles, and approval workflows
- Ensure travel bookings comply with corporate policies
- Provide consolidated invoicing and reporting
- Keep the internal travel coordinator updated on status and exceptions

## Why this matters

Internal staff travel has different operational needs than consumer travel.
A corporate-grade process reduces admin overhead and increases adoption.

## Success criteria

- The system supports centralized invoicing and policy control
- The internal coordinator has clear visibility over employee travel
- The agency delivers corporate travel with minimal administrative friction
