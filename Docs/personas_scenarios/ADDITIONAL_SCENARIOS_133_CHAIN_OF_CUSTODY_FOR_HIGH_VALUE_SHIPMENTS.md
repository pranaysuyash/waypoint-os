# Additional Scenario 133: Chain of Custody for High-Value Shipments

**Scenario**: A customer needs to transport high-value items securely, and the system must ensure chain-of-custody and transparency.

---

## Situation

A traveler is sending or receiving valuable equipment, documents, or cargo as part of their trip.
They expect the agency to manage logistics, security, and accountability at every handoff.

## What the system should do

- Identify trusted logistics partners and secure transport options
- Clarify chain-of-custody procedures and documentation requirements
- Alert the traveler to checkpoints, transfers, and handoff responsibilities
- Provide support if an item is delayed, lost, or needs special handling

## Why this matters

High-value shipments carry financial and reputational risk.
Transparent custody management protects the traveler and reduces anxiety.

## Success criteria

- The traveler knows exactly how their shipment is handled
- The agency ensures secure handoffs and tracking visibility
- The customer can rely on the shipment arriving intact and accountable
