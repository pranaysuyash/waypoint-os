# Additional Scenario 91: Private Jet or VIP Transfer Coordination

**Scenario**: A customer requests private jet service or VIP transfer coordination, and the system must align luxury transport with the itinerary and delivery timeline.

---

## Situation

A traveler wants premium transfers or private aviation logistics for a high-end trip.
They expect the agency to handle charter availability, landing permissions, and seamless ground transfers.

## What the system should do

- Verify availability for private flights, helicopters, or VIP road transfers
- Coordinate timing with the rest of the itinerary and arrival windows
- Explain any additional permissions, fees, or airport restrictions
- Confirm the luxury service without compromising the overall schedule

## Why this matters

VIP transport requires detailed coordination.
A missed transfer in this category is especially damaging to the traveler experience.

## Success criteria

- The system synchronizes premium transfers with the itinerary
- The customer receives clear confirmation of availability and conditions
- The agency delivers a seamless luxury arrival or departure experience
