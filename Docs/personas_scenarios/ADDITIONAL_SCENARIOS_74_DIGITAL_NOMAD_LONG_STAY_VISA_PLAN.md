# Additional Scenario 74: Digital Nomad Long-Stay Visa Plan

**Scenario**: A digital nomad wants to stay abroad for an extended period, and the system must plan visas, workspace access, and local compliance.

---

## Situation

A customer wants a long-term remote stay in a foreign country.
They need help understanding visa rules, tax implications, and practical living arrangements.

## What the system should do

- Identify long-stay or digital nomad visa requirements
- Confirm visa length, application timelines, and renewal rules
- Recommend workspace-friendly accommodations and stable connectivity
- Warn about local compliance or tax considerations when relevant

## Why this matters

Long stays are more complex than short holidays.
A good plan protects the traveler from overstays and compliance issues.

## Success criteria

- The system provides a viable long-stay visa option
- The traveler understands the timeline and renewal requirements
- The itinerary supports remote work and living needs sustainably
