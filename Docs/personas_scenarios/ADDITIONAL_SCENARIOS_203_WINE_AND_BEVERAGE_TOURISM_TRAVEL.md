## Scenario: Wine and Beverage Tourism Travel

### Situation
Wine enthusiasts and beverage connoisseurs need travel arrangements for wine tours, brewery visits, distillery experiences, and beverage tasting events with transportation and accommodation coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange wine tour and tasting reservations, coordinate beverage production facility visits, provide transportation between venues, and ensure beverage tourism experience logistics.

### Why this matters
Beverage tourism involves specialized knowledge of wine regions, production processes, and tasting experiences that require expertise in beverage industry coordination.

### Success criteria
- Wine tour and tasting reservation coordination
- Brewery and distillery visit arrangements
- Transportation between beverage venues and logistics support
- Beverage tourism experience and education enhancement</content>
