## Scenario: Digital Nomad and Remote Work Travel

### Situation
Digital nomads and remote workers need travel arrangements for extended stays, coworking spaces, and work-friendly destinations with reliable connectivity and visa requirements.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange extended stay accommodations, coordinate coworking space access, provide connectivity and infrastructure support, and ensure digital nomad visa compliance.

### Why this matters
Digital nomad travel involves work requirements, connectivity needs, and extended stay logistics that require specialized knowledge of remote work destinations.

### Success criteria
- Extended stay accommodation and housing arrangements
- Coworking space and workspace access coordination
- Connectivity and infrastructure reliability support
- Digital nomad visa and work permit assistance</content>
