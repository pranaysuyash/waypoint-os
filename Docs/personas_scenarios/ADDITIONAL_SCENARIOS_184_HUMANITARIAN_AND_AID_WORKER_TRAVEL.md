## Scenario: Humanitarian and Aid Worker Travel

### Situation
Humanitarian workers and aid professionals need travel arrangements for deployment to crisis zones, refugee camps, and disaster areas with security coordination and specialized logistics.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate with humanitarian organizations, arrange secure transportation to conflict zones, provide crisis area accommodation, and ensure emergency evacuation procedures.

### Why this matters
Humanitarian travel involves high-risk environments that require security coordination, organizational partnerships, and emergency preparedness for aid worker safety.

### Success criteria
- Humanitarian organization coordination and deployment support
- Secure transportation to crisis and conflict zones
- Crisis area accommodation and logistics arrangements
- Emergency evacuation and security procedure coordination</content>
