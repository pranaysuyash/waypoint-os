# Additional Scenario 56: Remote Work Travel

**Scenario**: A customer wants to combine travel with remote work, and the system must verify that the itinerary supports reliable connectivity and workspace needs.

---

## Situation

A traveler books a trip but plans to work from the destination for part of the stay.
They expect the agency to ensure the accommodation and schedule support remote work.

## What the system should do

- Verify Wi-Fi quality and workspace options in the planned accommodation
- Check time zones and meeting availability for the customer’s work schedule
- Highlight any itinerary days that are unsuitable for focused work
- Offer alternatives like coworking spaces or quieter days

## Why this matters

Remote work travel is common and requires different planning than leisure-only trips.
The agency must treat work needs as a core travel constraint, not an afterthought.

## Success criteria

- The system validates connectivity and workspace suitability
- The traveler knows which days are best for work
- The itinerary balances productivity with leisure effectively
