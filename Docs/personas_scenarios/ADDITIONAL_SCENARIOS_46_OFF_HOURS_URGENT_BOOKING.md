# Additional Scenario 46: Off-Hours Urgent Booking

**Scenario**: A customer requests an urgent booking outside business hours, and the system must handle availability while preserving service reliability.

---

## Situation

A customer contacts the agency late at night with a next-day departure request.
They need the agency to confirm logistics quickly, even if some suppliers are not available immediately.

## What the system should do

- Identify which suppliers can confirm on short notice
- Surface any unavailable elements clearly
- Offer the fastest viable alternative if the preferred plan cannot be completed
- Communicate response expectations for off-hours processing

## Why this matters

Urgent bookings are high-value, high-stress opportunities.
Poor handling can lose the customer or create expectations the agency cannot meet.

## Success criteria

- The system triages urgent requests immediately
- It separates confirmed elements from pending ones clearly
- The customer understands the likely delivery timeline
