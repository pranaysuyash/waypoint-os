# Additional Scenario 328: Post-Trip Loyalty Migration

**Scenario**: A traveler returns and realized they stayed "One Night Short" of a major status upgrade (e.g., Marriott Titanium). The system must identify the "Gap" and suggest a "Staycation" or "Workcation" to bridge the status before the year ends.

---

## Situation

- Traveler (S1) returns from a 20-night trip.
- The system checks their "Loyalty Health": They are at 49 nights; 50 is the threshold for the next tier.
- The year ends in 3 weeks.

## What the system should do

- Alert the traveler to the "Status Cliff."
- Calculate the "Value of the Tier": (e.g., "Titanium status gives you $2,000 in free breakfasts and lounge access next year").
- Suggest a "Local Mattress Run": A highly-efficient, low-cost stay at a nearby brand-hotel to cross the threshold.
- Offer to book it with "1-Click" using their saved preferences.

## Why this matters

"Loyalty Optimization" is a high-value advisory service.
Helping a traveler "Win the Game" of loyalty builds massive brand affinity for the agency.

## Success criteria

- The traveler crosses the threshold before the year-end.
- The system calculates the ROI of the "Mattress Run."
- The traveler considers the agency their "Loyalty Strategist."
