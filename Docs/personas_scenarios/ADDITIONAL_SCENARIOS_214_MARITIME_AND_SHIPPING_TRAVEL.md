## Scenario: Maritime and Shipping Travel

### Situation
Maritime professionals and shipping industry specialists need travel arrangements for port visits, shipyard tours, and maritime conferences with vessel access and industry networking opportunities.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange port visit and shipyard tour access, coordinate maritime conference attendance, provide vessel transportation and logistics, and ensure shipping travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Maritime travel involves specialized knowledge of shipping industry access, vessel operations, and maritime safety that requires expertise in maritime tourism coordination.

### Success criteria
- Port visit and shipyard tour access coordination
- Maritime conference and industry event attendance arrangements
- Vessel transportation and maritime logistics support
- Shipping travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
