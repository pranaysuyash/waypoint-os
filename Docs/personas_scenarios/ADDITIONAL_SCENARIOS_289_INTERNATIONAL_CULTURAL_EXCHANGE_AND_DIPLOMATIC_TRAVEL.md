## Scenario: International Cultural Exchange and Diplomatic Travel

### Situation
Cultural exchange organizers, diplomats, and arts delegations need travel arrangements for international exchange programs, embassy visits, and cultural diplomacy events.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate diplomatic and cultural event access, arrange secure travel, manage visa and protocol support, and support delegation schedules.

### Why this matters
Diplomatic travel demands protocol-aware planning, cultural sensitivity, and smooth coordination to foster international relationships.

### Success criteria
- Diplomatic and cultural event coordination
- Secure travel and protocol-aware logistics
- Visa support and delegation scheduling
- Smooth execution of international exchange programs
