## Scenario: Accessible Travel Accommodation Matching

### Situation
A traveler with mobility impairments needs to book accommodations that meet their specific accessibility requirements, including wheelchair-accessible rooms, ramps, and proximity to medical facilities.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should proactively identify and recommend accommodations that match the traveler's accessibility needs, verify accessibility features through property descriptions and reviews, and coordinate with the property to confirm accommodations before booking.

### Why this matters
Ensuring accessible accommodations prevents travel disruptions and provides peace of mind for travelers with disabilities, demonstrating the agent's commitment to inclusive travel experiences.

### Success criteria
- Accommodation recommendations include verified accessibility features
- Property confirmation of accessibility requirements before booking
- Seamless coordination with transportation and activity providers for accessibility continuity</content>
