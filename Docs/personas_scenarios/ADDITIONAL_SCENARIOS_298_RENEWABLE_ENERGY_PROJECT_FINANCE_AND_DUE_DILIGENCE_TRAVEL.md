## Scenario: Renewable Energy Project Finance and Due Diligence Travel

### Situation
Investment teams, project finance specialists, and due diligence consultants need travel arrangements for renewable energy project site visits, financing meetings, and technical reviews.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate project site access, arrange investor meeting logistics, manage technical due diligence schedules, and support cross-party transport planning.

### Why this matters
Renewable energy finance travel requires alignment across investors, developers, and technical experts to validate project viability and close financing.

### Success criteria
- Project site and due diligence visit coordination
- Investor meeting and financing logistics support
- Technical review and expert travel planning
- Alignment with renewable energy financing timelines
