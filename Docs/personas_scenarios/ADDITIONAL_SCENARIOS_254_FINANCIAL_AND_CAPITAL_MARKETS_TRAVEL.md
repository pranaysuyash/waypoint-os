## Scenario: Financial and Capital Markets Travel

### Situation
Financial executives, capital markets teams, and investors need travel arrangements for meetings, roadshows, and market due diligence.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate boardroom availability, local market access, investor event attendance, and secure transportation for high-value financial travel.

### Why this matters
Financial travel needs confidentiality, trust, and precise timing to support investment decisions and market negotiations.

### Success criteria
- Boardroom and investor event coordination
- Secure and reliable transportation planning
- Itinerary planning for due diligence meetings
- Support for sensitive financial engagements
