## Scenario: Religious and Spiritual Pilgrimage Travel

### Situation
Pilgrims and spiritual travelers seek travel arrangements for religious sites, spiritual retreats, and sacred journey experiences with cultural sensitivity and accommodation requirements.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange pilgrimage site visits and accommodations, coordinate with religious institutions, provide culturally sensitive services, and support spiritual journey logistics.

### Why this matters
Religious travel involves cultural sensitivity, spiritual significance, and institutional coordination that requires respectful and knowledgeable service delivery.

### Success criteria
- Pilgrimage site visit and sacred location arrangements
- Religious institution coordination and accommodation support
- Culturally sensitive and respectful service delivery
- Spiritual journey logistics and experience enhancement</content>
