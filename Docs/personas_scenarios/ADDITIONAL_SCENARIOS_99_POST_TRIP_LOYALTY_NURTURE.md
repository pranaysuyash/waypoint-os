# Additional Scenario 99: Post-Trip Loyalty Nurture

**Scenario**: A customer completes a trip successfully, and the system must follow up with loyalty offers, feedback collection, and future booking incentives.

---

## Situation

A traveler has returned from a trip, and the agency wants to convert the positive experience into repeat business.
They need a thoughtful post-trip follow-up strategy.

## What the system should do

- Send a timely thank-you and feedback request after the trip
- Offer loyalty incentives or tailored future trip ideas based on the customer’s experience
- Capture satisfaction signals and any issues to improve service
- Keep the follow-up personal, not generic marketing noise

## Why this matters

Post-trip engagement drives retention and referrals.
A strong follow-up turns customers into advocates.

## Success criteria

- The system activates a post-trip nurture sequence
- The customer feels appreciated and understood
- The agency converts satisfaction into future bookings or referrals
