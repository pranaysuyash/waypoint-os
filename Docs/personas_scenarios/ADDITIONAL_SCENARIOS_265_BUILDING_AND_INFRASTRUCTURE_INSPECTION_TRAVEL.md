## Scenario: Building and Infrastructure Inspection Travel

### Situation
Inspectors, engineering teams, and infrastructure owners need travel arrangements for site inspections, safety audits, and compliance reviews.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate inspection appointments, arrange travel to industrial and construction sites, manage accommodation near remote locations, and support safety compliance logistics.

### Why this matters
Inspection travel demands coordination with site teams, safety planning, and precise timing to ensure reviews happen without disrupting operations.

### Success criteria
- Inspection and audit appointment coordination
- Remote site travel and lodging planning
- Safety compliance and logistics support
- Minimal disruption to ongoing infrastructure work
