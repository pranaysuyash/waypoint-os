## Scenario: Pharma Regulatory Submission and Clinical Trial Travel

### Situation
Pharmaceutical regulatory affairs teams and clinical trial sponsors need travel arrangements for submission meetings, regulatory authority visits, and trial oversight activities.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate regulatory office visits, arrange secure transport for review teams, manage trial site inspection logistics, and support documentation handoff schedules.

### Why this matters
Pharma travel must align with strict regulatory timelines, documentation requirements, and clinical inspection protocols to keep drug development on track.

### Success criteria
- Regulatory submission and review meeting coordination
- Secure travel and documentation logistics support
- Trial site and oversight inspection planning
- Compliance with health authority requirements
