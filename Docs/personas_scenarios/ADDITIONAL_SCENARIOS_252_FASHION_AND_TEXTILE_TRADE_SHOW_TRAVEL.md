## Scenario: Fashion and Textile Trade Show Travel

### Situation
Fashion designers, buyers, and textile industry professionals need travel arrangements for trade shows, runway events, and sourcing trips.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate trade show access, schedule supplier visits, arrange logistics for sample transport, and manage event-related travel details.

### Why this matters
Fashion and textile trade show travel requires careful timing, supplier coordination, and management of fragile materials to keep the sourcing journey seamless.

### Success criteria
- Trade show and runway event access coordination
- Supplier and manufacturing sourcing trip planning
- Logistics support for samples and materials transport
- Comfortable arrangements for design and buying teams
