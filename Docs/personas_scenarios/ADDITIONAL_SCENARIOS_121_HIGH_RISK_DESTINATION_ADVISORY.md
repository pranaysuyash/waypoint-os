# Additional Scenario 121: High-Risk Destination Advisory

**Scenario**: A traveler plans to visit a destination with safety or health risks, and the system must provide accurate advisories and contingency planning.

---

## Situation

A customer requests travel to a location that has current advisories due to political unrest, health alerts, or natural hazards.
They expect the agency to advise them honestly and offer safer alternatives if needed.

## What the system should do

- Identify relevant travel advisories and risk factors for the destination
- Share clear safety, health, and entry requirement information
- Recommend contingency plans and evacuation options
- Help the customer decide whether to proceed or choose another destination

## Why this matters

High-risk destinations require careful planning.
Customers need accurate risk guidance rather than overly optimistic sales pitches.

## Success criteria

- The system provides a fact-based assessment of destination risk
- The customer understands the implications and possible safety measures
- The agency helps the customer make a responsible travel decision
