# Additional Scenario 90: Seasonal Packing Checklist

**Scenario**: A traveler needs a destination-specific packing checklist, and the system must provide the right list for weather, activities, and local conditions.

---

## Situation

A customer asks what to pack for a destination in a specific season.
They want to avoid packing too much or leaving critical items behind.

## What the system should do

- Provide a checklist tailored to destination, season, and planned activities
- Include weather gear, local dress expectations, and activity-specific items
- Call out any items that are difficult or expensive to replace locally
- Offer a concise summary and printable checklist if needed

## Why this matters

Packing is a common source of pre-trip anxiety.
A good checklist improves comfort and prevents avoidable problems.

## Success criteria

- The traveler has a complete, relevant packing list
- The list balances essentials with local purchase options
- The agency reduces packing-related customer uncertainty
