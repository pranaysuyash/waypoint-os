## Scenario: Pharmaceutical Supply Chain and Logistics Travel

### Situation
Pharmaceutical supply chain teams, cold-chain logistics experts, and distribution managers need travel arrangements for manufacturing site visits, regulatory logistics reviews, and shipment coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate access to manufacturing and distribution facilities, arrange refrigerated transport logistics, schedule regulatory compliance reviews, and support cross-border shipment planning.

### Why this matters
Pharmaceutical logistics travel requires tight timing, temperature-controlled handling, and regulatory compliance to protect product integrity and avoid supply disruptions.

### Success criteria
- Manufacturing and distribution facility access coordination
- Cold-chain transport and logistics planning
- Regulatory review and compliance meeting support
- Cross-border shipment coordination and timeline alignment
