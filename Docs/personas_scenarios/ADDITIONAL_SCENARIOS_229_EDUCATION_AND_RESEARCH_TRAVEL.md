## Scenario: Education and Research Travel

### Situation
Education professionals and research specialists need travel arrangements for academic conferences, research facilities, and educational institutions with technical access and coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange academic conference and research facility access, coordinate educational institution attendance, provide technical transportation and logistics, and ensure education travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Education travel involves specialized knowledge of research protocols, academic safety, and institutional access that requires expertise in education industry tourism.

### Success criteria
- Academic conference and research facility access coordination
- Educational institution and event attendance arrangements
- Technical transportation and education logistics support
- Education travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
