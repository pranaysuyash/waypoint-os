# Additional Scenario 41: Supplier Invoice Discrepancy

**Scenario**: The agency receives a supplier invoice that differs from the agreed quote, and the system must identify the discrepancy and direct the agent to resolve it.

---

## Situation

After confirmation, a hotel or tour provider sends an invoice that is higher than the original proposal.
The agent needs to know whether to absorb the difference, dispute the invoice, or pass it to the customer.

## What the system should do

- Compare the supplier invoice to the original agreed itinerary and costs
- Highlight any unexpected increases or missing credits
- Recommend whether the difference is the supplier’s responsibility, the customer’s, or negotiable
- Provide the agent with the correct next step and communication strategy

## Why this matters

Supplier discrepancies directly impact margins and customer trust.
Catching them early prevents last-minute surprises and protects the agency’s reputation.

## Success criteria

- The system detects invoice mismatches automatically
- It identifies the root cause (rate change, extra fee, exchange rate)
- The agent gets a clear recommendation for resolution
