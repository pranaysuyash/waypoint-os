# Additional Scenario 42: Age-Mix Group Complexity

**Scenario**: A mixed-age group needs a vacation that is safe and enjoyable for toddlers, teens, and seniors, and the system must balance activity, rest, and accessibility.

---

## Situation

A family booking includes infants, teenagers, and grandparents.
They want a single trip that satisfies everyone without one group being left behind.

## What the system should do

- Recognize the different age bands and their needs
- Recommend activities with built-in rest and optional escalation
- Flag any accessibility or mobility concerns for hotels and transport
- Offer a flexible itinerary that can be adjusted for energy levels

## Why this matters

Age-diverse groups are common and hard to satisfy.
A poorly designed plan can ruin the whole trip for one or more members.

## Success criteria

- The itinerary addresses multiple age groups explicitly
- The system balances active and gentle options
- The customer feels the agency understands the group dynamics
