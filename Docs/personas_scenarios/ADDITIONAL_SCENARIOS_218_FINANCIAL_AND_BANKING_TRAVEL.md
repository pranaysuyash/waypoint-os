## Scenario: Financial and Banking Travel

### Situation
Financial professionals and banking specialists need travel arrangements for financial conferences, banking institutions, and investment events with industry access and networking opportunities.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange financial conference and banking institution access, coordinate investment event attendance, provide industry transportation and logistics, and ensure financial travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Financial travel involves specialized knowledge of banking regulations, investment events, and financial industry coordination that requires expertise in financial tourism.

### Success criteria
- Financial conference and banking institution access coordination
- Investment event and industry networking attendance arrangements
- Industry transportation and financial logistics support
- Financial travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
