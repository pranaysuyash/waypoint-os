# Additional Scenario 114: Multiple Time Zone Meeting Support

**Scenario**: A traveler must attend meetings across multiple time zones during their trip, and the system must optimize the itinerary and schedule to reduce jet lag and meeting conflict.

---

## Situation

A business traveler has meetings across different time zones during a multi-destination trip.
They need the agency to minimize fatigue and ensure reliable attendance.

## What the system should do

- Map meeting times and locations across time zones
- Recommend an itinerary that preserves rest and avoids impossible same-day travels
- Provide timezone-aware scheduling guidance and logistics
- Offer alternatives when meetings are too compressed or poorly aligned

## Why this matters

Cross-time-zone business travel is challenging.
A smart plan maintains productivity and prevents missed commitments.

## Success criteria

- The system optimizes the trip for meeting timing and rest
- The traveler feels the schedule is realistic and manageable
- The agency reduces the risk of no-shows or meeting fatigue
