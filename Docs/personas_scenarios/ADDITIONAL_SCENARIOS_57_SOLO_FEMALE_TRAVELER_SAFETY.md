# Additional Scenario 57: Solo Female Traveler Safety

**Scenario**: A solo female traveler requests a safe itinerary, and the system must prioritize security, appropriate accommodations, and safe transfer options.

---

## Situation

A customer is traveling alone and specifically asks for safety-first planning.
They expect the agency to recommend secure choices and avoid risky situations.

## What the system should do

- Choose accommodations and transport providers with strong safety reputations
- Avoid late-night transfers and remote check-ins when possible
- Provide local safety guidance and emergency support information
- Reassure the traveler with facts, not stereotypes

## Why this matters

Safety is a top concern for solo travelers, especially women.
Good planning reduces risk and increases confidence in the trip.

## Success criteria

- The itinerary favors safe, reputable providers
- The customer receives practical safety information
- The agency shows sensitivity and real attention to traveler needs
