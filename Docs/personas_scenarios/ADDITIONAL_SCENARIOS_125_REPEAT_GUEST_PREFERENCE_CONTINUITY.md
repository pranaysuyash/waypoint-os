# Additional Scenario 125: Repeat Guest Preference Continuity

**Scenario**: A returning customer wants their previous travel preferences honored, and the system must preserve repeat guest continuity across bookings.

---

## Situation

A traveler has booked with the agency before and expects past preferences—room type, dietary needs, and service style—to carry over consistently.
They want the agency to avoid repeating requests and anticipate their needs.

## What the system should do

- Recall repeat guest preferences and apply them to the new itinerary
- Verify whether existing preferences are still accurate before booking
- Communicate relevant changes to suppliers and ensure continuity
- Make it easy for the customer to update preferences if needed

## Why this matters

Returning customers expect personalization and recognition.
Preserving preferences improves loyalty and reduces friction.

## Success criteria

- The system honors prior preferences while validating them for the new trip
- The customer feels recognized and understood
- The agency avoids needless repetition and delivers a smoother repeat booking experience
