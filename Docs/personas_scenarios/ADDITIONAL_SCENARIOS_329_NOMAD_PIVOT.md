# Additional Scenario 329: The "I want to move here" Digital Nomad Pivot

**Scenario**: A traveler mid-trip (or post-trip) decides they want to extend their stay by 3-6 months and become a "Digital Nomad." The system must pivot from "Leisure Trip" to "Long-Stay Residency" logistics.

---

## Situation

- Traveler (S1) is in Portugal for 2 weeks.
- They message the agency: "I love it here. Can I stay for 6 months? What about my visa?"
- They need a co-working space, a long-term apartment, and a "Digital Nomad Visa."

## What the system should do

- Check the "Visa Reciprocity" for the traveler's nationality and Portugal.
- Trigger the "Digital Nomad Visa" playbook (referring to [VISA_AND_DOCUMENTATION_FUNDAMENTALS.md](../industry_domain/regulatory_compliance/VISA_AND_DOCUMENTATION_FUNDAMENTALS.md)).
- Switch the "Sourcing Engine" to "Long-Stay Mode" (searching for apartments vs. hotels).
- Coordinate the "Tax Residency" advice (notifying the traveler of the 183-day rule).
- Propose a "Relocation Package" including health insurance and local banking setup.

## Why this matters

"Trip Extension" is the ultimate growth hack.
Pivoting to "Relocation Logistics" captures a massive share of wallet and moves the agency into the "Lifestyle Management" category.

## Success criteria

- The traveler has a 6-month residency plan within 24 hours.
- Visa documents are pre-filled by the AI.
- The agency's margin shifts from "Commission" to "Service Fee + Subscription."
