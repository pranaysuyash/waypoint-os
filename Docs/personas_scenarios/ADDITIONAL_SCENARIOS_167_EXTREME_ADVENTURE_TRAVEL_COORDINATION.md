## Scenario: Extreme Adventure Travel Coordination

### Situation
Adrenaline-seeking travelers need specialized arrangements for high-risk activities like mountaineering, deep-sea diving, or extreme sports with proper safety protocols.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange certified guides and equipment, coordinate emergency evacuation services, provide comprehensive safety briefings, and ensure proper insurance coverage for extreme activities.

### Why this matters
Extreme adventure travel carries significant risks that require expert coordination, safety protocols, and emergency preparedness to ensure participant safety.

### Success criteria
- Certified guide and equipment arrangements
- Emergency evacuation and medical service coordination
- Comprehensive safety briefings and risk assessments
- Specialized insurance coverage for extreme activities</content>
