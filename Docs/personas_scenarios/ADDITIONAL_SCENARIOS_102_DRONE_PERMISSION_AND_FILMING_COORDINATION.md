# Additional Scenario 102: Drone Permission and Filming Coordination

**Scenario**: A customer wants to use a drone or film on location, and the system must handle permits, local regulations, and venue permissions.

---

## Situation

A traveler plans to capture aerial footage or produce video content in a foreign destination.
They need the agency to manage legal permissions and location rules.

## What the system should do

- Identify local drone and filming regulations for the destination
- Secure permits and approvals when required
- Advise on restricted areas, no-fly zones, and insurance requirements
- Coordinate permissions with venues and local authorities

## Why this matters

Filming without permission can result in fines or confiscated equipment.
Proper coordination protects the customer and the agency.

## Success criteria

- The system confirms the legal ability to film or fly drones
- The customer has a clear permit and compliance plan
- The agency avoids regulatory or enforcement issues
