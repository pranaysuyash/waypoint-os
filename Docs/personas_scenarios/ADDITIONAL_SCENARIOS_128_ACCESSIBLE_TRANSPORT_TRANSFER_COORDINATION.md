# Additional Scenario 128: Accessible Transport Transfer Coordination

**Scenario**: A traveler with accessibility needs requires coordinated transport transfers, and the system must manage accessible vehicles and assistive services.

---

## Situation

A customer needs wheelchair-accessible transport, mobility assistance, or other accommodations during transfers.
They expect the agency to coordinate transport providers and minimize barriers.

## What the system should do

- Identify transport partners that support mobility and accessibility needs
- Reserve the right vehicle type and confirm service availability
- Provide transfer instructions that account for assistive equipment and accessibility paths
- Communicate clearly about any limitations or alternative plans

## Why this matters

Accessible travelers depend on reliable, inclusive transport support.
A coordinated transfer reduces risk and helps the traveler feel safe and respected.

## Success criteria

- The transfer plan accommodates the traveler’s accessibility needs
- The customer receives accurate logistics and confirmation
- The agency avoids last-minute accessibility gaps during transport handoffs
