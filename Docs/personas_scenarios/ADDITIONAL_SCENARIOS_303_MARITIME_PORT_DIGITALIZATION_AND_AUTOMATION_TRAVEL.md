## Scenario: Maritime Port Digitalization and Automation Travel

### Situation
Port operators, logistics technologists, and maritime automation teams need travel arrangements for digital transformation site visits, automation demonstrations, and pilot program reviews.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate port access, arrange meetings with operations and technology teams, manage equipment and demonstration logistics, and support digitalization workshops.

### Why this matters
Maritime digitalization travel requires coordination with complex port stakeholders, security protocols, and operational timing to successfully pilot automation technology.

### Success criteria
- Port digitalization and automation site visit coordination
- Technology demonstration and stakeholder meeting logistics support
- Equipment and operations planning for pilot programs
- Smooth access to maritime operations and secure facilities
