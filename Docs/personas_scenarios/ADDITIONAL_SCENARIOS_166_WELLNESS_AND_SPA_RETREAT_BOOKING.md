## Scenario: Wellness and Spa Retreat Booking

### Situation
Travelers seeking rejuvenation need to book wellness retreats with appropriate accommodations, spa treatments, and wellness activities that match their health goals.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should evaluate retreat credibility and offerings, arrange transportation to remote locations, coordinate wellness activities and dietary requirements, and provide post-retreat follow-up resources.

### Why this matters
Wellness travel requires matching traveler goals with authentic retreat experiences, ensuring the journey contributes to physical and mental well-being.

### Success criteria
- Credible wellness retreat selection and booking
- Transportation coordination to retreat locations
- Personalized wellness activity and dietary arrangements
- Post-retreat integration and follow-up support</content>
