## Scenario: World Health Organization and Global Health Summit Travel

### Situation
Global health delegates, WHO representatives, and health summit organizers need travel arrangements for international health summits, policy meetings, and diplomatic health coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate summit attendance, arrange secure and compliant travel, manage delegation lodging, and support high-level policy meeting logistics.

### Why this matters
Global health summit travel requires diplomatic protocol, health security, and cross-border coordination to align international health policy efforts.

### Success criteria
- Summit and delegation coordination for global health events
- Secure and compliant travel planning
- Lodging and policy meeting logistics support
- Alignment with international health diplomacy requirements
