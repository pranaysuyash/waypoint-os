## Scenario: Solar Plus Storage and Grid Integration Travel

### Situation
Renewable energy developers, grid integration specialists, and storage project teams need travel arrangements for solar-plus-storage site visits, interconnection reviews, and grid impact studies.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange project site access, coordinate meetings with utilities and regulators, manage technical review logistics, and support battery storage deployment planning.

### Why this matters
Solar-plus-storage travel demands cross-sector coordination between developers, grid operators, and regulators to ensure successful clean energy integration.

### Success criteria
- Site visit and interconnection review coordination
- Utility and regulator meeting logistics support
- Technical and deployment planning travel arrangements
- Alignment with grid integration and project timelines
