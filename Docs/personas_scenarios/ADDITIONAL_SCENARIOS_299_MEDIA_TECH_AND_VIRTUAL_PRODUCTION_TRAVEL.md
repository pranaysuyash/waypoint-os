## Scenario: Media Tech and Virtual Production Travel

### Situation
Virtual production teams, media technologists, and content studios need travel arrangements for on-set technology demonstrations, virtual production workshops, and studio facility visits.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate studio access, arrange transport for virtual production equipment, manage talent and technical crew itineraries, and support digital content production logistics.

### Why this matters
Media tech travel requires close coordination with production schedules, technology vendors, and studio operations to avoid costly delays during shoots.

### Success criteria
- Studio and virtual production site coordination
- Equipment transport and crew logistics support
- Talent and technical schedule alignment
- Smooth execution of production technology demos
