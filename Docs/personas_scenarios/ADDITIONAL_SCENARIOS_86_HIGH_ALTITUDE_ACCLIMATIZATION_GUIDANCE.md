# Additional Scenario 86: High-Altitude Acclimatization Guidance

**Scenario**: A traveler books a high-altitude destination and needs acclimatization advice so the trip is safe and enjoyable.

---

## Situation

A customer requests a mountain or high-elevation itinerary.
They expect the agency to advise on altitude sickness, pacing, and health precautions.

## What the system should do

- Identify high-altitude segments of the itinerary
- Recommend acclimatization days and gradual ascent plans
- Advise on hydration, medication, and emergency procedures
- Provide destination-specific guidance for altitude-sensitive travelers

## Why this matters

High-altitude trips carry health risks.
A thoughtful plan reduces the chance of illness and improves the experience.

## Success criteria

- The system includes acclimatization in the itinerary plan
- The traveler receives clear health and pacing advice
- The agency demonstrates expertise in high-elevation travel
