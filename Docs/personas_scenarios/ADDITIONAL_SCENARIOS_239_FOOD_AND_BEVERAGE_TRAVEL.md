## Scenario: Food and Beverage Travel

### Situation
Food and beverage professionals need travel arrangements for food processing facilities, beverage conferences, and culinary research centers with technical access and coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange food processing facility and beverage conference access, coordinate culinary research center attendance, provide technical transportation and logistics, and ensure food travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Food travel involves specialized knowledge of culinary protocols, food safety, and facility access that requires expertise in food industry tourism.

### Success criteria
- Food processing facility and beverage conference access coordination
- Culinary research center and event attendance arrangements
- Technical transportation and food logistics support
- Food travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
