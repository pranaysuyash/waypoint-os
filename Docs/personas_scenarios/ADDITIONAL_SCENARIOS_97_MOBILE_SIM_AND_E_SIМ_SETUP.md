# Additional Scenario 97: Mobile SIM and eSIM Setup

**Scenario**: A traveler needs reliable mobile connectivity abroad, and the system must recommend SIM or eSIM options and setup guidance.

---

## Situation

A customer is traveling internationally and wants dependable data and calling options.
They expect the agency to advise on the best local or global mobile solution.

## What the system should do

- Recommend whether to buy a physical SIM, eSIM, or use roaming
- Provide the best carriers or plans for the destination and length of stay
- Explain setup steps and any device compatibility requirements
- Warn about coverage gaps, local registration rules, or hidden fees

## Why this matters

Connectivity is essential for travelers today.
A poor mobile plan can create stress, lost directions, and broken communications.

## Success criteria

- The system delivers a clear mobile connectivity plan
- The customer understands how to activate it before or during travel
- The agency avoids connectivity surprises and enhances trip confidence
