# Additional Scenario 70: Health Advisory Travel Restrictions

**Scenario**: A destination has travel restrictions due to a health advisory, and the system must help the customer understand entry rules, testing, and quarantine requirements.

---

## Situation

A traveler is planning a trip to a destination with health-related entry requirements.
They need the agency to interpret health advisories and make the plan compliant.

## What the system should do

- Summarize current entry requirements, testing rules, and quarantine policies
- Confirm the customer’s vaccination or health certificate status
- Offer a compliant alternative if the destination is too restrictive
- Explain the process for changes if advisories update before travel

## Why this matters

Health restrictions can invalidate a trip at the border.
Accurate advisory handling prevents denied entry and expensive cancellations.

## Success criteria

- The system provides up-to-date health compliance guidance
- The traveler knows exactly what they must present at arrival
- The agency plans the trip so entry requirements are met without surprises
