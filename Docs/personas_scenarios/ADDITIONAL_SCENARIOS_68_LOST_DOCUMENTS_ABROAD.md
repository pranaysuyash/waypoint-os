# Additional Scenario 68: Lost Documents Abroad

**Scenario**: A traveler loses their passport or travel documents abroad, and the system must guide them through emergency replacement and itinerary impact.

---

## Situation

A traveler reports losing a passport or visa while overseas.
They need urgent support to replace documents and continue or return home safely.

## What the system should do

- Provide country-specific emergency passport replacement guidance
- Explain the nearest embassy/consulate process and required documents
- Assess how the delay affects the itinerary and flights
- Offer clear next steps and keep the customer calm

## Why this matters

Lost documents are a worst-case travel emergency.
Fast, accurate guidance is essential to prevent being stranded.

## Success criteria

- The system delivers local emergency replacement instructions
- The customer understands the impact on travel plans
- The agency helps minimize disruption and expense
