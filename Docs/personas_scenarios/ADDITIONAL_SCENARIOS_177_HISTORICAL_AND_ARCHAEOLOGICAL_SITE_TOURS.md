## Scenario: Historical and Archaeological Site Tours

### Situation
History buffs and archaeology enthusiasts seek guided tours of ancient sites, museums, and historical landmarks with expert interpretation and educational context.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange guided tours with certified historians or archaeologists, coordinate site access and permits, book educational accommodations, and provide historical context materials.

### Why this matters
Historical travel requires expert interpretation and educational context to bring ancient sites and events to life, enhancing understanding and appreciation of cultural heritage.

### Success criteria
- Guided tours with expert historians and archaeologists
- Site access and special permit coordination
- Educational accommodation and resource provision
- Historical context and interpretation services</content>
