## Scenario: Legal and Compliance Travel

### Situation
Legal professionals and compliance specialists need travel arrangements for legal conferences, compliance audits, and regulatory meetings with technical access and coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange legal conference and compliance audit access, coordinate regulatory meeting attendance, provide technical transportation and logistics, and ensure legal travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Legal travel involves specialized knowledge of compliance protocols, legal safety, and regulatory access that requires expertise in legal industry tourism.

### Success criteria
- Legal conference and compliance audit access coordination
- Regulatory meeting and event attendance arrangements
- Technical transportation and legal logistics support
- Legal travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
