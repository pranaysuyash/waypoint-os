## Scenario: Sponsored Sports Fan Travel and Hospitality Travel

### Situation
Sports sponsorship teams, hospitality partners, and fan engagement organizers need travel arrangements for sponsored fan travel, corporate hospitality, and event VIP experiences.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate fan hospitality packages, arrange travel and lodging, manage VIP access, and support sponsor-driven experience logistics.

### Why this matters
Sponsored fan travel requires seamless coordination between event organizers, hospitality teams, and brand sponsors to deliver memorable experiences.

### Success criteria
- Fan hospitality and VIP access coordination
- Travel and lodging planning for sponsored experiences
- Sponsorship event logistics support
- Smooth delivery of premium fan engagement experiences
