# Additional Scenario 103: Chronic Medication Refill Abroad

**Scenario**: A traveler on chronic medication needs a refill or prescription support while abroad, and the system must handle medical continuity safely.

---

## Situation

A customer with a long-term health condition travels abroad and requires medication continuity.
They expect the agency to help with prescriptions, pharmacy access, and documentation.

## What the system should do

- Confirm local regulations for prescription medication import and refill
- Identify nearby pharmacies or medical providers who can support refills
- Advise on carrying medical documentation and prescriptions
- Provide a safe backup plan if the primary medication is unavailable

## Why this matters

Medication continuity is essential for health and safety.
A gap can disrupt the trip and cause serious health issues.

## Success criteria

- The system secures a medication refill plan before travel
- The traveler knows how to access medical support abroad
- The agency prevents a lapse in essential medication
