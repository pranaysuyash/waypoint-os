# S2-S2: Document Chaos

**Persona**: Family Coordinator
**Scenario**: A family is trying to get everyone’s passports, visas, and medical documents together before travel, and the agency must manage the chaos.

---

## Situation

A coordinator is organizing a large group trip and is struggling to collect and track travel documents for everyone.
Some people have passports, some need renewals, and others need visas or health certificates.

### Coordinator message
> "I have passports for 6 people, my brother’s passport is expiring, and one kid needs a yellow fever certificate. Can you help me keep track?"

### Coordinator’s state
- Stressed by document requirements
- Confused about deadlines and who has what
- Needs the agency to manage the paperwork, not just the booking

## What the system should do

1. **Track document status**
   - List each traveler and their passport expiry date
   - Show visa requirements by destination
   - Track medical document needs

2. **Highlight urgent items**
   - Flag passports expiring within 6 months
   - Warn about visa timelines for specific destinations
   - Alert about required health certificates

3. **Provide a shared document checklist**
   - Share the checklist with the coordinator
   - Update status as documents arrive
   - Reduce the need for repeated follow-up questions

4. **Support coordination**
   - Allow the coordinator to assign document tasks to family members
   - Show who still needs to provide what

## Required system behaviors

- Traveler-level document tracking
- Deadline alerts for visas and health requirements
- Shared checklist for coordinators
- Status updates that avoid repeated manual follow up
- Integration with booking and compliance checks

## Why this matters

Document chaos is one of the hardest parts of group travel.
If the agency can stabilize this, the coordinator will trust them to keep the whole group safe.

## Success criteria

- The system tracks all traveler documents in one place.
- The coordinator sees urgent issues clearly.
- The agency can use document status to prevent booking mistakes.
- The coordinator can share and update the checklist easily.
