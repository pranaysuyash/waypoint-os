# Additional Scenario 119: Eco Travel Carbon Offset Plan

**Scenario**: A customer wants to offset their travel carbon footprint, and the system must recommend credible offset options and integrate them into the itinerary.

---

## Situation

A traveler wants to reduce or offset emissions from flights, hotels, and activities.
They expect the agency to offer meaningful environmental offset options.

## What the system should do

- Recommend credible carbon offset providers and programs
- Estimate emissions for the planned trip components
- Explain how offsets work and what they actually support
- Offer optional eco-add ons that reduce impact beyond offsets

## Why this matters

Carbon offset requests are common for eco-conscious travelers.
Credible, transparent options reinforce responsible travel.

## Success criteria

- The system offers real, vetted offset options
- The customer understands the environmental impact and tradeoffs
- The agency supports eco-conscious booking decisions without greenwashing
