# Additional Scenario 98: Medical Evacuation Readiness

**Scenario**: A traveler requires medical evacuation coverage guidance, and the system must ensure the trip includes appropriate emergency medical support.

---

## Situation

A customer has a health condition or is traveling to a remote area where medical facilities are limited.
They need assurance about emergency evacuation and medical support.

## What the system should do

- Identify medical evacuation coverage requirements for the destination
- Confirm travel insurance or evacuation plan eligibility
- Advise on nearby medical facilities and emergency contacts
- Ensure the customer has a clear process if urgent care is needed

## Why this matters

Medical emergencies demand fast, reliable support.
A solid evacuation plan can be life-saving and reduces customer anxiety.

## Success criteria

- The system verifies evacuation readiness before travel
- The traveler understands the emergency procedure and contacts
- The agency delivers a safety-first plan for remote or high-risk travel
