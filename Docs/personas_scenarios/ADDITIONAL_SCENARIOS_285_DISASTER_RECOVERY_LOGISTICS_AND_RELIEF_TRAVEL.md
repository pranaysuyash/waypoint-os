## Scenario: Disaster Recovery Logistics and Relief Travel

### Situation
Relief coordinators, logistics teams, and emergency response partners need travel arrangements for disaster zones, relief distribution centers, and recovery planning.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate access to impacted areas, manage transport for relief supplies, arrange accommodations for response teams, and support coordination with local authorities.

### Why this matters
Disaster recovery travel demands agility, safety planning, and logistics expertise to deliver aid quickly while protecting responders.

### Success criteria
- Impacted area access and relief coordination
- Transport logistics for supplies and personnel
- Accommodation and safety planning for response teams
- Strong alignment with local recovery plans
