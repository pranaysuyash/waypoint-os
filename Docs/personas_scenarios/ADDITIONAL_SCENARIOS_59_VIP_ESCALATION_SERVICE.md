# Additional Scenario 59: VIP Escalation Service

**Scenario**: A high-value customer requests VIP-level service, and the system must escalate the booking to premium handling with concierge-level attention.

---

## Situation

A VIP client demands an elevated experience, including personalized service, fast response, and premium upgrades.
The agency must ensure the request is handled by the right team and with the correct priority.

## What the system should do

- Recognize VIP status and escalate the request automatically
- Assign a senior agent or concierge to manage the case
- Confirm premium amenities and fast-track service without overpromising
- Keep the customer informed about the escalation path

## Why this matters

VIP customers expect a different service level.
Failing to escalate properly undermines the value of high-ticket business.

## Success criteria

- The system triggers VIP handling immediately
- The customer receives prioritized, bespoke service
- The agency delivers an experience consistent with premium expectations
