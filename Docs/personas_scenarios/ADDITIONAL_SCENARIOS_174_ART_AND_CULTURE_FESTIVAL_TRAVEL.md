## Scenario: Art and Culture Festival Travel

### Situation
Art lovers and culture enthusiasts need travel arrangements for art fairs, cultural festivals, and exhibitions with gallery access, artist meet-and-greets, and related cultural experiences.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should secure festival passes and VIP access, arrange gallery and exhibition visits, coordinate artist events and workshops, and provide cultural context and interpretation services.

### Why this matters
Art and culture festivals require specialized access arrangements, timing coordination with exhibitions, and cultural knowledge to enhance the artistic experience.

### Success criteria
- Festival pass and VIP access procurement
- Gallery and exhibition visit coordination
- Artist event and workshop arrangements
- Cultural context and interpretation services</content>
