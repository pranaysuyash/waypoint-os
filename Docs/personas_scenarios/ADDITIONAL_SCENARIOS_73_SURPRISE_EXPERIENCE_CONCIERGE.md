# Additional Scenario 73: Surprise Experience Concierge

**Scenario**: A traveler wants a surprise experience for a loved one, and the system must arrange it discreetly while preserving the reveal moment.

---

## Situation

A customer asks the agency to plan a surprise birthday, anniversary, or proposal experience.
They expect confidentiality, coordination with vendors, and a flawless reveal.

## What the system should do

- Capture the surprise details without alerting the guest of honor
- Coordinate timing, venue, and any special requests with vendors
- Confirm contingency plans if the schedule changes
- Keep the customer updated on progress and execution status

## Why this matters

Surprise experiences create high expectations and unique risk.
A seamless execution strengthens customer loyalty and brand reputation.

## Success criteria

- The system preserves the surprise while coordinating logistics
- It provides backup options for schedule or vendor changes
- The customer feels confident the reveal will go smoothly
