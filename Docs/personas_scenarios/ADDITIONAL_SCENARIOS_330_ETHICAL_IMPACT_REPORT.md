# Additional Scenario 330: Ethical "Insetting" Impact Report

**Scenario**: After a trip, the traveler receives a report detailing the actual "Community Impact" of their travel—where their money went, how many local jobs were supported, and the status of their "Ethical Insets" (not just carbon offsets).

---

## Situation

- Traveler (S1) visited an eco-lodge in Kenya.
- They chose the "Community Impact" upsell.
- Post-trip, they want to see the "Proof."

## What the system should do

- Compile data from the [REGENERATIVE_TRAVEL_AND_ETHICAL_INSETTING.md](../industry_domain/regulatory_compliance/REGENERATIVE_TRAVEL_AND_ETHICAL_INSETTING.md) module.
- Show the "Economic Leakage" map (how much money stayed in the local village).
- Provide a "Status Update" on the specific project funded (e.g., "The well you funded is now at 50% construction").
- Generate a "Social Sharing" asset for the traveler to show their impact.
- Invite the traveler to join a "Supporter Circle" for that destination.

## Why this matters

"Greenwashing" is a risk. "Proof of Impact" is a differentiator.
Transparency on "Where the Money Goes" builds deep ethical trust with the Gen-Z and Millennial market.

## Success criteria

- The traveler receives a data-rich report within 14 days post-trip.
- The "Community Score" of the supplier is verified.
- The traveler feels their trip had a "Net Positive" effect.
