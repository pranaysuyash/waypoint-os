## Scenario: Remote Work Travel Setup

### Situation
A digital nomad or remote worker needs to combine work requirements with travel, requiring reliable internet, workspace setups, and work-life balance in their destination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should verify high-speed internet availability, recommend co-working spaces or workspace-equipped accommodations, and coordinate work permits or visa requirements for extended stays.

### Why this matters
Remote work travel allows professionals to explore while maintaining career obligations, requiring careful planning to ensure productivity and work-life balance.

### Success criteria
- Verified high-speed internet and workspace availability
- Co-working space recommendations and memberships
- Work visa and permit coordination for extended stays
- Balance of work and leisure activities in itinerary</content>
