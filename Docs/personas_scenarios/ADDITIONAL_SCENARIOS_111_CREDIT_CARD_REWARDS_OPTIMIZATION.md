# Additional Scenario 111: Credit Card Rewards Optimization

**Scenario**: A traveler wants to maximize credit card rewards for a booking, and the system must match payment options to the itinerary without sacrificing value.

---

## Situation

A customer has premium credit card benefits or airline miles and wants to use them effectively for their trip.
They expect the agency to recommend the best payment path and rewards strategy.

## What the system should do

- Identify rewards programs relevant to the trip
- Recommend payment methods that maximize points or status benefits
- Explain trade-offs between different reward options and fees
- Confirm any partner or co-branded carrier rules

## Why this matters

Reward optimization can save customers money and increase satisfaction.
A helpful travel agency turns benefits into a smarter purchase.

## Success criteria

- The system recommends the best rewards-aligned payment strategy
- The customer understands the value and any constraints
- The agency preserves the quality of the booking while optimizing rewards
