# Additional Scenario 115: Sustainable Hotel Rating Comparison

**Scenario**: A traveler wants sustainable accommodation, and the system must compare hotels based on verified sustainability ratings and eco-friendly practices.

---

## Situation

A customer requests hotels ranked by sustainability credentials rather than just comfort or location.
They expect the agency to compare options using reliable eco metrics.

## What the system should do

- Identify verified sustainability ratings and certifications for hotels
- Compare hotels on energy, waste, water, and community impact criteria
- Present options with clear trade-offs between green credentials and price or convenience
- Avoid vague claims by using specific certifications and practices

## Why this matters

Sustainable travel requires transparency.
Customers want to support green stays that are credible, not marketing claims.

## Success criteria

- The system compares hotels based on real sustainability data
- The traveler can choose a hotel aligned with their eco values
- The agency provides a responsible, fact-based recommendation
