# Additional Scenario 54: Last-Minute Room Upgrade

**Scenario**: A customer wants a room upgrade right before arrival, and the system must check availability, cost, and supplier flexibility.

---

## Situation

A traveler decides they want an upgraded room only days before check-in.
They expect the agency to find availability without making the situation confusing.

## What the system should do

- Check supplier inventory for upgrade availability
- Calculate the additional cost and any booking implications
- Confirm whether the upgrade can be guaranteed before arrival
- Present the option clearly and avoid surprise charges

## Why this matters

Last-minute upgrades can delight customers or cause confusion.
The agency must handle them with transparency and operational precision.

## Success criteria

- The system verifies upgrade availability accurately
- It communicates the extra cost and confirmation status clearly
- The customer gets the upgrade fast or a clear no if unavailable
