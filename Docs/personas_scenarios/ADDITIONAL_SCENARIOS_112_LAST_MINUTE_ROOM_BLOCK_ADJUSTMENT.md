# Additional Scenario 112: Last-Minute Room Block Adjustment

**Scenario**: A group needs to add or change hotel rooms shortly before arrival, and the system must adjust the room block without breaking group rates.

---

## Situation

A travel group wants to change the number of rooms after the booking is already blocked.
They expect the agency to keep the group rate while handling availability.

## What the system should do

- Check current room availability and negotiated group rates
- Calculate the cost impact of adding or reducing rooms
- Negotiate with the hotel to preserve as much of the block terms as possible
- Communicate the revised group status and any new deadlines

## Why this matters

Last-minute room-block changes are common in group travel.
A good agency prevents penalties and keeps the group together.

## Success criteria

- The system adjusts the room block while minimizing extra cost
- The customer receives an updated group room plan quickly
- The agency preserves the group’s overall booking benefits
