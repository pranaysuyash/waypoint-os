## Scenario: Volunteer and Community Service Travel

### Situation
Volunteers and community service participants need travel arrangements for volunteer programs, community development projects, and service-learning experiences with organizational coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate volunteer program placement and orientation, arrange community service accommodations, provide organizational support coordination, and ensure volunteer project logistics.

### Why this matters
Volunteer travel involves organizational partnerships, project coordination, and community impact that requires specialized knowledge of volunteer programs and service organizations.

### Success criteria
- Volunteer program placement and orientation coordination
- Community service accommodation and logistics arrangements
- Organizational support and partnership coordination
- Volunteer project impact and safety management</content>
