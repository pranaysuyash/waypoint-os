## Scenario: Solar and Renewable Energy Travel

### Situation
Renewable energy experts and solar project stakeholders need travel arrangements for solar farms, renewable energy conferences, and green infrastructure site visits.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange renewable energy site access, coordinate conference attendance, organize transportation to remote solar installations, and support sustainability-focused logistics.

### Why this matters
Renewable energy travel requires knowledge of energy project timing, site safety, and environmental sensitivities that are critical for effective green industry engagement.

### Success criteria
- Renewable energy site access and conference coordination
- Remote solar and green infrastructure travel logistics
- Sustainability-conscious transportation planning
- Support for technical and environmental stakeholder needs
