## Scenario: Film and Media Production Travel

### Situation
Film crews, media producers, and content teams need travel arrangements for shoots, location scouting, and production support.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange permits, location access, crew transport, equipment logistics, and contingency planning for production schedules.

### Why this matters
Film and media production travel demands precision around shooting windows, local regulations, and bulky equipment movement for a successful production.

### Success criteria
- Location permit and access coordination
- Crew transport and equipment logistics support
- Itinerary alignment with shooting schedules
- Contingency planning for production delays
