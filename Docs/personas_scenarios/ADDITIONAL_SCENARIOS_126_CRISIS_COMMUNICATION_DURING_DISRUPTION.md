# Additional Scenario 126: Crisis Communication During Disruption

**Scenario**: A travel disruption occurs mid-trip, and the system must provide crisis communication, alternative plans, and reassurance.

---

## Situation

A traveler experiences flight cancellations, weather delays, or sudden destination closures.
They need the agency to communicate clearly, propose alternatives, and keep them updated.

## What the system should do

- Detect disruption signals and proactively notify the traveler
- Offer practical rerouting, accommodations, or refunds options
- Explain next steps, timelines, and customer support availability
- Keep communication calm and factual to reduce stress

## Why this matters

Travel disruptions erode trust quickly.
Strong crisis communication preserves customer confidence and keeps the trip salvageable.

## Success criteria

- The traveler receives clear, timely guidance during the disruption
- The agency proposes realistic recovery options
- The customer feels supported rather than abandoned when plans change
