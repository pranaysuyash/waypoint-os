# Additional Scenario 65: Emergency Contact Update

**Scenario**: A customer needs to update their emergency contact after booking, and the system must ensure the new information is captured across all relevant suppliers.

---

## Situation

A traveler changes their emergency contact or adds new health details after confirmation.
They expect the agency to update the information promptly and ensure it is used if needed.

## What the system should do

- Capture the updated emergency contact details clearly
- Propagate the change to hotels, transport providers, and insurance where required
- Confirm that the update has been received by all relevant parties
- Provide the traveler with confirmation of the update

## Why this matters

Emergency contact accuracy is critical for traveler safety.
A quick update prevents confusion and ensures the right person can be reached.

## Success criteria

- The system updates emergency contact details promptly
- It confirms the change across all relevant vendors
- The traveler receives clear confirmation of the update
