## Scenario: Road Trip and Self-Drive Adventures

### Situation
Adventure travelers want self-drive road trips with vehicle rental, route planning, accommodation along the way, and emergency roadside assistance.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange vehicle rental with appropriate insurance, plan scenic driving routes, book accommodation along the route, and provide emergency roadside assistance coordination.

### Why this matters
Self-drive travel requires careful route planning, vehicle preparation, and emergency preparedness to ensure safe and enjoyable independent adventures.

### Success criteria
- Vehicle rental with comprehensive insurance arrangements
- Scenic route planning and navigation support
- Accommodation booking along planned routes
- Emergency roadside assistance and breakdown services</content>
