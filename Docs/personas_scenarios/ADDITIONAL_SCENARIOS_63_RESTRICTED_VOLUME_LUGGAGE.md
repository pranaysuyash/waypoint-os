# Additional Scenario 63: Restricted Volume Luggage

**Scenario**: A traveler has oversized luggage or special equipment, and the system must handle carrier restrictions, fees, and logistics.

---

## Situation

A customer is traveling with sports equipment, musical instruments, or bulky gear.
They need the agency to account for the extra logistics and cost transparently.

## What the system should do

- Check airline and transfer provider rules for oversized luggage
- Estimate any extra fees or handling requirements
- Adjust the itinerary or transport providers if needed
- Communicate the estimated cost and any special preparation steps

## Why this matters

Unplanned luggage restrictions can cause a major travel disruption.
The agency must prepare for these needs proactively.

## Success criteria

- The system accounts for special luggage accurately
- The customer knows the costs and procedures ahead of time
- The trip logistics are adjusted to avoid surprises
