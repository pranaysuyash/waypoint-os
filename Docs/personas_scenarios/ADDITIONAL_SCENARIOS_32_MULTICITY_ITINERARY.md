# Additional Scenario 32: Multicity Itinerary

**Scenario**: A customer wants a multicity tour and the agency must coordinate connections, timing, and lodging without breaking the overall flow.

---

## Situation

A traveler requests a multicity route across three countries.
They want an exciting itinerary but are unsure about transfer times and logistics.

## What the system should do

- Validate the proposed travel legs and connection windows
- Recommend changes if the schedule is too tight
- Present a balanced route with rest days and smooth transitions
- Highlight where the trip is most demanding

## Why this matters

Multicity trips are attractive but fragile.
A well-designed route turns complexity into confidence.

## Success criteria

- The system identifies risky connections
- It suggests safer alternatives without losing the theme
- The traveler feels the plan is realistic and inspiring
