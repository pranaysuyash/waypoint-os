# Exploration Areas Open for Later Addition

**Status:** open for later exploration and expansion.

This note captures additional discovery and documentation areas that we can explore after continuing the current scenario build. These are intentionally broad and extensible so future work can add more focus areas without being blocked.

## Note
We will explore these areas later. The current scenario work continues, and this list is open to addition.

## Suggested exploration areas

1. Operational workflows and agent handoffs
   - Booking → fulfillment → disruption recovery → post-trip follow-up
   - Exception case playbooks for supplier failure, customer escalation, and trust repair
   - Agent-to-agent and agent-to-human handoff protocols

2. Persona journey maps and traveler archetypes
   - C-suite executives, digital nomads, families, special-needs travelers, event operators, and medical concierge travelers
   - Emotional state analysis across booking, travel, disruption, and loyalty phases
   - Decision-making triggers and service expectations for each persona

3. Risk, compliance, and policy
   - Visa, passport, customs, insurance, and data privacy compliance
   - Security and biosecurity for high-risk travel and field operations
   - Government contractor and regulated-industry travel policies

4. Emergency, crisis, and disruption response
   - Rapid response workflows for disasters, medical emergencies, and geopolitical disruptions
   - Crisis logistics, safe rerouting, and emergency communications
   - Continuity planning for travelers and supplier networks

5. Multi-party and group coordination
   - Family groups, corporate teams, multiple-paying parties, and mixed readiness cohorts
   - Per-group readiness tracking, shared itinerary coordination, and consensus-building
   - Document and approval workflows for multi-party travel

6. Budget feasibility, audit, and commercial intelligence
   - Budget stretch, feasibility checks, and price benchmark analysis
   - Market-aware audit mode for better value and waste detection
   - Margin protection, sponsorship hospitality, and referral / incentive logic

7. Industry vertical deep dives
   - Healthcare and clinical trials
   - Energy, utilities, and infrastructure
   - Education, EdTech, and academic exchange
   - Media production, virtual events, and entertainment
   - Finance, fintech, RegTech, and professional services
   - Transportation innovation, ports, and logistics

8. Post-trip care, loyalty, and review systems
   - Post-trip follow-up, feedback capture, and review requests
   - Loyalty program matching and repeat guest continuity
   - Customer nurture and retention workflows

9. Coverage and taxonomy analysis
   - Gap analysis by industry, traveler intent, risk profile, and service type
   - Scenario taxonomy mapping and prioritization framework
   - Coverage dashboards that show what has been documented versus what is still open

10. Future-proof capability scaffolds
   - Knowledge base triggers, FAQ / “don’t know answer” fallback systems
   - Documentation and training systems for agents
   - Analytics instrumentation and scenario validation metrics

## Open invitation
This list is a living artifact. Add new exploration areas whenever we identify a meaningful domain, strategic gap, or promising new scenario cluster.
