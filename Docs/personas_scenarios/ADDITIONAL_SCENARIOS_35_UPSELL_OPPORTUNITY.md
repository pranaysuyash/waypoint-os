# Additional Scenario 35: Upsell Opportunity

**Scenario**: A customer is already booking a trip, and the system identifies a well-timed upsell opportunity that increases value without feeling pushy.

---

## Situation

A traveler is booking a premium resort stay, and the system spots a relevant upgrade: a private dinner, airport transfer, or room enhancement.
They expect the agency to suggest the add-on in a way that feels natural and useful.

## What the system should do

- Surface relevant, contextually appropriate upsells
- Explain the benefit of each add-on clearly
- Avoid making the customer feel pressured
- Keep the focus on improving the travel experience

## Why this matters

Smart upsells grow revenue and customer satisfaction simultaneously.
The right recommendation should feel like service, not sales.

## Success criteria

- The system proposes meaningful add-ons aligned with the itinerary
- The customer understands why each option matters
- The agency increases per-trip value without degrading trust
