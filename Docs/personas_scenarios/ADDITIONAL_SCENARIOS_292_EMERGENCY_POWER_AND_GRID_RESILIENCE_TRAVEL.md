## Scenario: Emergency Power and Grid Resilience Travel

### Situation
Utility operators, grid resilience planners, and emergency power teams need travel arrangements for outage response, resilience drills, and backup power deployment.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate site access, arrange transport for crews and equipment, support emergency response logistics, and manage resilient power planning meetings.

### Why this matters
Power resilience travel needs rapid coordination, access to critical sites, and reliable logistics to restore service and protect infrastructure.

### Success criteria
- Outage response and resilience site visit coordination
- Transport planning for crews and equipment
- Emergency logistics support for utility operations
- Alignment with grid recovery and resilience goals
