## Scenario: Urban Resilience and Climate Adaptation Travel

### Situation
City planners, resilience officers, and climate adaptation teams need travel arrangements for resilience workshops, floodplain visits, and infrastructure adaptation reviews.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate city resilience site visits, arrange transport for planners and engineers, manage workshop logistics, and support stakeholder briefings.

### Why this matters
Urban resilience travel requires awareness of local climate risks, infrastructure vulnerabilities, and coordination with emergency management agencies.

### Success criteria
- Resilience site visit and workshop coordination
- Planner and engineer transport logistics support
- Stakeholder briefing and workshop planning
- Alignment with local climate adaptation efforts
