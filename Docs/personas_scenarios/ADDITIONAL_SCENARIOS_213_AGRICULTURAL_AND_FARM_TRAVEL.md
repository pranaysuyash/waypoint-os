Agriculture enthusiasts and farming professionals need travel arrangements for farm stays, agricultural tours, and farming experience programs with harvest coordination and rural accommodation access.

The travel agent should arrange farm stay and agricultural tour access, coordinate harvest and farming experience participation, provide rural transportation and logistics, and ensure agricultural travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Agricultural travel involves specialized knowledge of farming seasons, rural access, and agricultural industry coordination that requires expertise in agritourism.

### Success criteria
- Farm stay and agricultural tour access coordination
- Harvest and farming experience participation arrangements
- Rural transportation and accommodation logistics support
- Agricultural travel experience and educational enhancement</content>
