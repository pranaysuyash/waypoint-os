## Scenario: Architectural Awards Jury and Design Review Travel

### Situation
Architecture jurors, design critics, and awards committees need travel arrangements for site visits, review sessions, and publicity events.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate site access to nominated buildings, arrange expert transport, support presentation logistics, and manage design review timetables.

### Why this matters
Design review travel requires high-touch coordination with architects, project teams, and venues to preserve the credibility of the awards process.

### Success criteria
- Nominated site visit and building access coordination
- Expert transport and review logistics support
- Presentation and committee schedule alignment
- High-quality experience for jurors and submitters
