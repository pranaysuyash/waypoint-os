## Scenario: Supply Chain Cybersecurity and Logistics Travel

### Situation
Supply chain security teams, logistics managers, and cyber risk officers need travel arrangements for supplier audits, logistics facility reviews, and cybersecurity integration meetings.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate facility access, arrange secure transport, support audit schedules, and manage cross-functional logistics and cyber risk meetings.

### Why this matters
Supply chain cybersecurity travel needs careful coordination to protect logistics networks, critical suppliers, and sensitive operational data.

### Success criteria
- Supplier audit and logistics facility review coordination
- Secure transport planning for critical meetings
- Cross-functional cyber risk and logistics scheduling support
- Alignment with supply chain security requirements
