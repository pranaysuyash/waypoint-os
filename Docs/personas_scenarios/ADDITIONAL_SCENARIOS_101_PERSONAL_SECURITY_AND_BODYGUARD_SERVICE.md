# Additional Scenario 101: Personal Security and Bodyguard Service

**Scenario**: A traveler requests personal security or bodyguard service, and the system must arrange vetted protection while matching itinerary needs.

---

## Situation

A customer requires security services due to high-profile status, sensitive travel, or perceived risk.
They expect the agency to coordinate vetted protection seamlessly and discreetly.

## What the system should do

- Identify appropriate security providers for the destination
- Confirm services, coverage hours, and any legal restrictions
- Coordinate the security plan with arrival, movement, and accommodations
- Keep the customer informed without exposing sensitive details

## Why this matters

Personal security is a specialized travel need.
A failure here can put the traveler at risk and damage the agency’s reputation.

## Success criteria

- The system secures qualified protection aligned with the itinerary
- The customer receives discreet, professional security service
- The agency avoids legal or operational security gaps
