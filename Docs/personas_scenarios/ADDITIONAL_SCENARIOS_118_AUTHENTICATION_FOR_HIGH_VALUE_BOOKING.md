# Additional Scenario 118: Authentication for High-Value Booking

**Scenario**: A high-value booking requires additional customer authentication, and the system must verify identity without creating friction.

---

## Situation

A customer wants to confirm an expensive trip or luxury package.
The agency needs to ensure the person making the booking is authorized.

## What the system should do

- Request secure verification for high-value transactions
- Offer authentication methods like OTP, document scan, or signed consent
- Keep the process fast and explain why it’s needed
- Prevent fraud without alienating the customer

## Why this matters

High-value travel is a fraud target.
Strong but smooth authentication protects customers and the agency.

## Success criteria

- The system verifies identity for expensive bookings securely
- The customer understands the verification requirement
- The agency balances security with a premium service experience
