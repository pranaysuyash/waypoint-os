# Additional Scenario 52: Political Unrest Alternative Plan

**Scenario**: A destination experiences unrest before travel, and the system must offer safe alternatives or reschedule options.

---

## Situation

Shortly before departure, a customer’s destination becomes politically unstable or unsafe.
They need the agency to respond with options rather than leaving them uncertain.

## What the system should do

- Detect relevant travel advisories or alerts for the destination
- Recommend safe alternatives or a change of plan
- Explain refund or rescheduling policies clearly
- Ensure the customer understands the agency’s safety-first approach

## Why this matters

Safety issues are non-negotiable.
An agency that handles them proactively protects customers and avoids reputational damage.

## Success criteria

- The system flags the risk before departure
- It provides realistic alternative plans and next steps
- The customer trusts the agency to manage unexpected geopolitical risk
