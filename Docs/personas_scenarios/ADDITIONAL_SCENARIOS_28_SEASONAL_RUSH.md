# Additional Scenario 28: Seasonal Rush

**Scenario**: A customer wants a summer holiday during peak season, and the system must explain availability limits, demand pressure, and timing tradeoffs.

---

## Situation

A customer requests a popular destination during the highest-demand window.
They expect a straightforward booking, but the agency needs to explain limited availability and alternatives.

## What the system should do

- Flag peak-season demand for the selected dates
- Explain why price and availability are constrained
- Offer alternative windows or less crowded destination variants
- Provide a sense of urgency without panic

## Why this matters

Peak-season bookings can easily frustrate customers if the agency overpromises.
Clear guidance preserves credibility and helps customers make an informed choice.

## Success criteria

- The system detects the timing constraint immediately
- The customer is offered both timing and destination options
- The response feels helpful, not defensive
