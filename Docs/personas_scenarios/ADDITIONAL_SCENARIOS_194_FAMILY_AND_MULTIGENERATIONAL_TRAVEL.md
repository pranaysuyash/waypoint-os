## Scenario: Family and Multigenerational Travel

### Situation
Families with multiple generations need travel arrangements accommodating different age groups, accessibility requirements, and family bonding experiences with varied interests.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange family-friendly accommodations and activities, coordinate multigenerational accessibility needs, provide age-appropriate experiences, and ensure family group logistics.

### Why this matters
Family travel involves diverse needs across age groups, accessibility considerations, and group dynamics that require specialized family travel coordination.

### Success criteria
- Family-friendly accommodation and activity arrangements
- Multigenerational accessibility and mobility support
- Age-appropriate experience and entertainment coordination
- Family group logistics and bonding opportunity management</content>
