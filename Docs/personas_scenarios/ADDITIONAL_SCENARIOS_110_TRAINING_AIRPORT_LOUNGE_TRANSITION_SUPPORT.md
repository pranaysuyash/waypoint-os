# Additional Scenario 110: Training Airport Lounge Transition Support

**Scenario**: A traveler needs help transitioning through airport lounges and transfers during long layovers, and the system must coordinate seamless lounge access and rest options.

---

## Situation

A customer has a long layover and wants to use airport lounges, rest areas, or transfers efficiently.
They expect the agency to make the transit comfortable and stress-free.

## What the system should do

- Identify available lounges and rest options for each airport segment
- Confirm access rules, guest policies, and timing for transfers
- Recommend transport or lounge options if the layover location changes
- Ensure the customer can use the available amenities without delays

## Why this matters

Layovers can make or break travel comfort.
Good transition support improves the customer experience during the least enjoyable part of the trip.

## Success criteria

- The system coordinates lounge and transfer support clearly
- The traveler knows where to go and how to access services
- The agency turns a long layover into a manageable, even pleasant, experience
