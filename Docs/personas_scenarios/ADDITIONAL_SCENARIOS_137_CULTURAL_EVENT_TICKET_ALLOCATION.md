# Additional Scenario 137: Cultural Event Ticket Allocation

**Scenario**: A traveler wants tickets to a cultural event, and the system must secure the right seats while protecting the overall itinerary.

---

## Situation

A customer requests tickets to a concert, performance, or festival that has limited availability.
They expect the agency to book the right experience without jeopardizing other travel arrangements.

## What the system should do

- Confirm event timing and seating availability before finalizing the itinerary
- Align event logistics with transportation and accommodation timing
- Provide alternatives if exact tickets are unavailable
- Communicate any additional costs or conditions clearly

## Why this matters

Cultural event tickets can make or break a trip.
Poor coordination between event timing and travel logistics causes stress and missed experiences.

## Success criteria

- The traveler’s event tickets are booked and aligned with the rest of the trip
- The agency balances the event priority with practical logistics
- The customer receives a clear plan for attendance and timing
