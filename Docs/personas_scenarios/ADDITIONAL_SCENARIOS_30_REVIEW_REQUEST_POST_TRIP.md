# Additional Scenario 30: Review Request Post-Trip

**Scenario**: A traveler asks how to leave feedback after their trip, and the system must make the review process easy while encouraging positive reviews.

---

## Situation

A guest has completed a trip and wants to share their experience.
They ask the agency how to leave a review, what platforms are available, and whether there is any follow-up reward.

## What the system should do

- Provide the exact submission path for reviews
- Mention any incentives or thank-you process
- Suggest the best platform for public feedback
- Keep the tone appreciative and simple

## Why this matters

Post-trip reviews are valuable social proof.
An easy, well-handled review request can turn satisfied travelers into repeat customers and referral sources.

## Success criteria

- The system gives a clear call-to-action for the review
- The traveler feels thanked and acknowledged
- The agency maximizes the chance of a review being submitted
