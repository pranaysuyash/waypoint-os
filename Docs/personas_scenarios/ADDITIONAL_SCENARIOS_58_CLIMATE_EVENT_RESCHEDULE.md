# Additional Scenario 58: Climate Event Reschedule

**Scenario**: A trip is disrupted by a climate event like wildfires or floods, and the system must offer rescheduling or alternate destinations.

---

## Situation

A traveler’s destination is affected by a climate-related event, requiring a change of plans.
They need the agency to provide a safe reschedule or relocation quickly.

## What the system should do

- Detect the climate event impact on the itinerary
- Recommend alternate dates or destinations with comparable value
- Explain the rescheduling policy and customer options clearly
- Maintain calm, factual communication during the disruption

## Why this matters

Climate events are increasingly common travel disruptors.
The agency must manage both logistics and customer confidence.

## Success criteria

- The system recognizes the event risk quickly
- It offers choices that keep the customer’s trip experience viable
- The traveler feels supported rather than abandoned
