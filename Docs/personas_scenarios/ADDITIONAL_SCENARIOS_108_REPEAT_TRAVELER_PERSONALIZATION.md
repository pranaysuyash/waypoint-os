# Additional Scenario 108: Repeat Traveler Personalization

**Scenario**: A returning customer wants a personalized trip based on past preferences, and the system must use historical booking data to tailor the new experience.

---

## Situation

A loyal customer books again and expects the agency to remember their preferences and previous trip learnings.
They want the new itinerary to feel more personal, not generic.

## What the system should do

- Retrieve prior travel preferences, accommodations, and service notes
- Use the customer’s history to suggest relevant upgrades or changes
- Avoid repeating past frustrations while preserving favorite elements
- Explain how the new plan is tailored based on previous trips

## Why this matters

Repeat customers expect better service over time.
Personalization strengthens loyalty and improves satisfaction.

## Success criteria

- The system uses past trip data effectively
- The customer feels the itinerary reflects their tastes
- The agency demonstrates memory and care for repeat travelers
