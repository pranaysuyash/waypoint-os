# Additional Scenario 135: Multiple Language Translation Support

**Scenario**: A traveler needs translation support across several languages, and the system must coordinate multilingual assistance for the trip.

---

## Situation

A customer is visiting regions with multiple languages and needs help arranging translation support for meetings, guides, and documents.
They expect the agency to ensure language barriers are minimized.

## What the system should do

- Identify critical moments that require live or written translation
- Arrange interpreters, multilingual guides, or translation technology as needed
- Ensure important documents are available in the traveler’s preferred language
- Explain which language support is included and what requires extra coordination

## Why this matters

Language barriers can turn a good trip into a frustrating one.
Reliable translation support keeps the traveler confident and engaged.

## Success criteria

- The traveler has the right level of multilingual support for their itinerary
- The agency mitigates language risk in key interactions
- Important communications are handled accurately and respectfully
