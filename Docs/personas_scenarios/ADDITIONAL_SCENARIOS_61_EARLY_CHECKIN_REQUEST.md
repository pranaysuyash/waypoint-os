# Additional Scenario 61: Early Check-In Request

**Scenario**: A customer wants an early check-in, and the system must check availability, explain hotel policies, and coordinate the request.

---

## Situation

The traveler arrives in the morning before standard check-in time and requests access to their room early.
They expect the agency to handle this request in advance, not as an afterthought.

## What the system should do

- Check hotel early check-in policies and availability
- Explain if an extra fee is required or if the hotel can confirm the request
- Offer interim options (luggage storage, lounge access, early meal)
- Avoid promising early check-in unless it is guaranteed

## Why this matters

Early check-in is a common request that can improve traveler comfort.
Handling it well shows attention to detail and keeps first impressions positive.

## Success criteria

- The system verifies the hotel’s policy and availability
- It communicates the likely outcome clearly
- The traveler has an alternative plan if early check-in isn’t possible
