# Additional Scenario 122: Loyalty Program Award Optimization

**Scenario**: A frequent traveler wants to maximize loyalty program benefits, and the system must optimize bookings for points, upgrades, and award availability.

---

## Situation

A customer is traveling on a loyalty program and wants the agency to use their status, points, and partner benefits effectively.
They expect optimized award bookings that protect their hard-earned rewards.

## What the system should do

- Evaluate loyalty program rules, upgrade availability, and award pricing
- Suggest the best combination of points, cash, and partner benefits
- Preserve elite status perks and avoid unnecessary point waste
- Explain how changes will affect earning and award balances

## Why this matters

Frequent travelers rely on loyalty value.
Poor handling of awards can cost customers points and damage trust.

## Success criteria

- The system recommends the most valuable award and upgrade approach
- The customer understands the trade-offs between cash and points
- The booking preserves loyalty status and maximizes reward value
