## Scenario: Pet Travel Documentation and Logistics

### Situation
A traveler wants to bring their pet on the trip, requiring health certificates, quarantine arrangements, and accommodation coordination for pet-friendly stays.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should handle all pet travel documentation, arrange veterinary certificates and vaccinations, coordinate quarantine requirements, and book pet-friendly accommodations and transportation.

### Why this matters
Pet travel involves complex regulations and documentation that vary by destination, requiring expert coordination to ensure compliance and pet welfare.

### Success criteria
- Complete documentation package for pet travel requirements
- Coordination with veterinarians for health certificates
- Pet-friendly accommodation and transportation arrangements
- Emergency veterinary care coordination at destination</content>
