# Additional Scenario 26: Last-Minute Cancellation

**Scenario**: A client cancels two days before departure and expects the agency to recover the situation, rebook or refund, and maintain confidence.

---

## Situation

A customer calls to cancel a high-value trip 48 hours before departure due to an emergency.
They look to the agency for support with penalties, refunds, and alternative dates.

## What the system should do

- Quickly identify the ticketing/refund rules for the itinerary
- Explain the cancellation penalties in plain language
- Offer next best steps: refund, travel credit, or rebooking options
- Keep the tone empathetic and solutions-focused

## Why this matters

Last-minute changes are high-stress and high-risk for churn.
Handling them well protects customer trust and ensures the agency can recover revenue where possible.

## Success criteria

- The system retrieves the itinerary rules automatically
- The customer is offered clear next steps
- The agency avoids an impression of rigid policy enforcement
