# Additional Scenario 143: Late-Night Arrival Transport Arrangements

**Scenario**: A traveler arrives late at night, and the system must provide safe, reliable transport and check-in support.

---

## Situation

A customer lands at a destination after midnight and requires secure transport and accommodation access.
They expect the agency to handle airport transfers and late check-in smoothly.

## What the system should do

- Arrange trusted transfers or meet-and-greet services for late arrivals
- Confirm the hotel can accommodate a late check-in and prepare the room
- Provide emergency contact details and contingency options
- Advise the traveler on local safety and arrival procedures

## Why this matters

Late-night travel heightens safety and logistical risk.
A dependable arrival plan keeps the traveler safe and reduces stress.

## Success criteria

- The traveler arrives and checks in without confusion or delay
- The agency coordinates transport and late-night access clearly
- The customer feels secure when arriving at night
