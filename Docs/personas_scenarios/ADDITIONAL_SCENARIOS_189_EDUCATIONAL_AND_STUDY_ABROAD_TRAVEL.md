## Scenario: Educational and Study Abroad Travel

### Situation
Students and educators need travel arrangements for study abroad programs, educational exchanges, and academic field trips with visa coordination and institutional support.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate student visa and enrollment processes, arrange educational accommodations, provide institutional support coordination, and ensure academic program logistics.

### Why this matters
Educational travel involves visa complexities, institutional coordination, and academic program requirements that require specialized educational travel expertise.

### Success criteria
- Student visa and enrollment process coordination
- Educational accommodation and housing arrangements
- Institutional support and academic program coordination
- Study abroad logistics and safety management</content>
