# Additional Scenario 138: Adventure Activity Safety Screening

**Scenario**: A traveler wants to book adventure activities, and the system must screen providers for safety and reliable execution.

---

## Situation

A customer requests high-risk activities like climbing, scuba diving, or off-road excursions.
They expect the agency to vet providers and ensure safety standards are met.

## What the system should do

- Screen adventure providers for safety certifications, reviews, and emergency protocols
- Confirm participant fitness requirements and liability terms
- Advise the traveler on what to bring, what to expect, and how to prepare
- Provide backup options if a preferred provider is unavailable or unsafe

## Why this matters

Adventure travel can expose customers to real physical risk.
A responsible agency ensures safety without dampening the adventure.

## Success criteria

- The traveler books with a vetted, safety-conscious provider
- The system communicates all safety expectations clearly
- The agency reduces risk while preserving the desired experience
