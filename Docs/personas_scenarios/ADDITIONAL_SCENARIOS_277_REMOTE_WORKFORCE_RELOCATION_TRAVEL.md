## Scenario: Remote Workforce Relocation Travel

### Situation
Companies relocating remote employees, digital nomads, and distributed teams need travel arrangements for relocation support, visa services, and housing transitions.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate relocation schedules, arrange temporary housing, support visa and immigration logistics, and provide orientation travel for remote workers.

### Why this matters
Remote workforce relocation travel requires extra help with settling in, compliance, and continuity for employees moving to new cities or countries.

### Success criteria
- Relocation travel coordination for remote workers
- Temporary housing and orientation planning
- Visa and immigration support logistics
- Smooth transition for distributed team members
