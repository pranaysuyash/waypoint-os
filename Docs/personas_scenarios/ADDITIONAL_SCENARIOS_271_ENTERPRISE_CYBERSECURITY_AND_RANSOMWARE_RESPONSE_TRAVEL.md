## Scenario: Enterprise Cybersecurity and Ransomware Response Travel

### Situation
Cybersecurity teams, incident response experts, and enterprise security leaders need travel arrangements for crisis response, ransomware negotiation, and secure operations coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange secure transportation, coordinate meetings with legal and IT teams, organize access to affected sites, and support confidential travel logistics.

### Why this matters
Security incident travel demands rapid response, trust, and secure handling of sensitive stakeholders to minimize disruption and restore operations.

### Success criteria
- Secure travel for incident response teams
- Coordination with legal, IT, and executive stakeholders
- Access to affected facilities and secure meeting venues
- Confidential logistics support during the security crisis
