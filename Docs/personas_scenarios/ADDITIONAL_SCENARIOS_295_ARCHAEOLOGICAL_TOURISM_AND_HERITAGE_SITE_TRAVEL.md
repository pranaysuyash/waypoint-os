## Scenario: Archaeological Tourism and Heritage Site Travel

### Situation
Cultural tourism planners, heritage site managers, and archaeological tour operators need travel arrangements for archaeological excursions, heritage site access, and educational tours.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate guided access, arrange interpretive tour logistics, manage heritage permissions, and support educational program travel.

### Why this matters
Heritage travel requires respectful access, protection of archaeological sites, and curated experiences for education and preservation.

### Success criteria
- Guided archaeological and heritage site access coordination
- Interpretive tour logistics and permit support
- Educational program travel planning
- Preservation-aware travel management
