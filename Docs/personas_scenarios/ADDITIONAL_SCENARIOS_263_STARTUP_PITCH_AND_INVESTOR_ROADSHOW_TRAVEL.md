## Scenario: Startup Pitch and Investor Roadshow Travel

### Situation
Founders, startup leadership teams, and investor relations professionals need travel arrangements for pitch events, investor roadshows, and demo days.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate investor meeting schedules, secure presentation venues, arrange team transport, and manage pitch deck logistics across cities.

### Why this matters
Startup roadshow travel requires high-touch coordination, timing around investor availability, and flawless execution to maximize fundraising momentum.

### Success criteria
- Investor meeting and demo day coordination
- Venue and presentation logistics support
- Team transport and itinerary synchronization
- High-touch execution for fundraising readiness
