# Additional Scenario 326: The Anniversary "Surprise & Delight" Loop

**Scenario**: One year after a major honeymoon trip, the system triggers a personalized memory-gift and a pre-engineered "Next Chapter" proposal based on the couple's taste-graph.

---

## Situation

- Couple (S1/S2) celebrated their 1st honeymoon in the Maldives last year.
- It is now T-minus 30 days to their anniversary.
- The system has their "Taste Graph": They loved "Overwater Villas" but hated "Loud Buffets."

## What the system should do

- Send a "Memory Pulse": An AI-generated video of their Maldives highlights (using photos they shared during the trip).
- Offer a "Next Chapter" invitation: "You loved the privacy of the Maldives; have you considered a Private Island in the Seychelles?"
- Pre-apply the "Loyalty Credit" earned from the first trip.
- Draft the invitation for the Agent (P1) to send with a 1-click personal touch.

## Why this matters

Retention is cheaper than acquisition.
Using "Taste Graphs" to predict the next trip transforms the agency from a "Booking Engine" into a "Life Partner."

## Success criteria

- The traveler feels "Seen and Remembered" without it being creepy.
- The conversion rate on "Anniversary Proposals" increases.
- The Taste Graph is updated with their response (Yes/No/Later).
