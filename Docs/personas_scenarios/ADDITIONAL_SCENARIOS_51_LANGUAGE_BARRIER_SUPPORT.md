# Additional Scenario 51: Language Barrier Support

**Scenario**: A traveler arrives in a destination where they do not speak the local language, and the system must ensure they have support for communication, transfers, and services.

---

## Situation

A customer books a trip to a non-English-speaking country and is concerned about how they will manage logistics, especially during transfers and tours.

## What the system should do

- Identify when the destination language differs from the traveler’s language
- Offer translator services, bilingual guides, or app-based support
- Ensure key suppliers (airport transfers, hotel front desk, guides) can communicate
- Explain what help will be available and where it is most critical

## Why this matters

Language challenges can make an otherwise smooth trip feel stressful.
Providing reassurance and support reduces customer anxiety and improves satisfaction.

## Success criteria

- The system confirms language support for critical touchpoints
- The customer feels their communication needs are taken seriously
- The agency prevents language-related breakdowns in service
