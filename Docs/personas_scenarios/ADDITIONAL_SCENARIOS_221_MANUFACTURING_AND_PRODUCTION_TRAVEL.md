## Scenario: Manufacturing and Production Travel

### Situation
Manufacturing professionals and production specialists need travel arrangements for factory visits, production facilities, and industrial conferences with technical access and safety coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange factory and production facility access, coordinate industrial conference attendance, provide technical transportation and logistics, and ensure manufacturing travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Manufacturing travel involves specialized knowledge of industrial protocols, factory safety, and production access that requires expertise in manufacturing industry tourism.

### Success criteria
- Factory and production facility access coordination
- Industrial conference and industry event attendance arrangements
- Technical transportation and production logistics support
- Manufacturing travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
