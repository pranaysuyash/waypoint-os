# Additional Scenario 83: School Group Supervision Plan

**Scenario**: A school group trip requires chaperone coordination, permissions, and safety planning, and the system must support institutional requirements.

---

## Situation

A school is booking a field trip or educational tour for minors.
The agency must help ensure adequate supervision, permissions, and compliance with school policies.

## What the system should do

- Capture chaperone ratios, emergency procedures, and permission forms
- Confirm activity suitability for minors and educational goals
- Coordinate transport, meal planning, and lodging with school safeguards
- Provide a clear plan for incident handling and parent communication

## Why this matters

School travel requires higher diligence and documentation.
A strong plan protects both students and the institution.

## Success criteria

- The system documents supervision and safety protocols clearly
- The organizer has a single source of truth for permissions and logistics
- The trip is compliant with school policy and parental expectations
