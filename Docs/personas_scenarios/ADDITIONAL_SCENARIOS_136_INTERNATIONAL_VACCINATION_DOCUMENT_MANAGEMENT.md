# Additional Scenario 136: International Vaccination Document Management

**Scenario**: A traveler needs help managing required vaccination documents for an international trip.

---

## Situation

A customer is traveling to a country with vaccine-required entry or health screening policies.
They want the agency to handle documentation, timing, and carry requirements so they can travel without delay.

## What the system should do

- Identify destination vaccination requirements and accepted documentation
- Confirm the traveler has current and valid certificates or digital proof
- Advise on timing for vaccinations, boosters, and any required medical forms
- Help the traveler organize and store documents for quick access during travel

## Why this matters

Vaccination rules are enforced strictly at borders.
Incomplete or invalid documents can lead to denied boarding or quarantine.

## Success criteria

- The traveler travels with the correct vaccination documents
- The agency prevents last-minute compliance failures
- The customer understands what to carry and how the documents are used
