# Additional Scenario 139: Senior Travel Medical Support

**Scenario**: A senior traveler needs medical support and comfort planning, and the system must coordinate health services and accessible accommodations.

---

## Situation

A customer is an older traveler with medical needs or mobility concerns.
They expect the agency to plan accommodations, transport, and healthcare support appropriate to their condition.

## What the system should do

- Confirm the traveler’s medical needs and accessibility requirements
- Arrange senior-friendly accommodations, transport, and onsite support where needed
- Provide medical provider contacts and emergency plan details
- Ensure the itinerary is paced for comfort and rest

## Why this matters

Senior travelers often need more planning for health and mobility.
A thoughtful travel plan preserves safety and dignity while still delivering a positive experience.

## Success criteria

- The traveler receives a senior-appropriate itinerary with medical support
- The agency anticipates mobility and health comfort factors
- The customer feels cared for and able to travel safely
