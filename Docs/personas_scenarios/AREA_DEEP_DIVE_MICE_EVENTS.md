# Area Deep Dive: MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, Exhibitions)

**Domain**: Corporate Group Travel  
**Focus**: Scalable logistics, venue management, and complex financial reconciliation.

---

## 1. Large Group Logistics

MICE trips involve 20 to 2,000+ travelers, requiring "Industrial Strength" planning.

### Group Air Blocks
- **Contracting**: Negotiating "Blocked Space" with airlines (e.g., 50 seats on 3 different flights).
- **Name Lists**: Managing "Manifest Deadlines". If names aren't submitted 14 days before, seats are lost.
- **System Action**: Automating the "Manifest Countdown" and sending reminders to the Corporate HR/Admin (P3).

### Venue & Accommodation Blocks
- **Rooming Lists**: Managing complex rooming lists (Singles vs Doubles vs Twins) for 100+ guests.
- **Attrition Clauses**: Monitoring the "Attrition Window" (e.g., "Can cancel up to 20% of rooms for free until 30 days before").

---

## 2. Event Execution & On-Site Support

### The "War Room" Protocol
- **On-Site Coordination**: The agency often has a "Help Desk" at the hotel.
- **System Action**: Providing a "Real-Time Group Dashboard" for the on-site agent to track arrivals, baggage delays, and dietary needs.

### Dietary & Accessibility Manifests
- **Critical Path**: Ensuring the Banquet Manager at the venue has accurate "Allergy Data" for 500 guests.
- **System Action**: Aggregating data from individual "Preference Forms" into a single "Catering Master Sheet".

---

## 3. Commercial & Financial Complexity

### Multi-Payer Reconciliation
- **The Bill**: The corporate pays the "Master Bill" (Rooms/Meals), but individuals pay for "Extras" (Mini-bar/Spa).
- **System Action**: Automated reconciliation of the Master Bill vs Individual Folios.

### Event Insurance & Liability
- **Risk**: Event cancellation due to unforeseen circumstances (e.g., speaker illness, venue failure).
- **Action**: Managing "Event Cancellation Insurance" policies as part of the booking package.
