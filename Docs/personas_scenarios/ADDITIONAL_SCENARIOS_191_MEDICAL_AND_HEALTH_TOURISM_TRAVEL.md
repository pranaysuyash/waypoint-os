## Scenario: Medical and Health Tourism Travel

### Situation
Medical tourists and health seekers need travel arrangements for medical procedures, wellness retreats, and health-focused destinations with medical coordination and recovery accommodations.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate medical appointments and procedures, arrange recovery accommodations, provide health-focused transportation, and ensure medical tourism logistics.

### Why this matters
Medical travel involves healthcare coordination, recovery planning, and medical facility access that requires specialized knowledge of healthcare systems and patient care.

### Success criteria
- Medical appointment and procedure coordination
- Recovery accommodation and wellness retreat arrangements
- Health-focused transportation and accessibility services
- Medical tourism logistics and patient care support</content>
