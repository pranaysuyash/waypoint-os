### Situation
Textile and fashion professionals need travel arrangements for textile mills, fashion conferences, and design studios with technical access and coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange textile mill and fashion conference access, coordinate design studio attendance, provide technical transportation and logistics, and ensure textile travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Textile travel involves specialized knowledge of fashion protocols, textile safety, and studio access that requires expertise in textile industry tourism.

### Success criteria
- Textile mill and fashion conference access coordination
- Design studio and event attendance arrangements
- Technical transportation and textile logistics support
- Textile travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
