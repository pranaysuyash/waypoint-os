## Scenario: High-Speed Rail and Transport Innovation Travel

### Situation
Transport planners, rail engineers, and mobility innovators need travel arrangements for high-speed rail site visits, innovation showcases, and rail corridor planning.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate rail system access, arrange technical site visits, manage stakeholder workshop logistics, and support transport innovation event participation.

### Why this matters
High-speed rail travel requires coordination with transport authorities, technical teams, and regulatory stakeholders to advance infrastructure innovation.

### Success criteria
- Rail system and corridor planning coordination
- Technical site visit and stakeholder workshop logistics
- Support for transport innovation showcases
- Compliance with rail authority requirements
