## Scenario: Senior Travel Assistance Services

### Situation
Older travelers need accommodations for mobility limitations, health considerations, and simplified travel processes that reduce stress and ensure comfort.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange accessible transportation and accommodations, coordinate medical support services, provide simplified booking processes, and offer companion travel options.

### Why this matters
Senior travelers often have specific health and mobility needs that require thoughtful accommodation to ensure safe, comfortable, and enjoyable travel experiences.

### Success criteria
- Accessible transportation and accommodation arrangements
- Medical and health support service coordination
- Simplified booking and communication processes
- Emergency assistance and companion travel options</content>
