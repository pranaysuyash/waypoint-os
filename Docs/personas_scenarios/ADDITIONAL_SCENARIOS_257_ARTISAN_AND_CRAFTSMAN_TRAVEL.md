## Scenario: Artisan and Craftsman Travel

### Situation
Artisans, craftsmen, and makers need travel arrangements for workshops, residencies, and craft fairs with tools, materials, and inventory management.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange workshop access, coordinate materials logistics, schedule craft fair attendance, and support workspace transportation needs.

### Why this matters
Artisan travel requires handling specialty tools, material sourcing, and maker schedules to preserve creative flow and avoid expensive interruptions.

### Success criteria
- Workshop and craft fair coordination
- Materials and tool logistics support
- Maker residency travel planning
- Smooth creative and production-focused travel experience
