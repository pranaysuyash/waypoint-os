## Scenario: Student Educational Travel Programs

### Situation
Students participating in educational travel programs need structured learning experiences, safety supervision, and coordination with academic requirements.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange educational tours with qualified guides, coordinate with schools for academic credit, ensure appropriate supervision ratios, and provide emergency contact systems.

### Why this matters
Educational travel combines learning objectives with travel logistics, requiring specialized coordination to ensure both educational value and student safety.

### Success criteria
- Coordination with educational institutions for curriculum alignment
- Qualified educational guides and supervision arrangements
- Safety protocols and emergency response systems
- Academic documentation and credit arrangements</content>
