# Additional Scenario 36: Loyalty Discount Request

**Scenario**: A returning customer asks if they can get a loyalty discount, and the system must answer based on policy while preserving relationship value.

---

## Situation

A repeat traveler is booking again and wants to know whether their loyalty or past spend qualifies them for a discount.
They expect the agency to reward their repeat business without making the answer feel transactional.

## What the system should do

- Check whether loyalty discounts or repeat-customer credits apply
- Explain the policy clearly if there is no formal discount
- Offer a customer-focused alternative if no discount exists (e.g., extra service, welcome gift)
- Keep the response appreciative and relationship-oriented

## Why this matters

Loyalty questions are emotionally sensitive.
A poor answer can make a loyal customer feel unvalued, while a thoughtful response can strengthen retention.

## Success criteria

- The system validates the request against program rules
- The customer receives a respectful and clear response
- The agency preserves goodwill even when the formal policy is limited
