## Scenario: High-Performance Computing and Data Center Travel

### Situation
HPC engineers, data center operations teams, and cloud infrastructure specialists need travel arrangements for data center site visits, hardware deployments, and capacity planning meetings.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate data center access, arrange transport for gear and technicians, manage security clearance logistics, and support facility review schedules.

### Why this matters
Data center travel requires strict security, precise timing, and coordination with infrastructure teams to maintain uptime and deploy complex technology safely.

### Success criteria
- Data center access and capacity planning coordination
- Equipment and technician transport logistics support
- Security clearance and facility review planning
- Alignment with infrastructure deployment timelines
