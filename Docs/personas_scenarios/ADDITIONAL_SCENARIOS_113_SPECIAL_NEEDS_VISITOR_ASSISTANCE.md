# Additional Scenario 113: Special Needs Visitor Assistance

**Scenario**: A traveler requires special assistance as a visitor, and the system must coordinate services for hearing, vision, mobility, or cognitive needs.

---

## Situation

A customer has a disability or special need and requires support from airports, hotels, and activities.
They expect the agency to coordinate accessible services throughout the trip.

## What the system should do

- Capture the specific special needs accurately
- Verify support services across all touchpoints, including transport and venues
- Confirm any required assistive equipment or personnel
- Communicate the plan with dignity and clarity

## Why this matters

Special needs travel requires consistent, empathetic support.
A failure in one touchpoint can compromise the entire trip.

## Success criteria

- The system coordinates accessible services end-to-end
- The traveler feels safe and respected by the planning
- The agency avoids gaps in assistance coverage
