# Additional Scenario 85: Financial Refund Timeline Communication

**Scenario**: A customer is owed a refund, and the system must communicate the refund timeline clearly while tracking supplier and payment-provider processing.

---

## Situation

A trip is canceled or modified and the customer expects a refund.
The agency needs to explain when the funds will arrive and why processing may take time.

## What the system should do

- Calculate the expected refund timeline for each payment path
- Communicate the timeline to the customer in simple terms
- Track the refund status across vendors and payment providers
- Proactively update the customer if the timeline changes

## Why this matters

Refund delays damage trust, even when the policy is correct.
Clear communication saves customers from repeated follow-up inquiries.

## Success criteria

- The customer receives an accurate refund timeline
- The agency tracks the refund status until completion
- The customer understands any third-party delay factors
