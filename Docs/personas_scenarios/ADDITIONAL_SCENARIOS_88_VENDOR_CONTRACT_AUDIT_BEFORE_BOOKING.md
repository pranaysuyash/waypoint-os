# Additional Scenario 88: Vendor Contract Audit Before Booking

**Scenario**: A large or sensitive booking requires a contract audit, and the system must highlight vendor obligations, cancellation terms, and liability clauses.

---

## Situation

A customer wants a high-value booking with a supplier contract rather than a standard reservation.
The agency must verify terms before committing.

## What the system should do

- Review vendor contract terms for cancellation, force majeure, and payment schedule
- Flag any unfavorable clauses or hidden fees
- Recommend changes or alternative suppliers if needed
- Keep the customer informed of the contract risks and protections

## Why this matters

Contractual liability outstrips routine booking risk.
Good audit avoids expensive surprises and protects the agency and customer.

## Success criteria

- The system audits contract risks before booking
- It identifies and explains any red flags clearly
- The customer decides with confidence based on contract transparency
