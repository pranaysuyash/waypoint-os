# Additional Scenario 48: Pet Travel Arrangement

**Scenario**: A customer wants to bring a pet, and the system must handle pet-friendly transport, accommodation, and regulations.

---

## Situation

A traveler plans to bring a dog or cat and needs a trip that supports pet travel.
They expect the agency to manage airline pet policies, hotel pet fees, and local restrictions.

## What the system should do

- Identify pet policies for flights, hotels, and local transport
- Estimate extra fees and documentation required
- Suggest pet-friendly itinerary components only
- Warn if the requested destination is restrictive or high-risk for pets

## Why this matters

Pet travel has specific logistics that can derail a trip if overlooked.
Managing it properly is a strong service differentiator.

## Success criteria

- The system provides a complete pet travel plan
- It prevents hidden denials or last-minute rejections
- The customer feels the agency understands pet travel complexity
