# Additional Scenario 67: Mobility Accessibility Support

**Scenario**: A traveler needs wheelchair access or mobility assistance, and the system must ensure the itinerary supports accessible transport, accommodation, and activities.

---

## Situation

A customer requires mobility support due to a disability or injury.
They expect the agency to provide accessible options at every stage of the trip.

## What the system should do

- Verify accessible room types, transport, and venue access
- Confirm support services like airport assistance or ramps
- Notify the customer of any remaining gaps or limitations
- Keep communication respectful and fact-based

## Why this matters

Accessibility is both a legal and ethical priority.
A good plan prevents serious travel barriers and delivers dignity.

## Success criteria

- The system validates accessibility requirements across the itinerary
- The traveler is informed of all accommodations and limitations
- The agency prevents last-minute accessibility failures
