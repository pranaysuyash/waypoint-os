## Scenario: Industrial Automation and Robotics Travel

### Situation
Automation engineers, robotics integrators, and manufacturing technology teams need travel arrangements for factory installations, robotics demonstrations, and systems integration meetings.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate plant access, arrange transport for tools and test equipment, support safety and compliance logistics, and schedule integration team travel.

### Why this matters
Automation travel requires precise timing, technical equipment readiness, and coordination with manufacturing operations to avoid downtime.

### Success criteria
- Factory installation and robotics demo coordination
- Equipment transport and technical logistics planning
- Safety and compliance support for manufacturing sites
- Smooth scheduling for integration teams
