# Additional Scenario 76: Travel Warning Cancellation

**Scenario**: A travel warning is issued for a booked destination, and the system must help the customer cancel, reschedule, or choose a safer alternative.

---

## Situation

A traveler has already booked a trip when an official travel warning is issued for their destination.
They need the agency to interpret the warning, explain options, and preserve value.

## What the system should do

- Detect travel advisory changes relevant to the itinerary
- Explain cancellation, rescheduling, and insurance options clearly
- Recommend safer destinations or later travel dates if needed
- Keep the messaging calm and focused on customer safety

## Why this matters

Travel warnings change the trip calculus overnight.
A prompt, well-informed response protects customers and the agency’s credibility.

## Success criteria

- The system identifies the advisory and its impact quickly
- The customer gets a clear path forward without confusion
- The agency minimizes losses while prioritizing safety
