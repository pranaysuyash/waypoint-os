## Scenario: Luxury Yacht and Marina Travel

### Situation
Yacht owners, charter guests, and marine professionals need travel arrangements for marinas, luxury yacht transfers, and waterfront hospitality.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate marina reservations, yacht transfers, dock access, and luxury waterfront amenities for high-end maritime experiences.

### Why this matters
Luxury yacht travel requires bespoke coordination across ports, crew logistics, and premium hospitality details to deliver an exceptional waterfront journey.

### Success criteria
- Marina reservation and dock access coordination
- Luxury yacht transfer and crew logistics support
- Waterfront hospitality and amenity planning
- Seamless premium maritime travel experience
