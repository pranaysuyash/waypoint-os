# Additional Scenario 104: Concert or Festival Camping Logistics

**Scenario**: A traveler attends a multi-day festival with camping, and the system must manage camping logistics, amenities, and safety.

---

## Situation

A customer books a festival or outdoor event that includes campsite stays.
They need the agency to coordinate camping needs, gear, and facility access.

## What the system should do

- Confirm camping access, tent setup, and on-site facilities
- Advise on gear, security, and shuttle logistics
- Highlight any festival-specific rules or restrictions
- Provide contingency plans for bad weather or site changes

## Why this matters

Festival camping introduces event-specific logistics.
A strong plan reduces discomfort and improves the overall experience.

## Success criteria

- The system coordinates camping logistics clearly and accurately
- The traveler knows what amenities and restrictions to expect
- The agency anticipates and mitigates common festival camping issues
