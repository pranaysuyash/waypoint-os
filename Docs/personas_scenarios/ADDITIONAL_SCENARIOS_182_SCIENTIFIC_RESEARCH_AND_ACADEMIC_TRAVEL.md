## Scenario: Scientific Research and Academic Travel

### Situation
Researchers and academics need travel arrangements for conferences, field research, laboratory visits, and academic collaborations with specialized equipment and permit requirements.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange research permits and institutional access, coordinate laboratory and facility visits, provide specialized equipment transportation, and support academic conference attendance.

### Why this matters
Scientific travel involves complex logistics for equipment, permits, and institutional access that require coordination with academic and research institutions.

### Success criteria
- Research permit and institutional access coordination
- Laboratory and research facility visit arrangements
- Specialized equipment transportation services
- Academic conference and collaboration support</content>
