### Situation
Food enthusiasts want immersive culinary experiences, cooking classes, and food tours that explore local cuisines, markets, and dining traditions.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange cooking classes with local chefs, coordinate food tours and market visits, book reservations at renowned restaurants, and provide dietary accommodation information.

### Why this matters
Culinary tourism offers cultural insights through food, requiring knowledgeable coordination to ensure authentic, safe, and memorable gastronomic experiences.

- Authentic cooking class and chef experience arrangements
- Food tour and local market visit coordination
- Restaurant reservations with culinary expertise
