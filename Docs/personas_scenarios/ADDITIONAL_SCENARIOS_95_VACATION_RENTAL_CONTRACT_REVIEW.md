# Additional Scenario 95: Vacation Rental Contract Review

**Scenario**: A customer books a vacation rental, and the system must review the rental contract for rules around deposits, damages, and guest limits.

---

## Situation

A traveler chooses a vacation rental instead of a hotel.
They need the agency to verify contract terms and guest requirements before confirming.

## What the system should do

- Audit rental contract terms for deposit, damage responsibility, and cancellation
- Confirm maximum guest count and any additional fees
- Highlight any unusual house rules or local restrictions
- Recommend rental alternatives if the contract is too risky or restrictive

## Why this matters

Vacation rentals have unique contract risks.
A poorly reviewed rental can lead to disputes or denied check-in.

## Success criteria

- The system identifies rental contract risks before booking
- The traveler understands the terms and any extra costs
- The agency recommends safe rental options with clear expectations
