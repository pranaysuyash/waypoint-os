## Scenario: International Sports Team Training and Facility Scouting Travel

### Situation
Sports team managers, coaches, and athletic directors need travel arrangements for training camps, facility scouting, and pre-season preparation tours.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate training facility access, arrange accommodation for athletes and staff, manage equipment transport, and support competitive scouting itineraries.

### Why this matters
Sports travel requires meticulous planning to keep training schedules on track while ensuring athletes have the right facilities and recovery support.

### Success criteria
- Training camp and facility scouting coordination
- Athlete and staff accommodation planning
- Equipment transport and logistics support
- Competitive preparation aligned with team schedules
