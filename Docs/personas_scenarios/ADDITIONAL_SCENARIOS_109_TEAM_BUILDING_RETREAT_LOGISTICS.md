# Additional Scenario 109: Team Building Retreat Logistics

**Scenario**: A company plans a team-building retreat, and the system must coordinate group activities, meeting space, and team logistics.

---

## Situation

A business customer wants a retreat with both corporate sessions and team-building experiences.
They expect the agency to manage venue selection, group pacing, and logistical coordination.

## What the system should do

- Recommend venues with meeting space, accommodation, and team activity options
- Coordinate group transport, meals, and session schedules
- Provide flexibility for team energy levels and spontaneous adjustments
- Ensure the retreat balances corporate goals with team bonding

## Why this matters

Team retreats need both structure and flexibility.
A strong plan ensures the event delivers business results and employee experience.

## Success criteria

- The system creates a cohesive retreat plan with logistics covered
- The customer sees clear links between retreat goals and trip design
- The agency manages group dynamics and event reliability effectively
