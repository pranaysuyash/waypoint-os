# Additional Scenario 123: Virtual Health Screening Requirement

**Scenario**: A traveler must complete a virtual health screening before travel, and the system must manage the health-check workflow and documentation.

---

## Situation

A client is traveling to a destination with a requirement for remote health screening or self-reporting before departure.
They need the agency to collect the necessary information and make sure they remain compliant.

## What the system should do

- Identify the destination’s virtual health screening requirements
- Guide the traveler through the required questionnaire or telehealth check
- Confirm that all health documents are complete and delivered on time
- Provide support if the traveler has follow-up questions or fails the screening

## Why this matters

Health screening requirements are time-sensitive and can block travel.
Completing them reliably is crucial to avoid denied boarding or entry.

## Success criteria

- The system ensures the traveler meets the required health-screening criteria
- The traveler receives clear instructions and reminders
- The agency prevents last-minute travel disruption from missing health documentation
