## Scenario: Hospitality Industry Training Travel

### Situation
Hospitality trainers, service staff, and hotel teams need travel arrangements for training sessions, operational workshops, and service excellence programs.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange group training travel, coordinate facility access, plan hospitality workshop schedules, and manage lodging for staff development.

### Why this matters
Hospitality training travel must align staff availability with hands-on learning environments while maintaining service standards and operational coverage.

### Success criteria
- Training program and facility coordination
- Group travel planning for hospitality teams
- Accommodation and workshop schedule alignment
- Support for operational continuity during staff development
