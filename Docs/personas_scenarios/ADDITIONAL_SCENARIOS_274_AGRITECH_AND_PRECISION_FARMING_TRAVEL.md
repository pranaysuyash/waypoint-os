## Scenario: Agritech and Precision Farming Travel

### Situation
Agritech founders, precision farming specialists, and agricultural extension teams need travel arrangements for farm pilots, tech deployments, and field demonstrations.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange farm visits, coordinate equipment deployment, support remote field team logistics, and manage meetings with growers.

### Why this matters
Agritech travel must align with planting cycles, weather windows, and on-farm operational realities to prove technology in real conditions.

### Success criteria
- Farm pilot and field demonstration coordination
- Equipment deployment and grower meeting logistics
- Remote field team travel support
- Alignment with agricultural timing and operational needs
