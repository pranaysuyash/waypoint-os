## Scenario: Agriculture and Farming Travel

### Situation
Agricultural professionals and farming specialists need travel arrangements for farm visits, agricultural conferences, and farming cooperatives with technical access and industry coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange farm and agricultural facility access, coordinate farming conference attendance, provide technical transportation and logistics, and ensure agriculture travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Agricultural travel involves specialized knowledge of farming protocols, agricultural safety, and farm access that requires expertise in agriculture industry tourism.

### Success criteria
- Farm and agricultural facility access coordination
- Farming conference and industry event attendance arrangements
- Technical transportation and agricultural logistics support
- Agriculture travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
