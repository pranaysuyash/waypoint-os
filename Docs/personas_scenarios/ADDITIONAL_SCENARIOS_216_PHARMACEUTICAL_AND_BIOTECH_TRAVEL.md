Pharmaceutical professionals and biotech specialists need travel arrangements for research facilities, pharmaceutical conferences, and biotech laboratory visits with technical access and industry coordination.

The travel agent should arrange research facility and laboratory access, coordinate pharmaceutical conference attendance, provide technical transportation and logistics, and ensure biotech travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Pharmaceutical travel involves specialized knowledge of research protocols, laboratory access, and biotech industry coordination that requires expertise in pharmaceutical tourism.

### Success criteria
- Research facility and laboratory access coordination
- Pharmaceutical conference and industry event attendance arrangements
- Technical transportation and research logistics support
- Biotech travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
