## Scenario: Gaming and Esports Event Travel

### Situation
Gamers and esports enthusiasts need travel packages for tournaments, conventions, and gaming events with VIP access, meet-and-greets, and gaming-specific accommodations.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange tournament tickets and VIP packages, coordinate gaming convention access, book esports venue accommodations, and provide gaming equipment transportation services.

### Why this matters
Gaming events have unique timing, access requirements, and technical needs that require specialized coordination for optimal participant and spectator experiences.

### Success criteria
- Tournament ticket and VIP package procurement
- Gaming convention and event access coordination
- Esports venue accommodation arrangements
- Gaming equipment transportation and setup services</content>
