# Additional Scenario 96: Corporate Travel Policy Approval

**Scenario**: A business traveler needs a trip approved under corporate travel policy, and the system must validate compliance before booking.

---

## Situation

A customer is booking on behalf of a company with travel policy rules for budget, class, and preferred suppliers.
They need the agency to verify approval criteria and avoid invalid bookings.

## What the system should do

- Check the itinerary against corporate policy rules
- Flag any non-compliant elements like hotel class or flight fare type
- Provide a clear summary for approval, including policy exceptions if needed
- Reduce back-and-forth by surfacing compliant alternatives upfront

## Why this matters

Corporate travel requires extra governance.
A policy-aware booking process saves time and prevents rejected reimbursements.

## Success criteria

- The system validates policy compliance before booking
- The traveler receives a clear approval summary
- The agency minimizes approval delays and incorrect bookings
