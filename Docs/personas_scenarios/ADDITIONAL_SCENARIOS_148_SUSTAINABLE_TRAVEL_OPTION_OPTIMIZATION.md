## Scenario: Sustainable Travel Option Optimization

### Situation
An environmentally conscious traveler wants to minimize their carbon footprint by selecting the most sustainable transportation and accommodation options for their trip.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should analyze transportation options for carbon emissions, recommend eco-friendly accommodations (green certifications, energy-efficient practices), and suggest offsetting programs or sustainable activities to balance the trip's environmental impact.

### Why this matters
Travelers increasingly prioritize sustainability, and providing eco-friendly options helps meet this demand while contributing to environmental conservation.

### Success criteria
- Carbon footprint analysis for all transportation options
- Recommendations of certified sustainable accommodations
- Integration of carbon offset programs into booking process
- Educational information about sustainable travel choices</content>
