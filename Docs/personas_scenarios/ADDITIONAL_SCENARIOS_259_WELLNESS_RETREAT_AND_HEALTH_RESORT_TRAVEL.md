## Scenario: Wellness Retreat and Health Resort Travel

### Situation
Wellness travelers, retreat organizers, and health resort guests need travel arrangements for restorative programs, spa stays, and holistic health retreats.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate retreat reservations, wellness program schedules, personalized health accommodations, and restorative transport experiences.

### Why this matters
Wellness travel requires seamless transitions, dietary accommodations, and calm logistics to preserve the retreat's restorative purpose.

### Success criteria
- Retreat reservation and program coordination
- Wellness accommodations and dietary support
- Restorative transport and arrival planning
- Tranquil guest experience from arrival to departure
