# Additional Scenario 43: Visa Expiration Mid-Trip

**Scenario**: The customer’s passport or visa expires during the trip, and the system must warn the agent and propose a safe itinerary adjustment.

---

## Situation

A traveler plans a long stay or multi-country route, and one leg would extend beyond the visa validity period.
They need the agency to catch the issue before tickets are issued.

## What the system should do

- Validate the visa/entry requirements against the full itinerary duration
- Flag any mid-trip expiration or overstay risk
- Recommend itinerary changes or a visa extension process
- Communicate the urgency without causing panic

## Why this matters

Visa non-compliance can stop a trip entirely and cause legal trouble.
Proactive checks protect customers and the agency from serious failure.

## Success criteria

- The system catches the validity gap before confirmation
- It provides concrete corrective options
- The customer gets reassurance that the agency is managing risk
