## Scenario: EdTech Innovation Launch and Recruitment Travel

### Situation
Education technology founders, recruitment teams, and innovation program leaders need travel arrangements for product launches, pilot school visits, and teacher recruitment events.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate launch event logistics, arrange school and district visits, manage training sessions, and support recruitment networking travel.

### Why this matters
EdTech travel requires thoughtful planning for educational stakeholders, demonstration readiness, and alignment with academic calendars.

### Success criteria
- Product launch and pilot school visit coordination
- Training session and recruitment event logistics support
- Stakeholder engagement travel planning
- Alignment with academic calendar and adoption milestones
