# Additional Scenario 78: Last-Minute Activity Confirmation

**Scenario**: A customer requests confirmation for an activity shortly before departure, and the system must secure availability and manage expectations.

---

## Situation

A traveler asks for a booking confirmation for a high-demand activity two days before travel.
They expect the agency to make the confirmation happen fast without overpromising.

## What the system should do

- Check activity availability and booking cutoffs immediately
- Confirm any additional fees or expedited processing requirements
- Communicate the likelihood of confirmation clearly
- Offer alternative activities if the preferred option is unlikely

## Why this matters

Last-minute activity requests are a real revenue opportunity.
The agency needs to move quickly while still being honest about the chances.

## Success criteria

- The system secures or declines the request quickly
- The customer knows what is confirmed, pending, or unavailable
- The agency provides a viable fallback instead of leaving the request unresolved
