# Additional Scenario 75: Community Impact Travel Plan

**Scenario**: A traveler wants to support local communities, and the system must recommend responsible experiences that benefit residents.

---

## Situation

A customer asks for travel that contributes positively to local communities.
They expect curated experiences that are responsible, authentic, and respectful.

## What the system should do

- Identify community-based tourism experiences and ethically sourced vendors
- Explain how the trip supports local economies or conservation
- Avoid experiences that exploit communities or wildlife
- Provide transparency about fees and the actual benefit delivered

## Why this matters

Responsible travel is a growing expectation for many customers.
The agency should enable meaningful impact without compromising quality.

## Success criteria

- The system recommends authentic, responsible travel options
- The customer understands the social/environmental benefit
- The itinerary balances impact with comfort and convenience
