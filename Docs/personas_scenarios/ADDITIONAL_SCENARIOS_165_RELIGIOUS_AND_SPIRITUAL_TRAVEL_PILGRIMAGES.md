## Scenario: Religious and Spiritual Travel Pilgrimages

### Situation
Pilgrims seeking spiritual journeys need guidance on sacred sites, religious accommodations, and culturally sensitive travel arrangements that respect religious practices.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should provide expert knowledge of religious sites and traditions, arrange appropriate accommodations and transportation respecting religious requirements, and coordinate with local religious authorities.

### Why this matters
Religious travel involves deep spiritual significance and cultural sensitivity, requiring knowledgeable guidance to ensure meaningful and respectful pilgrimage experiences.

### Success criteria
- Expert guidance on religious sites and traditions
- Culturally sensitive accommodation and transportation arrangements
- Coordination with religious authorities and local communities
- Respect for religious practices and observances throughout the journey</content>
