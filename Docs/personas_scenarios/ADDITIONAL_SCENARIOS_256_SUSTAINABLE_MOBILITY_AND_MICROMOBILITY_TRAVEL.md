## Scenario: Sustainable Mobility and Micromobility Travel

### Situation
Urban mobility planners, micromobility startups, and sustainable transport teams need travel arrangements for city-based mobility pilots, event visits, and infrastructure assessments.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate access to mobility hubs, arrange local micromobility transport, manage event logistics, and support sustainable urban transit planning.

### Why this matters
Sustainable mobility travel demands knowledge of local regulations, parking strategy, and first/last-mile coordination to ensure pilot programs and events run smoothly.

### Success criteria
- Mobility hub and pilot event coordination
- Local micromobility transport planning
- Sustainable urban transit logistics support
- Clear alignment with city mobility requirements
