## Scenario: Ecotourism and Sustainable Travel

### Situation
Environmentally conscious travelers seek sustainable travel experiences, conservation projects, and eco-friendly destinations with minimal environmental impact.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange eco-friendly transportation and accommodations, coordinate conservation project participation, provide sustainable travel education, and support environmental impact minimization.

### Why this matters
Ecotourism involves environmental responsibility, conservation awareness, and sustainable practices that require specialized knowledge of eco-friendly travel options.

### Success criteria
- Eco-friendly transportation and accommodation arrangements
- Conservation project and volunteer opportunity coordination
- Sustainable travel education and awareness support
- Environmental impact minimization and carbon offset services</content>
