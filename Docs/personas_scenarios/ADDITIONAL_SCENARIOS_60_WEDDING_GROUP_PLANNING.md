# Additional Scenario 60: Wedding Group Planning

**Scenario**: A couple books travel for wedding guests, and the system must coordinate blocks of rooms, event logistics, and group communication.

---

## Situation

A wedding party needs coordinated travel for multiple guests and event-related accommodations.
The agency must manage room blocks, guest lists, and special event logistics.

## What the system should do

- Allocate hotel room blocks and group rates
- Track guest RSVPs, special requests, and transport needs
- Coordinate event timing, venue transfers, and welcome activities
- Keep the wedding planner informed and aligned with the group schedule

## Why this matters

Weddings are complex group events with high expectations.
A single planning failure can affect multiple travelers and the success of the celebration.

## Success criteria

- The system creates a coherent group travel plan
- It supports event-related logistics beyond standard trip booking
- The wedding planner feels coordinated and supported
