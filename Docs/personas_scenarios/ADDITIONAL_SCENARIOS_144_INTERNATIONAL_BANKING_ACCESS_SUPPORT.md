# Additional Scenario 144: International Banking Access Support

**Scenario**: A traveler needs international banking access, and the system must help them manage cards, cash, and secure account access.

---

## Situation

A customer is traveling internationally and needs access to funds through cards, accounts, or local banking services.
They expect the agency to help avoid payment declines and ensure secure money access.

## What the system should do

- Advise on card acceptance, foreign transaction fees, and local currency options
- Recommend backup payment methods and secure cash handling
- Help the traveler prepare for bank notifications, holds, or authentication checks
- Provide guidance on local ATM access and emergency support if payments fail

## Why this matters

Financial access problems can derail a trip quickly.
A robust payment plan reduces the risk of being stranded without funds.

## Success criteria

- The traveler maintains reliable, secure access to money abroad
- The agency anticipates banking restrictions and payment challenges
- The customer avoids surprise declines or fee-related confusion
