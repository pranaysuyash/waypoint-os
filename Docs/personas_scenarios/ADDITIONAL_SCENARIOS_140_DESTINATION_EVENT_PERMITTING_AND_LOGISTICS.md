# Additional Scenario 140: Destination Event Permitting and Logistics

**Scenario**: A traveler attends or hosts a destination event, and the system must manage event permitting, logistics, and local coordination.

---

## Situation

A customer requires planning for an event like a conference, wedding, or offsite group meeting at their destination.
They expect the agency to handle permits, venue logistics, and local rules.

## What the system should do

- Confirm local permitting requirements and vendor obligations
- Coordinate venue, catering, transport, and event support
- Ensure the schedule aligns with travel and arrival timing
- Warn the customer of risks, approvals, or additional approvals needed

## Why this matters

Destination events add complexity to travel logistics.
Proper permitting and coordination prevent costly delays and compliance issues.

## Success criteria

- The event is supported with the necessary local approvals and logistics
- The agency keeps the customer informed of all event-related requirements
- The travel plan integrates the event without creating unmanageable risk
