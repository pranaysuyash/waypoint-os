## Scenario: Non-Profit and NGO Travel

### Situation
Non-profit professionals and NGO specialists need travel arrangements for humanitarian projects, NGO conferences, and community development sites with technical access and coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange humanitarian project and NGO conference access, coordinate community development site attendance, provide technical transportation and logistics, and ensure non-profit travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Non-profit travel involves specialized knowledge of humanitarian protocols, NGO safety, and project access that requires expertise in non-profit industry tourism.

### Success criteria
- Humanitarian project and NGO conference access coordination
- Community development site and event attendance arrangements
- Technical transportation and non-profit logistics support
- Non-profit travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
