# Additional Scenario 94: Trip Concierge Post-Booking Care

**Scenario**: A customer wants post-booking concierge support, and the system must provide a high-touch service layer for itinerary tweaks and on-trip requests.

---

## Situation

A traveler wants ongoing support after the booking is made—including itinerary updates, dining reservations, and on-trip requests.
They expect a concierge-level relationship rather than just a booking confirmation.

## What the system should do

- Offer a dedicated concierge contact or service channel
- Handle post-booking changes elegantly and quickly
- Track customer requests and ensure timely follow-up
- Provide proactive service such as weather alerts and local tips

## Why this matters

Post-booking care is where premium travel services differentiate themselves.
Great ongoing support turns travelers into repeat customers.

## Success criteria

- The customer feels supported throughout the trip lifecycle
- The agency resolves post-booking requests swiftly
- The concierge service adds perceived value beyond the booking
