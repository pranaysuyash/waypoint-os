# Additional Scenario 82: Influencer Content Requirements

**Scenario**: An influencer requests a trip that supports content creation requirements, and the system must align logistics with filming and posting needs.

---

## Situation

A customer is traveling for content creation and requires specific imagery, permissions, or internet access.
They expect the agency to understand the production brief, not just the itinerary.

## What the system should do

- Capture content needs (location shots, lighting, access permissions)
- Recommend venues and experiences that support filming or high-quality photo opportunities
- Verify any filming permits, drone restrictions, or influence policies
- Coordinate timing to match golden hour, event schedules, or connectivity requirements

## Why this matters

Influencer travel has different success metrics than leisure travel.
A well-curated trip can make or break the content objective.

## Success criteria

- The system packages the trip around content creation needs
- It avoids venues with restrictive media policies
- The customer gets an itinerary that supports both travel and production goals
