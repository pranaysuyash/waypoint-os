# Additional Scenario 64: Cultural Sensitivity Guidance

**Scenario**: A customer asks for cultural guidance for a destination, and the system must provide respectful, practical advice.

---

## Situation

A traveler is visiting a country with cultural norms different from their own.
They want to know how to behave, dress, tip, and interact respectfully.

## What the system should do

- Provide destination-specific cultural etiquette advice
- Highlight common local taboos and respectful behaviors
- Explain expectations for dress, tipping, and negotiation
- Avoid stereotyping and keep advice practical and current

## Why this matters

Cultural missteps can make trips awkward or worse.
Good guidance helps travelers enjoy their trip and respect local norms.

## Success criteria

- The system delivers accurate, current cultural guidance
- The advice is respectful and not patronizing
- The traveler feels prepared for social norms at the destination
