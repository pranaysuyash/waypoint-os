## Scenario: Cultural Immersion Activity Curation

### Situation
A traveler wants to experience authentic local culture through meaningful activities, workshops, and interactions that go beyond typical tourist attractions.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should curate personalized cultural experiences based on the traveler's interests, arrange authentic local interactions (cooking classes, artisan workshops, community events), and provide cultural context and etiquette guidance.

### Why this matters
Cultural immersion creates memorable, transformative travel experiences that foster understanding and appreciation of different cultures.

### Success criteria
- Personalized activity recommendations based on cultural interests
- Arrangements with authentic local providers and artisans
- Cultural context and preparation information provided
