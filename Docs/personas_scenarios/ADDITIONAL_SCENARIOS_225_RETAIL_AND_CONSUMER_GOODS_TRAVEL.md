## Scenario: Retail and Consumer Goods Travel

### Situation
Retail professionals and consumer goods specialists need travel arrangements for retail stores, consumer goods facilities, and industry conferences with technical access and coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange retail store and consumer goods facility access, coordinate industry conference attendance, provide technical transportation and logistics, and ensure retail travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Retail travel involves specialized knowledge of consumer goods protocols, retail safety, and facility access that requires expertise in retail industry tourism.

### Success criteria
- Retail store and consumer goods facility access coordination
- Industry conference and event attendance arrangements
- Technical transportation and retail logistics support
- Retail travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
