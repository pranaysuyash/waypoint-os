# Additional Scenario 100: Group Seat Assignment Coordination

**Scenario**: A large travel group needs coordinated seat assignments for flights or events, and the system must secure the best seating configuration.

---

## Situation

A group booking requires seats together or near each other on flights, trains, or event venues.
The agent must coordinate availability and pricing to keep the group close.

## What the system should do

- Check seat maps and availability for the entire group
- Recommend seating options that satisfy group proximity and budget
- Secure seats together or in preferred sections before release
- Communicate any trade-offs if perfect grouping isn’t possible

## Why this matters

Group seating matters for comfort and cohesion.
A poor seating plan can turn a shared trip into a fragmented experience.

## Success criteria

- The system coordinates group seating proactively
- The customer understands the seating plan and any limitations
- The agency maximizes group togetherness within constraints
