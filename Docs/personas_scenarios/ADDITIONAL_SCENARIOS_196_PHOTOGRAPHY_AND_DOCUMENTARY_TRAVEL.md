## Scenario: Photography and Documentary Travel

### Situation
Photographers and documentary filmmakers need travel arrangements for photo shoots, filming locations, and visual storytelling projects with equipment transportation and location scouting.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange photography location access and permits, coordinate equipment transportation, provide location scouting support, and ensure visual project logistics.

### Why this matters
Photography travel involves specialized equipment, location permissions, and visual project coordination that requires knowledge of photography industry requirements.

### Success criteria
- Photography location access and permit coordination
- Equipment transportation and insurance arrangements
- Location scouting and visual project support
- Documentary and filming logistics management</content>
