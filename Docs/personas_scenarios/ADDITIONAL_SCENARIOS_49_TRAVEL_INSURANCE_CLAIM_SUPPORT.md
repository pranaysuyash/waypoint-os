# Additional Scenario 49: Travel Insurance Claim Support

**Scenario**: A customer files a travel insurance claim after a disruption, and the system must support documentation and submission with clarity.

---

## Situation

A traveler faces a covered event—flight cancellation, illness, or lost baggage—and needs insurance claim guidance.
They expect the agency to help them collect the right documents and understand the process.

## What the system should do

- Identify the type of claim and relevant policy coverage
- Provide a list of required documents and deadlines
- Explain whether the agency can assist with submission or only advise
- Keep the customer informed about next steps

## Why this matters

Insurance claims are stressful and procedural.
Better guidance improves recovery outcomes and reinforces trust.

## Success criteria

- The system gives a precise claim checklist
- The customer understands what to file and when
- The agency helps reduce mistakes that delay reimbursement
