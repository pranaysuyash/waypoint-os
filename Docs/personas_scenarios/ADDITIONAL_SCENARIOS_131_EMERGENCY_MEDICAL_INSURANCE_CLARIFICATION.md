# Additional Scenario 131: Emergency Medical Insurance Clarification

**Scenario**: A traveler needs clear, accurate emergency medical insurance guidance for a destination with unique healthcare requirements.

---

## Situation

A customer is unsure whether their current insurance covers emergency care at their destination.
They want the agency to clarify coverage, recommend top-ups, and explain the claims process.

## What the system should do

- Review applicable travel insurance and destination-specific medical requirements
- Explain what is covered, what is excluded, and whether additional coverage is needed
- Identify trusted emergency medical providers and claims resources
- Keep the information simple and avoid legalese where possible

## Why this matters

Medical emergencies abroad can be expensive and logistically difficult.
Clear insurance guidance prevents gaps in coverage and panic if something goes wrong.

## Success criteria

- The traveler understands their medical insurance coverage clearly
- The agency identifies any necessary top-up or emergency insurance
- The customer can proceed knowing their healthcare risks are managed
