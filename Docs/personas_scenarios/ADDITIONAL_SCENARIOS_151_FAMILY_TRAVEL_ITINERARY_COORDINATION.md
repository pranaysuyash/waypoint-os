## Scenario: Family Travel Itinerary Coordination

### Situation
A family with children of different ages needs a coordinated itinerary that accommodates varying interests, energy levels, and developmental stages while maintaining family bonding time.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should create age-appropriate activity schedules, coordinate family-friendly accommodations and transportation, and build in flexibility for unexpected family needs or changes in plans.

### Why this matters
Family travel requires balancing individual needs with group cohesion, ensuring everyone has an enjoyable experience that strengthens family bonds.

### Success criteria
- Age-appropriate activity recommendations for all family members
- Coordinated scheduling that allows family time together
- Flexible booking options for changing family needs
- Emergency contact coordination for separated family members</content>
