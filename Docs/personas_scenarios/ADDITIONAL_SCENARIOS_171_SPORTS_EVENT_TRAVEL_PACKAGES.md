## Scenario: Sports Event Travel Packages

### Situation
Sports fans need comprehensive travel packages for major sporting events, including tickets, accommodations near venues, and transportation to and from games.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should secure event tickets and hospitality packages, arrange venue-proximate accommodations, coordinate transportation schedules around game times, and provide event-specific information and amenities.

### Why this matters
Sports event travel requires coordination of tickets, timing, and logistics that are unique to each event and venue, ensuring fans have optimal viewing experiences.

### Success criteria
- Event ticket and hospitality package procurement
- Strategic accommodation placement near venues
- Transportation coordination aligned with event schedules
- Event-specific amenities and fan experience enhancements</content>
