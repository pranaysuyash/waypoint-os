## Scenario: Launch Event and Product Demo Travel

### Situation
Product teams, marketing leaders, and launch support staff need travel arrangements for product launches, demo events, and customer showcases.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate event access, manage demo equipment logistics, arrange transportation for launch teams, and support press and customer engagement schedules.

### Why this matters
Launch event travel requires tight coordination, presentation readiness, and seamless logistics to deliver a successful product debut.

### Success criteria
- Launch event and demo coordination
- Demo equipment and transportation logistics support
- Team travel and engagement scheduling
- Smooth press and customer showcase execution
