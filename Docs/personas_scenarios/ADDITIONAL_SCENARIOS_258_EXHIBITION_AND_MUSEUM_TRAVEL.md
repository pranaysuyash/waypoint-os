## Scenario: Exhibition and Museum Travel

### Situation
Curators, exhibition teams, and museum professionals need travel arrangements for artwork transport, exhibition setup, and cultural exchange programs.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate exhibition access, arrange artwork shipping support, manage curator itineraries, and ensure compliance with museum logistics and insurance requirements.

### Why this matters
Exhibition travel demands precise timing, climate-controlled transport, and institutional coordination to protect high-value artifacts and cultural installations.

### Success criteria
- Exhibition setup and museum access coordination
- Art transport and logistics support
- Curator and cultural program scheduling
- Compliance with preservation and insurance requirements
