## Scenario: Political and Diplomatic Travel

### Situation
Government officials, diplomats, and political delegations need secure travel arrangements, protocol coordination, and diplomatic accommodations with appropriate security measures.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate with diplomatic services, arrange secure transportation and accommodations, provide protocol support, and ensure compliance with international diplomatic requirements.

### Why this matters
Diplomatic travel involves security protocols, protocol requirements, and international relations considerations that require specialized coordination and discretion.

### Success criteria
- Diplomatic service and protocol coordination
- Secure transportation and accommodation arrangements
- International diplomatic requirement compliance
- Discreet and professional service delivery</content>
