## Scenario: Business Travel Expense Tracking

### Situation
A business traveler needs to track and manage all travel-related expenses for reimbursement, including transportation, accommodations, meals, and incidentals.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should integrate expense tracking into the booking process, provide real-time expense updates, categorize expenses automatically, and generate detailed reports for accounting submission.

### Why this matters
Proper expense tracking ensures compliance with company policies and simplifies the reimbursement process for business travelers.

### Success criteria
- Automatic expense categorization and tracking
- Real-time expense updates during travel
- Integration with company expense management systems
- Comprehensive reimbursement documentation</content>
