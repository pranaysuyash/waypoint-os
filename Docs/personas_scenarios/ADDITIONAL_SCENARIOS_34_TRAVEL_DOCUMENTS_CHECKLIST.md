# Additional Scenario 34: Travel Documents Checklist

**Scenario**: A traveler asks what documents they need before departure, and the system must provide a complete, destination-specific checklist.

---

## Situation

A customer wants reassurance that they have all necessary paperwork for an international trip.
They expect the agency to cover passports, visas, vaccination certificates, insurance, and any destination-specific forms.

## What the system should do

- Provide a clear checklist tailored to the destination and traveler profile
- Include timing guidance for visas and health documents
- Call out any exceptional requirements loudly
- Offer a follow-up reminder if a needed document is missing

## Why this matters

Missing documents can ruin a trip before it even starts.
A robust checklist builds trust and reduces last-minute panic.

## Success criteria

- The checklist is destination-appropriate and complete
- The customer feels confident that nothing critical is overlooked
- The agency demonstrates command of travel requirements
