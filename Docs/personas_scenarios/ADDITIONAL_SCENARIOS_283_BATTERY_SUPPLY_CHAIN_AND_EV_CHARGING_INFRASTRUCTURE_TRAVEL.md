## Scenario: Battery Supply Chain and EV Charging Infrastructure Travel

### Situation
Electric vehicle manufacturers, charging network developers, and battery supply chain teams need travel arrangements for factory audits, charging site inspections, and deployment coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange access to battery plants and charging sites, coordinate equipment logistics, manage engineering team itineraries, and support regulatory and community engagement meetings.

### Why this matters
Electrification travel requires precise coordination across technical teams, infrastructure partners, and local authorities to keep deployment schedules on track.

### Success criteria
- Battery plant and charging site visit coordination
- Equipment and engineer transportation planning
- Regulatory and community engagement support
- Alignment with infrastructure rollout timelines
