## Scenario: Public Relations and Media Press Travel

### Situation
Public relations teams, brand managers, and media spokespeople need travel arrangements for press tours, media interviews, and public appearances.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate press schedules, arrange secure and timely transport, manage media event logistics, and ensure brand presentation readiness.

### Why this matters
PR travel requires precise timing, backup plans for media access, and a polished travel experience to protect brand reputation and maximize coverage.

### Success criteria
- Media tour and press interview coordination
- Secure and punctual transportation planning
- Media event and logistics support
- Support for brand presentation and contingency needs
