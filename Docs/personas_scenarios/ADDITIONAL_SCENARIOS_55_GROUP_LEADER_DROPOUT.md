# Additional Scenario 55: Group Leader Dropout

**Scenario**: The person who organized a group booking cancels, and the system must preserve the group’s itinerary and payment plan during the handoff.

---

## Situation

A group leader drops out mid-booking, and a new coordinator must take over the arrangements.
The agency has to maintain continuity and protect deposits.

## What the system should do

- Transfer the group case with full context and contact list
- Confirm who now has authority for approvals and changes
- Keep deposit and cancellation rules transparent for the new leader
- Minimize repeated questions for the group

## Why this matters

Group leadership changes are disruptive.
A clean handoff prevents booking delays and reduces customer frustration.

## Success criteria

- The system preserves group history and decision context
- The new leader gains control without losing momentum
- The agency prevents any gap that could risk the booking
