# Additional Scenario 89: VIP Complaint Triage

**Scenario**: A VIP customer submits a complaint, and the system must triage it to senior service quickly and resolve it with priority.

---

## Situation

A high-value customer reports an issue during or after their trip.
The agency must escalate the case and deliver a premium resolution path.

## What the system should do

- Recognize the VIP status and urgency immediately
- Route the complaint to senior service or concierge support
- Propose a fast, high-touch resolution plan
- Keep the customer updated with reassurance and next steps

## Why this matters

VIP complaints can be reputationally costly.
A swift, premium response preserves relationships and brand value.

## Success criteria

- The complaint is escalated without delay
- The customer receives a high-touch response and resolution
- The agency reduces churn risk among top clients
