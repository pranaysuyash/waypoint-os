# Additional Scenario 141: Inclusive Dietary Meal Planning

**Scenario**: A traveler has multiple dietary restrictions, and the system must arrange meals that are safe, enjoyable, and culturally appropriate.

---

## Situation

A customer traveling with dietary restrictions such as allergies, religious requirements, or medical diets wants meals planned throughout their trip.
They expect the agency to coordinate with suppliers and avoid last-minute menu issues.

## What the system should do

- Collect and confirm dietary needs early in the planning process
- Coordinate with hotels, restaurants, and activity providers to ensure compliant meals
- Provide clear instructions for the traveler and suppliers about restrictions
- Suggest suitable local dining options and alternatives

## Why this matters

Dietary restrictions can cause serious health issues and ruin a travel experience.
A proactive meal plan protects health and preserves the trip’s enjoyment.

## Success criteria

- The traveler receives safe, tailored meals throughout their journey
- The agency avoids dietary miscommunication with suppliers
- The customer can travel without worrying about food-related issues
