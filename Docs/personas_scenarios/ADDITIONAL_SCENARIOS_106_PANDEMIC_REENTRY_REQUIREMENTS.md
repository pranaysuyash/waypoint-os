# Additional Scenario 106: Pandemic Reentry Requirements

**Scenario**: A traveler must comply with changing reentry requirements for their home country, and the system must provide up-to-date testing, quarantine, and documentation guidance.

---

## Situation

A customer is returning home from an international trip and needs to know what is required to reenter their country safely.
They expect the agency to provide clear, current guidance on testing, vaccine proof, and quarantine rules.

## What the system should do

- Verify current reentry rules for the traveler’s home country
- Explain required tests, vaccine documentation, and timing
- Advise on reliable local testing or certification options
- Highlight any risks of non-compliance or late changes

## Why this matters

Reentry rules can change rapidly and invalidate travel plans.
A precise plan avoids airport denial and costly last-minute changes.

## Success criteria

- The system provides accurate, up-to-date reentry guidance
- The customer understands which documents and tests are required
- The agency helps the traveler comply without unnecessary stress
