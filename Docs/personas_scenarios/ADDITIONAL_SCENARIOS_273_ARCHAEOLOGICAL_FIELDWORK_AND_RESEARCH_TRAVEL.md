## Scenario: Archaeological Fieldwork and Research Travel

### Situation
Archaeologists, field researchers, and heritage teams need travel arrangements for excavation sites, research camps, and artifact documentation.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate field site access, arrange equipment-friendly transportation, manage accommodation near dig sites, and support research team logistics.

### Why this matters
Archaeological travel requires careful handling of fragile sites, remote logistics, and collaboration with local heritage authorities.

### Success criteria
- Field site access and local heritage coordination
- Equipment transport and remote accommodation planning
- Support for research team collaboration and documentation
- Protection of fragile archaeological sites
