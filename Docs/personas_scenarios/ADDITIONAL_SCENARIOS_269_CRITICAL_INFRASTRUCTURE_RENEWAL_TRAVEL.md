## Scenario: Critical Infrastructure Renewal Travel

### Situation
Infrastructure renewal teams, utility planners, and project managers need travel arrangements for inspection, planning, and coordination trips for critical infrastructure upgrades.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate site access, arrange travel to critical infrastructure locations, support cross-disciplinary team itineraries, and manage safety and compliance logistics.

### Why this matters
Critical infrastructure travel must balance operational continuity, regulatory compliance, and multi-stakeholder coordination to avoid service disruptions.

### Success criteria
- Site inspection and coordination planning
- Cross-disciplinary team travel and lodging support
- Safety and compliance logistics management
- Minimal disruption to infrastructure operations
