# Additional Scenario 129: Hotel Loyalty Benefit Matching

**Scenario**: A traveler wants to maximize hotel loyalty benefits, and the system must match hotel options to their elite perks, upgrades, and recognition programs.

---

## Situation

A customer has elite status with certain hotel groups and expects bookings to preserve benefits like upgrades, breakfast, and late checkout.
They need the agency to select properties that honor their loyalty level.

## What the system should do

- Identify hotels that participate in the traveler’s loyalty program
- Prioritize properties with available elite perks and recognition
- Explain whether the selected booking preserves status benefits
- Provide alternatives when a loyalty-qualified property is unavailable

## Why this matters

Loyalty travelers value recognition and perks.
A mismatch between booking and status benefits leads to disappointment and loyalty erosion.

## Success criteria

- The selected hotel maximizes the traveler’s elite loyalty benefits
- The customer understands any benefit trade-offs or gaps
- The agency maintains loyalty program expectations rather than treating them as optional
