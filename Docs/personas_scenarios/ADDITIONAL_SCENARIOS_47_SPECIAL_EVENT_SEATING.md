# Additional Scenario 47: Special Event Seating

**Scenario**: A customer needs reservations for a major event, and the system must secure tickets and travel together while managing demand risk.

---

## Situation

A traveler wants a package around a sold-out concert, festival, or sporting event.
They expect the agency to coordinate venue seating, hotel, and transfers without losing the event window.

## What the system should do

- Verify event dates and ticket availability before confirming travel
- Recommend event-adjacent lodging and transport options
- Show the risk if event tickets are limited or secondary-market only
- Keep the package focused on making the event experience reliable

## Why this matters

Event-driven travel is highly time-sensitive.
If the event element fails, the entire trip loses its reason.

## Success criteria

- The system secures event logistics before locking in the trip
- It communicates any event-specific premium or scarcity clearly
- The traveler feels confident the event is the center of the package
