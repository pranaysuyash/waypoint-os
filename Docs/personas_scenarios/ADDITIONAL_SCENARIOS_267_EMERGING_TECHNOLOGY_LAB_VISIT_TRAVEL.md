## Scenario: Emerging Technology Lab Visit Travel

### Situation
Technology researchers, lab directors, and innovation teams need travel arrangements for lab visits, prototype demonstrations, and research partner meetings.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate lab access, arrange secure transportation, manage prototype logistics, and support research collaboration schedules.

### Why this matters
Emerging technology travel demands confidentiality, safety, and equipment-ready logistics to ensure research visits are productive and secure.

### Success criteria
- Lab visit and demonstration coordination
- Secure travel and logistics planning
- Prototype transport and access support
- Alignment with research partner schedules
