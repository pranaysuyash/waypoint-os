## Scenario: Advanced Manufacturing and 3D Printing Travel

### Situation
Manufacturing innovators, additive manufacturing teams, and production engineers need travel arrangements for facility visits, pilot builds, and technology demonstrations.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate manufacturing site access, arrange transport for prototypes and equipment, manage demo logistics, and support engineering collaboration schedules.

### Why this matters
Advanced manufacturing travel demands precision, equipment readiness, and collaboration with production teams to validate new manufacturing technologies.

### Success criteria
- Manufacturing site access and pilot build coordination
- Prototype and equipment transport logistics planning
- Demo and engineering collaboration scheduling support
- Alignment with manufacturing technology validation timelines
