# Additional Scenario 62: Custom Dietary Menu Request

**Scenario**: A traveler requests a special dietary menu for meals, and the system must ensure restaurants or hotels can accommodate without compromising the itinerary.

---

## Situation

A customer has a food allergy or dietary preference and needs meal plans that comply with it.
They expect the agency to secure appropriate dining options, not just note the request.

## What the system should do

- Capture the dietary requirement precisely
- Confirm vendor capability to provide compliant meals
- Offer alternative dining options if the preferred restaurants cannot comply
- Highlight any additional costs or limitations

## Why this matters

Food requirements can be a health or cultural issue.
A mistake here can ruin the trip and damage trust.

## Success criteria

- The itinerary includes verified dietary accommodations
- The customer understands exactly what is covered
- The agency avoids last-minute meal disruptions
