# Additional Scenario 117: Travel Budget Flexibility Scenario

**Scenario**: A customer wants to keep the trip within a flexible budget range, and the system must suggest options that trade quality and features incrementally.

---

## Situation

A traveler provides a budget range rather than a fixed amount.
They expect the agency to show tiered options and the impact of spending more or less.

## What the system should do

- Create budget sensitivities for key trip components
- Show what is possible at low, mid, and premium ends of the range
- Explain which features or comforts change with budget moves
- Keep the pricing transparent and choice-driven

## Why this matters

Budget flexibility is common and needs structured guidance.
A good agency helps customers choose intentionally rather than guessing.

## Success criteria

- The system presents clear budget tiers and trade-offs
- The customer feels empowered to choose the right level
- The agency avoids overpromising on the low end and upselling needlessly
