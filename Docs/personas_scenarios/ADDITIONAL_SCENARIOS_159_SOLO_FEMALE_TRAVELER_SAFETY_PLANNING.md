## Scenario: Solo Female Traveler Safety Planning

### Situation
A woman traveling alone needs comprehensive safety planning, including secure accommodations, transportation safety, and emergency support systems.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should recommend women-friendly accommodations and neighborhoods, arrange safe transportation options, provide safety briefing and emergency contacts, and coordinate with local safety networks.

### Why this matters
Solo female travelers face unique safety considerations that require proactive planning and reliable support systems to ensure confidence and security throughout the journey.

### Success criteria
- Women-friendly accommodation and neighborhood recommendations
- Safe transportation arrangements with tracking capabilities
- Comprehensive safety briefing and emergency contact systems
- Coordination with local women's safety networks and embassies</content>
