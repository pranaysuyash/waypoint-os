## Scenario: International Education Conference and Faculty Exchange Travel

### Situation
University academics, faculty exchange directors, and education conference organizers need travel arrangements for international conferences, visiting professor stays, and academic collaborations.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate conference registration, arrange academic visas, manage faculty accommodation, and support collaborative meeting schedules.

### Why this matters
Academic travel requires attention to visa timing, teaching commitments, and collaborative event coordination to support research and education outcomes.

### Success criteria
- Conference and faculty exchange coordination
- Academic visa and accommodation planning
- Collaboration schedule support for visiting scholars
- Alignment with teaching and research commitments
