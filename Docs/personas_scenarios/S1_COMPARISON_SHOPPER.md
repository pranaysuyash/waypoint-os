# S1-S1: The Comparison Shopper

**Persona**: Individual Traveler
**Scenario**: A customer wants to compare multiple travel options before deciding, and the agency must make the comparison easy and fair.

---

## Situation

A customer is shopping around and wants to see several options side by side.
They are not ready to commit yet and expect the agency to help them choose.

### Customer request
- Destination: Thailand or Bali
- Duration: 9-10 days
- Party: 2 adults
- Preferences: beach, relaxation, good food
- Priority: value for money

### Customer state
- Wants to compare options before deciding
- Will likely ask the agent to explain why one choice is better
- Expects a clean, easy-to-understand comparison

## What the system should do

1. **Create a side-by-side comparison**
   - Option 1: Thailand beach resort
   - Option 2: Bali villa stay
   - Option 3: Hybrid city + beach split

2. **Highlight the tradeoffs**
   - Cost, travel time, relaxation vibe, food quality, ease of access
   - Which option best fits a value-minded couple

3. **Make the decision easy**
   - Provide a short recommendation based on priorities
   - If the customer wants to save money, recommend the lowest-cost yet satisfying option

4. **Support follow-up**
   - Allow the agent to save the comparison and return to it
   - Avoid requiring the customer to remember details from multiple conversations

## Required system behaviors

- Comparison generation with clear tradeoffs
- Value-oriented recommendation
- Easy-to-read customer-facing summary
- Support for follow-up and revision
- Avoidance of overwhelming the customer

## Why this matters

Comparison shoppers want clarity, not confusion.
This scenario proves the agency can earn the business by making options easier to choose.

## Success criteria

- The customer receives a compact comparison of options.
- The agency highlights the best fit for the customer’s priorities.
- The customer can choose without needing multiple rounds of explanation.
- The agent can keep the comparison organized for follow-up.
