# Additional Scenario 116: Data Privacy Encryption Request

**Scenario**: A traveler asks how their sensitive data is protected during bookings, and the system must explain encryption and storage safeguards.

---

## Situation

A customer is concerned about the privacy of passport details, payment data, and travel preferences.
They expect the agency to clearly explain how their data is safeguarded.

## What the system should do

- Describe encryption and secure handling of personal data
- Explain which data is stored, how long, and who can access it
- Provide details on vendor data-sharing protections
- Reassure the customer without oversimplifying security practices

## Why this matters

Sensitive travel data demands high trust.
Clear privacy communication reduces customer anxiety and legal risk.

## Success criteria

- The system provides a clear, accurate privacy explanation
- The customer understands data protection and consent
- The agency demonstrates responsible handling of sensitive travel data
