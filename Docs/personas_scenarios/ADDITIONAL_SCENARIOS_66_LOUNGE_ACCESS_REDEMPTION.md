# Additional Scenario 66: Lounge Access Redemption

**Scenario**: A customer wants to redeem lounge access or important airport amenities, and the system must confirm eligibility and booking logistics.

---

## Situation

A traveler has premium status or lounge passes and expects the agency to include lounge access in their travel plan.
They want to know whether the access is valid for the airline, airport, and date.

## What the system should do

- Verify lounge eligibility based on the customer’s ticket class, status, or pass
- Confirm the correct airport lounges and access rules
- Explain how to use the pass at each airport, including guest rules
- Alert the customer if a lounge is unavailable and offer alternatives

## Why this matters

Lounge access is a valued premium benefit.
Getting it wrong can sour a luxury travel experience immediately.

## Success criteria

- The system confirms lounge access accurately
- The customer receives clear guidance for arrival
- The agency provides a fallback plan when needed
