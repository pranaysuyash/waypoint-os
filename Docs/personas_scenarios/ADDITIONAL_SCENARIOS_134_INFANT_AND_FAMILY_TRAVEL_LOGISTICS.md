# Additional Scenario 134: Infant and Family Travel Logistics

**Scenario**: A family traveling with infants requires logistics support for gear, seating, and comfort, and the system must plan accordingly.

---

## Situation

A customer is traveling with infants or very young children and needs help managing strollers, car seats, seating arrangements, and family-friendly accommodations.
They expect the agency to plan reliable, low-stress logistics.

## What the system should do

- Confirm childcare-friendly accommodations and seating
- Arrange for necessary infant gear, transport seats, and priority boarding when available
- Advise on family-friendly check-in, security, and transfer procedures
- Offer backup support if a family travel item is delayed or unavailable

## Why this matters

Family travel with infants is especially sensitive to timing and comfort.
A strong plan reduces stress and makes the experience manageable for parents.

## Success criteria

- The family has a comfortable, support-rich itinerary
- The agency anticipates infant gear and seating needs
- The trip avoids unnecessary family travel friction points
