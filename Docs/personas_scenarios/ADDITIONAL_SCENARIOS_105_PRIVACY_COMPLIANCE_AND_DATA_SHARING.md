# Additional Scenario 105: Privacy Compliance and Data Sharing

**Scenario**: A customer asks how their personal travel data is shared, and the system must explain privacy controls, vendor sharing, and compliance.

---

## Situation

A traveler wants to know how their trip data, passport details, and preferences are handled across vendors.
They expect clear privacy boundaries and compliant data sharing.

## What the system should do

- Explain which data is shared with which suppliers and why
- Highlight privacy protections, consent steps, and data retention policies
- Offer the customer control over sensitive data where possible
- Ensure compliance with GDPR, PDPA, or local privacy standards

## Why this matters

Travel planning involves highly sensitive personal data.
Transparent privacy handling builds trust and reduces regulatory risk.

## Success criteria

- The system provides a clear privacy and data-sharing explanation
- The customer understands consent and control options
- The agency demonstrates responsible data handling practices
