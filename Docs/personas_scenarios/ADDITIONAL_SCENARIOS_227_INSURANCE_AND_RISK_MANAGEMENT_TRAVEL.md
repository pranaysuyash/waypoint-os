### Situation
Insurance professionals and risk management specialists need travel arrangements for insurance conferences, risk assessment sites, and industry events with technical access and coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange insurance conference and risk assessment site access, coordinate industry event attendance, provide technical transportation and logistics, and ensure insurance travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Insurance travel involves specialized knowledge of risk management protocols, insurance safety, and site access that requires expertise in insurance industry tourism.

### Success criteria
- Insurance conference and risk assessment site access coordination
- Industry event and conference attendance arrangements
- Technical transportation and insurance logistics support
- Insurance travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
