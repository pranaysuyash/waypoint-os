# Additional Scenario 142: Remote Conference Attendance Support

**Scenario**: A traveler is attending a remote conference or hybrid event, and the system must ensure remote access, equipment, and venue support.

---

## Situation

A customer participates in a conference or event where some sessions are virtual or hybrid.
They need support for reliable connectivity, access passes, and on-site help when needed.

## What the system should do

- Confirm conference access requirements and available virtual platforms
- Secure appropriate workspace, internet, and device support at the destination
- Arrange passes, credentials, and any event-specific registration details
- Provide contingency plans for connection or access failures

## Why this matters

Hybrid conferences require both physical and virtual coordination.
A strong plan prevents missed sessions and reduces technology friction.

## Success criteria

- The traveler can attend hybrid conference sessions seamlessly
- The agency provides all event access and remote-work logistics
- The customer avoids missed sessions due to connectivity or access issues
