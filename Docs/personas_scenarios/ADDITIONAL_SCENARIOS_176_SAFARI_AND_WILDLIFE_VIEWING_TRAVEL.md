## Scenario: Safari and Wildlife Viewing Travel

### Situation
Wildlife enthusiasts need guided safari experiences with accommodation in game reserves, transportation in specialized vehicles, and expert guides for optimal animal viewing.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange safari lodge bookings, coordinate guided game drives, provide photography equipment recommendations, and ensure responsible wildlife tourism practices.

### Why this matters
Safari travel requires specialized knowledge of wildlife behavior, seasonal migration patterns, and responsible tourism practices to ensure meaningful wildlife encounters.

### Success criteria
- Safari lodge and game reserve accommodation booking
- Guided game drive and wildlife viewing coordination
- Photography and equipment recommendations
- Responsible wildlife tourism education and practices</content>
