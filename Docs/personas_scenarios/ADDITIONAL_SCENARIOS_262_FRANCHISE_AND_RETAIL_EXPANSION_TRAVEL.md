## Scenario: Franchise and Retail Expansion Travel

### Situation
Retail executives, franchise operators, and expansion teams need travel arrangements for new store openings, site inspections, and rollout training.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate site visits, arrange team travel for opening events, manage retail rollout logistics, and support local permitting and supplier meetings.

### Why this matters
Retail expansion travel needs precise timing, cross-functional coordination, and on-the-ground support to synchronize brand, operations, and supply chain readiness.

### Success criteria
- Store opening and site visit coordination
- Team travel planning for launch and training
- Retail rollout logistics support
- Local permitting and supplier meeting alignment
