Industry professionals and manufacturing specialists need travel arrangements for factory tours, industrial exhibitions, and manufacturing facility visits with technical access and equipment coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange factory tour and facility access coordination, coordinate industrial exhibition attendance, provide technical transportation and logistics, and ensure manufacturing travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Industrial travel involves specialized knowledge of manufacturing processes, safety protocols, and technical access that requires expertise in industrial tourism coordination.

- Factory tour and facility access coordination
- Industrial exhibition and trade show attendance arrangements
- Technical transportation and equipment logistics support
