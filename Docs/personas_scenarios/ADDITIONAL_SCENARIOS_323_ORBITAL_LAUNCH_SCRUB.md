# Additional Scenario 323: Orbital Launch Window Postponement

**Scenario**: A traveler's sub-orbital "Space Experience" is postponed due to solar radiation or weather. The system must manage the hyper-complex ground logistics (security, high-end suites, support teams) for a "T-minus indefinite" wait.

---

## Situation

- Traveler (S1) is at a Spaceport (e.g., Spaceport America) for a 3-day launch window.
- At T-minus 4 hours, the launch is "Scrubbed" for at least 48 hours.
- The traveler's support team (family, media, medical) and VVIP suites must be extended or moved.

## What the system should do

- Detect the "Launch Scrub" alert from the Space Carrier's API.
- Automatically extend the "Spaceport Suites" or find alternative luxury villas in the "Recovery Zone."
- Coordinate with the "Psychological Itinerary" engine to suggest "High-Adrenaline Distractions" for the frustrated traveler.
- Re-calculate the "Solar Radiation Risk" for the new window and update the traveler's "Biometric Wellness" profile.
- Notify the traveler's global support network (media/insurance).

## Why this matters

Space travel has 0% predictability on timing.
Handling "Launch Volatility" is the highest tier of frontier logistics.

## Success criteria

- All support staff and suites are extended within 30 minutes of the scrub.
- The traveler feels "Cared for" during the high-stress delay.
- Zero "Logistical Friction" for the Space Carrier's staff.
