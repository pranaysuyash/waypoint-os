# Additional Scenario 92: Child-Friendly Activities Scoring

**Scenario**: A family wants age-appropriate activities, and the system must score and recommend options for kids, tweens, and teens.

---

## Situation

A family is planning a trip with children of different ages.
They need the agency to suggest activities that are engaging, safe, and suitable for every child.

## What the system should do

- Score activities for age appropriateness, safety, and engagement
- Recommend options that work for multiple age groups or offer alternatives
- Capture any child-specific needs like stroller access or dietary restrictions
- Communicate why each activity is a good fit for the family

## Why this matters

Child-friendly planning is more than picking “family fun.”
A good plan keeps kids engaged and parents relaxed.

## Success criteria

- The system recommends appropriate activities for each age group
- The family feels the itinerary is balanced and manageable
- The agency provides clarity on what makes activities child-friendly
