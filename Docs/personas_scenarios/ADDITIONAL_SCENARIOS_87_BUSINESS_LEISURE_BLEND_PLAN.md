# Additional Scenario 87: Business + Leisure Blend Plan

**Scenario**: A traveler combines business and leisure, and the system must integrate meetings with downtime without overloading the schedule.

---

## Situation

A customer is on a bleisure trip and needs both professional meeting support and leisure experiences.
They expect the agency to balance productivity and relaxation.

## What the system should do

- Confirm business requirements such as meeting locations, tech needs, and timing
- Build leisure activities around the business schedule
- Recommend convenient lodging with workspace and evening activities
- Avoid overscheduling the traveler on days with business commitments

## Why this matters

Bleisure travel needs planning that respects both purposes.
A poor blend can leave the traveler drained or unproductive.

## Success criteria

- The itinerary supports both work and leisure seamlessly
- The traveler has dedicated time for business responsibilities
- The leisure plan feels natural and not forced around meetings
