## Scenario: Virtual Concert and Digital Entertainment Production Travel

### Situation
Live entertainment producers, virtual concert technologists, and digital event teams need travel arrangements for venue scouting, production rehearsals, and digital performance coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange venue access, coordinate technical crew travel, manage rehearsal and broadcast logistics, and support media and sponsor meetings.

### Why this matters
Virtual entertainment travel requires synchronization of physical and digital production workflows to deliver immersive experiences without downtime.

### Success criteria
- Venue and virtual production coordination
- Technical crew and media travel logistics support
- Rehearsal and broadcast schedule planning
- Smooth execution of digital entertainment experiences
