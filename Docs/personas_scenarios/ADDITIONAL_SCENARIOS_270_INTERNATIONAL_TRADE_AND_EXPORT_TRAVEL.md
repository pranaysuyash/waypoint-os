## Scenario: International Trade and Export Travel

### Situation
Export managers, trade compliance teams, and international sales reps need travel arrangements for trade shows, customs planning, and export partner meetings.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate trade event access, support customs and export documentation logistics, arrange transportation for samples and goods, and manage partner meeting schedules.

### Why this matters
Trade and export travel demands compliance with cross-border regulations, reliable logistics, and precise scheduling for successful international market entry.

### Success criteria
- Trade show and partner meeting coordination
- Customs and export documentation support
- Sample and goods transport logistics
- Reliable scheduling for cross-border business travel
