## Scenario: Agile Software Development Travel

### Situation
Software development teams, product managers, and engineering leaders need travel arrangements for sprint planning workshops, agile conferences, and remote collaboration sessions.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate team travel to collaborative workspaces, schedule agile training events, manage flexible accommodations, and support rapid planning cycles.

### Why this matters
Agile development travel requires coordination around changing schedules, team alignment sessions, and collaboration-ready environments to keep delivery momentum.

### Success criteria
- Agile workshop and planning session coordination
- Team travel and collaboration space booking
- Flexible accommodation support
- Alignment with sprint goals and remote work needs
