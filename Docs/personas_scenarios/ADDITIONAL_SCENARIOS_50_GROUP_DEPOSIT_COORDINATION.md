# Additional Scenario 50: Group Deposit Coordination

**Scenario**: A large group needs coordinated deposits, and the system must manage multiple payers, deadlines, and refund terms.

---

## Situation

A group trip requires separate deposits from multiple participants.
The agent must keep track of who has paid, what remains due, and the cancellation rules per person.

## What the system should do

- Track deposit status for each group member
- Show the overall plan status and individual payment deadlines
- Capture refund/cancellation terms by participant
- Alert the agent to gaps or late payers before the group is at risk

## Why this matters

Group bookings are complex by nature.
Poor coordination leads to missing deposits, broken hold times, and unhappy leaders.

## Success criteria

- The system gives a clean group deposit dashboard
- It prevents missed deadlines through proactive alerts
- The agent can manage the group as one cohesive booking
