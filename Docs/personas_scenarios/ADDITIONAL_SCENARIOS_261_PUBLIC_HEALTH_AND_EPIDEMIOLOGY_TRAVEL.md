## Scenario: Public Health and Epidemiology Travel

### Situation
Public health experts, epidemiologists, and global health teams need travel arrangements for outbreak investigations, health summits, and emergency response coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate access to medical sites, arrange secure transportation, support health authority meetings, and manage quarantine-aware logistics.

### Why this matters
Public health travel requires rapid response, infection control, and sensitive coordination with local authorities to protect communities and teams.

### Success criteria
- Health investigation and summit access coordination
- Secure, infection-aware transportation planning
- Local authority and emergency response support
- Compliance with public health protocols and quarantine rules
