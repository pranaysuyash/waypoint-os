# Additional Scenario 40: Agent Handoff Readiness

**Scenario**: A senior agent hands off a long-running case to a junior agent, and the system must preserve context and customer trust.

---

## Situation

A senior agent transitions a complex trip to a junior colleague mid-booking.
The customer should feel continuity rather than abandonment.

## What the system should do

- Capture a concise handoff brief describing status, pending actions, and decision history
- Notify the customer of the handoff with reassurance
- Preserve key notes, customer preferences, and vendor commitments
- Ensure the junior agent can pick up the case without repeated questions

## Why this matters

Handovers are high-risk moments for customer experience.
Well-managed handoffs preserve trust and keep production moving.

## Success criteria

- The handoff brief is complete and actionable
- The customer is informed proactively and positively
- The incoming agent has enough context to continue without friction
