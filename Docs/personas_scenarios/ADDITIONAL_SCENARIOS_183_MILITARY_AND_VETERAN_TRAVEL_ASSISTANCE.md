## Scenario: Military and Veteran Travel Assistance

### Situation
Active duty military personnel, veterans, and their families need travel arrangements utilizing military benefits, space-available flights, and veteran-specific accommodations.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate Space-A flight reservations, arrange military lodging and benefits, provide veteran service organization support, and assist with military family travel requirements.

### Why this matters
Military travel involves unique benefits, restrictions, and support services that require specialized knowledge of military travel systems and veteran resources.

### Success criteria
- Space-A flight and military transportation coordination
- Military lodging and benefit utilization
- Veteran service organization support and resources
- Military family travel requirement assistance</content>
