# Area Deep Dive: Sustainable & Regenerative Travel

**Domain**: Ethical & Environmental Stewardship  
**Focus**: Carbon transparency, community impact, and "Green" supplier vetting.

---

## 1. Carbon Metrics & Offsetting

Travel is inherently carbon-intensive. The system must provide "Visibility" and "Actionability" for emissions.

### Carbon Footprint Calculation
- **Logic**: Calculating KG of CO2 based on flight class, distance, and hotel sustainability ratings.
- **System Action**: Surfacing "Lower Carbon" alternatives in the NB02 decision state (e.g., "Direct flight vs 1-stop" or "Train vs Short-haul flight").

### Offsetting Strategies
- **Integration**: Partnering with vetted offset providers (e.g., Patch, Squake) to allow travelers to pay for "Certified Offsets" at checkout.
- **System Action**: Automating the purchase of offsets and issuing a "Sustainability Certificate" in Phase 4 (Post-Trip).

---

## 2. Ethical Supplier Vetting

Moving beyond "Greenwashing" to real impact.

### "Impact" Ratings
- **Criteria**: Does the hotel use local labor? Do they eliminate single-use plastics? Do they support local conservation?
- **System Action**: Tagging suppliers as "Impact+ Verified" in the domain knowledge base.

### Community-Led Tourism
- **Focus**: Promoting tours and experiences that are owned and operated by local communities (Regenerative Travel).
- **System Action**: Proactively suggesting "Local Impact" activities in NB01/NB02 fact extraction.

---

## 3. Corporate CSR Compliance

### Corporate Sustainability Goals
- **Requirement**: Many companies now have "Carbon Budgets" alongside financial budgets.
- **System Action**: Flagging bookings that exceed the company's carbon-per-km limit.

### Reporting
- **Output**: Generating "Quarterly Sustainability Audits" for the Agency Owner (P2) to share with corporate clients.
