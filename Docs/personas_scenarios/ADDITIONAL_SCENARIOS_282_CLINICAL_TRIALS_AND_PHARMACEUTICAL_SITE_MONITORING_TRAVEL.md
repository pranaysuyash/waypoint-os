## Scenario: Clinical Trials and Pharmaceutical Site Monitoring Travel

### Situation
Clinical research associates, trial monitors, and pharmaceutical quality teams need travel arrangements for site visits, patient safety reviews, and regulatory audits.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate hospital and trial site access, arrange secure transportation, support documentation review schedules, and manage investigator meeting logistics.

### Why this matters
Clinical trial travel demands precise timing, compliance with health regulations, and coordination with sensitive research staff to protect trial integrity.

### Success criteria
- Trial site visit and monitoring coordination
- Secure transport and documentation logistics support
- Compliance-aware scheduling for regulatory review
- Smooth collaboration between research teams and sites
