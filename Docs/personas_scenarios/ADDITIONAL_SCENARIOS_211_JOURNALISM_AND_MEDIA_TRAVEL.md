## Scenario: Journalism and Media Travel

### Situation
Journalists and media professionals need travel arrangements for press events, media conferences, and reporting assignments with press credentials and media access coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange press credential and media access coordination, coordinate press event and conference attendance, provide media transportation and logistics, and ensure journalism travel experience support.

### Why this matters
Media travel involves specialized knowledge of press access, credential requirements, and media industry coordination that requires expertise in journalism tourism.

### Success criteria
- Press credential and media access coordination
- Press event and conference attendance arrangements
- Media transportation and logistics support
- Journalism travel experience and professional enhancement</content>
