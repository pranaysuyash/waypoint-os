# Additional Scenario 45: Eco-Conscious Traveler Request

**Scenario**: An environmentally conscious customer wants a low-impact trip, and the system must offer green options without making it feel like a compromise.

---

## Situation

A customer asks for sustainable travel choices, including hotels with green certifications, low-carbon transport, and responsible experiences.
They expect the agency to offer meaningful eco alternatives, not token gestures.

## What the system should do

- Identify certified eco-friendly accommodations and activities
- Explain the sustainability benefits in clear terms
- Preserve the trip experience while minimizing environmental impact
- Avoid greenwashing by being specific and honest

## Why this matters

Eco-conscious travel is increasingly important for many customers.
Delivering real options builds credibility and supports long-term loyalty.

## Success criteria

- The system provides verifiable sustainable choices
- The customer understands the tradeoffs and benefits
- The itinerary feels premium and responsible, not watered down
