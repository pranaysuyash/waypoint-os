## Scenario: Retail Innovation Lab and Popup Store Travel

### Situation
Retail innovation teams, merchandising specialists, and popup store operators need travel arrangements for innovation lab visits, popup event setup, and customer experience trials.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate showroom access, arrange logistics for temporary retail activations, manage supplier visits, and support customer experience trials.

### Why this matters
Retail innovation travel requires fast-moving logistics, temporary space planning, and close alignment with consumer marketing calendars.

### Success criteria
- Showroom and popup activation coordination
- Temporary retail logistics and supplier schedule support
- Customer experience trial planning
- Alignment with marketing and innovation timelines
