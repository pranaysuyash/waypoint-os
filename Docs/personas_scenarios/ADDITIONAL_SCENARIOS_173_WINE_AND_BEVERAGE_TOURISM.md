## Scenario: Wine and Beverage Tourism

### Situation
Wine and beverage enthusiasts seek tours of vineyards, breweries, and distilleries with tastings, accommodations, and transportation between locations.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should arrange guided tours of beverage production facilities, coordinate transportation between venues, book winery accommodations, and provide designated driver services for tasting tours.

### Why this matters
Beverage tourism involves responsible consumption coordination, transportation logistics between multiple venues, and specialized knowledge of production processes and regional specialties.

### Success criteria
- Guided tour arrangements at production facilities
- Transportation coordination between beverage venues
- Winery or brewery accommodation bookings
- Designated driver and responsible consumption services</content>
