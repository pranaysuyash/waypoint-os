# Additional Scenario 72: Festival Power Outage Plan

**Scenario**: A customer attends a festival where power outages are possible, and the system must provide practical backup and comfort options.

---

## Situation

A traveler books a festival or event in a location known for unpredictable power.
They need the agency to help them stay comfortable and safe if utilities fail.

## What the system should do

- Advise on festival-specific risks like power outages and cell coverage
- Recommend backup options such as battery packs, quieter accommodations, or mobile hotspots
- Explain how the itinerary can adapt if the event experiences interruptions
- Keep the focus on maintaining a positive experience despite the risk

## Why this matters

Festivals are high-emotion trips, and infrastructure issues can ruin the experience.
Smart prep turns a potential problem into a manageable detail.

## Success criteria

- The system flags infrastructure risks proactively
- The traveler receives practical backup suggestions
- The agency reduces the risk of disappointment during the event
