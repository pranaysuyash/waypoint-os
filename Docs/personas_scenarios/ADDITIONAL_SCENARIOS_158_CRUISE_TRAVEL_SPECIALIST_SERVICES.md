## Scenario: Cruise Travel Specialist Services

### Situation
Cruise passengers need comprehensive pre- and post-cruise arrangements, including airport transfers, shore excursions, and onboard experience optimization.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate airport-to-port transfers, arrange pre-cruise hotel stays, book shore excursions and onboard experiences, and provide cruise line expertise and troubleshooting.

### Why this matters
Cruise travel involves multiple transportation modes and complex logistics that require specialized knowledge to ensure smooth transitions and optimal experiences.

### Success criteria
- Seamless airport-to-port transportation coordination
- Pre- and post-cruise accommodation arrangements
- Shore excursion and onboard experience bookings
- Cruise line policy expertise and issue resolution</content>
