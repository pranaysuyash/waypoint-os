## Scenario: Government Contractor Compliance Travel

### Situation
Government contractors, compliance teams, and program managers need travel arrangements for contract site visits, audit preparation, and classified program coordination.

### What the system should do
The travel agent should coordinate secure travel, manage clearance-aware itineraries, support audit and compliance meetings, and ensure documentation is in place for government requirements.

### Why this matters
Government contractor travel requires strict adherence to security, compliance, and contractual terms to maintain program integrity and avoid penalties.

### Success criteria
- Secure, clearance-aware travel coordination
- Audit and compliance meeting logistics support
- Documentation and contractual requirement planning
- Smooth coordination for government program activities
