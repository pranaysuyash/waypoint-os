# Creator Research Gap List

This document lists the creator/app-related gaps identified in the current travel industry taxonomy.

## Identified research gaps

1. Creator travel business model framing
   - Creator-as-supplier vs creator-as-distribution-channel
   - Creator-led travel company economics vs traditional travel agency
   - Travel product-market fit for creator-driven experiences

2. Creator reputation, trust, and brand safety
   - Creator reputation risk operations
   - Rating/trust systems for creator-led experiences
   - Crisis response for social backlash or content controversies

3. Creator labor and legal compliance
   - Paid creator travel appearances and local work permits
   - Influencer labor classification across borders
   - Content production labor law vs travel services

4. Platform architecture for creator apps
   - Creator travel platform system design
   - Integrated creator signal + inventory + loyalty stack
   - Multi-tenant creator marketplace and API orchestration

5. Attribution, measurement, and monetization
   - Creator conversion and marketing attribution for travel bookings
   - Multi-touch creator attribution in omnichannel travel commerce
   - Creator ROI models linking content and travel revenue

6. Creator supply and destination curation
   - Creator-owned / creator-curated destination inventory as supplier
   - Packaging, ranking, sourcing of creator-curated experiences
   - Governance differences for creator-curated supply

7. Experience orchestration
   - Creator content production logistics, set permits, local crew coordination
   - Sequencing pre-trip, on-trip, post-trip creator experience flows
   - Creator production support as an operational discipline

8. Data ethics beyond privacy
   - Influence transparency and dark-pattern detection
   - Ethics of creator-driven recommendation systems
   - Disclosure requirements for paid creator content inside apps

9. Country-specific regulatory and compliance frameworks
   - Country/region visa, transit, and stopover rules
   - Local influencer/ad disclosure and creator event regulations
   - Cross-border data, tax, insurance, and labor variations

10. Layover/stopover content and partner strategy

- Layover content as a GTM wedge for consumers and vendors
- Service packaging for stopovers, transfers, and short-stay experiences
- Partner enablement for lounges, transfers, local tours, and airport services

11. SEO, content, and ranking research for creator travel

- High-intent keyword clusters for layover and creator travel
- Multi-audience content for end users, vendors, agents, and creators
- Country-level content that combines compliance and experience guidance

12. Multi-audience taxonomy for content and product strategy

- User-related, market-related, customer-related, vendor-related, and agent-related angles
- Tech-related, feature-related, scenario-related, legal, docs, comms, services, booking, agent touchpoints
- Audience-specific pages for search, acquisition, partner enablement, and trust
