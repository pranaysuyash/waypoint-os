# Creator Engagement Automation and Notification Workflows

This document covers automation and notification workflows for creator-driven travel engagement.

## Overview

- Creator products benefit from automated engagement that keeps travelers connected, informed, and excited.
- Notifications must be timely, personalized, and aligned with creator voice and campaign cadence.
- Automation helps scale creator programs without overwhelming support teams.

## Engagement Automation

- Triggered campaigns: send messages based on booking milestones, pre-trip readiness, and compound creator events.
- Personalized sequences: tailor communications to traveler preferences, creator affinities, and destination interests.
- Reminder flows: automate pre-trip reminders, event alerts, and on-site logistics updates.
- Post-trip reactivation: re-engage travelers with creator follow-up offers, loyalty prompts, and feedback requests.

## Notification Workflows

- Channel mix: use email, app push, SMS, and creator-owned channels where appropriate.
- Creator alignment: coordinate platform notifications with creator announcements and social activations.
- Consent layering: respect traveler communication preferences for creator-related messages.
- Analytics: measure open rates, engagement, and conversion from creator-triggered workflows.

## Product and Ops

- Composer tools: give creators and product teams templates for creator campaign automation.
- Governance: ensure creator messaging meets platform policy and destination compliance.
- Performance tracking: measure which workflows drive booking completion and satisfaction.
- Safeguards: avoid notification fatigue by limiting frequency and respecting opt-out preferences.
