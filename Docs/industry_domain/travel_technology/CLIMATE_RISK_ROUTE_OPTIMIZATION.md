# Climate Risk Route Optimization

This document covers how travel planning can incorporate climate risk, extreme weather, and environmental factors into route optimization.

## Overview
- Climate-aware travel planning for flights, rail, and ground transport.
- Risks from storms, floods, heat, and infrastructure vulnerability.
- The value of resilient routing for traveler safety and cost control.

## Data and Modeling
- Climate data sources, weather forecasts, and risk indices.
- Dynamic route optimization across multiple transport modes.
- Predictive disruption modeling and contingency routing.

## Operational Impact
- Scheduling, rebooking, and partner coordination.
- Communication with travelers about climate risk and alternatives.
- Supplier contracts and risk-sharing for climate-related disruption.

## Commercial Implications
- Premium products for climate-safe travel.
- Insurance and refund terms tied to environmental risk.
- Carbon-aware route planning as a differentiator.

## Future Trends
- Integrated climate risk scoring for travel itineraries.
- Resilient travel ecosystems and adaptive logistics.
- Climate-smart travel compliance and disclosure.
