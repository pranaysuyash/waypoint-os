# Personalized Creator Content Routing and Push Optimization

This document describes how travel apps can route creator content to the right travelers with personalized channels and push strategies.

## Overview

- Creator content is most effective when delivered to travelers who are already interested in the destination, theme, or creator style.
- Personalized routing combines creator metadata with user intent, engagement data, and product availability.
- Push optimization ensures creator content arrives at the right moment without overwhelming travelers.

## Routing Principles

- Intent matching: map traveler preferences, search history, and itinerary interests to creator themes.
- Context awareness: use real-time signals like location, upcoming trips, and event timing.
- Creator affinity: surface content from creators a traveler has engaged with, followed, or purchased from before.
- Inventory alignment: only route creator-led offers that are bookable and capacity-compatible.

## Push Strategy

- Relevance: send creator content when the traveler is predisposed to act, such as planning a trip or browsing similar destinations.
- Frequency control: limit push volume per creator and per traveler to avoid fatigue.
- Channel mix: balance in-app recommendations, email summaries, mobile push, and live event alerts.
- Feedback loop: measure open, click, and conversion rates to refine routing rules.

## Product Features

- Dynamic cards: personalize feed cards with creator names, trust signals, and relevant booking prompts.
- Smart nudges: prompt travelers with creator-led offers only when their profile and calendar signals indicate readiness.
- Content fallback: if a creator-led product is unavailable, suggest adjacent creator content or stable app recommendations.
- Creator discovery: make it easy for travelers to request more content from creators they like.

## Risk Controls

- Avoid spam: protect the app experience by keeping creator streams contextual and useful.
- Maintain trust: clearly distinguish between sponsored creator content and editorial or personalized recommendations.
- Data privacy: respect user opt-out settings for personalized creator marketing.
- Performance guardrails: pause or reroute campaigns with poor engagement or high unsubscribe impact.
