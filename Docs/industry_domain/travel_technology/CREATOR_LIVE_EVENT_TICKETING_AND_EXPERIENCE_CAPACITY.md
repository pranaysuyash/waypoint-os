# Creator Live Event Ticketing and Experience Capacity

This document explains how creator-led travel apps manage live event ticketing, capacity, and experience delivery.

## Overview

- Creator events often require ticketed admission, limited capacity, and coordination with physical venues.
- Travel apps need systems for event inventory, capacity forecasting, and guest experience delivery.
- Strong ticketing and capacity controls reduce overbooking risk and protect the creator's reputation.

## Ticketing Architecture

- Event inventory: seats, access passes, and segmented admission for VIP, general, and creator fan groups.
- Dynamic allotments: release inventory in phases aligned with creator promotions and demand bursts.
- Ticket validation: support QR code scanning, guest lists, and access control at destinations.
- Cross-product bundling: attach event tickets to itineraries, accommodations, or transportation.

## Capacity Planning

- Real-time occupancy tracking: monitor event fill rates, no-show risk, and on-site demand.
- Peak management: design flexible overflow and standby options for creator events.
- Supplier agreements: coordinate capacity with venues, local operators, and event producers.
- Contingency plans: define substitution rules, event postponement terms, and traveler notification flows.

## Traveler Experience

- Communicate event details clearly: location, timing, dress code, and creator interactions.
- Personalize guest access: offer creator-branded welcome packs, meet-and-greet options, and loyalty lounges.
- Integrate with app notifications for last-minute updates and attendance reminders.
- Capture post-event ratings to refine future creator event capacity assumptions.
