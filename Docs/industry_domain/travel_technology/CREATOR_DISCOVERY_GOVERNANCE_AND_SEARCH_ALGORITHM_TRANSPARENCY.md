# Creator Discovery Governance and Search Algorithm Transparency

Apps that surface creator content need governance over discovery mechanisms and algorithmic transparency. This document covers policies, metrics, and safeguards for creator search rankings, recommendation fairness, and discovery transparency.

## 1. Discovery governance

- Ranking principles: relevance, recency, creator credibility, destination diversity.
- Neutrality guardrails: prevent pay-to-play ranking, opaque boosting, and incentive-driven bias.
- Governance bodies: review councils, creator advisory panels, and data science oversight.

## 2. Algorithm transparency

- Explainability: clear signals that influence discovery, such as engagement, conversion, and sponsorship status.
- Visibility: creator and traveler transparency about why a piece of content surfaced.
- Feedback loops: mechanisms to appeal ranking decisions and to report unfair visibility.

## 3. Fairness and trust

- Bias monitoring: rule-based audits for geographic, demographic, and supplier bias.
- Diversity lifts: intentional surface area for emerging creators, under-represented destinations, and low-cost travel options.
- Brand safety: moderation alignment between discovery algorithms and policy enforcement.

## 4. Operational controls

- Experimentation governance: A/B test controls, feature flagging, and rollback criteria for discovery changes.
- Measurement: discovery health metrics, creator uplift tracking, and algorithm performance scorecards.
- Partner integration: third-party content feeds, creator API access, and cross-platform distribution governance.
