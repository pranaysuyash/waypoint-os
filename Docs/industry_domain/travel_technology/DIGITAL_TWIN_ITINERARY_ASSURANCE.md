# Digital Twin Itinerary Assurance

This document covers the application of digital twin technology to travel itinerary assurance, operational resilience, and end-to-end service validation.

## Overview
- What a digital twin means for travel operations.
- The value of real-time itinerary simulation and monitoring.
- Use cases for pre-trip validation, disruption mitigation, and quality assurance.

## Architecture
- Digital twin data sources: reservations, weather, infrastructure, and supply.
- Real-time synchronization between physical itinerary and digital model.
- Feedback loops for exception detection and correction.

## Assurance Use Cases
- Predictive disruption modeling and rerouting.
- Compliance validation for documents, visas, and health checks.
- Service-level monitoring for transfers, accommodations, and ground connections.

## Integration and Operations
- API ingestion from carriers, hotels, and ground suppliers.
- Automated alerts, audit logs, and traveler notifications.
- Collaboration between operations, customer care, and technology.

## Future Direction
- Autonomous itinerary recovery.
- Digital twin-driven pricing and capacity planning.
- Convergence with metaverse and virtual pre-trip experiences.
