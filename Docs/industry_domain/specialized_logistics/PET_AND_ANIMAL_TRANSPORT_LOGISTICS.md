# Domain Knowledge: Pet & Animal Transport Logistics

**Category**: Specialized Logistics  
**Focus**: International pet travel, emotional support animals, and climate control rules.

---

## 1. The "Pet Manifesto"

Traveling with animals involves three distinct layers of regulation: Airline, Export Country, and Import Country.

### Airline Rules (Cabin vs. Hold)
- **PETC (Pet in Cabin)**: Small animals (usually <8kg including the carrier).
- **AVIH (Animal in Hold)**: Larger animals in a temperature-controlled, pressurized cargo hold.
- **SOP**: The agent must confirm the **"Manifest Limit"** (many planes only allow 2 pets per cabin).

---

## 2. The "Short-Nosed" (Brachycephalic) Ban

- **Logic**: Pugs, Bulldogs, and Persian cats are prone to respiratory failure at high altitudes.
- **SOP**: Many airlines (e.g., Emirates, BA) have a **Total Ban** on these breeds in the hold. The agent must find a "Pet Charter" or a specialized carrier (like *Cunard* for ships) as an alternative.

---

## 3. "Rabies-Free" Import Rules (UK, Japan, Australia)

- **Logic**: These countries have "No Quarantine" only if the "Pet Passport" is perfect.
- **SOP**:
    - **Microchip Timing**: Must be implanted *before* the Rabies shot.
    - **Titer Test**: A blood test required 3–6 months before travel.
    - **Wait Period**: 180 days wait after the blood test for Australia/Japan.

---

## 4. Emotional Support Animals (ESA) vs. Service Animals

- **Logic**: As of 2021/2022, many US and EU airlines no longer recognize "Emotional Support" animals as different from pets.
- **SOP**: Only **Fully Trained Service Dogs** (for blind/deaf/mobility) are allowed in the cabin for free. The agent must obtain the "Department of Transportation Service Animal Form."

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Heat Stress" Delay**: A flight is delayed on the tarmac in 35°C heat. The agent must trigger the "Animal Extraction" protocol to get the pet off the plane to a cool environment.
- **The "Microchip" Mismatch**: A traveler arrives in Hawaii, but the vet recorded the wrong microchip number. The pet is facing 120 days of mandatory quarantine.
- **The "ESA" Rejection**: A client arrives at the airport with a "Comfort Cat" and is denied boarding. The agent must find an alternative "Pet-friendly Ground" route or book a "Pet Cargo" flight.
- **The "Breed" Trap**: A client books a flight with a French Bulldog. The agent discovers the breed ban too late.
