# Creator High-Touch Event Logistics and VIP Servicing

This document explains high-touch logistics and VIP servicing for creator-led travel events.

## Overview

- Creator events often include VIP attendees, personal service, and curated logistics.
- High-touch servicing differentiates creator offerings by delivering premium, white-glove support.
- Logistics must be precise, flexible, and aligned with both creator brand and traveler expectations.

## High-Touch Event Logistics

- VIP coordination: manage guest lists, access control, transportation, and special amenities.
- Personalized itineraries: tailor schedules, experiences, and hospitality services for VIP travelers.
- On-site concierge: deploy dedicated staff or local partners to handle VIP requests and issues.
- Seamless transitions: ensure travel, event access, and post-event follow-up work smoothly.

## VIP Servicing

- Expectations management: set clear service promises and manage VIP traveler expectations.
- Host alignment: train creators and local partners on VIP protocols, privacy, and personalization.
- Incident escalation: prepare rapid-response teams for VIP service failures or disruptions.
- Extra touches: include branded gifts, premium welcome experiences, and exclusive access.

## Product Implications

- Premium pricing: price VIP creator services to reflect logistics complexity and special access.
- Transparency: show what VIP service includes and what requires upgrade requests.
- Feedback loops: capture VIP traveler satisfaction to refine high-touch creator offerings.
- Operational readiness: ensure the platform can scale VIP service support across creator event types.
