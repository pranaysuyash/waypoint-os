# Domain Knowledge: Humanitarian & NGO Field Logistics

**Category**: Advanced Operations  
**Focus**: Moving aid workers, medical teams, and cargo into complex, often non-standard environments.

---

## 1. "Missionary" & NGO Fares
- **Logic**: Specialized fares (e.g., Cat 25) with unique rules:
    - No "Saturday Night Stay" requirement.
    - Massive baggage allowances (for medical supplies).
    - Extremely low cancellation fees.
- **SOP**: The agency must be verified as a "Humanitarian Desk" by the airline. The traveler must provide a **"Letter of Affiliation"** from a registered NGO (e.g., Red Cross, MSF).

---

## 2. The "Non-Standard" Route
- **Logic**: Moving from a hub (e.g., Nairobi) to a "Bush Strip" via a UN flight or a local "NGO Shuttle."
- **SOP**: The agent manages the transition from the commercial flight to the "UNHAS" (United Nations Humanitarian Air Service) manifest.

---

## 3. Conflict Visa Waivers
- **Logic**: Entering a war zone (e.g., Yemen, South Sudan) often requires a "Visa on Arrival" or a "Military Clearance" that standard visas don't cover.
- **SOP**: The agent coordinates with the local **"Mission HQ"** for an "Entry Permit."

---

## 4. Cold Chain Cargo (Vaccines)
- **Logic**: Moving vaccines requires a "Cold Chain" (constant -70°C).
- **SOP**: The agent coordinates "Dry Ice" replenishments at every hub and ensures the "Envirotainer" (battery-powered fridge) has priority loading.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Lost" Letter of Affiliation**: An aid worker is at the gate in Geneva. They don't have their NGO letter. The airline cancels their Missionary Fare. The agent must send a digital copy within 10 minutes to the Gate Supervisor.
- **Bush Strip Closure**: A heavy rainstorm washes out a dirt landing strip in the Congo. 10 aid workers are stuck. The agent must coordinate a "Boat" or "4x4" extraction to the nearest paved airport.
- **Cold Chain Failure**: A vaccine shipment is delayed in Doha. The Envirotainer battery is at 10%. The agent must coordinate an "Emergency Charge" with the airline's ground engineering team.
- **The "Evac" Trigger**: A security situation deteriorates. All NGO staff must leave. The agent must "Void" all return tickets and issue 50 "One-way" tickets on the only departing flight.
