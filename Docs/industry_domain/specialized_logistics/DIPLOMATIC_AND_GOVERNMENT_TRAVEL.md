# Domain Knowledge: Diplomatic & Government Travel

**Category**: Elite & Specialized Verticals  
**Focus**: Managing the travel of ambassadors, heads of state, and official delegations.

---

## 1. The Passport Hierarchy
- **Diplomatic Passport (Red/Black)**: Offers "Immunity" and "Expedited Clearance."
- **Official/Service Passport**: For government employees on non-diplomatic missions.
- **Laissez-Passer**: Issued by the UN or EU for international civil servants.
- **SOP**: The travel agent must ensure the **"Note Verbale"** (official government request) matches the visa application exactly.

---

## 2. Security Detail Logistics (PSOs)
- **Logic**: Dignitaries travel with **Personal Security Officers (PSOs)** who carry firearms.
- **SOP**: The agent must notify the airline's **"Security Desk"** at least 72 hours prior. They must provide the firearm serial numbers, ammunition counts, and "Carry Permits" for the cockpit crew's awareness.

---

## 3. The "Secret" Itinerary
- **Logic**: Itineraries for high-profile figures are often "Ghosted" in the GDS to prevent leaking to the press/hostile actors.
- **SOP**: Use "Passive Segments" or "Manual PNRs" that don't reveal the true name of the traveler until the last possible moment.

---

## 4. Government Travel Policy (GTP)
- **Logic**: Most governments have a "Fly National" policy (e.g., US "Fly America Act").
- **SOP**: The agent must prioritize the national carrier unless "No seat is available" or "Connectivity is impossible."

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Note Verbale" Error**: An ambassador is delayed at the border because the "Note Verbale" lists the wrong passport number. The agent must coordinate with the Foreign Ministry for an "Emergency Telegram."
- **Armored Car Mismatch**: A delegation arrives in a "High-Risk" city, but the armored car provider sent "Standard SUVs." The agent must source "B6/B7 Level Armor" within 1 hour.
- **The "Press Leak"**: A secret diplomatic mission is leaked because an agent entered the true itinerary in a public GDS. The agency must implement "GDS Masking" protocols.
- **Laissez-Passer Rejection**: A country doesn't recognize the UN Laissez-Passer. The agent must prove "Treaty Compliance" to the airline supervisor.
