# Domain Knowledge: Charter & Private Aviation

**Category**: Specialized Logistics  
**Focus**: Jet types, FBOs, and the "Empty Leg" economy.

---

## 1. Aircraft Selection & Categorization

Private aviation is not "One Size Fits All." Selecting the wrong jet can lead to "Fuel Stops" or "Landing Refusals."

### Jet Categories
- **Very Light Jets (VLJ)**: (e.g., Phenom 100). 4 passengers. Short hops (2 hours). No lavatory usually.
- **Mid-Size Jets**: (e.g., Learjet 60). 7-8 passengers. Transcontinental (4-5 hours).
- **Large/Heavy Jets**: (e.g., Gulfstream G650). 14+ passengers. Ultra-long range (12+ hours). Global reach.

---

## 2. The FBO (Fixed Base Operator)

Private jets do not use the main airport terminals. They use **FBOs**.

### FBO Logistics
- **Ground Handling**: Managing the "VIP Lounge" and "Ramp Access."
- **Slot Management**: Private jets still need "Landing Slots" (Phase 2).
- **Catering**: Bespoke requests (e.g., "Sushi from Nobu") must be coordinated with the FBO 24 hours in advance.

---

## 3. Commercial Mechanics

### The "Empty Leg"
- **Logic**: A jet flies from London to Paris to pick up a client. The London -> Paris leg is "Empty."
- **Opportunity**: These legs can be sold at a 75% discount.
- **SOP**: Monitor "Empty Leg" boards for high-value clients (S1) who want a deal.

### Charter Contracts
- **Key Terms**: 
    - **Demurrage**: Fees for keeping the jet waiting on the tarmac.
    - **De-icing**: A variable cost (can be $5,000+) that is billed after the flight.
    - **Broker Fee**: The agency's markup (usually 5-10%).

---

## 4. Operational SOP
1. **Briefing**: Send the "Tail Number" and "Pilot Contact" to the traveler 12 hours before departure.
2. **Manifest**: Ensure the "Passenger Manifest" is sent to the FBO and Customs 24 hours in advance.
3. **Tracking**: The agent must track the "Position" of the aircraft as it positions for the client.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios for this Domain
- **Tail Swap**: The booked aircraft has a technical fault; the broker swaps it for a smaller jet.
- **Slot Loss**: The jet misses its "Take-off Slot" in Nice due to a late passenger.
- **Catering Error**: The requested Kosher meal is missing for a 10-hour flight.
