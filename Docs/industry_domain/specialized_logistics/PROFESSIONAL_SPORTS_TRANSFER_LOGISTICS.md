# Domain Knowledge: Professional Sports Transfer Logistics

**Category**: Niche Global Logistics  
**Focus**: The high-velocity travel required during "Transfer Windows" (e.g., Football/Soccer, NBA).

---

## 1. "Deadline Day" Standby
- **Logic**: A $100M transfer might be agreed upon at 10 PM, with the deadline at Midnight.
- **SOP**: The agent keeps a **"Private Jet on Standby"** (engines warm) at a nearby airport, ready to fly the player for their "Medical" in a different city.

---

## 2. NDA Medical Checks
- **Logic**: If a player is seen at a public airport, the transfer might be leaked, ruining the deal.
- **SOP**:
    - **Private Terminals (FBOs)**: Mandatory.
    - **NDA Hotels**: Booking a hotel floor under a "Corporate Alias" to hide the player's presence.
    - **Clinic Coordination**: Secure transport from the jet to a private hospital for the medical check.

---

## 3. Player Relocation (The "Moving Day")
- **Logic**: Moving a player, their family, their cars, and their pets.
- **SOP**: Coordination with "Luxury Relocation" firms. The agent manages the "Immediate Residence" (Penthouse/Hotel) while the permanent house is sourced.

---

## 4. International ITC (International Transfer Certificate)
- **Logic**: A player cannot play without the ITC.
- **SOP**: The agent coordinates the "Visa for Professional Athletes" (e.g., UK GBE / US P-1 Visa) with the club's legal team.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Deadline" Weather Out**: A private jet is grounded by fog on Deadline Day. The agent must find a "High-Speed Rail" or "Road" alternative and coordinate with the league to extend the deadline due to "Force Majeure."
- **The "Leaked" Hotel**: A paparazzi discovers the player at the "Secret" hotel. The agent must move the player to a "Private Residence" in 15 minutes.
- **Visa Rejection (Previous Criminal Record)**: A player's visa is rejected due to a minor incident 10 years ago. The agent must find a "Legal Specialist" to secure an "O-1 Extraordinary Ability" waiver.
- **The "Pet" Border Closure**: A player won't sign unless their dog can fly with them. The dog's rabies certificate is expired. The agent must find a "Pet Quarantine" solution that doesn't separate the player from the pet.
