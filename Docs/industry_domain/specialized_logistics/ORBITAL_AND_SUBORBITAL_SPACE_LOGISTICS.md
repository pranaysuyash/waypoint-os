# Domain Knowledge: Orbital & Suborbital Space Logistics

**Category**: Ultra-Luxury & Frontier  
**Focus**: The management of civilian "Spaceflights" and the complex pre-flight residency requirements.

---

## 1. Suborbital vs. Orbital
- **Suborbital (Virgin Galactic / Blue Origin)**: 10–15 minute experience.
- **Orbital (SpaceX / Axiom)**: Multi-day stay at the International Space Station (ISS).
- **Agency Role**: The agent acts as the "Mission Coordinator" for the traveler.

---

## 2. Pre-Flight "Residency" & Training
- **Logic**: You don't just "show up" for a spaceflight.
- **Residency**: Travelers must live at the "Spaceport" (e.g., Spaceport America or Kennedy Space Center) for 3–14 days before flight.
- **Training**: Coordination of centrifuge training, zero-G parabolic flights, and emergency egress drills.

---

## 3. Medical Isolation (The "Quarantine")
- **Logic**: Especially for ISS missions, travelers must enter a "Bio-Isolation" period (e.g., 14 days) to ensure they don't bring a cold/flu to the space station.
- **SOP**: The agent manages the "Clean Room" accommodation and ensures only "Screened Staff" have contact with the traveler.

---

## 4. The "Space Visa" & Legal Liability
- **Logic**: International law (The Outer Space Treaty) and FAA/AST regulations.
- **Informed Consent**: Massive legal documents where the traveler waives all rights in the event of "Catastrophic Failure."
- **Insurance**: "Spaceflight Life Insurance" is a specialized product that the agent must source.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Common Cold" Delay**: A traveler develops a cough 48 hours before an ISS launch. They are "Disqualified" from the mission. The agent must manage the "Re-booking" onto a mission that might not happen for 12 months.
- **Training Failure**: A traveler fails the "Centrifuge Stress Test." The agent must coordinate with "Sports Psychologists" to help the traveler pass the re-test.
- **Launch Scrub (Weather)**: A launch is scrubbed 5 days in a row. The guest's "High-Profile" entourage is frustrated. The agent must manage the "Hospitality Fatigue" for 50 VIP guests.
- **Spaceport Entry Denial**: A foreign national traveler is denied entry to a "Military" spaceport (e.g., Baikonur). The agent must coordinate with the "Defense Ministry."
