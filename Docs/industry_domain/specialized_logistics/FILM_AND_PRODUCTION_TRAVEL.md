# Domain Knowledge: Film & Production Travel

**Category**: Specialized Logistics  
**Focus**: Cast & crew logistics, excess baggage (equipment), and production tax credits.

---

## 1. The "Production Desk" (High-Pressure Logistics)

Film travel is characterized by extreme volatility. A "Call Sheet" change can mean moving 50 people across borders in 6 hours.

### Key Requirements
- **24/7/365 Coverage**: The desk never sleeps.
- **Privacy (NDAs)**: High-profile cast members require pseudonym bookings in the GDS.
- **SOP**: The agent must work directly with the "Production Coordinator."

---

## 2. Excess Baggage & Equipment (The "Media Rate")

Production crews travel with "Carnets" (customs documents for gear) and hundreds of kilos of equipment.

### The "Media Rate" SOP
- **Logic**: Many airlines (Delta, United, BA) offer a flat "Media Rate" (e.g., $50 per bag) for production companies, bypassing standard "Overweight" fees.
- **SOP**: The agent must add the **"Media ID"** to the PNR to trigger these rates.
- **Carnet Coordination**: The agent must ensure the flight itinerary allows for "Customs Clearance" of gear during layovers.

---

## 3. Location Tax Credits & Financials

### The "Spend" Audit
- **Logic**: Many governments (e.g., Georgia, Iceland, New Zealand) offer tax rebates if the production "spends" a certain amount on local travel/hotels.
- **SOP**: The agency must provide a "Qualified Spend Report" that breaks down costs by region and vendor type.

---

## 4. Housing & "Long-Stay" Rentals

Productions often stay for 3-6 months.
- **Apart-Hotels**: Preferred for crew (kitchens/laundry).
- **Security**: "Securing the Floor" — booking an entire hotel floor for cast safety.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Call Sheet" Change**: The director moves the shoot from Morocco to Jordan tonight. 40 crew members need new flights, visas, and hotels immediately.
- **Gear Stuck in Customs**: A camera rig is held at customs because the agent didn't provide enough time between flights for the Carnet inspection.
- ** Cast Name Leak**: A celebrity's real name is leaked because the agent didn't use the agreed-upon pseudonym in the GDS.
- **Baggage Overload**: The crew arrives at the airport with 150 extra bags. The "Media Rate" isn't in the PNR, and the airline demands $20,000 in fees.
