# Biosecurity and Inspection Logistics

This document examines the logistics of biosecurity controls, inspections, and secure transport in travel and cargo operations.

## Overview
- Biosecurity risks in passenger and cargo movement.
- Inspection regimes for biological samples, livestock, and sensitive goods.
- Coordination between travel operators, customs, and health authorities.

## Operational Design
- Secure routing for bio-sensitive shipments.
- Inspection planning, chain of custody, and compliance documentation.
- Integration with customs, TSA, and health screening.

## Risk and Mitigation
- Containment, quarantine, and decontamination protocols.
- Operational resilience for inspection delays.
- Partner training and response playbooks.

## Commercial and Regulatory Impacts
- Liability for biosecurity breaches.
- Tariff and permit requirements for biological cargo.
- Customer communication and transparency.

## Future Trends
- Automated biosecurity inspection systems.
- Cross-border health security data sharing.
- Biosecurity as part of travel logistics risk management.
