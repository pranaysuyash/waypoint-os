# Cryptocurrency Settlements and Tokenized Travel Assets

This document explores the use of cryptocurrency, tokenization, and blockchain for travel settlements and assets.

## Overview
- Cryptocurrency payment acceptance for travel services.
- Tokenized travel assets: vouchers, loyalty points, and digital passports.
- Settlement efficiency and global currency access.

## Financial Structures
- Stablecoin settlement for cross-border travel bookings.
- Tokenized loyalty and reward transfer mechanisms.
- Smart contracts for refunds, cancellations, and conditional access.

## Operational Implications
- Wallet onboarding, custodial versus non-custodial models.
- Reconciliation between crypto and fiat systems.
- Fraud prevention, AML, and KYC for digital payments.

## Regulatory and Risk Considerations
- Travel-related crypto compliance and jurisdictional risk.
- Price volatility, insolvency risk, and consumer protections.
- Interoperability with traditional travel finance systems.

## Future Direction
- Digital travel asset marketplaces.
- Tokenization of experience rights and premium access.
- Programmable travel finance for loyalty and subscription services.
