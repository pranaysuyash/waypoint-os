# Orbital Hospitality and Space Station Logistics

This document covers the logistics, supply chain, and operational design of hospitality services in orbital and suborbital environments.

## Overview
- The intersection of hospitality and aerospace logistics.
- Space station guest operations, crew support, and orbital mobility.
- Unique supply chain constraints for food, life support, and guest experiences.

## Core Elements
- Orbital accommodation models: station stays, capsule hotels, research hospitality.
- Supply chain cadence: resupply missions, waste evacuation, spare parts.
- Guest safety, medical support, and emergency evacuation protocols.

## Service Design
- Customer experience in zero/low gravity.
- Sustainable provisioning and reuse of consumables.
- Integration with launch providers and ground operations.

## Regulatory and Commercial Considerations
- Risk-sharing across hospitality operators, insurers, and agencies.
- International coordination for orbital customers and crew.
- Pricing, reservation commitments, and cancellation structures.

## Future Direction
- Commercial orbital hotels and hybrid station/resort models.
- Demand forecasting for commercial space tourism.
- Logistics planning for orbital hospitality at scale.
