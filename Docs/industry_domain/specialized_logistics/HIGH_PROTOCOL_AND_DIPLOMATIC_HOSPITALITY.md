# Domain Knowledge: High Protocol & Diplomatic Hospitality

**Category**: Metaphysical & Philosophical Horizon  
**Focus**: The unwritten logistical rules for Gulf, East Asian, and European elite hospitality.

---

## 1. The "Majlis" Protocol (Middle East)
- **Logic**: Hospitality is a "Sovereign Duty."
- **SOP**: The agent must ensure the client (if a host) has a "VVIP Greeting" at the aircraft steps.
- **Logistics**: Always booking a "Lead Car" for the luggage and a "Secondary Car" for the staff to arrive *before* the VVIP.

---

## 2. "Saving Face" (East Asia)
- **Logic**: Mistakes in public are unacceptable.
- **SOP**: The agent must have a **"Ghost Contingency."** If the restaurant is closed, a "Better" alternative must be presented as if it were the "Original Plan" to save the client's face.

---

## 3. The "Protocol" Rank
- **Logic**: Who sits where, who enters first.
- **SOP**: The agent coordinates the **"Manifest Seating"** not just for comfort, but for "Diplomatic Seniority."
    - "Don't put the junior minister in a better seat than the CEO."

---

## 4. "Gift" Logistics
- **Logic**: Entering a meeting without a "Culturally Appropriate" gift.
- **SOP**: The agent sources and delivers the gift to the client's hotel 4 hours before the meeting.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Majlis" Breach**: A VVIP arrives at the hotel, but the "Welcome Team" is not at the door. This is a "Protocol P0." The agent must immediately contact the "General Manager" and secure a "Suite Upgrade" as a face-saving gesture.
- **The "Wrong" Seat**: A PNR is split, and the "Senior Partner" ends up in 2B while the "Associate" is in 1A. The agent must "Swap" them in the GDS before check-in.
- **The "Inappropriate" Gift**: An agent provides a "Leather" gift to a client traveling to a region where that animal is sacred. The agent must "Recall" the gift and replace it with "Silk" or "Precious Metal."
- **The "Face-saving" Pivot**: The private yacht is delayed by 2 hours. Instead of telling the client "It's late," the agent tells the client "We have upgraded you to a 2-hour helicopter tour of the coast, which will land you directly on the yacht."
