# Domain Knowledge: Superyacht & Private Island Logistics

**Category**: Ultra-Luxury & Frontier  
**Focus**: The management of exclusive properties and vessels where the travel agent acts as a "Chief of Staff."

---

## 1. Private Island "Buyouts"
- **Logic**: Renting an entire island (e.g., Necker Island, North Island) for a single group.
- **SOP**:
    - **NDA Management**: All agency staff and suppliers must sign strict non-disclosure agreements.
    - **Staffing**: Coordinating the "Fly-in" of specialized staff (e.g., Michelin chefs, private security, personal assistants).
    - **Customs**: For islands in remote territories, the agent must arrange for a "Mobile Customs Officer" to meet the private jet on the island's strip.

---

## 2. Superyacht Provisioning & Crew
- **Logic**: The agent manages the travel for the **"Rotational Crew"** (Captains, Engineers, Stews).
- **APA (Advanced Provisioning Allowance)**: The agent may manage a portion of the APA to source high-end items (e.g., specific vintages of wine) and fly them to the yacht's next port of call.
- **Tender Logistics**: Managing the "Chase Boat" or "Tender" transfers from the mainland to the yacht at anchor.

---

## 3. High-Stakes Privacy (VVIP)
- **Manifest Masking**: Names on the guest list are often aliases until the manifest is sent to the coast guard/immigration.
- **Security Audit**: The agent must verify the island's "Electronic Perimeter" and the yacht's "Anti-Piracy" protocols (in specific regions).

---

## 4. The "Ghost" Resort
- **Logic**: Booking a resort and paying for all rooms to ensure no other guests are present, even if the group only uses 10% of the capacity.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "NDA Breach"**: A junior driver at a transfer company posts a photo of a celebrity client on the private island. The agent must manage the legal fallout and the "Blacklisting" of the vendor.
- **The "Provisioning Gap"**: A yacht in the Maldives needs a specific brand of organic baby formula that isn't available locally. The agent must use an **OBC (On-Board Courier)** to fly it in from London.
- **The "Storm Evacuation"**: A private island is in the path of a cyclone. The agent must coordinate 3 private jets to evacuate 50 guests and 100 staff in a 4-hour window.
- **Yacht Rotation Crisis**: The Yacht Captain is stuck in a visa dispute in Spain. The yacht cannot sail without him. The agent must find an "Emergency Replacement" with the same certifications (MCA Master 3000GT).
