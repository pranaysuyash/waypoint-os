# Creator Event Transportation and Last-Mile Coordination

Creator events and destination activations need transportation logistics that match the content experience. This document explains last-mile coordination, guest movement, and event transport support for creator-led travel programs.

## 1. Transportation planning

- Guest flows: map arrival, transfer, and departure movements for creators, production teams, and travelers.
- Vehicle mix: match shuttles, private cars, minibuses, and experiential transport to event scale and brand requirements.
- Routing: optimize service windows, loading zones, and destination access for creator event locations.

## 2. Last-mile coordination

- Real-time tracking: provide arrival ETAs, transport notifications, and disruption alerts.
- Service handoff: ensure transport providers coordinate with on-site event teams and creator production managers.
- Contingency routing: plan alternate pickup/drop-off options for traffic, weather, or venue changes.

## 3. Experience alignment

- Branded transport: use transport as part of the creator story, with signage, music, and route narratives.
- Seamless transitions: connect transportation handoffs with check-ins, event registrations, and content activation zones.
- Privacy and discretion: manage VIP movement, paparazzi risk, and private access for high-profile creator events.

## 4. Operations and risk

- Supplier sourcing: vet transport providers for safety, reliability, and creator experience standards.
- Incident management: establish escalation paths for delays, vehicle breakdowns, and guest service issues.
- Reporting: capture transport performance data, passenger feedback, and event logistics scorecards.
