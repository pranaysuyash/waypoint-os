# Domain Knowledge: Space & Extreme Frontier Travel

**Category**: Specialized Logistics  
**Focus**: Suborbital flights, Zero-G, and Polar expeditions.

---

## 1. Space Tourism (The "New Horizon")

By 2026, companies like Blue Origin, Virgin Galactic, and SpaceX are moving into "Regular" commercial windows.

### The "Astronaut" Onboarding
- **Logic**: A space ticket is not a boarding pass; it's a 3-day "Training Program."
- **SOP**: The agent must manage the **Medical Clearance** (G-force tolerance) and the **Training Itinerary** at the Spaceport (e.g., Spaceport America).

---

## 2. Zero-G Flights

### The "Vomit Comet" SOP
- **Logic**: Zero-G flights involve parabolic arcs that cause extreme nausea.
- **SOP**: Coordinate with companies like *Zero-G Corp*. Ensure the traveler has the "Physiological Prep" (diet/medication) and that the booking includes "GoPro/Media" packages.

---

## 3. Extreme Frontier (South Pole / Deep Sea)

### The "Search & Rescue" (SAR) Mandate
- **Logic**: Trips to the South Pole or the Mariana Trench are beyond the reach of standard insurance.
- **SOP**:
    - **High-Altitude/Extreme SAR Insurance**: Mandatory $500k+ coverage.
    - **Evacuation Plans**: Identifying the nearest "Landing Strip" or "Pressure Chamber."

---

## 4. Legal & Liability Waivers

- **Logic**: These trips carry a "Risk of Death."
- **SOP**: The agent must ensure the client signs **"Irrevocable Liability Waivers"** reviewed by the agency's legal counsel.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Medically Unfit" Astronaut**: A client pays $450,000 for a space flight but fails the "Centrifuge Test" at the spaceport. The agent must negotiate a "Partial Refund" or "Reschedule."
- **Polar Extraction**: A private expedition to the South Pole is hit by a "Whiteout" storm. The agent must coordinate with **ALE (Antarctic Logistics & Expeditions)** for an emergency extraction.
- **Submersible Technical Failure**: A deep-sea tour is cancelled due to a "Hull Integrity" warning. The agent must manage the client's disappointment and the massive refund.
- **Launch Delay Loop**: A space launch is delayed 5 times due to "Upper-level winds." The agent must manage the client's "Spaceport Housing" and logistics for 14 days.
