# Creator Experiential Partner Quality and Source Verification

Creator-curated travel experiences depend on verified supplier partners and quality source validation. This document defines the governance, verification processes, and partner scorecards that support creator-driven supplier ecosystems.

## 1. Supplier verification

- Credential checks: licenses, insurance, safety certifications, and local authority approvals.
- Experience authenticity: verifying that supplier capabilities match creator claims and traveler expectations.
- Cross-checks: local partner references, guest reviews, and on-site inspections.

## 2. Quality scorecards

- Partner performance metrics: on-time delivery, traveler satisfaction, incident rates, and refund frequency.
- Creator alignment: how well a partner executes creator requirements, content staging needs, and brand standards.
- Risk scoring: compliance risk, operational risk, and reputational risk.

## 3. Source validation

- Provenance: document the origin of experiences, supplier sourcing, and creator narrative claims.
- Content-to-service mapping: ensure creator marketing content matches actual experience deliverables.
- Audit readiness: supplier documentation, verification logs, and content claim validation.

## 4. Governance integration

- Partner onboarding: verification gates, contract clauses, and destination-specific approvals.
- Continuous monitoring: recurring quality reviews, feedback loops, and corrective action plans.
- Transparency: make verification status visible to internal teams, creators, and, where appropriate, traveler audiences.
