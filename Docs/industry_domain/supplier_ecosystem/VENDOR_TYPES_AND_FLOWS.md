# Supplier Ecosystem & Vendor Types

Travel agencies act as aggregators across a fragmented landscape of inventory providers.

---

## 1. The Inventory Stack

### Global Distribution Systems (GDS)
- **Examples**: Amadeus, Sabre, Travelport.
- **Function**: The "backbone" of airline and hotel connectivity. Used for real-time inventory and ticketing.
- **Key Detail**: Requires high technical expertise (Command Line Interface) and accreditation (IATA).

### Bed Banks / Wholesale Aggregators
- **Examples**: Hotelbeds, WebBeds, Expedia Partner Solutions.
- **Function**: Aggregators of hotel rooms globally. They buy in bulk and resell to agencies.
- **Key Detail**: Typically provide "Net Rates" (no commission, agent adds markup).

### Destination Management Companies (DMCs)
- **Function**: Local ground handlers (in Dubai, Bali, Europe, etc.). They manage transport, tours, and local hotel relationships.
- **Key Detail**: Vital for "Package" tours and high-touch service. Often work on WhatsApp or custom portals.

### Consolidators
- **Function**: Bulk buyers of airline tickets who resell them to smaller agents without IATA licenses.
- **Key Detail**: Good for competitive airfare but have strict refund/change policies.

---

## 2. Booking Flows

### Flow A: Direct API (Modern)
`Agent Platform -> GDS/BedBank API -> Immediate Confirmation`
- **Pros**: Speed, accuracy.
- **Cons**: High tech cost, requires pre-funding.

### Flow B: Request-Based (Traditional/DMC)
`Agent -> Email/WhatsApp -> DMC -> Wait for Confirmation -> Vouchering`
- **Pros**: Better local expertise, often better pricing for groups.
- **Cons**: Slow (2-24 hours), prone to human error.

---

## 3. Vendor Relationship Management

Agencies maintain a "Vendor Registry" with the following data:
- **Commercial Terms**: Credit limits, markup rules, commission % (if any).
- **Service Quality**: Response time, refund ease, traveler feedback.
- **Tech Integration**: Does the vendor have an API or is it "Offline"?
- **Niche Expertise**: "Best for Maldives Luxury" vs. "Best for Europe Budget Groups".
