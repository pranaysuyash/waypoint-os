# Creator Destination Partner Inventory and Experience Exchange

Creator programs can unlock inventory and experiences through destination partner exchange models. This document describes how creators can collaborate with destination operators, local suppliers, and experience marketplaces to exchange content for inventory exposure.

## 1. Exchange models

- Inventory-for-exposure: creators receive partner access in exchange for storytelling, promotion, or content creation.
- Experience swaps: creator-led events, guides, and campaigns are licensed to partners in return for booked capacity.
- Revenue share exchanges: allocate partner inventory based on booked value, content reach, or performance milestones.

## 2. Partner alignment

- Destination partner roles: DMOs, local operators, hotels, and attraction owners providing inventory to creator programs.
- Experience validation: ensure partner inventory meets creator promise, service levels, and traveler expectations.
- Capacity signaling: transparent inventory availability, reservation windows, and partner commitment levels.

## 3. Commercial and operational governance

- Contract terms: inventory exchange clauses, performance commitments, and content usage rights.
- Quality assurance: partner scorecards, traveler feedback, and destination monitoring.
- Risk control: protect against overcommitment, cancellations, and promotional misrepresentation.

## 4. Product integration

- Cataloging: present partner inventory as creator-curated offers, bundles, or destination experiences.
- Distribution: link partner exchange offers into marketplaces, app feeds, and campaign pages.
- Reporting: capture partner contribution, creator yield, and traveler satisfaction.
