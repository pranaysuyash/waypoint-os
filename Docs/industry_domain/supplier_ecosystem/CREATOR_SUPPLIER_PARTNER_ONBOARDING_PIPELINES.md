# Creator Supplier Partner Onboarding Pipelines

This document describes pipelines for onboarding suppliers into creator-driven travel products.

## Overview

- Creator product success depends on smooth supplier onboarding and active partner management.
- A dedicated onboarding pipeline makes it easier to integrate local operators, experiences, and services into creator ecosystems.
- Strong onboarding reduces time to market and improves creator confidence.

## Pipeline Stages

- Discovery: identify suitable suppliers for creator product types and destination themes.
- Qualification: vet supplier credentials, safety, partner fit, and capacity.
- Integration: map supplier products to the app’s booking, pricing, and inventory systems.
- Creator alignment: brief creators on supplier capabilities, constraints, and storytelling opportunities.
- Launch: publish the partner-enabled creator product and monitor early engagement.

## Product Benefits

- Faster rollout: suppliers can support creator-led products sooner with clear onboarding steps.
- Quality: standardized onboarding enforces minimum partner standards.
- Flexibility: pipelines support add-ons, pop-ups, seasonal offers, and creator-collab products.
- Transparency: creators see which suppliers are approved and what product features they can use.

## Operational Controls

- Onboarding checklist: define documentation, contract terms, insurance status, and content requirements.
- Partner training: educate suppliers on creator expectations, delivery standards, and traveler communication.
- Feedback loops: capture issues from creators and travelers to refine onboarding criteria.
- Deactivation policy: remove suppliers when quality, compliance, or capacity issues arise.
