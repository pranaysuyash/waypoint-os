# GDS vs Bedbanks vs DMCs

## Overview
Travel agencies rely on several vendor classes to source inventory and manage bookings. The three primary supplier categories are:
- Global Distribution Systems (GDS)
- Bedbanks
- Destination Management Companies (DMCs)

## GDS
### What it is
A GDS is a global booking network that connects travel agents with airlines, hotels, car rental companies, and other travel services.

### Strengths
- Wide, standardized inventory
- Real-time availability and pricing
- Booking and ticketing automation

### Weaknesses
- Limited access to local, offline inventory
- Higher transaction fees
- Less flexibility for bespoke or experiential travel

## Bedbanks
### What it is
A bedbank is a wholesaler of hotel inventory, often focused on negotiated rates and bulk room allocations.

### Strengths
- Competitive inventory pricing
- Access to negotiated room blocks and blackout-free allotments
- Good for package builders and leisure-focused bookings

### Weaknesses
- Inventory may lag in real-time updates
- Often pre-paid or non-refundable
- Limited support for dynamic itinerary changes

## DMCs
### What it is
A Destination Management Company provides local ground services, tours, transfers, and event logistics in a specific destination.

### Strengths
- Local expertise and onsite coordination
- Access to experiences, local guides, and event support
- Strong for inbound travel, special events, and complex ground logistics

### Weaknesses
- Usually destination-specific, not global
- May require more manual contracting
- Variable quality and service levels

## How agencies choose
### Use cases
- GDS: corporate travel, airline-centric itineraries, standardized bookings
- Bedbank: leisure hotel packaging, group room allocations, rate optimization
- DMC: local experiences, event logistics, ground transport, destination support

### Typical combinations
- Corporate agency: GDS + DMC for destination ground support
- Leisure agency: Bedbank + DMC for hotel and local activity sourcing
- Hybrid agency: GDS + Bedbank + DMC to balance scale, price, and local service
