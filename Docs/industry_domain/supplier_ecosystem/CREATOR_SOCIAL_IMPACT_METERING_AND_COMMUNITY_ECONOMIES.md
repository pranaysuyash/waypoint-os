# Creator Social Impact Metering and Community Economies

This document covers how travel apps can meter social impact and community economies for creator programs.

## Overview

- Creator-driven travel should be measured not only by bookings, but also by social impact and local economic contribution.
- Social impact metering provides accountability for creators, suppliers, and destinations.
- Community economies help quantify how creator activities benefit local stakeholders.

## Impact Metrics

- Economic contribution: measure local spend, community supplier utilization, and creator-driven bookings.
- Social value: assess support for local jobs, cultural preservation, and community-led experiences.
- Environmental externalities: capture footprint from creator campaigns, including transport and event impact.
- Traveler benefit: evaluate satisfaction, learning outcomes, and repeat engagement in community-centric creator trips.

## Community Economies

- Partner inclusion: track the participation of local operators, artisans, and small businesses in creator supply.
- Value sharing: model how creator revenue is shared across community economies and supplier networks.
- Sustainability incentives: reward creator products that demonstrate positive local or social outcomes.
- Reporting: provide creators and partners with impact dashboards and community performance summaries.
