# Domain Knowledge: Post-Scarcity & Gift Economy Travel

**Category**: Frontier & Future Logistics  
**Focus**: Managing logistics in de-monetized zones or "Post-Scarcity" societies.

---

## 1. The "De-monetized" Zone (Burning Man / Commune)
- **Logic**: No money is exchanged. Everything is a "Gift" or a "Shared Resource."
- **Agency Role**: The agent acts as a **"Resource Broker"** rather than a "Payment Processor."
- **SOP**: Coordinating "Contributions" (e.g., "Client A brings the water, Client B brings the solar power") instead of processing credit cards.

---

## 2. Resource Barter & Reputation Credit
- **Logic**: In a post-scarcity environment, status is gained by **"Giving"** (The Potlatch principle).
- **SOP**: The agent tracks the client's **"Social Credit"** or "Reputation" within the community to secure "Premium" locations or access.

---

## 3. The "Last Mile" Survival Logistics
- **Logic**: These zones are often in hostile environments (Deserts, Antarctic).
- **SOP**: The agent must ensure the client is **"Radically Self-Reliant"** while still providing the "Invisible Safety Net" (Satellite comms, medical extraction).

---

## 4. Circular Economy & Zero-Waste
- **Logic**: "Leave No Trace" (LNT) as a legal requirement.
- **SOP**: The agent coordinates the "Back-haul" of all waste generated by the client.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Merchant" Mindset Failure**: A client tries to "Buy" their way into a gift-economy camp with cash. The camp rejects them. The agent must explain the **"Social Contract"** and find a way for the client to "Contribute" their skills or resources instead.
- **Resource Depletion**: A shared "Water Tank" is empty. The agent must coordinate a "Peer-to-Peer" share from a neighboring camp using "Reputation Credits."
- **The "LNT" Violation**: A client leaves trash at their site. The agent is fined "Socially" and "Legally." The agent must coordinate a "Cleanup Crew" to restore the site and the agency's reputation.
- **Solar Failure**: The "Shared Power" grid fails in a post-scarcity zone. The agent must find a "Manual Power" alternative or a "Kinetic" contribution from the client.
