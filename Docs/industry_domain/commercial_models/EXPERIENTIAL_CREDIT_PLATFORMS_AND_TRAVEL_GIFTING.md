# Experiential Credit Platforms and Travel Gifting

This document examines experiential credit platforms, giftable travel currencies, and flexible experience credits.

## Overview
- Travel gift cards and experiential credits are evolving into programmable platforms.
- Credits can represent experiences, upgrades, loyalty value, or bundled services.
- Gifting models support corporate incentives, loyalty gifting, and marketplace exchange.

## Business Design
- Credit issuance, redemption rules, and expiry management.
- Experience catalogs and partner onboarding for credit acceptance.
- Dynamic pricing and credit top-up/sharing models.

## Operational Implications
- Claims management, credit reconciliation, and fraud controls.
- Integrating credits with booking systems and supplier settlements.
- Customer support flows for gift redemption and credit adjustments.

## Commercial Impact
- New revenue from gift sales, corporate rewards, and packaged experiences.
- Higher margin on premium experience credits and exclusive access.
- Brand extension through marketplace-based gifting.

## Future Trends
- Blockchain-enabled experience credits and transferability.
- Cross-brand credit wallets for travel, hospitality, and entertainment.
- Personalized gifting experiences with embedded travel planning.
