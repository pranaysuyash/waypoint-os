# Subscription and Access Economy Travel

This document covers emerging travel business models based on subscription access, membership, and loyalty-as-a-service.

## Overview
- Access economy principles applied to travel.
- Subscription passes, travel credits, and membership cohorts.
- Industry shift from transaction-based to recurring access.

## Product Models
- Unlimited access passes, tiered subscriptions, and hybrid bundles.
- Membership benefits, priority handling, and ancillary access.
- Corporate travel subscriptions and executive travel programs.

## Financial and Operational Impacts
- Revenue recognition models for prepaid access.
- Risk management for unused entitlements.
- Customer lifetime value and churn control.

## Distribution and Channel Strategy
- Partnered hotel, airline, and ground-transport access.
- Direct-to-consumer versus white-label subscription offerings.
- Incentivizing usage through loyalty and experience design.

## Future Trends
- Platform ecosystems for travel memberships.
- Subscription-based travel insurance and flexibility products.
- Access economy governance and regulatory treatment.
