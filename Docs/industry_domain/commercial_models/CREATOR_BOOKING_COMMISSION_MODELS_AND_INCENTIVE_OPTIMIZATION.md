# Creator Booking Commission Models and Incentive Optimization

Creator-driven travel commerce requires commission structures that balance creator upside with booking economics. This document outlines the commission model design, incentive optimization, and live performance governance for creator-led travel bookings.

## 1. Commission frameworks

- Source-based commissions: fixed percentage for creator-originated bookings, tiered by product category.
- Performance multipliers: bonuses for high-value itineraries, repeat customer bookings, or cross-sell conversion.
- Hybrid splits: base commission plus revenue share on ancillary spend, membership upgrades, or campaign bundles.

## 2. Incentive alignment

- Quality controls: commission holdbacks tied to traveler satisfaction, refund rates, and content compliance.
- Incentive caps: payment floors, upper bounds, and clawback provisions for cancellations and disputes.
- Long-term performance: lifecycle pacing, creator retention incentives, and deferred rewards for loyalty growth.

## 3. Data-driven optimization

- Attribution modeling: multi-touch signals, influencer-assisted conversions, and channel mix weighting.
- A/B test design: commission plan experiments, treatment/control cohort measurement, and payout elasticity tracking.
- Transparency: creator dashboards with commission forecasts, earned vs. payable splits, and audit-ready reporting.

## 4. Risk and governance

- Fraud controls: invalid booking detection, fake traveler schemes, and creator collusion monitoring.
- Compliance: affiliate disclosure, platform terms, and marketplace commission reporting.
- Contract integration: alignment of commission terms with creator agreements, platform policy, and supplier constraints.
