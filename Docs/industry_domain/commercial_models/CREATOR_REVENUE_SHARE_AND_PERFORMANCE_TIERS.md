# Creator Revenue Share and Performance Tiers

This document outlines the commercial structures that govern creator payouts and incentives for travel apps.

## Overview
- Travel creator partnerships typically combine fixed fees, revenue share, and performance-based incentives.
- A clear tiered model helps align creator incentives with app bookings, retention, and long-term brand fit.
- Performance tiers also enable travel apps to manage creator costs predictably while rewarding high-quality contribution.

## Revenue Share Models
- Affiliate share: creators earn a percentage of bookings generated through tracked referrals.
- Co-branded product share: creators receive a share of revenue from creator-branded experiences or bundles.
- Subscription share: creator subscribers pay for premium access, with revenue split between app and creator.
- Hybrid offers: base guarantee plus bonus payments based on conversion rate, retention, or traveler satisfaction.

## Tier Structures
- Discovery tier: lightweight creators with low access requirements and modest payout rates.
- Growth tier: creators with proven conversion and repeat booking performance.
- Premium tier: high-impact creators with strong brand fit, lead quality, and content production capabilities.
- Strategic tier: destination ambassadors and long-term partners with dedicated product co-development.

## Measurement and Incentives
- Trackable metrics: bookings, conversion lift, average order value, refund rate, and traveler satisfaction.
- Quality adjustments: reduce payouts for returns, disputes, or compliance failures.
- Upsell incentives: reward creators for driving higher-value packages or ancillary purchases.
- Retention rewards: bonus payments for creator-sourced travelers who book again within a set timeframe.

## Operational Rules
- Define clear attribution windows and cookie/link expiry policies.
- Use transparent reporting for creators, including bookings, commission earned, and adjustment reasons.
- Manage tax and cross-border payment complexity for international creator payouts.
- Avoid one-size-fits-all terms; tailor tiers to creator role, market, and content type.
