# Domain Knowledge: Pricing Packages & Fee Structures

**Category**: Commercial Models  
**Focus**: Professional fees, subscription models, and commission optimization.

---

## 1. The Shift to "Professional Fees"

Modern agencies are moving away from being "Free Travel Agents" to "Professional Consultants."

### Planning Fees (Research Fees)
- **Logic**: A non-refundable fee (e.g., $100-$500) charged *before* any booking is made to cover the agent's time for research and itinerary design.
- **SOP**: The fee is collected at the "Lead Qualification" stage (Phase 1). It may or may not be applied toward the final trip cost.

### Ticketing Fees
- **Logic**: A flat fee per ticket (e.g., $50 for international, $25 for domestic) to compensate for the low/zero commission on airfares.
- **SOP**: Automatically added to the TST (Transitional Stored Ticket) or billed as a separate service fee.

---

## 2. Subscription & Membership Models

For high-frequency travelers (S1/S2), agencies are implementing "Retainer" models.

### The "Concierge" Subscription
- **Price Point**: $1,000 - $5,000 per year.
- **Inclusions**: 24/7 priority support, unlimited bookings, free upgrades, and lounge access.
- **SOP**: These travelers have a "Flag" in the CRM that bypasses standard support queues and routes directly to a P1 (Concierge Agent).

---

## 3. Revenue Optimization (Markups & Overrides)

### Markup Strategies
- **Hidden Markups**: Adding a margin to a "Net Fare" from a Bed Bank (e.g., Hotelbeds). The client sees a single "Total Price."
- **Transparent Fees**: Charging the "Public Price" and adding a visible "Service Fee."
- **Logic**: Use "Hidden Markups" for opaque packages (MICE/Luxury) and "Transparent Fees" for corporate clients who require audit trails.

### Ancillary Revenue
- **Insurance**: One of the highest margin items (25-40% commission).
- **Airport Services**: Fast-track, Meet & Greet, and Lounges.
- **System Action**: Proactively offering these in the NB02 "Decision State" to maximize the "Per-Trip Margin."

---

## 4. Operational Best Practices
- **Value over Price**: Never compete on price alone; document the "Hidden Savings" (upgrades, free breakfast) provided by the agency.
- **Automated Invoicing**: Ensure the Mid-Office system correctly separates "Base Price" from "Agency Fee" for tax purposes.
- **Refund Policy**: Clearly state that "Agency Fees" are non-refundable even if the trip is cancelled by the traveler.
