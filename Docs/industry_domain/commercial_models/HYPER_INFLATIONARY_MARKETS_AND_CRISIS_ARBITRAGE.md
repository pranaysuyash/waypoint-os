# Domain Knowledge: Hyper-Inflationary Markets & Crisis Arbitrage

**Category**: Advanced Security & Ethical Systems  
**Focus**: Booking and paying for travel in countries where the local currency is in freefall (e.g., Turkey, Argentina, Lebanon).

---

## 1. Parallel Markets (Blue Dollar)
- **Logic**: The "Official" exchange rate is 100, but the "Street" (Blue) rate is 500.
- **SOP**: The agent must know when to pay in **"USD/EUR"** (via international credit card) vs. **"Local Cash"** (exchanged on the street).
- **The Arbitrage**: If a hotel is priced in "Local Currency" but the exchange rate hasn't updated in the GDS, the trip might be 80% cheaper if booked correctly.

---

## 2. "Locking" the Rate
- **Logic**: In hyper-inflation, a price quoted at 9 AM might be invalid by 2 PM.
- **SOP**: **"Instant Ticketing."** No "Hold" periods. The agent must pay the supplier *immediately* to lock the rate.

---

## 3. The "Fuel" Arbitrage
- **Logic**: In some crisis countries (e.g., Venezuela, Iran), fuel is heavily subsidized.
- **SOP**: Domestic flights or private charters may be "Irrationality Cheap." The agent must factor in the "Safety Risk" of these subsidized carriers.

---

## 4. Barter & "Social" Currency
- **Logic**: When the banking system collapses (e.g., Lebanon), the agent must use "Relationship Networks" to secure inventory.
- **SOP**: Paying a local DMC in "Hard Currency" (USD cash) via a courier to ensure the client's hotel stays "Confirmed."

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "GDS" lag**: The GDS is using an exchange rate of $1 = 50 Lira, but the street rate is $1 = 150 Lira. The agent books 10 rooms at the "Old" rate. The hotel tries to "Cancel" or "Re-price" upon arrival. The agent must fight for the "Contracted Price."
- **Cash "Liquidity" Crisis**: A client is in a country where ATMs have run out of cash. The agent must coordinate a **"Cash Drop"** via a local security firm or a "Trusted DMC Partner."
- **The "Re-pricing" Nightmare**: A client pays 50% deposit. By the time the final 50% is due, the price in USD has doubled due to hyper-inflation.
- **Subsidized Safety**: An agent finds a $5 flight in a crisis zone. They must perform a "Maintenance Audit" on the airline to ensure the $5 price isn't due to "Skipped Engine Checks."
