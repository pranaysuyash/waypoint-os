# Domain Knowledge: Loyalty Arbitrage & Status Management

**Category**: Advanced Operations  
**Focus**: The professional management of client reward points, status challenges, and "Cash + Points" optimizations.

---

## 1. The "Points" Portfolio
- **Logic**: For ultra-high-volume travelers (S1), their "Miles" are a financial asset worth $100k+.
- **Agency Role**: The agent acts as a "Portfolio Manager," deciding when to use "Cash" (to earn more miles) vs. "Points" (to save money on expensive last-minute tickets).

---

## 2. Status Challenges & Matching
- **Status Challenge**: "If you fly 10 times in 90 days, we will give you Platinum status."
- **Status Match**: "You are Platinum on United? We will give you Platinum on Delta for free."
- **SOP**: The agent tracks the client's "Status Expiry" and proactively triggers a "Challenge" on a competing airline before a busy travel season.

---

## 3. "Cash + Points" Upgrades
- **Logic**: Buying an Economy ticket with cash and using points to upgrade to Business.
- **Complexity**: Only specific "Fare Classes" (e.g., Y, B, M) are upgradeable.
- **SOP**: The agent must "Force" the GDS to book the more expensive "Upgradeable" class if the client wants to use points.

---

## 4. GHA (Global Hotel Alliance) & Virtuoso Amenities
- **Logic**: Overlapping loyalty. A client is Marriott Titanium *and* the agent is booking through Virtuoso.
- **SOP**: The agent must "Double-dip" the benefits (e.g., Marriott points + Virtuoso free breakfast/upgrade).

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Points" Devaluation**: An airline announces that "Points prices will double" in 48 hours. The agent must scan all pending bookings and "Burn" the points before the deadline.
- **Status Match Failure**: A client moves to Delta, but the "Status Match" is rejected because of a previous match 3 years ago. The agent must appeal to the "Delta Sales Manager" for a "Manual Exception."
- **Upgrade Availability Change**: An agent books a "Class Y" ticket for a points upgrade, but the airline "Zeroes out" the upgrade inventory. The agent must "Waitlist" the upgrade and monitor it hourly.
- **The "Points" Brokerage Risk**: A client buys points from a "Grey Market" broker. The airline freezes the account. The agent must help the client "Regularize" the account without losing the flights.
