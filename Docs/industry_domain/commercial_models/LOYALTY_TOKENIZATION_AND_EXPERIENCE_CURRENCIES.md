# Loyalty Tokenization and Experience Currencies

This document explores new loyalty models using tokenized assets and experience-based currencies in travel.

## Overview
- Tokenization of loyalty points, credits, and experiential rewards.
- How experience currencies differ from traditional miles and points.
- The role of blockchain and ledger systems in travel loyalty.

## Product Models
- Tokenized loyalty that can be redeemed for experiences, upgrades, or access.
- Experience currencies for destination services and curated events.
- Hybrid models combining fiat, points, and tokenized rewards.

## Operational Design
- Managing currency creation, issuance, and redemption rules.
- Partner ecosystems for hospitality, transport, and events.
- Reconciliation, fraud detection, and expiry management.

## Commercial Implications
- New revenue streams from experiential access tokens.
- Differentiation through curated, specialty offers.
- Loyalty value perception and customer engagement.

## Future Trends
- Interoperable experience currencies across travel networks.
- Programmable loyalty and tokenized travel rights.
- Regulatory oversight for tokenized loyalty assets.
