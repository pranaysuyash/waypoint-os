# Travel Insurance and Risk Management

## Overview
Travel insurance and risk management are core components of agency service, especially for business travel, medical tourism, and high-risk itineraries.

## Insurance categories
- Trip cancellation/interruption insurance
- Medical evacuation and emergency medical insurance
- Liability and event insurance
- Supplier failure and insolvency protection

## Risk management practices
- Assess traveler risk profile at intake
- Recommend insurance products based on destination, traveler health, and trip complexity
- Document coverage requirements for corporate and group travel
- Coordinate claims support when disruptions occur

## Why this matters
Travel agencies must manage client expectations and protect themselves from liability. Insurance and risk protocols are essential for trusted, responsible travel delivery.
