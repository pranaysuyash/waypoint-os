# Creator Multi-Jurisdiction Compliance and Destination Regulatory Mappings

Creator travel programs move across borders and regulatory regimes. This document defines the compliance framework for multi-jurisdiction creator programs and the mapping of destination-specific regulatory requirements.

## 1. Jurisdictional scope

- Cross-border coverage: identify which creator activities span the creator’s home market, the destination, and any intermediary markets.
- Destination regulatory domains: local advertising, influencer marketing, tour operations, and event permitting.
- Traveler-facing rules: destination-specific consumer protection, privacy, and disclosure laws.

## 2. Regulatory mapping

- Rule catalog: maintain a living map of destination requirements for creator content, promotions, and bookings.
- Compliance flags: tag creator campaigns with required approvals, disclosures, and restricted content.
- Integration with operations: embed regulatory rules into campaign planning, partner onboarding, and content approvals.

## 3. Risk controls

- Pre-launch review: legal checks for new destination campaigns, sponsorship terms, and ad disclosures.
- Monitoring: detect destination-specific compliance breaches in creator content and partner promotions.
- Incident response: localized remediation plans, destination partner coordination, and regulatory notice handling.

## 4. Governance

- Policy framework: define when to use global creator policies vs destination-specific supplements.
- Training: educate creator managers, partners, and product teams on jurisdictional compliance risks.
- Audit readiness: preserve evidence of approvals, disclosures, and compliance decisions for each destination.
