# Domain Knowledge: EU Package Travel Directive (PTD)

**Category**: Regulatory & Compliance  
**Focus**: Consumer protections, insolvency insurance, and liability for European travelers.

---

## 1. Scope of the Directive

The EU **Package Travel Directive (2015/2302)** applies to any "Package" sold to a traveler in the European Union.

### What is a "Package"?
- A combination of at least two different types of travel services (e.g., Flight + Hotel, Hotel + Car Hire, or Tour + Flight).
- **Linked Travel Arrangements (LTA)**: A lighter version where the agency facilitates the booking of a second service from another provider within 24 hours.

---

## 2. Key Protections & Agency Liability

### Strict Liability
- **Logic**: The "Organizer" (the agency) is responsible for the performance of *all* services in the package, even those provided by third parties (airlines/hotels).
- **SOP**: If the hotel in Italy is overbooked, the agency MUST provide a comparable or better alternative at no extra cost.

### Insolvency Protection
- **Requirement**: Agencies MUST have insurance or a bond to ensure that if the agency goes bankrupt, the traveler is refunded and, if already on the trip, repatriated.
- **UK Equivalent**: **ATOL (Air Travel Organisers' Licensing)**.

---

## 3. Traveler Rights during Disruptions

### "Significant Changes"
- If the flight time changes by more than 3 hours or the hotel category is downgraded, the traveler has the right to:
    - Accept the change.
    - Accept an alternative.
    - **Cancel with a full refund within 14 days.**

### Price Increases
- Agencies can only increase the price (e.g., due to fuel costs) by a maximum of 8%. If it exceeds 8%, the traveler can cancel for free.

---

## 4. Operational SOP
1. **The "Standard Information Form"**: Agencies must provide a specific legal form *before* the contract is signed, explaining the traveler's rights.
2. **24/7 Assistance**: The agency must provide an emergency contact point for travelers during the trip.
3. **Refund Management**: In case of cancellation, refunds must be processed within **14 days**.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **Airline Bankruptcy**: An EU-based airline goes bust; the agency must repatriate all "Package" travelers at their own expense and then claim from their insolvency insurance.
- **Significant Flight Change**: A flight is moved from Morning to Evening; the agency must offer a full refund if the traveler refuses the change.
