# Creator Cross-Border Marketplace Regulatory Oversight

Creator marketplaces often move beyond a single jurisdiction. This document captures the regulatory oversight required when creator commerce, bookings, and content licensing cross borders.

## 1. Cross-border commerce rules

- Marketplace registration: intermediary obligations, VAT/GST registrations, digital services taxes.
- Consumer protection: cross-border refund rights, dispute resolution, jurisdiction clauses.
- Payment compliance: AML/KYC, sanctions screening, cross-border settlement reporting.

## 2. Platform responsibility

- Marketplace operator oversight: due diligence, supplier verification, dispute management.
- Content and advertising compliance: truth-in-advertising, disclosure across jurisdictions.
- Data transfer constraints: privacy law alignment, data residency, third-country transfers.

## 3. Creator-specific controls

- Creator disclosure obligations: sponsored travel, brand partnerships, affiliate earnings.
- Intellectual property rights: cross-border licensing, territory-specific usage rights.
- Tax and remittance: creator income reporting, withholding, and marketplace tax reporting.

## 4. Risk mitigation

- Regulatory incident response: cross-border notice, local counsel escalation, compliance remediation.
- Governance framework: marketplace policies, creator code of conduct, regulatory audit trails.
- Destination coordination: local authority engagement, permit reciprocity, cultural compliance.
