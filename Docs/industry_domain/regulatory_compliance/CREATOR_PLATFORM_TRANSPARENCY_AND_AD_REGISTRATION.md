# Creator Platform Transparency and Ad Registration

This document explains transparency and advertising registration requirements for creator content on travel platforms.

## Overview

- Creator content often functions as advertising and may require registration or disclosure under ad regulations.
- Travel platforms should be transparent about creator relationships, sources of compensation, and targeting methods.
- Compliance protects the app from regulatory actions and builds traveler trust.

## Transparency Requirements

- Disclosure: label creator promotions, sponsored content, and affiliate links.
- Source transparency: show when a creator is compensated or when content is algorithmically boosted.
- Targeting transparency: explain why creators or ads are shown to specific travelers, where required.
- Access to terms: make creator partnership policies and advertising rules available in the platform.

## Ad Registration

- Local rules: some jurisdictions require registration of digital ad campaigns or political-style promotions.
- Platform rules: comply with app store and social platform advertising policies for creator content.
- Travel-specific notices: disclose material terms for package travel, health-related offers, and invitation-only deals.
- Recordkeeping: retain copies of creator ads, timing, and targeting for audit purposes.

## Product Design

- Labeling: include clear markers for sponsored creator content in feeds, emails, and notifications.
- Consent: obtain traveler opt-in when required for personalized creator marketing.
- Reporting: provide auditors with data on creator ad performance, targeting, and disclosures.
- Governance: align creator transparency policies with platform advertising and privacy teams.
