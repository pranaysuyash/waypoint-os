# Domain Knowledge: Pandemic-Proof Architecture & Quarantine

**Category**: Frontier & Future Logistics  
**Focus**: Modular quarantine corridors and health-secure travel logistics.

---

## 1. Modular Quarantine Corridors
- **Logic**: Moving a traveler from a "Red Zone" to a "Green Zone" without physical contact with the general population.
- **SOP**: Use of **"Health-Secure FBOs"** (Fixed Base Operators) with HEPA-filtration and UV-C sterilization tunnels.

---

## 2. Real-Time Bio-Surveillance
- **Logic**: Travelers wear "Smart Patches" that monitor temperature, O2, and viral load.
- **SOP**: The agent monitors the **"Health Dashboard"**. If a traveler's vitals spike, the "Quarantine Extraction" protocol is triggered automatically.

---

## 3. "Air-Gap" Ground Transport
- **Logic**: Driverless, hermetically sealed vehicles that move the client from the jet to the hotel "Bio-Bubble."
- **SOP**: The vehicle is sterilized between every trip using a "Plasma Fogging" system.

---

## 4. Sovereignty Over Health Data
- **Logic**: Travelers own their health data (via ZKP) and only "Grant Access" to the border agent for 60 seconds.
- **SOP**: The agent coordinates the "Health Token" issuance.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Bio-Bubble" Breach**: A hotel staff member enters the client's "Bio-Bubble" without a mask. The agent must immediately "Reseal" the client in a different suite and perform a "Viral PCR" test.
- **Health Token Expiry**: A client's "Health Clearance" expires while they are in the air. The destination airport refuses landing. The agent must coordinate a "Mid-air Extension" via the health authority.
- **False Positive Crisis**: A traveler's smart patch gives a "False Positive" for a virus. The agent must coordinate an "Independent Lab Audit" while the client is held in the "FBO Quarantine Suite."
- **The "Red Zone" Closure**: A border closes while the client is inside. The agent must activate the **"Diplomatic Health Corridor"** to evacuate the client via a private medical jet.
