# Travel Data Mesh and Privacy Sandboxes

This document explores how the travel industry can adopt a data mesh architecture while preserving traveler privacy and regulatory compliance.

## Overview
- The travel data mesh concept for decentralized data ownership.
- Privacy sandboxes for sharing travel intelligence across partners.
- Benefits for airlines, agencies, hotels, and border systems.

## Components
- Domain-oriented data products for travelers, bookings, pricing, and risk.
- Governance at the mesh layer: policies, consent, and access control.
- Privacy sandbox mechanisms for aggregated analytics and secure collaboration.

## Operational Design
- Decoupled ownership across carriers, agencies, and suppliers.
- Semantic interoperability, federated identity, and metadata catalogs.
- Data quality, lineage, and observability for travel operations.

## Compliance and Privacy
- GDPR, CCPA, and cross-border privacy controls in travel data exchange.
- Anonymization, pseudonymization, and purpose-limited sharing.
- Sandboxing travel insights with audit trails and consent management.

## Future Trends
- Travel data mesh platforms for real-time disruption prediction.
- Privacy-preserving modeling for pricing and ancillary offers.
- Standards for interoperable travel data products.
