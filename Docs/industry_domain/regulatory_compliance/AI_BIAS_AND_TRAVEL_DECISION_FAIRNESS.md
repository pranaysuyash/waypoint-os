# AI Bias and Travel Decision Fairness

This document explores bias, fairness, and ethical governance for AI-powered travel decisions.

## Overview
- AI models influence pricing, upgrades, loyalty benefits, and itinerary recommendations.
- Bias can emerge from historical data, supplier mix, or demographic signals.
- Fairness is critical for compliance, trust, and brand protection.

## Sources of Bias
- Uneven training data from specific traveler segments.
- Supplier input bias reflected in offer selection and personalization.
- Hidden policy bias in automated rule systems.

## Governance
- Fairness metrics and monitoring for travel AI systems.
- Validation processes for pricing, disruption assistance, and concierge automation.
- Transparent disclosure of algorithmic decisions to customers and regulators.

## Operational Controls
- Model explainability for agent and traveler interactions.
- Escalation paths for disputed recommendations or denials.
- Data hygiene and feature controls for sensitive attributes.

## Future Trends
- Regulatory frameworks for AI fairness in travel service delivery.
- Certification programs for fair travel AI systems.
- Ethical design patterns for generative and recommendation-based travel assistants.
