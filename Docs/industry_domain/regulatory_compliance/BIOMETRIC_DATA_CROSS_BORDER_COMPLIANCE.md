# Biometric Data Cross-Border Compliance

This document explores the regulatory, operational, and privacy challenges of moving biometric traveler data across jurisdictions.

## Overview
- Biometric data as a travel credential and security layer.
- Cross-border transfer rules, consent regimes, and data localization.
- Stakeholders: airlines, border control, tech providers, and customers.

## Regulatory Landscape
- GDPR, China PIPL, and regional biometric controls.
- Data sharing agreements between visa systems and carriers.
- Passport data versus commercial biometric identity.

## Operational Implications
- Traveler onboarding and consent flows.
- Data retention, access logs, and audit trails.
- Interface with watchlists, risk screening, and passenger name records.

## Security and Privacy Controls
- Encryption, tokenization, and biometric hashing.
- Third-party processors and vendor risk.
- Breach response, notification, and remediation.

## Industry Trends
- Interoperable biometric identity standards.
- Privacy-preserving travel biometrics.
- Commercial use cases for expedited border crossing.
