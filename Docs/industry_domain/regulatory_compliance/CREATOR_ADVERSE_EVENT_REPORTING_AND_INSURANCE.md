# Creator Adverse Event Reporting and Insurance

This document describes how travel apps handle adverse events tied to creator-led travel products and manage insurance obligations.

## Overview

- Creator-led travel experiences can introduce unique risk exposures that require formal reporting and insurance controls.
- Travel apps should define adverse event workflows for incidents, safety issues, and partner claims.
- Insurance planning protects the app, travelers, creators, and supplier ecosystem.

## Reporting Framework

- Incident capture: record event details, affected travelers, creators, suppliers, and location.
- Severity triage: classify incidents by safety impact, financial loss, and regulatory risk.
- Notification protocol: inform travelers, creators, insurers, and internal risk teams promptly.
- Resolution tracking: manage follow-up actions, refunds, repairs, or remediations.

## Insurance Considerations

- Coverage mapping: align creator products with appropriate liability, cancellation, and adventure insurance.
- Policy clarity: make insurance requirements explicit in creator contracts and traveler disclosures.
- Claims support: provide documentation and evidence to insurers for creator-related incidents.
- Supplier vetting: use insurance status as part of supplier credentialing for creator experiences.

## Product Controls

- Risk badges: label creator experiences with required insurance or safety classifications.
- Traveler disclosures: explain what is covered, what is excluded, and how to report problems.
- Emergency access: provide travelers with rapid contact information and support for creator trips.
- Event analytics: track incident rates by creator, destination, and experience type to inform future curation.

## Business Outcomes

- Better trust: travelers feel safer booking creator-led experiences when adverse event protocols are clear.
- Lower liability: documented workflows reduce the app’s exposure from creator and supplier issues.
- Resilient creator network: creators are more likely to partner with platforms that manage risk responsibly.
- Compliance readiness: prepare for regulatory scrutiny and insurance audits.
