# Domain Knowledge: Regenerative Travel & Ethical Insetting

**Category**: Advanced Security & Ethical Systems  
**Focus**: The shift from "Sustainability" (Do No Harm) to "Regeneration" (Actively Heal).

---

## 1. Regenerative vs. Sustainable
- **Sustainable**: Using less plastic, saving water.
- **Regenerative**: The destination is **"Better"** because the traveler visited.
- **SOP**: The agent includes a **"Legacy Contribution"** in the trip price (e.g., funding a specific coral reef nursery or a school's 10-year teacher salary).

---

## 2. Ethical "Insetting"
- **Logic**: Moving away from "Offsets" (buying carbon credits in a forest far away).
- **Insetting**: Investing directly in the **"Supply Chain"** of the destination (e.g., the hotel installing a solar farm that also powers the local village).

---

## 3. "Over-tourism" Mitigation (De-marketing)
- **SOP**: The agent actively **"De-markets"** popular destinations (Venice, Santorini) and steers clients to "Under-visited" regions with "Fragile but Resilient" ecosystems.

---

## 4. Indigenous Knowledge "Royalties"
- **Logic**: If a trip involves visiting indigenous lands or learning from elders, a "Royalty" is paid directly to the community fund, not just a "Tip" to the guide.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Performative" Greenwash**: A hotel claims to be "Eco-friendly" because they don't wash towels, but they dump sewage into the ocean. The agent must conduct a **"Supply Chain Audit"** and "Blacklist" the property.
- **Regenerative Impact Failure**: A client pays for "Tree Planting," but the trees are an invasive species that kills the local ecosystem. The agent must use **"Ecological Experts"** to verify all regenerative projects.
- **The "De-marketing" Pushback**: A client *insists* on visiting a closed-for-rehabilitation site (e.g., Maya Bay). The agent must "Say No" and provide a "Better, Ethical Alternative."
- **Community Royalty Dispute**: A local guide claims the agency's "Community Fund" isn't reaching the village. The agent must perform a **"Financial Audit"** of the local DMC.
