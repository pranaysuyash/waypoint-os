# Creator Accessibility and Inclusive Design Compliance

This document explains accessibility and inclusive design compliance for creator-led travel experiences.

## Overview

- Creator travel products should be inclusive and accessible to a broad range of travelers.
- Compliance includes physical accessibility, communication accessibility, and inclusive product design.
- This ensures creator experiences are safe, welcoming, and legally compliant.

## Accessibility Requirements

- Physical access: verify that accommodations, venues, and activities support wheelchair access, mobility needs, and sensory considerations.
- Communication access: provide captioning, translation, and accessible content for creators and travelers.
- Inclusive design: design creator products for diverse traveler identities, ages, and ability levels.
- Legal compliance: align with laws like ADA, EU accessibility directives, and destination-specific accessibility requirements.

## Product Practices

- Tagging: label creator experiences with accessibility features and restrictions.
- Inclusive filters: let travelers search for accessible creator experiences and inclusive community offerings.
- Creator guidance: educate creators on inclusive messaging and experience delivery.
- Feedback: collect traveler feedback on accessibility and inclusion to improve future creator products.
