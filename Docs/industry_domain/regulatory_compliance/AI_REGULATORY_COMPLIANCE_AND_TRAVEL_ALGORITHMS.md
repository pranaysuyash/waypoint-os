This document explores the regulatory frameworks that govern AI-driven travel algorithms and decision systems.

## Overview
- AI in travel planning, pricing, and disruption response.
- Regulatory expectations for transparency, fairness, and accountability.
- Cross-border AI compliance in airlines, agencies, and platforms.

## Regulatory Landscape

- Explainability requirements for automated pricing and inventory decisions.
- Privacy, bias mitigation, and consumer protection.

## Operational Implications

- Monitoring for unfair outcomes in itinerary generation and upgrades.
- Audit trails and compliance controls for travel AI.

## Supplier and Partner Risks

- Regulatory liability allocation across the travel ecosystem.

## Future Trends
