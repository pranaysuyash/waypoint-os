# Creator Community Governance and Content Policy

This document describes the governance structures and content policies that travel apps use for creator communities.

## Overview

- Creator community governance ensures that creator content aligns with platform values, safety standards, and brand guidelines.
- Governance defines what creators may publish, how disputes are handled, and which behavior is rewarded or penalized.
- Strong governance supports healthy creator ecosystems and reduces operational friction.

## Governance Components

- Code of conduct: establish acceptable creator behavior and content standards.
- Editorial policy: define approval criteria for creator posts, product claims, and destination narratives.
- Moderation process: set roles for automated filters, editorial review, and appeals.
- Incentive policy: reward creators who comply with governance and contribute high-quality, trustworthy content.

## Community Structures

- Creator councils: representative groups that advise on policy, trends, and product fit.
- Trusted creator programs: designate creators with higher privileges and content autonomy.
- Dispute resolution: provide transparent processes for creator disagreements, takedown requests, and policy violations.
- Community health metrics: measure trust, compliance rate, and creator retention.

## Product Implications

- Use governance status to qualify creators for premium placements and exclusive campaigns.
- Display policy compliance as a trust signal to travelers.
- Offer creators templates, briefs, and examples that simplify governance adherence.
- Keep governance rules visible in creator portals and onboarding documentation.

## Operational Risk Control

- Review governance outcomes regularly to address emerging abuse or brand drift.
- Balance enforcement with support: help creators understand rules rather than only punishing breaches.
- Align governance with legal, regulatory, and destination-specific requirements.
- Maintain an escalation path for serious violations and platform-level risks.
