# Internal Workflows and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

How an agency moves a lead from "Inquiry" to "Departure" and "Recovery".

---

## 1. The Sales Funnel (Pre-Booking)

### Step 1: Qualification (The "Front Door")
- **Action**: Check if the traveler is a fit.
- **Criteria**: Budget vs. Destination feasibility, Intent (Price Shopper?), Number of travelers.
- **AI Role**: Automate initial data collection via WhatsApp/Forms.

### Step 2: Quotation Iteration
- **Action**: Researching and refining options.
- **Criteria**: Matching preferences to inventory.
- **SOP**: First quote sent within 2 hours of inquiry. Maximum 3 revisions before "Planning Fee" conversation.

### Step 3: Closing & Deposit
- **Action**: Converting intent to cash.
- **Criteria**: Securing inventory before it expires.
- **SOP**: "Courtesy Hold" for flights/hotels (if available).

---

## 2. The Operational Loop (Post-Booking)

### Step 4: Fulfillment
- **Action**: Booking with vendors.
- **SOP**: Verify traveler names match Passports exactly. Double-check cancellation policies before final payment.

### Step 5: Vouchering & Documentation
- **Action**: Preparing the traveler for departure.
- **SOP**: Vouchers sent 7 days before departure. Visa check performed 30 days before.

### Step 6: On-Trip Monitoring
- **Action**: Proactive support.
- **SOP**: Check flight status 4 hours before departure. Message traveler "Welcome to [Destination]" on arrival.

---

## 3. Escalation & Exception SOPs

### SOP: Hotel Overbooking
1. **Detect**: Traveler reports hotel doesn't have the booking.
2. **Action**: Contact local DMC/Supplier immediately.
3. **Resolution**: Secure alternative hotel of "Equal or Higher" category.
4. **Compensation**: Offer complimentary dinner or airport transfer for the inconvenience.

### SOP: Medical Emergency
1. **Detect**: Traveler/Family reports illness.
2. **Action**: Identify nearest "International Standards" hospital.
3. **Coordination**: Contact Insurance provider. Inform family (S2).
4. **Logistics**: Arrange transport/hospital visit.
