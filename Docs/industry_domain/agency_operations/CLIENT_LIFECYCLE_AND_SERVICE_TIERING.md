# Client Lifecycle and Service Tiering for Travel Agencies

## Overview
Travel agencies support different customer segments with distinct service levels. This doc covers how client journeys and service tiers map to agency operations.

## Typical client lifecycle stages
1. Prospect / inquiry
2. Qualification and needs assessment
3. Proposal and booking
4. Trip delivery and support
5. Post-trip follow-up and loyalty

## Service tiers
- Self-service / digital concierge
- Standard travel agent support
- Premium concierge and white-glove service
- Corporate account management

## Role of service tiers
- Defines response time commitments
- Determines the depth of itinerary validation
- Shapes pricing and retainer models
- Guides escalation rules for disruptions

## Why this matters
Understanding the client lifecycle and service tiers helps agencies route requests correctly, align staffing, and package the right level of care for each customer segment.
