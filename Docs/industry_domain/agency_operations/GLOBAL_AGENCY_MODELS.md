# Domain Knowledge: Global Agency Business Models

**Category**: Agency Operations  
**Focus**: Host Agencies, Independent Contractors (ICs), and Retail vs. TMC.

---

## 1. The North American Model (US/Canada)

The US market is heavily dominated by **Independent Contractors (ICs)** and **Host Agencies**.

### Host Agencies
- **Role**: A large, IATA-accredited agency that "Hosts" thousands of independent agents.
- **Benefits**: The Host provides the IATA number, GDS access, and supplier contracts. The IC pays a monthly fee or a commission split (e.g., 80/20).
- **Major Players**: Avoya, Virtuoso, Signature Travel Network.

### Independent Contractors (ICs)
- **Role**: Small "Agencies of One" who work from home.
- **SOP**: They use the Host's "Back-Office" for CRM and commission tracking.

---

## 2. The European/Global Model

### Retail Agencies (Brick-and-Mortar)
- **Focus**: High-street presence. High overhead, high trust.
- **Trend**: Shifting toward "Homeworking" networks similar to the US model.

### TMC (Travel Management Companies)
- **Focus**: Corporate B2B (e.g., AMEX GBT, CWT, BCD Travel).
- **Complexity**: Managing "SLA" (Service Level Agreements) and "Reporting" for Fortune 500 companies.

---

## 3. The Indian/Asian Model

### Sub-Agency Networks
- **Role**: Large "B2B Consolidators" (e.g., TBO) provide APIs to thousands of "Non-IATA" sub-agents in Tier 2/3 cities.
- **Payment Flow**: Sub-agents use a "Wallet" system where they top up cash and book tickets using the Consolidator's IATA number.

---

## 4. Operational Best Practices
- **Credentialing**: Always verify which "Consolidator" or "Host" is being used, as this determines the "Commission Tier" and "Support Priority."
- **Markup Logic**: In a Sub-agency model, the system must allow for "Double Markups" (Consolidator -> Sub-agent -> Client).
- **Liability**: Clearly define who owns the "Merchant of Record" (MoR) responsibility — the IC or the Host.

---

## 5. Summary Table

| Model | IATA Owner | MoR Responsibility | Main Revenue |
|-------|------------|--------------------|--------------|
| Host/IC | Host | Host (usually) | Commission Split |
| Sub-Agent | Consolidator | Sub-Agent | Markup |
| Full Agency | Own | Own | Commission + Fees |
