# Creator Hired Guide Management and Payroll

This document explains how travel apps can manage creator-hired guides, instructors, and local talent alongside payroll and contractor compliance.

## Overview

- Creator-led travel experiences often require hiring local guides, instructors, or specialists.
- Apps need structured management and payment workflows for these creator-connected service providers.
- Proper payroll and compliance reduce risk, improve quality, and protect travelers.

## Guide Management

- Qualification: verify guide credentials, local knowledge, and language skills.
- Scheduling: align guide availability with creator event dates and traveler itineraries.
- Training: brief guides on creator-specific product standards and traveler expectations.
- Communication: provide central channels for real-time coordination during experiences.

## Payroll and Payments

- Contractor status: classify hired guides correctly for tax and labor compliance.
- Payment terms: establish rates, advance payments, and insurance or cancellation policies.
- Invoicing: standardize guide invoices linked to creator events and traveler bookings.
- Dispute resolution: manage disputes over guide performance, hours worked, or deliverables.

## Product Implications

- Traveler confidence: show that creator experiences include vetted, compensated local partners.
- Creator usage: creators can more easily launch experiences when guide logistics are handled by the app.
- Quality control: track guide performance via traveler feedback and creator reviews.
- Cost transparency: model guide costs within creator product economics.
