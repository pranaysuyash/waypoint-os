# Agency Roles and Responsibilities

In a modern, tech-enabled travel agency, roles are divided between customer-facing sales and back-office operations.

---

## 1. Primary Roles

### Agency Owner (P2)
- **Focus**: Business growth, supplier relations, margin oversight.
- **Responsibilities**: 
    - Managing high-value partnerships.
    - Setting margin targets and discounting policies.
    - Resolving major customer escalations.
    - Reviewing agency performance metrics.

### Senior Travel Agent / Team Lead
- **Focus**: Complex itineraries, mentoring, quality control.
- **Responsibilities**:
    - Building multi-destination/high-complexity quotes.
    - Reviewing quotes generated by Junior Agents.
    - Handling "Recovery" phases for major disruptions.
    - Knowledge management (training juniors).

### Junior Travel Agent (P3) / Travel Consultant
- **Focus**: Lead intake, basic quotation, document collection.
- **Responsibilities**:
    - Extracting traveler requirements from leads.
    - Researching hotels/flights based on preferences.
    - Collecting passports and processing initial payments.
    - Following up with travelers for feedback.

---

## 2. Operations & Specialized Roles

### Operations Executive (The "Fulfillment" Desk)
- **Focus**: Booking execution and vouchers.
- **Responsibilities**:
    - Confirming inventory with suppliers once payment is received.
    - Issuing vouchers and travel documents.
    - Monitoring flight schedules and visa statuses.
    - Handling "Fulfillment" errors (e.g., failed payment to supplier).

### Accounts & Reconciliation Officer
- **Focus**: Financial integrity.
- **Responsibilities**:
    - Reconciling payments from multiple parties (S2 cases).
    - Managing GST/TCS compliance.
    - Processing supplier payments and agent commissions.
    - Managing refunds and credit notes.

### Tech/Platform Lead (In AI-Enabled Agencies)
- **Focus**: Tooling and automation.
- **Responsibilities**:
    - Configuring the AI Agent's decision logic and "Safety Rails".
    - Monitoring AI-to-Human handoff rates.
    - Managing API integrations with GDS and CRMs.

---

## 3. Interaction Matrix

| Task | Lead | Support | Approval |
|------|------|---------|----------|
| Lead Intake | Junior Agent | AI Agent | - |
| Complex Quoting | Senior Agent | AI Agent | Owner |
| Booking Execution | Ops Executive | Junior Agent | Senior Agent |
| Disruption Recovery | Senior Agent | Ops Executive | Owner (if budget > $X) |
| Reconciliation | Accounts | Ops Executive | Owner |
