# Domain Knowledge: Indirect Supply Chain Roles

**Category**: Roles & Human Capital  
**Focus**: The external partners who enable the agency to function.

---

## 1. Consolidator / Wholesaler Desk

- **Role**: The "Middle Man" between the airline and the small agency.
- **Why they exist**: They buy thousands of seats upfront and resell them to agencies who don't have their own IATA license.
- **Agency Interaction**: The agent calls the "Consolidator Support Desk" to fix PNR errors or request a "Manual Fare."

---

## 2. DMC Operations (Destination Management Company)

- **Role**: The "Ground Fixer."
- **Why they exist**: They have the local relationships (Drivers, Guides, Hotels).
- **Agency Interaction**: The agent emails the **"DMC Ops Manager"** to customize a tour or handle a "Last-minute" change in the destination city.

---

## 3. Airline Sales Manager (ASM)

- **Role**: The relationship owner at the airline.
- **Why they exist**: To drive "Market Share" for their airline.
- **Agency Interaction**: The agent calls the ASM to **"Waiver"** a cancellation fee or **"Clear a Waitlist"** for a VIP (S1) client.

---

## 4. GDS Helpdesk (Help Desk Support)

- **Role**: Technical support for Amadeus/Sabre/Travelport.
- **Agency Interaction**: When the GDS command `FXP/R,U` returns an error, the agent calls the GDS Helpdesk to understand the "Technical Logic" failure.

---

## 5. Travel Insurance Underwriter / Assistance Team

- **Role**: The people who pay for the MedEvac.
- **Agency Interaction**: During a crisis, the agent coordinates between the **"Assistance Team"** (Doctors/Logistics) and the traveler.

---

## 6. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "ASM Savior"**: A VIP is stuck at the airport with a cancelled flight. Every other flight is full. The agent calls the ASM to "Force-seat" the VIP on the next flight.
- **DMC Failure**: A guide in Peru doesn't show up. The agent must bypass the "Guide" and talk to the **"DMC Ops Director"** to get a replacement in 30 minutes.
- **Consolidator Ticketing Delay**: The consolidator is slow to issue a ticket, and the fare expires. The agency must hold the consolidator liable for the price difference.
- **GDS System Outage**: The GDS is down globally. The agent must use "Direct Supplier Portals" and manual "Offline" workflows to keep the business running.
