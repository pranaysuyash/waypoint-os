# Domain Knowledge: MICE & Group Logistics

**Category**: Agency Operations  
**Focus**: Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions.

---

## 1. Group Flight Logistics (10+ Travelers)

### Group Block Bookings
- **Logic**: Airlines allow booking blocks of 10+ seats without names initially.
- **Deposit**: A small fee (e.g., $50 per seat) is paid to hold the space.
- **Manifest**: Names must be submitted 30-45 days before departure.
- **SOP**: Monitor the "Utilization" (if the group is too small, release seats before the penalty date).

---

## 2. Incentive Travel (The "Luxury Incentive")

### The Goal
- **Logic**: Companies reward top performers with "Money-can't-buy" experiences.
- **SOP**: Higher service density. Requires "On-site Coordination" (the agent travels with the group).

---

## 3. Financial Reconciliation (The "Master Account")

### M-Folio (Master Folio)
- **Logic**: The company pays for "Common" costs (Rooms, Buffet, Meeting Space).
- **Incidentals**: Individual travelers pay for "Private" costs (Mini-bar, Spa, Laundry).
- **SOP**: The hotel must be instructed to "Split the Folio" at check-in.

---

## 4. Room Block Management (ROB)

### Cut-off Dates
- **Logic**: Hotels hold a block of rooms (e.g., 50 rooms) until a specific date (usually 30 days prior).
- **Wash Factor**: Historical data shows that 10-15% of group travelers cancel at the last minute.
- **SOP**: Adjust the block size dynamically to avoid "Attrition Penalties" (paying for empty rooms).

---

## 5. Event Tech Integration
- **Registration Portals**: Custom websites where attendees input their flight info and dietary needs.
- **Communication**: Using SMS/WhatsApp broadcasts for "Schedule Changes" during the event.

---

## 6. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Attrition Disaster"**: Only 40 people register for a 100-room block. The agency must negotiate with the hotel to reduce the penalty.
- **Flight Delay Chain**: The "Keynote Speaker" is delayed on their flight. The agent must re-arrange the entire event schedule.
- **Manifest Error**: A group name is misspelled on a non-changeable ticket. The agent must use "GDS Waiver" codes or negotiate with the airline's "Group Desk."
