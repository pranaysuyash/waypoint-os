# Creator App Ambassador Onboarding and Incentive Programs

This document explains how travel apps onboard creator ambassadors and design incentive programs.

## Overview

- Ambassadors bridge creator communities and the travel platform.
- Structured onboarding and incentives help creators adopt the app, promote products, and stay engaged.
- Incentive programs need to be transparent, measurable, and aligned with strategic goals.

## Onboarding Steps

- Recruitment: identify creators whose audience, content style, and destination focus fit the app.
- Training: provide creator onboarding materials, brand guidelines, and product playbooks.
- Certification: certify creators for quality, safety, and platform compliance.
- Enablement: give creators tools, dashboards, and content assets to accelerate launch.

## Incentive Design

- Performance rewards: commission tiers based on bookings, engagement, or retention.
- Activation bonuses: upfront incentives for launching new creator campaigns or packages.
- Community incentives: rewards for creator referrals, community growth, or content co-creation.
- Long-term programs: membership benefits, exclusive access, and compound earnings for top ambassadors.

## Operational Controls

- Metrics: track creator success, quality, and program ROI.
- Review cadence: periodically evaluate ambassador fit, performance, and compliance.
- Governance: maintain clear terms for payout eligibility, content standards, and partnership renewal.
- Feedback loops: incorporate creator input into product, pricing, and marketplace strategy.
