# Domain Knowledge: Sports Team & Athlete Logistics

**Category**: Agency Operations  
**Focus**: Professional sports, equipment management, and athlete welfare.

---

## 1. Athlete Welfare & Physiology

### The "Jet Lag" SOP
- **Logic**: For professional athletes (S1), arrival times are calculated to minimize circadian disruption.
- **SOP**: Book "Day Flights" for Westbound and "Night Flights" for Eastbound. Confirm "Flat Bed" seats for all athletes >185cm.

### Dietary Precision
- **Logic**: Team nutritionists dictate the hotel menu.
- **SOP**: Verify the "Private Dining Room" (PDR) and "Buffet Specs" (e.g., no cross-contamination, specific protein sources).

---

## 2. Equipment & Oversized Baggage

### The "Kit" Management
- **Logic**: Teams travel with "Kit" (Uniforms), medical gear, and training equipment (e.g., exercise bikes).
- **SOP**: Advance coordination with the "Airport Cargo Manager" to ensure everything is on the same plane as the team.

---

## 3. Privacy & "Fan Management"

### The "Secure Floor"
- **Logic**: Fans and paparazzi will try to book the same hotel.
- **SOP**:
    - Use "Blind Bookings" (no team name in the hotel system).
    - Block a dedicated "Equipment Room" on the same floor as the team.
    - Coordinate with the hotel's "Loss Prevention" manager for a "Private Entrance."

---

## 4. Charter vs. Commercial

- **Small Teams (e.g., Tennis/Golf)**: Commercial First/Business.
- **Large Teams (e.g., Football)**: Private Charters (e.g., Boeing 737 VIP).
- **SOP**: The agent must manage the "Charter Manifest" and "Weight & Balance" with the pilot.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **Kit Delay**: The team's uniforms and medical gear are stuck in a layover. The match is tomorrow. The agent must charter a "Small Cargo Plane" to move the gear.
- **Nutritional Failure**: The hotel chef serves a meal that contains an allergen (or a banned substance in the "WADA" list).
- **Privacy Breach**: Fans find the team hotel and are crowding the lobby. The agent must move the team to a "Secondary Secret Hotel."
- **Size Mismatch**: A basketball team is booked on a plane where the seats are too small for the 7ft athletes.
