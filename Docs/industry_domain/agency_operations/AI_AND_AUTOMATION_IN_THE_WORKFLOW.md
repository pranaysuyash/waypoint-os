# Domain Knowledge: AI & Automation in the Workflow

**Category**: Agency Operations  
**Focus**: Agentic workflows, hyper-personalization, and real-time disruption handling.

---

## 1. The "Augmented Agent" Model

In 2026, the agent is an orchestrator of multiple AI systems.

### The Stack
- **NLP (Natural Language Processing)**: For parsing messy client requests (e.g., "I want a beach vibe but not too touristy").
- **Predictive Analytics**: Forecasting price drops or predicting "Connection Risks" based on real-time airport congestion data.

---

## 2. Hyper-Personalization (The "Segment of One")

### The "Knowledge Graph"
- **Logic**: Moving beyond "Likes beach" to "Prefers low-acidity coffee, pillows with <10% down, and 6:30 AM gym access."
- **SOP**: The agent must maintain a **Dynamic Traveler Profile** that learns from every post-trip feedback.

---

## 3. Real-Time Disruption "Auto-Recovery"

- **Logic**: If a flight is delayed 30 mins, the AI automatically notifies the driver, the hotel (for late check-in), and the restaurant.
- **SOP**: The agent must monitor the **"Event Stream"** and only intervene when the AI cannot find a deterministic solution.

---

## 4. The "Trust" Guardrail

- **Logic**: AI can hallucinate "Ghost Flights" or "Closed Hotels."
- **SOP**: Every AI-generated itinerary must be **"Human-in-the-loop" (HITL)** verified against the GDS "Source of Truth" before being sent to the client.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Hallucinated Hotel"**: The AI suggests a "Stunning Cliffside Resort" that actually closed in 2024. The agent must catch this before the client pays.
- **Over-Automation Crisis**: The AI automatically re-books a delayed passenger on a new flight, but the passenger wanted to stay overnight and see the city. The agent must "Undo" the automation and restore the client's choice.
- **Data Privacy Breach**: The AI "Personalization Engine" accidentally includes one client's medical needs in another client's itinerary.
- **Disruption Loop**: The AI tries to re-book a cancelled flight, but because it doesn't understand "Minimum Connection Time" (MCT), it books an impossible 15-minute layover.
