# Creator Travel Ecosystem Map

This document defines the core components and value flows in a creator-led travel ecosystem. It is designed to guide research on how creators, travelers, suppliers, and technology interlock.

## 1. Core participants

- Creator: content creator, influencer, host, or brand with travel audience influence.
- Traveler: audience member, fan, or community participant booking creator experiences.
- Platform: the app or travel company orchestrating creator discovery, booking, and fulfillment.
- Supplier: destination partner, hotel, F&B provider, transport operator, local guide.
- Commerce partner: payment provider, marketplace partner, affiliate channel.

## 2. Value exchange flows

- Demand creation: creators generate intent through content, live events, and social commerce.
- Discovery: the platform surfaces creator-aligned experiences, supplier bundles, and trust signals.
- Booking and fulfillment: travelers purchase creator experiences, suppliers commit inventory, and operations manage delivery.
- Revenue sharing: creators, suppliers, and the platform split fees, commissions, and incentives.
- Feedback: traveler reviews, creator performance signals, and supplier quality data feed back into the system.

## 3. Supporting systems

- Creator lifecycle: onboarding, verification, brand safety review, contract management, payouts.
- Content supply: creator-produced itineraries, destination narratives, experience assets, and digital products.
- Regulatory operations: visa, permit, labor, onsite event compliance, and destination taxation.
- Metrics and analytics: creator ROI, conversion, retention, and community health.
- Trust network: reputation, ratings, moderation, and content compliance.

## 4. Strategic nodes

- Creator-curated inventory: destination or product bundles that are packaged around a creator’s brand.
- Creator marketplaces: discovery layers where travelers browse creator collections and collaborations.
- Creator merchant of record: structures for payment, liability, and revenue recognition in creator travel commerce.
- Post-trip content loop: re-use of creator content, community engagement, loyalty, and retargeting.

## 5. Research questions

- How does creator demand reshape traditional travel packaging and supplier sourcing?
- When is a creator a supplier, and when are they a distribution layer?
- What operational model supports creator-led experiences without fragmenting delivery?
- How should platform incentives balance creator revenue with travel quality and supplier fairness?
