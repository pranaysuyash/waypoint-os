# Creator Product Pricing Strategy and Value Packaging

This document explains pricing strategy and value packaging for creator-led travel products.

## Overview

- Creator products should be priced to reflect branded premium value while remaining accessible.
- Value packaging bundles creator experiences, local services, and exclusive content into differentiated offers.
- Strong pricing aligns creator incentives, supplier economics, and traveler expectations.

## Pricing Strategy

- Premium positioning: charge more for creator experiences that deliver unique access, VIP treatment, or community immersion.
- Tiered offers: create standard, premium, and elite versions of creator products with different levels of service.
- Anchoring: use high-value bundles to make mid-tier creator offers feel more affordable.
- Dynamic pricing: adjust creator product prices around creator demand, campaign timing, and inventory availability.

## Value Packaging

- Inclusive bundles: combine accommodations, experiences, meet-ups, and digital creator content into one package.
- Add-on modularity: let travelers customize creator journeys with optional upgrades and add-ons.
- Social packages: package creator community access, group travel, and loyalty benefits together.
- Outcome-focused packaging: tie value to unique traveler outcomes like creative retreats, wellness resets, or destination mastery.

## Product Implications

- Clear comparison: make it easy for travelers to compare creator packages by feature, price, and expected experience.
- Creator economics: ensure pricing supports creator payouts, supplier margins, and platform fees.
- Feedback loop: use post-purchase feedback to refine pricing and packaging over time.
- Transparency: clearly state what is included in each creator package and what is optional.
