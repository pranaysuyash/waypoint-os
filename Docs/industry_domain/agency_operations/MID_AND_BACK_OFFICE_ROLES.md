# Domain Knowledge: Mid & Back Office Roles

**Category**: Roles & Human Capital  
**Focus**: The "Internal Engine" that handles ticketing, auditing, and financials.

---

## 1. Mid-Office: The "Quality Control" (QC) Layer

While the Front Office sells, the Mid-Office ensures the PNR is "Clean."

### Roles
- **Ticketing Specialist**: The master of the GDS "Command Line." They issue the tickets, handle complex "Re-issues," and manage "Void" windows.
- **QC Auditor**: Reviews PNRs for missing fields (e.g., Passport info, SSR codes, APIS) before the file moves to ticketing.
- **Refund Clerk**: Handles the "BSP Link" for cancellations, fighting for cash refunds over vouchers.

---

## 2. Back-Office: The "Financial" Layer

Responsible for the agency's actual bank account and supplier settlements.

### Roles
- **BSP Coordinator (Bank Settlement Plan)**: Reconciles the massive weekly payment to IATA for all tickets issued.
- **Commission Recovery Agent**: The "Bounty Hunter." They track unpaid hotel/car commissions, often chasing hotels for months after the trip.
- **Accounts Payable (Supplier Desk)**: Pays the DMCs and wholesalers. Manages VCN (Virtual Card Number) generation.

---

## 3. Product & Contract Managers

- **Role**: They don't talk to travelers. They talk to hotels.
- **Tasks**: Loading "Negotiated Rates" into the agency's CMS; negotiating "Amenity Packages" (e.g., "Agency gets 4th night free").

---

## 4. The "Tech Admin" (GDS Admin)

- **Role**: Manages the GDS "Office ID" (OID).
- **Tasks**: Setting up "Queues," managing "User Sign-ins," and ensuring the "PCC" (Pseudo City Code) is secure.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Ticketing Timebomb"**: A Front Office agent promises a price, but the Ticketing Specialist finds the "Fare Rule" has expired. The agency must "Eat the loss."
- **Commission Leakage**: The agency realizes it is missing $20,000 in commissions from a specific hotel chain. The Commission Recovery Agent must perform a "Bulk Audit."
- **OID Breach**: A former employee still has access to the GDS OID. The Tech Admin must perform an "Emergency Lockout."
- **BSP Mismatch**: The weekly IATA bill is $5,000 more than the agency's records. The BSP Coordinator must find the "Ghost PNR."
