# Domain Knowledge: Luxury Train Operations

**Category**: Agency Operations  
**Focus**: Heritage rail, cabin logistics, and multi-border rail travel.

---

## 1. The "Golden Age" Rail Inventory

Luxury trains (e.g., *Venice Simplon-Orient-Express*, *The Blue Train*, *Maharajas' Express*) operate like moving hotels.

### Booking Windows
- **Logic**: These trains often sell out 12–18 months in advance for peak routes.
- **SOP**: The agent must maintain a "Waitlist Request" in the train's proprietary portal.

---

## 2. Cabin Configuration & Logistics

### The "Sleeper" Reality
- **Logic**: Cabin sizes vary wildly. Some don't have en-suite showers.
- **SOP**:
    - **Cabin Audit**: Verify if the cabin has a "Private Shower" vs. "Shared Facilities."
    - **Electrical Power**: Confirming voltage/plug types for CPAP machines or medical devices (often limited on heritage rail).

---

## 3. Baggage Restrictions

- **Logic**: Unlike planes, trains have very narrow corridors and limited cabin space.
- **SOP**:
    - **Baggage Transfer**: Most luxury trains require "Checked Baggage" to be sent to the arrival city via a separate truck/van.
    - **"Overnight Bag" SOP**: The traveler must prepare a small bag for the cabin while their large luggage is inaccessible.

---

## 4. Border Crossings on Rail

- **Logic**: Crossing borders at 2 AM on a train requires the "Passport Collection" SOP.
- **SOP**: The "Train Manager" collects all passports at the start of the journey to handle customs while guests sleep.

---

## 5. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Power Outage"**: A traveler's CPAP machine stops working at 3 AM because the train's vintage battery system failed.
- **The "Baggage Mismatch"**: A client's large luggage is sent to the wrong city by the train's transfer service.
- **Missed Departure**: A client is delayed by a flight and misses the once-weekly departure of the train. The agent must find a "Catch-up" point (e.g., a helicopter to the next station).
- **Cabin Downgrade**: A client pays for a "Grand Suite" but due to a mechanical issue, they are moved to a "Twin Cabin." The agent must negotiate the massive refund.
