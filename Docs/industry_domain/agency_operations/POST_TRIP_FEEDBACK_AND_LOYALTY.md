# Domain Knowledge: Post-Trip Feedback & Loyalty

**Category**: Agency Operations  
**Focus**: CSAT (Customer Satisfaction), NPS (Net Promoter Score), and lifetime value.

---

## 1. The "Welcome Home" Protocol

The service doesn't end when the plane lands.

### Timing
- **The "Soft Touch"**: SMS/WhatsApp 4 hours after landing. "Welcome home! Hope the flights were smooth."
- **The "Formal Survey"**: 48-72 hours after landing. "How was your stay at [Hotel Name]?"

---

## 2. Capturing "Trip Intelligence"

Every feedback is an asset for future bookings.

### Key Data Points to Extract
- **Room Number Preference**: "I loved room 502, but the elevator was noisy." (Save to CRM).
- **Service Gaps**: "The breakfast service was slow." (Add a "Warning Flag" for this hotel in the internal database).
- **Aha Moments**: "The private picnic was the highlight." (Suggest similar activities for the next trip).

---

## 3. The "CSAT-to-Google" Bridge

### The "Amaze" Review Logic
- **Logic**: If the CSAT score is 9 or 10, automatically send a link to the "Google Business Profile" or "Trustpilot" to capture a public review.
- **Incentive**: Many agencies offer a small "Future Trip Credit" for detailed public reviews.

---

## 4. Loyalty & Retention Strategies

### The "Trip Anniversary"
- **Logic**: One year after a major trip (e.g., a Honeymoon), reach out to the client. "Thinking of your African Safari last year? Where to next?"

### Tiered Loyalty (S1 vs S3)
- **S1 (Platinum)**: Receives a physical "Welcome Home" gift (e.g., a photo book of their trip).
- **S3 (Standard)**: Receives an automated email with "Suggested Next Destinations."

---

## 5. Handling Negative Feedback (Service Recovery)

### The "Speed-to-Call" SOP
- **Rule**: If a traveler gives a score of <6, a **Senior Manager** (P1/P2) must call them within 2 hours.
- **Resolution**: Do not just apologize. Offer a "Tangible Fix" (e.g., $100 off the next booking fee).

---

## 6. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Radio Silence" Failure**: A traveler had a terrible experience, but the agent never reached out. The traveler posts a viral negative review.
- **Data Capture Error**: A traveler mentioned they "Hate cilantro," but the agent didn't save it to the CRM. The traveler is served cilantro on their next trip.
- **Loyalty Confusion**: A loyal client (S1) is sent a generic "Mass Marketing" email that treats them like a stranger.
