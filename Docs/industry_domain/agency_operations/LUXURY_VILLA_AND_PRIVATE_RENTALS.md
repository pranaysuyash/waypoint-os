# Domain Knowledge: Luxury Villa & Private Rentals

**Category**: Agency Operations  
**Focus**: Short-term rentals, villa vetting, and staffing logistics.

---

## 1. The "Vetting" SOP (Avoiding the "Airbnb Trap")

Luxury agencies do not usually book via mass-market platforms (Airbnb/VRBO). They use "Vetted" villa wholesalers (e.g., A&K Villas, Onefinestay).

### Inspection Checklist
- **Staffing**: Does the villa include a Chef, Butler, and Housekeeping?
- **Security**: Gated entry, CCTV, and local security response.
- **Maintenance**: Last renovation date and "AC reliability."
- **SOP**: The agent must request a "Floor Plan" to ensure room distribution matches the family's needs (e.g., no stairs for elderly guests).

---

## 2. The "Pre-Arrival" Concierge (Grocery & Staffing)

Unlike hotels, villas require active setup.

### The "Shopping List"
- **Logic**: The fridge should be stocked *before* the guest arrives.
- **SOP**: The agent collects the "Pantry Preference" from the traveler (Phase 1) and coordinates with the Villa Manager.

### Staffing Logistics
- **In-villa Chef**: Dietary restrictions must be communicated 14 days prior.
- **Childcare**: Vetting local nannies or bringing a "Travel Nanny."

---

## 3. Financial & Contractual Risk

### The "Security Deposit"
- **Logic**: Villas often require a massive "Damage Deposit" (e.g., $10k).
- **SOP**: Ensure the deposit is "Held" rather than "Charged" if possible, to avoid currency exchange losses on the refund.

### Cancellation Policies
- **Risk**: Villa cancellations are often 100% non-refundable from the moment of booking.
- **SOP**: **Mandatory** "Cancel for Any Reason" (CFAR) insurance must be offered.

---

## 4. Proposed Scenarios
- **The "Dirty Pool" Disaster**: A traveler pays $50k for a week in Ibiza, but the pool is green and unusable. The agent must find a new villa or move them to a 5-star hotel immediately.
- **Staffing Conflict**: The in-villa chef refuses to cook a specific meal due to a lack of ingredients. The agent must source a private courier.
- **Hidden Neighbor**: The villa description said "Private," but there is a construction site next door.
