# AI Decision Governance and Travel Agent Ethics

This document examines the governance framework for AI-assisted travel decisions, agent supervision, and ethical guardrails.

## Overview
- The role of AI in travel planning and operational decisions.
- Ethical risk tradeoffs for automated bookings, rebookings, and disruption handling.
- Governance requirements for transparency and accountability.

## Governance Framework
- Decision ownership between machine and human agent.
- Auditability, explainability, and outcome tracking.
- Policy guardrails for acceptable AI behavior.

## Operational Integration
- Supervised AI workflows in reservations and itinerary changes.
- Exception handling, escalation, and override rules.
- A/B testing ethical policy changes with real travelers.

## Compliance and Trust
- Consumer disclosure for AI-assisted decisions.
- Bias mitigation in pricing, upgrades, and disruption recovery.
- Trust signals for customers and partners.

## Future Direction
- Embedded ethics review with travel agent workflows.
- Autonomous agent APIs for travel decisioning.
- Responsible AI certifications for travel operators.
