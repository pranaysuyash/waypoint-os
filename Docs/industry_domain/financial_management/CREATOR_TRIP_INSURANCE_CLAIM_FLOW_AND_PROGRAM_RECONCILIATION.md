# Creator Trip Insurance Claim Flow and Program Reconciliation

Creator travel packages need insurance claim workflows and financial reconciliation to protect travelers and sponsors. This document explains the design of trip insurance claims, creator program reconciliation, and the controls required for creator-enabled insurance products.

## 1. Claim flow design

- Booking-triggered coverage: embed travel insurance at checkout for creator-led experiences.
- Claim submission: traveler notice, creator support, and insurer case management.
- Emergency response linkage: connect claims with incident response, medical services, and repatriation.

## 2. Program reconciliation

- Premium allocation: apportion insurance costs between creators, platforms, travelers, and suppliers.
- Reconciliation cadence: monthly/quarterly settlement of insurance premiums, claims costs, and residual balances.
- Audit controls: validate claim payments, coverage eligibility, and reconciliation accuracy.

## 3. Risk management

- Coverage design: event cancellation, emergency medical, trip interruption, and content production risk overlays.
- Fraud detection: identify suspicious claims, abusive filing, and creator-related conflicts of interest.
- Reserve management: holdback and contingent reserve planning for creator program insurance liabilities.

## 4. Traveler experience

- Disclosure: explain insurance coverage, claim process, and creator program responsibilities.
- Support integration: smooth handoff between creator customer service, travel assistance, and insurer operations.
- Reporting: track claims outcomes, policy satisfaction, and program financial performance.
