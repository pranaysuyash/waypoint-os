# Real-time Payment Settlement for Travel

This document covers real-time payment settlement and reconciliation for travel transactions.

## Overview
- Travel transactions require accelerated settlement across agents, carriers, and suppliers.
- Real-time settlement improves cash flow and reduces reconciliation friction.
- The model supports prepaid, postpaid, and intermediary financing.

## System Design
- Payment rails, APIs, and settlement engines for travel payables and receivables.
- Reconciliation workflows for booking fees, refunds, and supplier disbursements.
- Exception handling for chargebacks, currency conversions, and split payments.

## Operational Implications
- Real-time visibility into settlement status and cash position.
- Fraud detection and controls during settlement flows.
- Collaboration across finance, operations, and supplier teams.

## Commercial Impact
- Reduced working capital requirements.
- Better supplier terms from faster settlement capability.
- Product innovation around instant travel fulfillment and pay-later settlement.

## Future Trends
- Embedded settlement services inside travel platforms.
- Electronic invoicing tied to real-time travel event status.
- Settlement orchestration for multi-supplier, multi-leg journeys.
