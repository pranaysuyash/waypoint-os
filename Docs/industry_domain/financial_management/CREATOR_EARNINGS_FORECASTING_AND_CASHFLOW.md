# Creator Earnings Forecasting and Cashflow

This document explains how travel apps can forecast creator earnings and manage cashflow for creator payouts.

## Overview

- Forecasting creator earnings helps align cash reserves with payout obligations.
- Travel apps need to estimate earned commissions, bonus payments, subscription revenue shares, and refund adjustments.
- Reliable forecasting reduces liquidity risk for creator programs and supports creator trust.

## Forecast Inputs

- Booking pipeline: current and forecasted creator-attributed bookings.
- Refund/dispute estimates: historical adjustment rates by creator, product type, and market.
- Subscription churn: projected renewal and cancellation patterns for creator memberships.
- Bonus triggers: expected incentive payments tied to campaign milestones or retention outcomes.

## Cashflow Controls

- Holdback reserves: retain a portion of revenue until return windows and dispute periods close.
- Payment schedules: align creator payout timing with actual revenue recognition.
- FX planning: model foreign currency timing for international creator payments.
- Scenario planning: stress-test payout obligations across demand shocks, cancellation spikes, and campaign delays.

## Product Implications

- Use forecast accuracy to decide when to scale creator campaigns and when to pause spend.
- Share high-level payout outlooks with creators to increase transparency and confidence.
- Integrate forecasting with the app’s partner portal to show upcoming payments and owed balances.
- Ensure payouts only ramp when product and supplier capacity can support creator-driven demand.
