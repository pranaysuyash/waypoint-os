# Industry Domain Knowledge - Master Index

This directory serves as the source of truth for industry-specific knowledge, independent of persona scenarios. It documents the "mechanics" of the travel agency business.

It is intentionally broader than persona scenarios: it covers how travel agencies operate, the roles inside an agency, vendor and supplier ecosystems, pricing models, compliance, and other industry domains.
