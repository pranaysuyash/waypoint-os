## Decision

**Selected**: **Waypoint OS**

Rationale: A waypoint is a reference point in navigation — a checkpoint on a journey. For a B2B copilot that helps agencies manage bookings, revenue, and operations, "Waypoint OS" captures the idea of being the operational anchor that keeps everything on course. "OS" signals infrastructure, not just a tool. More ownable than Tripline, which is crowded.

### Previous Consideration

**Tripline** — Clean, professional, but taken by a 2010 travel planning app (iOS/Android) and a cybersecurity company (Nebulon TripLine). Domain and trademark would be difficult to secure.
